# Suppression Emails

In JetSend, you can add and manage the suppression emails on the Suppression Emails page. In Navigation, go to EMAIL and then select Suppression.

![User-added image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/1.05T5g00000Ao6V1EAJ.png)

The Suppression Emails page shows all the email addresses that have been added for suppression and enables you to perform the following actions:

* Add email addresses to which emails must be suppressed. We recommend that you add the email addresses that have unsubscribed from your services. You must also add the addresses that have caused email bounces.
* Delete email addresses that no longer need to be suppressed.
* Export the list of suppressed email addresses.
