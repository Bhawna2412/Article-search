# Track Your Email Events

Furthermore, you can see the event timeline of each email by clicking on the email record. The event timeline opens in a side panel and contains the email details along with the timeline of events.

In Navigation, go to EMAIL, and select Events. 

![User-added image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/1.05T5g00000Ao4rqEAB.png)

Click an email record to open the Timeline panel for the email.
