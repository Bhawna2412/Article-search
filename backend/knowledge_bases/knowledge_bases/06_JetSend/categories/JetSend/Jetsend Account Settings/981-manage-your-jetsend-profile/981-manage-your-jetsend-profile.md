# Manage Your JetSend Profile

Click your user name on the top-right corner of the application and from the drop-down menu, select Account Settings. The Account Settings page opens and displays the PROFILE tab.

> You can reset your login password in the PROFILE tab; however, if you want to change your login email address, you must contact us at [support@jetsend.com](mailto:support@jetsend.com).
