# Account Settings – Overview

![User-added image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/AccountDetails.05T5g00000IASudEAH.png)

Click your username on the top-right corner of the application and then select Account Settings. The Account Settings page has the following tabs - each covering specific areas of account management:

* **ACCOUNT(S)**: View and update your company information. Add and manage multiple accounts. See [Manage Your JetSend Accounts](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Manage-Your-JetSend-Accounts) and [Multi-Account Management](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Multi-Account-Management).
* **PROFILE**: Manage your personal and login information. See [Manage Your JetSend Profile](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Manage-Your-JetSend-Profile).
* **USERS:** Add new users and manage existing users in your account. See [Add and Manage Users in JetSend](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Add-and-Manage-Users-in-JetSend).
* **PLAN:** View details of your existing plan and usage information. Manage your subscription plan and add-ons such as dedicated IP. See [JetSend Subscription Plan](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/JetSend-Subscription-Plan) and [JetSend Billing Details](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/JetSend-billing-details).
* **API KEYS:** Create and manage API Keys and Master API Key. See [Create and Manage API Keys in JetSend](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Create-and-Manage-API-Keys-in-JetSend).
