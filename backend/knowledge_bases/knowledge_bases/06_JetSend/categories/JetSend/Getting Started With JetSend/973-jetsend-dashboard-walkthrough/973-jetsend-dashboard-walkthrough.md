# JetSend Dashboard Walkthrough

### Email Performance Summary Widget

The Email Performance Summary widget shows various metrics that help you assess the performance of your emails. The summary is shown in terms of the following metrics – Accepted, Delivered, Opened, Clicked, Bounced, Complained, and Suppressed. You can use the filter to select the time period for which you want to see the summary.   ![User-added image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/1.05T5g00000Ao5gDEAR.png)

### Email Delivery Score Widget

The Email Delivery Score widget shows the email deliverability on a scale of 1 to 10. The score is calculated as the ratio of emails delivered to emails sent and is based on data from the last 30 days.

![User-added image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/2.05T5g00000Ao5gNEAR.png)

### Email Accepted Widget

The Email Accepted widget shows you the total number of email requests accepted by JetSend over a period of time. You can use the filter to select the time period for which you want to see the metrics.   ![User-added image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/3.05T5g00000Ao4VxEAJ.png)

### Email Delivered Widget

The Email Delivered widget shows you the total emails delivered by JetSend over a period of time. You can use the filter to select the time period for which you want to see the metrics.

![User-added image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/4.05T5g00000Ao5hQEAR.png)

### Monthly Email Usage Widget

The Monthly Email Usage widget shows the number of emails sent as compared to the total emails allocated as per your plan. The widget is aimed at keeping you apprised of your email usage. The use of different colors shows different usage thresholds. The email usage below 50% is denoted in blue, between 50-75% is denoted in yellow, and above 75% is denoted in red. ![User-added image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/5.05T5g00000Ao5hpEAB.png)
