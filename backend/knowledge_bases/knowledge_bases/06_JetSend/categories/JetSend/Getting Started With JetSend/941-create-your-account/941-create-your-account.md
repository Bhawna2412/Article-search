# Create Your Account

If you have more questions, please reach out to our support team at [support@jetsend.com](mailto:support@jetsend.com).

## Pricing Plan

The pricing plan for JetSend is available on our website ([https://jetsend.com/pricing](http://galaxy.maropost.com/home/leaving?allowTrusted=1&target=https%3A%2F%2Fjetsend.com%2Fpricing)) and outlines all the plans in detail.

If you are already a customer and want to know your pricing plan, go to the Account Settings page. For more information, see [Manage Your Subscription Plan](http://galaxy.maropost.com/home/leaving?allowTrusted=1&target=https%3A%2F%2Fsupport.jetsend.com%2Farticle%2Fplan%2F).

> For any queries regarding the payment plans, please reach out to our support team at support@jetsend.com.

## Register With JetSend

To get started, sign up for a free trial. Once you have completed the registration and verification process, your free account is created and available to you. When you decide to upgrade to a paid plan, you can do so on the Account Settings page of the application.
