# Manage your User Profile

Your profile is where you add your name, your contact telephone number, and your preferred time zone.

Click on your user name in the top right of the menu bar, and click on **Profile** from the menu.

InboxAware stores all timestamps in UTC. Set your preferred time zone to automatically display every date/time you see in InboxAware adjusted to your location.

To change your password, click the link to change password. InboxAware enforces best practices for strong passwords including:

* Must be at least 8 characters long.
* Must contain at least 1 upper case character.
* Must contain at least 1 special character (-,+!).
* Must contain at least 1 number.
* Cannot have 2 consecutive characters.
