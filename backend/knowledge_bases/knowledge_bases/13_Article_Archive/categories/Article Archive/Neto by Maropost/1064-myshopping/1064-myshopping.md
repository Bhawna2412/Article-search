# Myshopping

#### This add-on was built by Maropost

Add-ons and Integrations built in-house by Maropost are documented and supported by our customer service team. If you're having issues with this integration, [let us know](https://www.netohq.com/support/s/ask-us)!

### Set up the Myshopping Add-on

To install and set up the Myshopping add-on:

1. 
  Sign up for a [Myshopping](https://merchant.myshopping.com.au/Signup1.aspx) account. Copy your account ID as you'll need to enter this into Maropost Commerce soon.
2. 
  In your Maropost Commerce control panel navigate to **Other** > **Shopping feeds**.
3. 
  Find Myshopping and click the **Install** button.
4. 
  Enter your Account ID and click the **Install** button.
5. 
  You can modify the Feed URL, filtering products that you don’t wish to push to Myshopping. To do this, click the **Edit** button.
6. 
  Scroll down to the **File Builder** section and click the **Filter** tab to make products, categories, pricing and other changes. Scroll down and click **Save Changes**.

  ![image2](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/Myshopping2.05T5g00000w5qAHEAY.png)
7. 
  To make changes to the feed scroll back up the top and click the **Generate Data File** button and click **Download Data File** button to review the results of your changes.
