# Shopmania

#### ![Image1](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/Shopmania1.05T5g00000w5phxEAA.jpg)
This add-on was built by Maropost

Add-ons and Integrations built in-house by Maropost are documented and supported by our customer service team. If you're having issues with this integration, [let us know](https://www.netohq.com/support/s/ask-us)!

### Overview

ShopMania is a shopping comparison website which allows customers find and compare millions of products, all in one spot.

Maropost Commerce data feeds send information to ShopMania to update your products through the shopping comparison website.

**Tip**: Maropost Commerce partners can set this up for you. [Visit the job request portal](https://www.netohq.com/job-request-portal) for a quote.

![image2](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/Shopmania2.05T5g00000w5pmiEAA.png)

### Set up the Shopmania Add-on

To install and set up the Shopmania add-on:

1. 
  Sign up for a [Shopmania](https://partner.shopmania.com.au/?go=home) account.
2. 
  In your Maropost Commerce control panel navigate to **Other** > **Shopping feeds**.
3. 
  Find ShopMania and click the **Install** button.

  ![image3](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/Shopmania3.05T5g00000w5oNKEAY.png)
4. 
  You’ll be provided with a Feed URL. Provide this URL to ShopMania. This is a link to a file that contains a list of your products in the required format.

  ![image4](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/Shopmania4.05T5g00000w5pnqEAA.png)
5. 
  You can modify the Feed URL, filtering products that you don’t wish to push to ShopMania. To do this, click the **Edit** button.

  ![image5](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/Shopmania5.05T5g00000w5pooEAA.png)
6. 
  Scroll down to the **File Builder** section and click the **Filter** tab to make products, categories, pricing and other changes. Scroll down and click **Save Changes**.

  ![image6](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/Shopmania6.05T5g00000w5pprEAA.png)
7. 
  To make changes to the feed scroll back up the top and click the **Generate Data File** button and click **Download Data File** button to review the results of your changes.

  ![image7](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/Shopmania7.05T5g00000w5pqBEAQ.png)
