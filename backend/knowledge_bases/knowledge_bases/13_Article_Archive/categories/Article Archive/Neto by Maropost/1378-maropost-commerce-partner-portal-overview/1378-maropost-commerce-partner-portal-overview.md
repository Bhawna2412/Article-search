#### My referral forgot to use my referral link - is there a way that this can be linked?

The [referral link](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/create-edit-and-update-referrals) is the only way that the referrals can be tracked, therefore it's important that you use this link in all promotions.

#### How do I edit my contact details?

To change your contact details, please [contact the Maropost Commerce Cloud Accounts team](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/contactsupport/contact-commerce).

#### My stores are missing from the portal, how do I get them to show?

If you believe there are stores that you created within the Partner Portal are missing, please [contact our Support team](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/partner-portal/contact-support) with a list of the stores to investigate.
