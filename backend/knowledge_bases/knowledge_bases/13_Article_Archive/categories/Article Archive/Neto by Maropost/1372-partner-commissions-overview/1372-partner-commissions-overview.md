### What are the Current Partner Commission Structures?

There are currently two types of commission structures available for partners:

- 
  **Design and Solution partners** are offered a monthly ongoing commission based on the subscription value for the client. The commission rate is 20% of the subscription revenue for the lifetime that the customer is with Maropost Commerce.
- 
  **Referral partners**, where there is no management of the customer, are provided with a one-off $500.00 payment for new customers who start trading on the platform.

### Can I get paid for previous commissions before I joined the program?

The Maropost Commerce program tracks referrals that are new to Maropost Commerce. We cannot retrospectively apply payments for customers that have already signed up to Maropost Commerce.

### Commission Payments
