# Point of Sale Outlets and Product Prices

**Please Note**: Maropost Commerce Cloud POS is not available outside of Australia and New Zealand.

An outlet is another name for a store or shop front. You can keep track of stock and sales at multiple outlets from a single Maropost Commerce control panel. If you do have multiple shop fronts, you must setup an outlet for each one.

### In this Article

- [Add or Update Warehouses / Outlets](#)
- [Set up Different Prices for Different Outlets](#2)
- [Update Prices and Promotions on Different Outlets](#3)

### Add or Update Warehouses / Outlets

1. 
  In your Maropost Commerce control panel navigate to **Stock Control** and select **Locations/Warehouses**.
2. 
  Click on the green **Add New** button.

  ![image1](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/PointofSaleOutletsandProductPrices1.05T5g00000szbtwEAA.png)
3. 
  Fill out your warehouse or outlet details, and make sure to enter a **Phone Number** and an **Address**. This is important as Maropost POS registers linked to this location will use these details on tax invoices / receipts.

  ![image2](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/PointofSaleOutletsandProductPrices2.05T5g00000szcGyEAI.png)

### Set up Different Prices for Different Outlets

**Please Note**: The **Customer Groups** add-on is required from the Maropost Commerce Cloud add-on store.

[▶ YouTube Video](https://youtu.be/Xe9i2JuJa_I)
The Customer Groups feature allows you to have different pricing for different outlets by assigning warehouses with customer groups. Additionally, with webstore and POS store you can have different pricing on different channels.

1. 
  In your Maropost Commerce control panel navigate to **Stock Control** and select **Locations/Warehouses**.
2. 
  Edit an existing or **Add New** warehouse, and set your customer group.

  ![image3](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/PointofSaleOutletsandProductPrices3.05T5g00000szcHXEAY.png)
3. 
  In case you have a customer already assigned with a certain price group attached to a sale, then the warehouse/outlet pricing over rides the customer card.

  If there is a requirement to over ride the customer pricing group then the same can be achieved by disabling the **Use Customer Group** toggle on the customer card in POS.

  ![image4](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/PointofSaleOutletsandProductPrices4.05T5g00000szc4yEAA.png)
4. 
  Please ensure you've completed a synchronisation in POS once you've completed your setup.

### Update Prices and Promotions on Different Outlets

**Please Note**: The **Customer Groups** add-on is required from the Maropost Commerce add-on store

1. 
  You can edit this in the Maropost Commerce control panel dashboard via the product card by navigating to **Products** > **View Products** and editing the product you wish to change.
2. 
  Scroll down to the **Pricing & Tax** section and make changes to the pricing.

  ![image5](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/PointofSaleOutletsandProductPrices5.05T5g00000szc1aEAA.png)
3. 
  You can also edit the pricing in POS by clicking the 'i' icon on the individual product and clicking **Edit**.

  ![image6](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/PointofSaleOutletsandProductPrices6.05T5g00000szaw0EAA.png)
4. 
  Here you can edit the pricing on the product.

  ![image7](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/PointofSaleOutletsandProductPrices7.05T5g00000szcCTEAY.png)
5. 
  Please ensure you've completed a synchronisation in POS once you've completed your setup.
