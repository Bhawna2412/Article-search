# Customise POS Receipts

**Please Note**: Maropost Commerce Cloud POS is not available outside of Australia and New Zealand.

### In this Article

- [Customise Receipts](#1)
- [Update Business Details on Receipts](#2)

> **Important:** You will need to sync your registers after making changes to your details for them to display on printed receipts.

### Customise Receipts

To customise your POS Receipts:

1. 
  In your Maropost Commerce control panel, navigate to **Point of Sale** and click **Receipt Customisation**.

  ![image1](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/CustomisePOSReceipts1.05T5g00000szX2IEAU.png)
2.   - POS receipt logo.
  - Toggle the company name on your receipt.
  - Sales and returns headings.
  - Header and a footer text.
  - Layby terms (requires the Layby for POS add-on).

  On the receipt customisation page, you can edit:

> **Note:** For best results, use a black and white image. Logos will be resized automatically.

  ![image2](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/CustomisePOSReceipts2.05T5g00000szX6PEAU.png)
3. 
  Once complete, click **Save Changes**. Using the above example, here's how our new receipt looks when printed through a browser. Logos will be black and white when printed on a thermal printer.

  ![image3](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/CustomisePOSReceipts3.05T5g00000szX1VEAU.png)

**Please Note**: Email receipts will also include the header and footer information entered, and the invoice logo configured in Settings & Tools > Logos.

### Update Business Details on Receipts

Your receipt will display your business details including your business name, address, phone number and ABN. Follow these instructions to edit these details:

#### Business Name and ABN

1. 
  In your Maropost Commerce control panel navigate to **Settings & Tools** > **Business Details**.
2. 
  Update the ABN and/or business name fields as you'd like them to display on your receipts.

#### Outlet Address

Maropost POS supports multiple outlets, so your receipts will be printed with the details of each outlet rather than your primary address.

1. 
  In your Maropost Commerce control panel navigate to **Stock Control** > **Warehouses / Locations**.
2. 
  Select the outlet you want to update the details for.
3. 
  Follow the on-screen instructions to edit the address of the related outlet.
