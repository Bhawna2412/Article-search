# Release v3.12.4 - POS and Back Office

# Release v3.12.4 - POS and Back Office

**Release Date:** 10-08-20

 In this release we've made it easier to identify when a Special Order is ready for action, as well as made 3 improvements.

---

## Special Orders Alert in POS

Users can now see an alert within POS when there is a Special Order sale ready to action, making it easier than ever to dispatch your orders quickly to customers!

The alert is displayed on the left-hand side of the POS homepage for the Outlet the user is logged into

**Note:**The alert will only count orders where the current Outlet is the Fulfilment Outlet i.e. stock is being dispatched to the customer.

![mceclip0.png](https://ress.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/360004791996/mceclip0.png)

Click the alert to open the Order Search window.

![mceclip2.png](https://ress.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/360004671935/mceclip2.png)

A list of Special Orders for the current Outlet will be displayed. From this window you will be able to preview the sale, or click Open to open the order for editing.
