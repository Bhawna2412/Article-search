# Grant Access To a Client For A Webstore You've Created

When you create a webstore from [within the Partner Tools Home page](http://galaxy.maropost.com/home/leaving?allowTrusted=1&target=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.netohq.com%2Fsupport%2Fs%2Farticle%2FUnderstand-the-Partner-Tools-Home-page), your clients do not get access to the webstore by default.  
  
During the trial or at the point of transferring ownership, you can add a client to the webstore by adding a staff user with administrator access.

#### To add a staff user:

1. [Create A Maropost Commerce Store On Behalf Of A Client](/articles/Knowledge/Create-A-Store-On-Behalf-Of-A-Client).
2. Refer to the instructions for [Adding a User account here](http://galaxy.maropost.com/home/leaving?allowTrusted=1&target=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.netohq.com%2Fsupport%2Fs%2Farticle%2FDoes-each-staff-member-have-their-own-account-and-dashboard).
