# Deploy - Shopify Gift Vouchers

This article explains how to deploy a Shopify Gift Vouchers licence once a signed contract has been received:

Enable Shopify Gift Voucher Feature in MGMT

![rtaImage (61).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000powgJEAQ.jpg)

1. Login to MGMT

2. Navigate to **Licenses > Shopify Site List**

3. Filter by company name in **Admin Link**

4. Click on **Edit** and ensure that **Gift Vouchers**is ticked

5. Select **Update**

Zendesk - Sending the email 

![gift_voucher_snip.PNG](https://ress.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/360002668776/gift_voucher_snip.PNG)

1. Add a new ticket

2. Add the email address for the contact on the solutions agreement

3. Select the macro **Implementation > Shopify Gift Vouchers: Getting Started**

5. Submit the ticket as Solved
