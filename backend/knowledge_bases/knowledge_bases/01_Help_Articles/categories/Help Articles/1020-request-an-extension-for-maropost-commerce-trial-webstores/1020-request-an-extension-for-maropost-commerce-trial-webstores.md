# Request an Extension for Maropost Commerce Trial Webstores

### Steps

1. 
  Log in to the Maropost Commerce [partner portal](http://galaxy.maropost.com/home/leaving?allowTrusted=1&target=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.netohq.com%2Fpartners%2Fs%2F).
2. 
  The **Stores** page within the Partner Tools portal lists all the trials created through the Partner Portal and their trial period. Click on the **Store Name** of the store due to expire.
3. 
  Enter your **Username** and **Password**.
4. 
  Within the Tool bar, the number of days left in the customer's trial is displayed.

  To request a trial extension, [send an email request to the Maropost Commerce Partner Support team](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/partner-portal/contact-support).

### Result

You have successfully requested a further extension of a Maropost Commerce Trial webstore.
