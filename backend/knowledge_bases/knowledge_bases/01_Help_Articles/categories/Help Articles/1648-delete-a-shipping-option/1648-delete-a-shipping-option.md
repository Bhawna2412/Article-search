# Delete a Shipping Option

To delete a shipping option:

1. 
  In your Maropost Commerce control panel navigate to **Settings & Tools** > **Shipping**.
2. 
  Click the **Shipping Option** link in the **Shipping** section.
3. 
  Select the checkbox next to the shipping option you wish to delete.
4. 
  Click the **With x Selected** button at the bottom of the page.
5. 
  Click **Delete** from the options that appear.
6. 
  In the pop-up that appears, click the **OK** button to delete the shipping option.

Your shipping option is now deleted.
