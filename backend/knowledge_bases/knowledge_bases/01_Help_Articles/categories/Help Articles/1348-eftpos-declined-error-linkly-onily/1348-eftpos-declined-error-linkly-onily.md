# EFTPOS Declined error (LINKLY ONILY)

This guide is for when you receive a **Transaction Declined** prompt immediately after using the PC-EFTPOS button and your EFTPOS is showing   **ONLINE --**   for [Linkly](http://galaxy.maropost.com/home/leaving?allowTrusted=1&target=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.linkly.com.au%2F) connected EFTPOS terminals.

![mceclip0.png](https://ress.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/4614139677199/mceclip0.png)

![mceclip0.png](https://ress.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/4614123868943/mceclip0.png)

Please follow the steps below on how to rectify the error.

---

1. Press **Cancel** to close the EFTPOS payment window
2. **Log out** of POS ! IMPORTANT !
3. Click the **Start** menu
4. Type **Services**
5. Open '**Services**'  
   ![image](/attachments/token/nJm3Vo5Qwn3NyZyuKldmG8ztR/?name=inline-1813439599.png)
6. Find '**Linkly Client**'
7. Press '**Restart**'  
   ![mceclip1.png](https://ress.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/4603038208015/mceclip1.png)  
     

   Wait for **Linkly** to turn green

     
   ![image](https://ress.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/4614139677199/mceclip0.png)
8. Find '**Retail Express Hardware Service**'
9. Press '**Restart**'  
   ![image](/attachments/token/9JyjDQUowqjSk0wAEwl22GQ8r/?name=inline1920269069.png)
10. Log back into **POS**
