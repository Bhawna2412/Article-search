# Preparing for the V2 Upgrade

The below checklist outlines vital information required to prepare for the upgrade to **V2 Retail Express POS**. Each step details a different area of preparation; from hardware, through to staff training. Each step should be reviewed ahead of your upgrade.

> **Important:** each step is crucial to avoid disruption to your business post v2 Upgrade. Follow the steps below to ensure you can continue to print receipts and/or use integrated EFTPOS.

#### [Step 1 - Review your hardware and PCs](#collapseOne)

V2 POS has a different set of minimum computer requirements.

For supported POS Terminals, Operating Systems and hardware, please see:[**Supported Hardware Documentation**](http://galaxy.maropost.com/home/leaving?allowTrusted=1&target=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.retailexpressmedia.com%2Fdocuments%2Fimplementation%2FImplementation%2520Overview.pdf)

**Important Note:** Windows XP is no longer a supported Operating System and you will not be able to use Receipt Printers or POS on a Windows Xp Based machine.

#### [Step 2 - Install Receipt Printers and Integrated EFTPOS](#collapseTwo)

V2 POS requires a local application to be installed on your PCs to controls the receipt printers and integrated EFTPOS terminals (where applicable).

> **Important:** If you fail to complete this installation, you will be unable to print receipts or use integrated EFTPOS (Tyro or PC-eftpos) post-upgrade, until it is successfully installed.

For step-by-step installation instructions, please see :[Hardware Installation - Receipt Printers & Integrated Eftpos](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Installing-Receipt-Printers-and-Integrated-EFTPOS)

#### [ Step 3 - Integrated Web Store Details (if applicable)](#collapseThree)

If you **do not have an integrated web store** proceed directly to **Step 4 - Staff Training**.

If you **do have a web store:**

- If your web store was purchased and built by Retail Express, and hasn't been modified by a 3rd party, proceed directly to **Step 4 - Staff Training.**
- If you have an integrated web store, built by an external developer, additional steps will need to be implemented on the night of your v2 Upgrade; [click here to review the full details](http://galaxy.maropost.com/home/leaving?allowTrusted=1&target=https%3A%2F%2Fress.zendesk.com%2Fhc%2Fen-us%2Farticles%2F115002758373)

#### [ Step 4 - Staff Training](#collapseFour)

**V 2 POS** is significantly different to V1. Training **will** be required for your POS Users to familiarise themselves with new processes, and new look and feel.

We have made a staff training site available for preparation for v2 Upgrade.

To access the demonstration site, follow the below steps:

> **Note:** login details are case-sensitive

1. **Enter this URL** into your browser address bar:  [https://training.retailexpress.com.au](http://galaxy.maropost.com/home/leaving?allowTrusted=1&target=http%3A%2F%2Ftraining.retailexpress.com.au%2F)
2. **Enter the User ID :** V2USER
3. **Enter the Password :** v2upgrade

#### [ Step 5 - Final Details to Note](#collapseFive)

**The following processes have been locked down to increase data integrity:**

- Y*ou are no longer able to perform a negative dispatch nor un-fulfil a previously fulfilled product*
- *100% payment due is required for fulfilled items (excepting Account Customers)*
- *The following 'legacy' invoice statuses are no longer in use: * Accepted , Accepted_Collected, Accepted CT, Awaiting CHQ, Lodged, On Account, Partner Awaiting DD, Phone Order, Refunded, Requires Verification, Requires Verification - Email, Telesale Awaiting CC, Telesale Awaiting CHQ, Telesale Awaiting DD
- *There is a new invoice number and customer number format applicable*
