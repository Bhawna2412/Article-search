# Set up Maropost Commerce Cloud Pick'n Pack

Pick'n Pack is optimised for Apple iPhone and iPod devices, but can also be run from a PC or Android browser.

### In this Article

- [Install Pick'n Pack iOS App (for Apple mobile devices)](#1)
- [Set up Pick'n Pack iOS App Settings](#2)
- [Launch Pick'n Pack from a PC or Android device](#3)

### Install Pick'n Pack iOS App

Search for and Pick'n Pack in the app store, or by using [this link](http://galaxy.maropost.com/home/leaving?allowTrusted=1&target=https%3A%2F%2Fitunes.apple.com%2Fau%2Fapp%2Fneto-pick-n-pack%2Fid626685026%3Fmt%3D8) to install it..

### Set up Pick'n Pack iOS App Settings

Once installed, you can open and set up the app to see your sales orders:

1. 
  Open the app and tap on the icon at the top right of the screen to set up the app. In the **Account Name** field, enter your control panel domain address (eg. yourdomain.com.au)
2. 
  Toggle the **Barcode Scanner Settings** so that it is on. If this cannot be toggled on, you either do not have the drivers or adequate firmware installed for your barcode scanner. See [this article](http://galaxy.maropost.com/home/leaving?allowTrusted=1&target=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.netohq.com%2Fsupport%2Fs%2Farticle%2FLineaPro-Firmware-Update) to learn how to update your scanner.
3. 
  Leave **Barcode Type** as **Default Barcode List **, unless you have a special type of barcode you are trying to scan in the extended or ISO 15424 barcode list.
4.   - Set the **Label Printer ** setting to **Air Print**.
  - Toggle **Always Use Label Printer** to **On**
  - Set **Print Preview** to **Off**.

  If you wish to print labels from Pick'n Pack, in the **Printer Settings** section:

Your app is now set up. See the related articles on how to process orders through Pick'n Pack.

### Launch Pick'n Pack from a PC or Android device

To access Pick'n Pack from a browser, please  log into your control panel, and hover over your username. Click on **Mobile Pick & Pack Interface** to launch it in your browser.

You can also add **/do/suppliers/mobile** to the end of your website address (e.g. www.mymaropost.com/do/suppliers/mobile) to access the login screen.
