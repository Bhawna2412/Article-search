# Create a Maropost Commerce Store On Behalf of a Client

### Steps

1. 
  Log in to the partner portal and navigate to **Stores** > **Create New Store**.
2. 
  From the **Create New Store** page, enter the customer's **Business Name**.
3. 
  Enter the customer's email address in the **Merchant's email** field.
4. 
  Select the customer's location **Country** from the dropdown.
5. 
  Select the customer's business **Industry vertical** from the dropdown.
6. 
  Select the customer's **Trial type** (either 'Customer' or 'Sandbox') from the dropdown.
7. 
  Select a business decription from the **Described as** dropdown.
8. 
  Click the radio button next to the relevant trial period ('Choose the length of the trial').
9. 
  Click the **Create store **button. Maropost Commerce will advise you that a store is being created. Once completed, the store details will display.

### Result

You have successfully created a Maropost Commerce store on behalf of a client. You can either log in to the newly-created store, view the webstore or view the list of previously-created stores.
