# Deploy - Shopify Click & Collect

This article explains how to deploy a Shopify Click and Collect licence once a signed contract has been received:

Enable Shopify Click and Collect Feature in MGMT

![rtaImage (55).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000powffEAA.jpg)

1. Login to MGMT

2. Navigate to **Shopify > Shopify Site List**

3. Filter by company name in **Admin Link**

4. Click on **Edit** and ensure that **Click and Collect** is ticked

5. Select **Update**

Enable Shopify Click and Collect Feature in Retail Express db

![rtaImage (56).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000powfpEAA.jpg)

1. Login to client site eg. xxx.retailexpress.com.au

2. Navigate to **Settings > Global Settings**

3. Under section heading **8. Web Store** Ensure that Click and Collect Enabled is ticked

![rtaImage (57).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000powfuEAA.jpg)4. Select **Save Changes**

Zendesk - Sending the email 

1. Add a new ticket

2. Add the email address for the contact on the solutions agreement

3. Select the macro **Implementation > Shopify Click & Collect: Getting Started**

5. Submit the ticket as Solved
