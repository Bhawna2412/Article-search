# Troubleshooting POS performance and speed issues

If you're finding your system is performing slower than normal, Retail Express offers a handy troubleshooting guide. The guide is designed to step you through a series of checks to ensure there isn't anything on your network potentially impacting on the performance of your system.

The guide is broken into two sections:

- Basic troubleshooting, which can be performed by staff confident with using computers
- Advanced troubleshooting, recommended for IT professionals and technicians.
