# Admin Forms - Re-activating an outlet

This article explains how to re-activate a previously de-activated outlet/unmanned warehouse in an existing clients Retail Express database.

Re-Enabling the Licence

![User-added image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/1.05T5g00000nHIa2EAG.png)

1. Login to clients Retail Express

2. Navigate to **Settings > Locations / Outlets > Outlets**

3. Select the relevant outlet and click the blue Edit button which will populate the **Outlet Details** section in the top half of the screen

4. Tick the *Enabled* checkbox

5. Click **Save**

6. Repeat as necessary if they are re-activating more than one licence

Zoho Subscription - Updating Monthly Hosting

1. Login to *Zoho Subscriptions*

2. Search for clients monthly subscription

3. Edit subscription to update the quantity field for the number of outlets

4. Click **Save**

5. When it asks if you want this to be effective immediately or next term - say **Immediately** and it will automatically generate a pro-rata invoice

5. Add comment stating "Reactivation of outlet xxx [Zendesk Ticket #] pro-rata invoice raised"

6. If an Ezypay customer login to Ezypay to update their ongoing monthly fees from the next monthly invoice cycle including the pro-rata amount for the next invoice

Zendesk - Sending the email

![User-added image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/2.05T5g00000nHIaCEAW.png)

1. Update the email address to be the one on the completed online form in original ticket

3. Select the macro **Forms > Re-activate an Outlet - completed**

5. Submit the ticket as **Solved**
