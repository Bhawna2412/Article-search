# Hardware Services

# Hardware Services

***Settings** > **POS Settings** > **Hardware Services***

The Hardware Service settings relate to the Retail Express hardware utility which you download onto your PC when setting up a new computer for POS, or going through the implementation phase.

![image](https://ress.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/360000062015/blobid0.png)

### Installing Receipt Printers and Integrated EFTPOS

For information on how to install a Receipt Printer or Integrated EFTPOS refer to the article [Receipt Printer and Integrated EFTPOS - Hardware Utility Setup and FAQ](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Installing-Receipt-Printers-and-Integrated-EFTPOS).

The **Hardware Utility Configuration** tab will show you all hardware downloads done on your registers or back office. These downloads will help you track IP addresses and which registers have installed hardware. ![image](https://ress.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/360000061975/blobid1.png)

When clicking the blue pencil icon for edit, you will have the option to select whichever registers are assigned to this hardware service.

![image](https://ress.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/360000061995/blobid2.png)

The **Local Hardware Services tab**will allow you to select/deselect the "Forced Local" check box. This is a technical setting used by us to force the computer to use the local hardware.
