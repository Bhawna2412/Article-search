# Updating the Back Office notification sidebar

The Back Office notification sidebar is used to keep customers up-to-date with news, tips and tricks and any other important information. Previous content has included:

* Training Academy updates
* Releases (including a link to release notes)
* Helpful links (typically hardware)
* Tips for common issues (e.g. if there's a particular issue affecting POS, or Internet Explorer updates, we display links to step-by-step articles to help cut down on support queries)
* Tips and useful information

  
  

![rtaImage - 2023-07-10T035203.364.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppieVEAQ.jpg)

This notification bar is managed via an embedded Zoho form.

To update the notification bar:

1. Log into Zoho forms using the contact@ account (otherwise you may not have permission to access the form for editing)
2. Open the form [**Retail Express Notification & News**](http://galaxy.maropost.com/home/leaving?allowTrusted=1&target=https%3A%2F%2Fforms.zoho.com%2Fvirtualoffice112%2Fform%2FBackOfficeCommuniqu%2Fbuilder)
3. Click the main content
4. Select **Edit Content**on the right hand-side
5. The editor will open  
   ![rtaImage - 2023-07-10T035210.352.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppieaEAA.jpg)
6. Edit the content as required
7. The horizontal lines can be a pain to edit; enter some text before/after the line, click at the end of the line and press **Delete** on the keyboard - the line should be removed  
   ![rtaImage - 2023-07-10T035217.573.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppiepEAA.jpg)
8. Click **Save**
9. The changes will be automatically saved
