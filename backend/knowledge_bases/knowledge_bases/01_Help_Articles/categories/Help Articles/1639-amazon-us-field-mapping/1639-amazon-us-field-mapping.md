# Amazon US Field Mapping

Product field mapping is broken up into several parts based on Amazon’s requirements. You can select from product fields (including custom product fields), product specifics, or hard coded values when you match them to Amazon.

**Tip**: Now is also a good time to check the price you have mapped in **Amazon US** > **Settings** in the **Product Pricing** section.

To map your product fields:

1. In your Maropost Commerce control panel navigate to **Amazon US** > **Product Field Mapping**.
2. Click on **Compliance** to expand the fields which require mapping.
3. Match up the Amazon fields to appropriate Maropost Commerce fields, or use the defaults already selected. Click the **Save** button.

   **Tip**: HTML tags that aren’t allowed by Amazon are automatically stripped out of your description when Maropost Commerce sends the data to Amazon. If you map the **Shipping-Template** to a [custom product field](http://galaxy.maropost.com/home/leaving?allowTrusted=1&target=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.netohq.com%2Fsupport%2Fs%2Farticle%2FCan-I-add-my-own-additional-and-custom-product-fields-to-populate-with-data), or the shipping category, you can ensure each product uses the correct shipping in Amazon. Just make sure the names in Maropost Commerce and Amazon match exactly, so that the correct shipping costs are applied to the listing on Amazon. Otherwise Amazon will use the default template.

   [![](https://maropost-inc--c.documentforce.com/servlet/servlet.FileDownload?file=0155g000000uDaN)](http://galaxy.maropost.com/home/leaving?allowTrusted=1&target=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.netohq.com%2Fsupport%2Fservlet%2FrtaImage%3Feid%3Dka10K000000IUht%26feoid%3D00N2800000JL2j1%26refid%3D0EM0K000000rwWW)
4. Click **Images** and map your product images to the Amazon image fields. The defaults will be set, but you can change them to specific image fields where you’ve uploaded images that meet [Amazon’s requirements](http://galaxy.maropost.com/home/leaving?allowTrusted=1&target=https%3A%2F%2Fsellercentral.amazon.com.au%2Fgp%2Fhelp%2Fexternal%2F201270290).

   [![](https://maropost-inc--c.documentforce.com/servlet/servlet.FileDownload?file=0155g000000uDaS)](http://galaxy.maropost.com/home/leaving?allowTrusted=1&target=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.netohq.com%2Fsupport%2Fservlet%2FrtaImage%3Feid%3Dka10K000000IUht%26feoid%3D00N2800000JL2j1%26refid%3D0EM0K000000rwWb)

   Click the **Save** button.
5. Click **Offer** to map the selling dates, and max order quantity.

   [![](https://maropost-inc--c.documentforce.com/servlet/servlet.FileDownload?file=0155g000000uDaX)](http://galaxy.maropost.com/home/leaving?allowTrusted=1&target=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.netohq.com%2Fsupport%2Fservlet%2FrtaImage%3Feid%3Dka10K000000IUht%26feoid%3D00N2800000JL2j1%26refid%3D0EM0K000000rwWg)

   Click the **Save** button.

You’ve completed your product field mapping, and are now ready to list products on Amazon. To choose which products you list, please follow the steps to [approve products for Amazon](http://galaxy.maropost.com/home/leaving?allowTrusted=1&target=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.netohq.com%2Fsupport%2Fs%2Farticle%2Flist-products-on-amazon-australia%233).
