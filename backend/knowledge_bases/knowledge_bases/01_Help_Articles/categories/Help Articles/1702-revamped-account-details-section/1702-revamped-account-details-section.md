# Revamped Account Details Section

**Release Date** - 22nd January 2021

## Overview

In this special release update, we bring you the spruced-up Account Details section. Explore the modernized look and experience the comfort in managing your account information; we would love to know what you think!

## Enhancements

**Revamped Account Details**

We simplified how you access and manage your account information. As before, the Account Details page is available in the drop-down list when you click your user name on the top-right corner of the application.

![Account_Details_Nav.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/05T5g00000BkWjs.05T5g00000BkWjsEAF.png)

The page now consists of account-related information and tasks neatly grouped into specific tabs:

* PROFILE – Update your profile information, login information, and profile-specific preferences. To learn more, see [Managing Your Profile](http://galaxy.maropost.com/home/leaving?allowTrusted=1&target=https%3A%2F%2Fsupport.maropost.com%2Fhc%2Fen-us%2Farticles%2F360015616574).
* ACCOUNT – Update your account details and settings. To learn more, see [Managing Your Accounts](http://galaxy.maropost.com/home/leaving?allowTrusted=1&target=https%3A%2F%2Fsupport.maropost.com%2Fhc%2Fen-us%2Farticles%2F360015705613).
* USERS – Manage your account users and their privileges. To learn more, see [Managing Users and Their Privileges](http://galaxy.maropost.com/home/leaving?allowTrusted=1&target=https%3A%2F%2Fsupport.maropost.com%2Fhc%2Fen-us%2Farticles%2F360017804854).

For a detailed comparison of what has changed, see [Account Settings](http://galaxy.maropost.com/home/leaving?allowTrusted=1&target=https%3A%2F%2Fsupport.maropost.com%2Fhc%2Fen-us%2Farticles%2F360057419353).
