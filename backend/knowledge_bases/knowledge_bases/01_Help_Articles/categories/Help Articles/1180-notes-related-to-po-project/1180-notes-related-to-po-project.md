# Notes related to PO Project

## What's changed - Purchase Orders

* PLU scan - you can now continue to scan products to increase the quantity on the purchase order (instead of only being able to scan a product once and add the quantity manually)
* Advanced Search
  + No longer limited to 100 products
  + On-screen filters to further refine your search results
  + Column toggle to customise the displayed fields
  + Toggle to include disabled products
* Column select - control which columns are displayed
  + Changes between new PO (price-related) and on-order (quantity related)
  + Doesn't save the settings, will need to change each time
  + Preset preferences with intelligence depending on the stage of the PO
* Filtering
* Pagination
* Supplier SKU 2 - search, scan features
* Status - PO receiving in progress doesn't exist
* In-line editing for quantity and supplier buy (most complained-about item)
* New actions:
  + Bulk edit products e.g. increase supplier buy (new modal when ready)
  + Ability to enable/disable products from the PO screen
  + Publish to Webstores and Shopify
    - (streamlined processing, saves steps, saves time) - other channels to come in the future
  + Bulk Update your supplier buy pricing:
    - overwrite the supplier buy price on product edit screen to match the PO
    - overwrite the prices on the PO to match the supplier buy price![ReceiptViews.gif](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppVgYEAU.jpg)

## Different Page Views

### Stock Receipt and Invoice Details

You can now select between different "Views" to customise the layout based on your process (Stock Receipt or Invoice) or customise which columns are displayed to your needs.

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Stock Receipt** | **Invoice Detail** |
| Focused on Products and Quantities | Focused on Invoice Details and Costs |
| Quantity columns visible, costs hidden | Quantity columns hidden, costs visible |

### Custom View

In addition to this, you can also use the Column menu to enable/disable columns and create your own custom view (for that session only).
