# Creating Existing Client Quotes

**Creating A Quote**

Use ?National Warehouse Supplies? as the client when testing anything.

1. Search for the client in Zoho
2. Click Quotes on the left
3. Click New on the right  
   ![rtaImage (13).jpeg](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000powCdEAI.jpg)
4. Under Contact Name select the Contact  
     
   ![rtaImage (14).jpeg](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000powCiEAI.jpg)
5. Under Which Country is this client based select Australia or NZ  
     
   ![rtaImage (15).jpeg](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000povKnEAI.jpg)
6. Under Opportunity Name click +New Opportunity  
   ![rtaImage (16).jpeg](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000povvdEAA.jpg)
7. Under Amount enter the Upfront Total dollar value of the quote  
    If there is no upfront amount (ex. Shopify Addons) enter 0
8. Under Account Name enter the Zoho name for the client
9. Under Opportunity Name enter in the following format:  
    ?Account Name + What is being added?  
     
    ?National Warehouse Supplies Shopify Connector?
10. Select the Industry vertical the client works in
11. Under Stage select 5 ? Approval
12. Under Number of Stores enter the number *being added* as part of this quote  
      
     If you are adding 1 Additional Outlet Licence select 1  
     Ex 2. If you are adding a Shopify Connector only select 0
13. Under Resource Allocation Meeting select a date approx. 1 week ahead
14. Under Closing Date select the end of the current month if greater than 1 week away, otherwise select the end of the following month
15. Under Lead Source always select Inbound ? Phone
16. Under How Soon always select ASAP
17. Under Type select Existing Customer
18. Click Save and Associate
19. Under Special Business Processes/Workarounds discussed select No
20. If the Quote include the Shopify Click & Collect addon:  
     Under How Many Click and Collect Stores select the applicable number
21. Under Are you offering SAAS pricing Model select No
22. Under Subject enter a short summary of what is being added  
      
     Shopify Connector + CnC + Gift Vouchers
23. Under Quoted Items > Product Name search for the line item to be added:  
    ![rtaImage (17).jpeg](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000pow5FEAQ.jpg)  
      
     If there are multiple line items click + Add Row
24. If any Product lines are discounted do not change the price on the Product line directly, add Special One-off Discount as a separate line, set the List Price as a negative dollar value  
    ![rtaImage (18).jpeg](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000powCsEAI.jpg)
25. Once all line items are added click Save at the top

**Creating A PandaDoc**

1. Click the Subject of the Quote that was just created to open the quote  
   ![rtaImage (19).jpeg](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000poum9EAA.jpg)
2. In the top right click the down arrow next to Email PandaDoc and click Create PandaDoc  
   ![rtaImage (20).jpeg](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000pow6hEAA.jpg)
3. If the Client has provided any emails to add as a CC enter them under the Additional Email fields
4. Click Submit Proposal to PandaDoc
5. Check that the PandaDoc has the correct Name, Email and Company Name under Prepared for (Client)  
     
   ![rtaImage (21).jpeg](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000powDgEAI.jpg)
6. Check the correct Product Line items are displaying at the correct costs in Upfront and Ongoing Fee fields  
   ![rtaImage (22).jpeg](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000powDlEAI.jpg)
7. Click Email PandaDoc in the top right
8. Copy the full link after where it says Unique Link:  
   ![rtaImage (23).jpeg](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000powDqEAI.jpg)

**Creating Zendesk Ticket**

1. In Zendesk create a new ticket
2. Copy the Client Email Address into the Requester field in the top left
3. Take the ticket
4. Email the Client to state that the electronic paperwork has been created and paste the link from the PandaDoc earlier
5. Type: Task  
    Category: Task > Add  
    Sub Category: Sales > Existing Customer > New Outlet or Other  
    Priority: Normal  
    Resolution Code: Resolved Successfully  
    Resolution Cause: Knowledge > Sales Enquiry
6. Submit as Solved
