#### What training and support options are available to me?

There are a number of training and support options available to you:

- If you're after a **range of support articles** that you can browse / search for specific topics, then the **Maropost Commerce Support Hub**[can be found at this link](http://galaxy.maropost.com/home/leaving?allowTrusted=1&target=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.netohq.com%2Fsupport%2Fs%2F).
	Either click on one of the many links within the site to access a topic, or enter a term into the Search bar.
- If you can't find the support article or topic within Support Hub, check the [Maropost Commerce community](http://galaxy.maropost.com/home/leaving?allowTrusted=1&target=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.netohq.com%2Fsupport%2Fs%2Fcommunity) for commonly answered questions.
	Maropost Commerce partners even have exclusive access to their [own community](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/partner-portal) to discuss news, general discussions and business opportunities.
- If you're after **training that can be completed in your own time **at your own pace, we have a range of **free online training courses **at Learn Maropost Commerce [available within the Maropost Commerce Support Hub](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/commerce-articles?categories=Maropost_Galaxy__c%3AOnline_Courses__c).

#### **Can I see within Learn Maropost Commerce the courses my staff have completed?**

All courses in Learn Maropost Commerce are user based and there is currently no way to see within the Partner portal the courses that you staff members have completed.
If you need to know the courses your staff have completed, send a request to the [Maropost Commerce Support team](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/contactsupport) with a list of the staff members.

Maropost Commerce will only share information about a staff member that works at your company, and not any contractors or customer's user details.
