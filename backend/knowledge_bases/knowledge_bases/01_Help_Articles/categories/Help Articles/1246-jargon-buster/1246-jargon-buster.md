# Jargon Buster

# Jargon Buster

**Annexa** - Netsuite Partner

**API -** Application Program Interface, i.e. putting a dataset from REX into a standardised format for another 3rd party system to use**.** Retail Express as 3 old APIs for eCommerce, Inventory Planning System and Warehouse Management Systems and a new 'unified' API.  They used to be licenced individually but now they only have to pay for the API bundle and they get them all

**Black pepper** -

**Bootcamp** - 'old name' for Customer Training sessions for POS, Inventory, Customer Management. Now called Training Academy.

**Client ID** -the unique alphanumeric identifier for a clients Retail Express database (normally provided to 3rd parties to integrate their systems with REX through our APIs - like a security key).  Can also be referred to as a GUID

**EDM** - Electronic Direct Mail. Mass communication tool to email our customers about new features/releases and training academies etc.

**EDS** - Event Driven Synchronisation - means that an integration e.g. Retail Express to Shopify sync occurs when an event occurs.  For example a product name is updated in REX this will trigger an immediate sync to Shopify to update the product name there too (as opposed to say an overnight process)

**Employment Hero** - Human Resource management console where you request annual leave, log sick leave etc.

**EPL** -

**ESR**- abbreviation for *Early Settler Furniture*the first big client Retail Express ever had so alot of the original development of the software was based on their requirements

**Ezypay -** (Old) Payment Gateway that we use to direct debit customers for their monthly hosting service and support fees either via their bank account or credit card.

**Fiserv**- Integrated Eftpos (like PC-Eftpos/Tyro - but we earn a commission on this one)

**GL Code**- General Ledger code

**Macro** - within Zendesk. A saved action or standard templated response that can quickly and easily be used to update a ticket or comment. eg: our signatures are Macros

**MGMT** - Internal customer server database creation and management

**MOTO transaction** - stands for Mail Order/Telephone Order, but bascially means a 'card not present' transaction where you manually entering in the amount for the client to pay into an eftpos terminal

**NPS -** Net Promoter Score. Management tool that can be used to gauge the loyalty of a firm's customer relationships

**Panda**- Third party software we use to create our electronic contracts for clients to sign.  So contracts that clients sign are often referred to as Pandas/PandaDoc or alternative a 'Solution Agreements'

**PC-EFTPOS** - Integrated EFTPOS partner (also see: Tyro)

**PEP** ? Retail Express Product Enhancement Program. Enhancement suggestions and requests lodged by customers

**REX** - abbreviation for Retail Express

**RFID** - acronym for 'Radio-Frequency Identification' digital data is encoded into RFID tags or smart labels captured by a reader via radio waves. unlike normal barcode scanners you don't have to line up the barcode on the label to scan it so saves alot of time stocktaking etc.

**SaaS** - stands for Software as a Service which basically means software as a monthly subscription

**SDR -** SDRs are sales team members who focus on reaching out, prospecting and deciding if a lead is qualified or not. SDRs pretty much are doing the ground work for more experienced salespeople to approach qualified leads.

**Stripe -** (New) Payment Gateway that we use to charge customers cards for their monthly hosting service and support fees and/or their licence fees inc. payment plans

**Support Portal** - Retail Express customer facing online support containing knowledgebase, short videos and support contact details

**Tanda -**a third party (integrated) time and attendance system that automates rosters, time-sheets, award or agreement rates to assist with payroll

**Tyro** - Integrated EFTPOS partner (also see: PC-EFTPOS)

**VIP -** VIP clients. VIP status is collectively determined by the sales team and Aaron. VIP clients have a dedicated phone support line

**VSTS -**  'new' Microsoft Team Foundation Server. This is a Development team tool used to track and test development items, bugs for investigation/fix

**WindCave**-

**Zendesk -** Cloud based customer support ticketing software. Also hosts knowledgebase and Zendesk Talk (support phone system).

**zipPay -** post-pay solution partner

**Zoho -** ('New' CRM) Customer relationship management tool. Tracks customer implementation projects from sales signoff throughout.
