# Request Commission Statements From Maropost Commerce

#### Why have I not received my commission statement?

Maropost Commerce does not automatically send out commissions statements with the monthly commission payments.  
Commission statements are only sent sent upon request by contacting the [Maropost Commerce Cloud accounts team](http://galaxy.maropost.com/home/leaving?allowTrusted=1&target=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.netohq.com%2Fsupport%2Fs%2Fcontactaccounts).  
The accounts team will process this request and email a statement through to you.
