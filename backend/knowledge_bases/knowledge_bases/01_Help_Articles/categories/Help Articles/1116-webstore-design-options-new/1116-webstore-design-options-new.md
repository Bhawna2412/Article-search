# Webstore Design Options (New)

**Important**: This article covers the new Theme Store and Editor, which is currently only available on new webstores. If you the steps in this article do not reflect your webstore's Theme Store and Editor, please [refer to the standard instructions](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/webstore-design-options).

Your Maropost Commerce webstore design, in many cases, is the determining factor for a lot of customers as to whether they will buy from you or not. Broken or poor quality looking web pages will usually scare people off; the credibility and trust disappears and people are worried that you might process the orders with the same types of faults as your webstore.

When choosing a theme, try and think about layout you would like to use for your store, some of the designs you’ve seen in the past that have caught your eye and see if we have a pre-made theme that fits your requirements.

All of our themes are fully responsive, so any theme you choose will work out of the box on both desktops and mobiles.

With Maropost Commerce, you have three different ways to customise your webstore.

### Customise existing themes with the theme editor

Choose from one of Maropost Commerce's free and premium themes and customise it to suit your brand using the user friendly theme editor.

### Create your own webstore theme by editing the code

If you’re a web designer with skills in HTML and CSS, you can create and edit your webstore theme using Maropost Commerce's B@SE logic.

To get started, checkout our [designer documentation](http://galaxy.maropost.com/home/leaving?allowTrusted=1&target=https%3A%2F%2Fdevelopers.neto.com.au%2Fdocumentation%2Fweb-designers) which includes helpful tips and tricks.

You also need to [request access for SFTP](http://galaxy.maropost.com/home/leaving?allowTrusted=1&target=http%3A%2F%2Fforms.neto.com.au%2Fdesign%2Frequestftp.html) so you can edit and upload theme files.
