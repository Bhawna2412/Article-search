# Marketing Cloud Release Notes – 17 Jun 2025

## OVERVIEW

Hello Maropostians!!

With sheer delight, we have introduced some highly sought-after features in our acquisition forms. Such add-ons provide you with more flexibility in designing your forms and help you acquire more potential customers in the long run.

To learn more about these new updates, kindly read ahead!!

## ACQUISITION FORM ENHANCEMENTS

We have added two new add-ons in the “**Select Form Settings**” part of the Acquisition Builder. They are, namely, “**Enable double opt-in**” and “**Redirect to URL after form submission,**” respectively. With these two new add-ons, you can send an email confirmation (or SMS notification) request to your users, letting them confirm their interest in subscribing to your newsletters or email publications. Also, after a user submits a form or survey, you can redirect them to a particular URL or website to further bolster user engagement.

![acq15(1).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/EG1ROUPB2Y1Z/acq15-281-29.png)
You also have the option to add custom script codes to your Acquisition Builder via an HTML block.

![acq18(1).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/JE6S8KVHM4GG/acq18-281-29.png)
To improve the form submission experience, you can now choose what type of data you want to collect from users. You can collect both email and SMS or just one of them, thus offering you many possibilities when creating content in an acquisition form.

![acq16.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/EEF99ZMUIR69/acq16.png)
To further assist onboarding users in understanding how to fill out the form, labels and instructions can be added to the email and SMS fields.

![acq17.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/PUKFBCXETVLW/acq17.png)
Know how to customize a form to suit your needs by reading our [**Creating and Using Acquisition Forms**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/718-creating-and-using-acquisition-forms) article.
