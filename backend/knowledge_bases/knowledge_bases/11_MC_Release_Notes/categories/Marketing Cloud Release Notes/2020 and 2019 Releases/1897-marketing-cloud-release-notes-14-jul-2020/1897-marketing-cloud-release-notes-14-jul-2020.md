# Marketing Cloud Release Notes – 14 Jul 2020

## **Overview**

In this mid-July release, we bring you a better user experience across custom reporting by revamping the Deliverability reports and Growth & Attrition reports as part of our user experience transformation.

To learn more about the changes, read on!

## **Enhancements**

### **Enhanced User Experience for Deliverability Reports**

You will find the deliverability reporting more efficient now that they have been redesigned as per our new UI standards. The creation flow and the fields within the flow are more organized and meant to simplify your reporting needs.

For a step-by-step procedure on how to create a deliverability report, see [Deliverability Report](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Deliverability-Report).

### **Enhanced User Experience for Growth & Attrition Reports**

You will notice a similar improved user experience in growth & attrition reporting. Due to the redesign of the UI, you will notice a few shuffling of fields aimed at simplifying the overall process. While the number of steps has been reduced to two, you get the same functionalities as before, but more efficient.

For a step-by-step procedure on how to create a growth & attrition report, see [Creating Growth & Attrition Reports](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Creating-Growth-and-Attrition-Reports).
