# Marketing Cloud Release Notes – 7 May 2020

## **Overview**

In this release, we bring you some notable enhancements to the email builders along with a few minor changes to the Navigation. The send test feature has been redesigned to satisfy all your use cases while making it more effortless. The step-jumper functionality is introduced for easy navigation when editing email campaigns. You may also want to familiarize yourself with the changes in the Navigation (albeit small) that were introduced in this release.

Read on for more details!

## **Enhancements and Changes**

### **Campaign Creation - Sending Test Email**

The 'Send Test' feature in our campaign builders gives you the ability to see a test version of the emails your recipients will see. With the new enhancements in place, sending test emails now allows more flexibility and convenience.

### **Campaign Creation - Step Jumper**

When editing a finished campaign (saved as a draft or scheduled), you now have the flexibility to move between the steps just by clicking the step progress bar on the top.

> **Note:** After you make an edit, you can only move forward by clicking Next.
