# Marketing Cloud Release Notes – 14 Jun 2022

**OVERVIEW**

This release contains two new enhancements that will help you analyze and segment your contacts based on whether they have Apple’s Mail Privacy Protection (MPP) enabled or disabled.

For details, read on!

** **

**ENHANCEMENTS**

**Tracking Email Opens Based on Apple’s Mail Privacy Protection Feature**

In the Open reports, you’ll be able to identify which opens originated from Apple devices and whether their Mail Privacy Protection feature had been enabled or disabled.

- 
- 

**Note**: Marketing Cloud identifies an Open as* *MPP Open if it comes from a generic user agent of 'Mozilla/5.0' and an IP address that is associated with a known Apple Datacenter (Akamai or Cloudflare).

For more information, see the 'Open Report and Click Report' section in [**Campaign Report - Dashboard**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Campaign-Report-Dashboard).
