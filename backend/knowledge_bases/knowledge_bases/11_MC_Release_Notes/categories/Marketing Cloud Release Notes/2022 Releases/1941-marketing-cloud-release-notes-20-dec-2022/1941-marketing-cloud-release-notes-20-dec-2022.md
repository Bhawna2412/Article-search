# Marketing Cloud Release Notes – 20 Dec 2022

**OVERVIEW**

In this release, we added a significant enhancement to the **Product Purchase** Journey trigger.

For details, please read on!

## **ENHANCEMENT**

### **Product Purchased Journey Trigger Enhancement**

You can now trigger Journeys based on the purchase of specific product categories. The **Product Purchased** Journey trigger now enables you to select the Brands and product categories as Journey Trigger.

For detailed information, see the Product Purchased section in the [**Journey Trigger**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Journey-Triggers) article. 

The Product Categories settings have been introduced as a separate tab on the Product Purchased dialog box. You can select the integration source and then the Brands and Product Categories for which you want to trigger the Journey.

![Product Categories tab](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/ProductCategoriestab.0685g00000BIPYLAA5.png)

As before, you can still select specific products or all products to trigger the Journey. The product selection options remain the same and are available on the Product tab of the Product Purchased dialog box.

![Product tab](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/Producttab.0685g00000BIPYQAA5.png)
