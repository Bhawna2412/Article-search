# Marketing Cloud Release Notes – 30 Jun 2022

## **OVERVIEW**

In this release, we are bringing out several enhancements to enrich your previewing experience when creating and managing content.

For details, read on!

**  **

## **ENHANCEMENT**

### **Content Builders – Content Preview and Render Preview**

You’ll now be able to preview email content as part of content management, without having to go to the campaign builder.

The enhancements to preview functionality in content management include:

- 
- 
- 

### **Improvements in Content Preview**

You can now preview your email content based on how it will appear to any specific content. Select the contact by entering their email address manually or by selecting their email address from the contact list.

The content preview also renders the dynamic tags in your content. You can select a dynamic block and see the content based on your selection.

For more information, see the 'Content Preview' section in [**Previewing Content in Content Builders**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Previewing-Content-in-Content-Builders).

![Content Preview](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/ContentPreview.0685g000008MX9gAAG.png)

### **Introduction of the Render Preview**

You can now preview how your email content will render on various email clients.

Note that the Render Preview functionality is already available on the campaign building flow, but is now made available as part of content management too.

For more information, see the 'Render Preview' section in [**Previewing Content in Content Builders**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Previewing-Content-in-Content-Builders).
