# Marketing Cloud Release Notes – 21 Mar 2022

## **OVERVIEW**

This release contains an amazing enhancement for mobile marketing that helps you stay compliant with the SMS laws and best practices.

For details, read on!

## **ENHANCEMENT**

### **Compliance Settings for SMS Campaigns**

The SMS Campaign builder now includes a new section on SMS compliance settings. Introduced as the COMPLIANCE tab in step 1, the compliance settings are pre-selected as per SMS guidelines requirements in USA and Canada.

- 
- 
- 

To learn about how to create an SMS Campaign, see [Creating SMS Campaigns](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Creating-SMS-Campaigns).

![SMS Compliance](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/SMSCompliance.0685g000006wpr6AAA.png)

The Organization Name and Company Information Link are prepopulated with values derived from the ACCOUNT tab on the Account Details page. If you want to send out an SMS campaign with a different organization name or company information link, you can edit these field values on the COMPLIANCE tab of that specific campaign.

> **Note**: Updating the Organization Name and Company Information Link in the COMPLIANCE tab of an SMS campaign is reflected in the same campaign only and doesn't change the Account-wide compliance settings.

On the Account Details page, go to the ACCOUNT tab, and scroll down to the Manage SMS Campaign Compliance Settings section. To learn about managing your account, see [Managing Your Accounts](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Managing-Your-Accounts).
