# Marketing Cloud Release Notes – 22 Apr 2022

**OVERVIEW**

In this release, we launched a new functionality for improving your convenience in the signing-in process.

For more information, please read on!

**NEW FEATURE**

**﻿Signing Into Maropost Identity with Google and Microsoft Accounts**

You can now sign in to your Maropost account using your Google and Microsoft accounts too. While signing in with your Google account doesn’t require any additional setup, signing in with Microsoft account requires that you perform a one-time step of linking the accounts.
