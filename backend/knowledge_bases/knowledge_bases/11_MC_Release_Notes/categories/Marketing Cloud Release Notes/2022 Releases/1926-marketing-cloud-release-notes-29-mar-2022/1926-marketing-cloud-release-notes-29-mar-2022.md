# Marketing Cloud Release Notes – 29 Mar 2022

## **OVERVIEW**

In this release, we are happy to introduce a new feature called 'Process Queue' that will help you keep track of all your ongoing background processes. 

For more information, read on!

## **NEW FEATURE**

### **Process Queue**

The Process Queue feature enables you to view a list of all your ongoing processes along with their statuses. 

Did you send a campaign or export a contact list, and are now curious as to what's happening? You can know the exact status of your process without having to reach out to our support team. Just go to the Process Queue page where you can see all the ongoing background processes such as scheduled campaigns, importing/exporting contact lists, and counting/exporting segments.

You'll find the Process Queue icon (shown as a clock icon) on the top of the application UI.

![Dashboard - Process Queue](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/DashboardProcessQueue.0685g000006wprVAAQ.png)

Click the icon to go to the Process Queue page you can see all the background processes that are currently running in your account.
