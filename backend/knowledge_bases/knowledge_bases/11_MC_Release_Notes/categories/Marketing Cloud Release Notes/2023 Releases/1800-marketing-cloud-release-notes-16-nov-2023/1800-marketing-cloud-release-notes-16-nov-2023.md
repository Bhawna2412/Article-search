# Marketing Cloud Release Notes - 16 Nov 2023

### **OVERVIEW**

In this release, we launched a new Journey template – the [**Nurture Journey Template**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Nurture-Journey-Template).

To learn more, please read on! 

### **NEW FEATURE - NURTURE JOURNEY TEMPLATE**

We are introducing a new journey template wherein you can send emails to your customers who have recently purchased products or availed services from you.

#### **Two-Step Journey Creation**

Creating a Journey is much simpler using a template. Just configure your Journey details with basic information, journey timings, and trigger modes. Secondly, mention your email address, trigger criteria, and select the email content to complete the setup. Very easy!

![nurture11](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/nurture11.0685g00000IkwUdAAJ.png)

#### **Focused Product Marketing**

With Nurture Journeys, you can set specific product or brand triggers that allow you to send personalized emails to clients, reminding them about the items they have purchased.

![nurture12](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/nurture12.0685g00000Ikw9zAAB.png)

#### **Vivid Email Marketing Techniques**

Not sure how to approach customers after they have purchased products from you? Not to worry, Nurture Journeys takes care of that as well. This Journey will advertise three types of emails, ranging from product descriptions and feedback inquiries to upselling products to interesting clients. Overall, this approach will keep customers engaged with your emails and help promote brand awareness of your store.
