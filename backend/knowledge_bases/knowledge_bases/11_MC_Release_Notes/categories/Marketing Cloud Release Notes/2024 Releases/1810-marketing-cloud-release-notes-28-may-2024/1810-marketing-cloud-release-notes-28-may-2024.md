# Marketing Cloud Release Notes – 28 May 2024

### **OVERVIEW:**

In this release, we have rolled out a revamped version of the Drag and Drop Editor module to help you create highly engaging email-related content for your users.

In addition, we have also made some minor yet significant updates to our dynamic content feature, thereby enhancing the ease of use and accessibility aspects of Marketing Cloud.

To learn more, read ahead!! 

### **DRAG AND DROP EDITOR ENHANCEMENTS:**

#### **PRE-DESIGNED DRAG AND DROP TEMPLATES**

We have introduced a **library catalog** wherein you can choose your Drag and Drop templates from a wide collection of vivid themes to suit your marketing needs. In this way, you can design well-optimized email content much more quickly by using a preloaded blueprint.

![conteditor29](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/conteditor29.068J1000000XSv8IAG.png)To learn more about designing email content by using such a template, read our [**Drag and Drop Editor** ](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Drag-and-Drop-Editor)article. 

### **DYNAMIC CONTENT EDITOR ENHANCEMENT:**

Initially, users could only manually insert Product Feed tags to generate product recommendations in email content, thus making the overall process quite cumbersome. To circumnavigate this issue, we have introduced a **Product Feed Templates** drop-down list in the dynamic content editor itself. 

This allows you to select multiple product feeds with a few clicks, thereby saving time, and it provides access to a plethora of product templates you might not have initially considered using. 

![dyncontent1](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/dyncontent1.068J1000000X8VNIA0.png)To learn more about dynamic content creation and the pre-defined templates used for displaying product recommendations, read our [**Dynamic Content**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Dynamic-Content) and [**Product Feed Template**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Product-Feed-Templates) articles.
