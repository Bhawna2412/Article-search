# Marketing Cloud Release Notes – 13 Feb 2024

### **OVERVIEW:**

In this release, we have rolled out new customizable options in Acquisition Forms to make it even more user-friendly for our audiences. Also, we have introduced some important changes to our Delete SMS Contact and Cleansing Rule features.

To learn more, please read further!! 

### **ACQUISITION FORM ENHANCEMENT:**

We have introduced a new set of options for the Popup form type. These new options are available in the Display and Behaviour Options section and control the display behavior characteristics of the form. Additionally, the **Display & Behaviour,** plus the **Optional** functions, have their options neatly categorized into collapsible sections, thus making the page layout easier to use. 

![acq7](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/acq7.0685g00000KjlxwAAB.png)The two additional features introduced in the **Display and Behaviour** section are the **Page URL** and **Hide** options. With these two options, you can decide on which pages you want to display the Acquisition Forms, plus you can opt out of displaying a form to a user for some amount of time if they have exited the website and have not filled out the form.

![acq8](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/acq8.0685g00000KjlyVAAR.png)We have also made some fine-tuned adjustments in the way popups and embedded forms behave on a website. Initially, in an Acquisition Form, popups were hindered if an embedded form was also present on a website. With the help of this update, both forms can now coexist together. 

For more details on how to create and use an Acquisition Form, see [**Creating and Using Acquisition Forms**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Creating-and-Using-Acquisition-Forms).

Lastly, we have added a **Preview Form** option under the Actions menu, so you can easily examine and evaluate your forms from the index page itself. Thanks to such improvements, you won’t have to deal with the hassle of going through the whole process again to access the preview tool.

![acq9](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/acq9.0685g00000KjlPKAAZ.png)To know more about this feature and its usage for your marketing needs, read our [**Acquisition Forms**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Acquisition-Forms) article. 

### **DELETE SMS CONTACT ENHANCEMENT:**

Just like our email contacts, we are incorporating a delete option in SMS contact lists. This will unsubscribe the contact from the SMS lists and be replaced by an arbitrary value, i.e., it gets pseudonymized. It will also place the contact on the **Do Not Mail List**.

![delsms2](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/delsms2.0685g00000KjmMOAAZ.png)To learn more about the compliance rules concerning data safekeeping and user identity, visit and read our [**GDPR Guideline**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/23-exercising-the-right-to-erasure-new?utm_source=community-search&utm_medium=organic-search&utm_term=exercising+the) topic. Also, to learn more about managing your contacts, kindly read our [**SMS Contact Lists**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/SMS-Contact-Lists) article. 

### **CLEANSING RULE ENHANCEMENT:**

To improve the deliverability, click-through, and open rates of your email campaigns, plus keeping the sender and IP address reputation in mind, we have incorporated a backend feature that automatically cleanses contacts who show a “**bounced**” status consecutively 10 times. Such assistance will help you send email campaigns to your target audiences in an efficient manner.

To learn more about such rules and how to create your very own rulesets, have a look at our [**Cleansing Rules**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Cleansing-Rules) article.
