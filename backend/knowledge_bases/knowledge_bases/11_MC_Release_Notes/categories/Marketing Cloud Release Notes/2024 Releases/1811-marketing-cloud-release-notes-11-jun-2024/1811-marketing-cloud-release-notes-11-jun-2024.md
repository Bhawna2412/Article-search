# Marketing Cloud Release Notes – 11 Jun 2024

### **OVERVIEW:**

In this release, we have launched a new segment category in our newly refurbished Next Generation Segment Builder.

Kindly read ahead to know more!! 

### **ENHANCEMENT:**

#### **A NEW SEGMENT CATEGORY**

We have added a new category in our Next Generation Segment Builder known as “**Email Activity"**.** **This lets you fetch contacts who have either received or interacted with your email campaigns.

There are 4 segment rules clubbed in this segment category. They are, namely, **Was Sent Emails, Received Emails, Opened Emails, **and** Clicked Emails**. Such criteria enable you to construct segments, which you can subsequently utilize to send personalized campaigns to such contacts.
