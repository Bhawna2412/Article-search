# Marketing Cloud Release Notes - 25 Jan 2024

### **OVERVIEW:**

Hello, Maropostians!! We have introduced a new feature in our SMS campaign module, which will help users download and view additional metrics of their sent campaigns. 

To learn more, please read ahead!!

### **ENHANCEMENT:**

We are now giving an **Export** option to Marketing Cloud users to download SMS campaign reports for **Total Send Campaigns**. You can set the criteria you need to check in the report, such as its send status, keyword responses, etc. 

![smssend2](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/smssend2.0685g00000JgyEtAAJ.png)By accessing such reports, you can identify the overall outcomes of your sent SMS messages that can help you make informed decisions to increase your SMS campaigns' deliverability and click rate.

![smssend3](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/smssend3.0685g00000JgyEyAAJ.png)To know more about the various parameters present in an SMS campaign, check out our [**SMS Campaign Dashboard**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/SMS-Campaign-Dashboard) topic.
