# Marketing Cloud Release Notes – 16 Apr 2024

### **OVERVIEW:**

In this release, we have launched a new segment category in our newly refurbished Next Generation Segment Builder.

Kindly read ahead to know more!!

### **ENHANCEMENT:**

#### **A NEW SEGMENT CATEGORY**

We have added a new category in our Next Generation Segment Builder known as “**Purchase Activity**”. This allows you to fetch contacts who have made purchases from your store previously.

There are 4 segment rules clubbed in this segment category. They are, namely, **Purchased Products, Converted Campaigns, Placed Orders, **and** Lifetime Value**. You can use these segment rules to filter and fetch contacts that fulfill such purchasing criteria.
