# Marketing Cloud Release Notes - 9 Jan 2024

### **OVERVIEW:**

Hello, Maropostians!! In the upcoming release, we are introducing a new add-on to our SMS campaigns. 

To know more about this feature, kindly read ahead!!

### **ENHANCEMENT:**

We are now supporting Suppression lists for our SMS campaigns. Now marketers can add contacts to these lists so they cannot receive SMS campaigns that you send from your account. 

![suppress2](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/suppress2.0685g00000Jf6TxAAJ.png)This will also help you to send the campaign to the correct audiences within that list while suppressing the unwanted ones.

To learn more about creating SMS campaigns and the functions of the Suppression list, read our [**SMS Campaigns**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Creating-SMS-Campaigns) and [**Suppression (Do Not Mail) Lists**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Suppression-Do-Not-Mail-Lists) pages.
