# Marketing Cloud Release Notes – 24 Oct 2024

### **OVERVIEW:**

Greetings Maropostians!!

We have introduced a new feature in Marketing Cloud that will help you acquire a more comprehensive understanding of your contacts' daily interactions with your businesses and brands.

To learn more, read ahead!! 

### **NEW FEATURE:**

#### **eRFM Analysis Report**

We have launched a phase 1 iteration of the eRFM (**recency, frequency, monetary,** and **engagement insights**) report feature that consists of an engagement and RFM insights chart describing various contact groups engaging with your business at distinct levels whether it be subscribing to a newsletter, clicking on a product survey link, or completing a purchase at checkout. This data is available in real-time, showcasing customer behavioral insight, and the count changes dynamically depending on the date selected.

![erfm10](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/erfm10.068J1000005ajGGIAY.png)The chart gives an intuitive visual representation of how your brands and marketing strategies are being perceived by different sections of your contacts. This enables you to make data-driven decisions and gives you vivid marketing approaches for a diverse audience. For example, if the overall count of your most sought-after customers has dwindled in the past few days, you can take action by creating a segment of such contacts and bringing them back into the campaign funnel through a loyalty exclusive voucher or a winback email notifications program.

![erfm9](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/erfm9.068J1000005ajGsIAI.png)The chart also gives you the flexibility to modify your contact group names as per the type of audience you cater to.
