### **Pre-populated Content for New Footer**

The editor for creating the footer now comes pre-populated with standard content that you can edit as per your needs. The pre-populated content is generated based on the information available in your account. 

> **Note**: When editing, remember that your footer content must have an address tag {{campaign.address}} and an unsubscribe link tag {{campaign.unsubscribe_link}}, {{campaign.unsubscribe_url}} or {{campaign.one_click_unsub_url}} to comply with the anti-spam legislation.

To learn more about custom footers, see [Footer Management](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Footer-Management).
