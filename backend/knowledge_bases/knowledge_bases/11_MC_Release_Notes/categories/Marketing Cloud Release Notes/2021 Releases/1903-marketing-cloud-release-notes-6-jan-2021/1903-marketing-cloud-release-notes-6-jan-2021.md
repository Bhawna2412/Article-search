# Marketing Cloud Release Notes – 6 Jan 2021

## **Overview**

You can now resend a campaign to a contact with a simple click of a button. To learn more about the enhancement, read on!

## **Enhancement**

### **Resend Campaigns**

Maybe your email got soft-bounced, or maybe you want to give your recipient another chance to engage! Whatever your reasons are, if you are resending an email campaign to a contact, you can now do so with just a click of a button.

**Note**:** **The resend option will not be available if:

- 
- 

On the Contact Overview page (to learn more about contact overview, see [Viewing a Contact Overview](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Viewing-a-Contact-Overview)), the Customer Engagement Journey section now dynamically shows the Re-Send Campaign button for the campaigns sent to the contact.

![Contact_Overview](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/ContactOverview.0685g000006w7unAAA.png)

Click Re-Send Campaign, and you will be redirected to the Re-Send Campaign page where you can review your campaign and finally resend it.
