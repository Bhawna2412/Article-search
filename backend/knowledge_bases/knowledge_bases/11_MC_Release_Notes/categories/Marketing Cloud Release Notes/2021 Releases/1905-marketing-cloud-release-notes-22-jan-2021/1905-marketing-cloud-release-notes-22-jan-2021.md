# Marketing Cloud Release Notes – 22 Jan 2021

## **Overview**

In this special release update, we bring you the spruced-up Account Details section. Explore the modernized look and experience the comfort of managing your account information; we would love to know what you think!

## **Enhancements**

### **Revamped Account Details**

We simplified how you access and manage your account information. As before, the Account Details page is available in the drop-down list when you click your user name in the top-right corner of the application.
