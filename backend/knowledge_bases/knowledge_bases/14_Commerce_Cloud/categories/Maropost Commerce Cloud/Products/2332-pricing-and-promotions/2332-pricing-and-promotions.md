# Pricing and Promotions

### Pricing

You set the base price here, and if needed, you can use promotions tab to set pricing or discounts conditions. This is useful if you want to sell at a different price in a physical store versus online, or in different marketplaces.

MCC let you create **discount campaigns** for specific products, customer groups, or entire orders. These can be amount-based, percentage-based, or provide free shipping.

## **Promotion Types**

- **Amount Off Products** – Fixed value off products or the entire cart.
*Example:* `$10 off jackets` or `$50 off total order`.
- **Percentage Off Products** – Reduces price by a set percentage.
*Example:* `20% off sneakers` or `100% off` for a giveaway.
- **Free Shipping** – Removes delivery charges for eligible orders.
*Example:* `Free shipping on orders over $50`.

## **Setup Fields**

- **General** – Title, Code, Description, Discount Value (amount or percentage, per product or entire cart).
- **Eligibility (Who can use this code?)** – Choose customer attributes (e.g., Customer Group, Region) and matching rules.
- **Item Conditions** – Specify product attributes or categories the promotion applies to (Product, collection, Tags).
- **Discount Usage** – Set maximum quantity per order, optionally limit to once per customer.
- **Activate Dates** – Start and end date/time for the promotion.
