# Configure Regions

The **Regions** section provides a **read-only view** of how geographic regions are structured in your store. Each region represents a group of countries associated with a specific currency, helping define how pricing and regional settings are applied during checkout.

At this time, **Region settings are not configurable**. This page is intended for **display and reference only**.

## To access,

Go to **Settings → Region**. 

![image.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/SXN8HXZIEULF/image.png)

### What You Can See on the Region Page

On the **Manage Region Details** page, you can view:

- **Region Title**
The name of the region (for example, Australia or APAC).
- **Currency**
The currency associated with the region, which is used for pricing and checkout.
- **Countries**
The list of countries grouped under the region.
  ![image.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/1XF2VTD8SGE0/image.png)

 For example, an **APAC** region may display a selected currency along with countries such as China, Japan, and South Korea. These countries follow the region’s predefined currency and configuration.

### Why This Page Matters

Even though Regions cannot be configured currently, this view helps you:

- Understand how countries are grouped in your store
- Verify which currency applies to different regions
- Reference regional setup when managing products, pricing, and checkout behavior

As regional configuration options expand in future updates, this page will serve as the foundation for managing multi-country and multi-currency setups more actively.
