# Service Cloud Integrations Overview

**Service Cloud Integrations** enable you to import customer data from third-party platforms like Neto, Shopify, and Retail Express directly into your service environment. This provides agents with complete customer context, including order history and contact information, helping them deliver more informed and personalized support that strengthens customer relationships.

Connecting your e-commerce platform to Service Cloud ensures agents have access to up-to-date purchase data when handling customer inquiries. This helps improve service relevance by enabling agents to reference recent orders, track fulfillment status, and identify customer preferences, generally enhancing the quality and personalization of customer interactions.

The integrations that you set up in your Service Cloud account enable you to import customer data from your third-party accounts, such as Neto, Shopify, and Retail Express.

Such data is useful for tracking client orders, managing contact information, handling incoming tickets, and so on. Thus, it gives a good platform to cater to your customers' requests and resolve them appropriately.

The integrations currently available in Service Cloud are as follows:

- 
- [**Shopify Integration**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Shopify-Integration)
- [**Retail Express Integration**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/2263-retail-express-integration)
