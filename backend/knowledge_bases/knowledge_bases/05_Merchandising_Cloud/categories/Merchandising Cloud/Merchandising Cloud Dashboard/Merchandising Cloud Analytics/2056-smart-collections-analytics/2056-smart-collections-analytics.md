# Smart Collections Analytics

Smart collection analytics enable you to identify low-converting collections and optimize their configuration to improve performance on some of your most viewed pages. 

By monitoring traffic, conversion rates, and filter usage across collections, you can make strategic adjustments through merchandising rules and pinning to help ensure customers find relevant products faster.

Collection pages typically receive high traffic volumes, making optimization particularly important for revenue impact. Analytics reveal which filters customers use most frequently and which collections underperform despite strong traffic, enabling you to adjust product ordering, push popular filters to the top, or reconfigure collection settings to enhance the shopping experience and conversion rates.

## The Importance of Regular Monitoring

Constantly monitoring Smart collection analytics can help identify issues and low-converting collections and adjust the ordering or change collection configuration. Collection pages are usually the most viewed pages of a store, so it's crucial to optimize these pages as much as possible.

> **Note:** 📘 All analytics data in this guide have been randomly generated for informational purposes.

> **Note:** 👍 Selecting the most optimal date range  If you're going to check analytics on a weekly basis, it's best to select date range reflecting the previous week's analytics. This is so you can compare analytics on a week-by-week basis for optimal monitoring and comparison purposes.
