# Multimarket Overview

You can use multimarket functionality to manage search and merchandising for different regions, languages, or currencies from a single Merchandising Cloud account, enabling efficient management of international or multi-region ecommerce operations. This capability helps you deliver localized search experiences tailored to each market's language, currency, and preferences while centralizing configuration management-supporting global expansion while maintaining the personalized experiences that drive conversion in each market.

Implementing multimarket features helps scale your ecommerce operations across regions efficiently. You can configure market-specific search settings, synonyms, merchandising rules, and recommendations while sharing common configurations where appropriate-enabling you to balance localization needs with operational efficiency, supporting consistent brand experiences across markets while respecting regional differences that influence shopping behavior and conversion.

## What is Multimarket?

Multimarket is a cutting-edge solution designed to simplify the complexity of managing multiple markets within the ever-evolving world of ecommerce. Built with a modular architecture, Multimarket enables merchants to efficiently manage a single index across numerous market combinations - such as languages, currencies, inventories, product availabilities and merchandising - without the overwhelming complexity that typically accompanies such a setup.

Multimarket offers seamless integration with Shopify Markets and can also be customized for other ecommerce platforms via API integration. This flexibility makes it a versatile tool for any merchant aiming to scale globally while maintaining local relevance.

## The Need for Multimarket Solutions

In today's global ecommerce landscape, achieving local relevance for shoppers is more critical than ever. Shoppers want personalized experiences that reflect their local trends, preferences, and data. This demand places merchants in a challenging position - they must balance global efficiency with the need to cater to specific market nuances.

Traditionally, managing multiple markets required creating and maintaining separate indices for each combination of language, currency, warehouse, and product availability. As these variables increase, so does the complexity, often leading to administrative challenges that can slow down operations and limit scalability.

Multimarket is designed to address this complexity by providing a flexible, scalable solution that simplifies the management of multiple market variables through a single, modular index.

## Key Features of Multimarket

### **1. Unified Index with Modular Flexibility**

Instead of managing multiple indices for each market variation, Multimarket allows you to manage all your markets through a single index. This modular approach enables you to combine various market variables - such as languages, currencies, warehouses, and product availability - without creating an administrative burden.

### **2. Multi-Language and Multi-Currency Support**

Multimarket enables merchants to support multiple languages and currencies across different markets effortlessly. This ensures that your global customers have a consistent and relevant shopping experience, regardless of their location.

### **3. Flexible Fulfillment Strategies with Market-Specific Inventory Control**

Multimarket enables you to control which warehouses are assigned to each market, giving you the flexibility to set up customized fulfillment strategies. For instance, you can configure Warehouse 1 to supply Germany and France, while Warehouse 2 only supplies France. In this scenario, inventory availability in France reflects the combined quantities of both warehouses, while Germany's availability reflects only Warehouse 1. This ensures that inventory is displayed accurately for each market, helping you maintain precise product availability and meet customer expectations across regions.

### **4. Product Availability Per Market**

Multimarket gives merchants full control over which products are visible and available in each market, regardless of inventory. Even if a warehouse has stock for a given item, you can choose to display or hide the product based on market-specific requirements. This flexibility is critical for managing complex ecommerce operations, as it lets merchants navigate various legal, licensing, logistical, and tactical considerations:

- Licensing: Some products might only have sales rights in specific regions. Multimarket allows you to restrict availability to comply with these licensing agreements.
- Legal Restrictions: Certain products are prohibited in specific countries (e.g. Kinder Eggs in the U.S.). With Multimarket, you can prevent such items from displaying in restricted markets, ensuring compliance with local regulations.
- Shipping and Logistics: High shipping costs or logistical limitations might simply make it impractical to sell certain items internationally (e.g. oversized or heavy goods like canoes). Multimarket lets you limit these products to markets where delivery is feasible and cost-effective.
- Market-Specific Strategies: You may want to offer certain product lines exclusively in selected markets for tactical reasons, such as catering to local demand or running market-specific promotions.

By enabling precise control over product availability, our Multimarket capability helps merchants align their offerings with each market's unique needs and restrictions, enhancing both operational efficiency and customer experience.

### **5. Geo-Targeted and Flexible Merchandising Capabilities**

Multimarket empowers merchants to create both global and local campaigns, enabling the tailoring of product displays, promotions, and placements for specific markets. With Multimarket, you can easily apply merchandising rules and pin hero products to boost visibility, choosing whether to roll these strategies out across all markets or target specific regions.

This unified approach to merchandising lets you:

- **Run Localized Campaigns**: Promote market-specific items, such as Father's Day gifts in Denmark or discounts exclusive to a particular region.
- **Efficiently Pin and Highlight Products**: Use pinning rules to feature top-selling or promotional items for selected markets, ensuring shoppers see the most relevant products for their region.

With streamlined control over both geo-targeted and global merchandising, Multimarket simplifies the execution of strategic campaigns, helping you deliver a cohesive shopping experience that resonates with customers in every market.
