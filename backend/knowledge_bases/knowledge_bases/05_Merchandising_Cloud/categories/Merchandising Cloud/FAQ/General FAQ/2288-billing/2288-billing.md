# Billing

Here you can find answers to the following questions:

1. How and when am I charged
2. How can I modify my payment information?
3. Is my credit card safe?

## How and when am I charged?

#### Shopify

If you're using our Shopify public application, then the charging is performed by the Shopify platform. Please check with Shopify support for more information.

#### Other platforms

We use a platform called [Chargebee](https://chargebee.com/) to track and issue charges. Charges are made according to the agreed Order Form, in standard conditions on the basis of a rolling 30-day term.

## How can I modify my payment information?

Please visit the [Merchant Dashboard](https://dashboard.findify.io/account/billing?merchantId=3806) > Account> Section to alter your credit card information.

## Is my credit card safe?

Absolutely. We use a payment gateway called [Braintree](https://www.braintreepayments.com/), which is also used by other familiar, global companies such as Airbnb, Uber, Tictail, OpenTable, Hotel Tonight and Github.
