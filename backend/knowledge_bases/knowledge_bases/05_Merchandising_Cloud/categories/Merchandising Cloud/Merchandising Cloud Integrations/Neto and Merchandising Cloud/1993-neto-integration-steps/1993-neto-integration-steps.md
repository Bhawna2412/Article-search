# Neto Integration Steps

You can integrate Merchandising Cloud with your Neto ecommerce platform by providing your product feed URL and admin access, which establishes the foundation for AI-powered search and personalization capabilities. The Neto Add-on automatically generates a public feed URL, streamlining the setup process and accelerating time to value for implementing intelligent product discovery features.

Following these integration steps enables you to add search results, analytics, smart collections, and recommendations to your Neto store. Once integrated, you'll access the Merchant Dashboard to configure personalization settings and merchandising rules that help improve conversion rates, with the platform continuously learning from customer behavior to optimize product rankings and recommendations over time.

Integration steps:

1. Provide product feed URL
2. Provide access to the admin panel and SFTP
3. Integrating Search Results and Analytics
4. Integrating Smart Collections and Recommendations
5. References & Tips

## Step 1: Provide product feed URL

- A product feed URL contains all the information on your current products and is essential to help us integrate Maropost Merchandising Cloud (formerly Findify) into your store. It follows the following format [CSV Feed Specification](https://developers.findify.io/docs/feed-generation-manual-csv)
- By adding the Merchandising Cloud [Neto Add-on](https://www.netohq.com/add-ons), a public feed URL will automatically be generated and displayed directly in the add-on store *(Click Install > Confirm > Feed is created and URL displayed)*
- Share the public feed URL together with information about you *(first name, last name, your working email, the language of your store and currency used)* with our support platform through [support@maropost.com](mailto:support@maropost.com).
- Once we receive the product feed URL and the information, we will proceed onboarding you into our system and you will receive access to your Merchandising Cloud Merchant Account. The account will help you manage your online store and access all of Merchandising Cloud's features through the [dashboard](https://dashboard.findify.io/).

## Step 2: Provide access to the admin panel and SFTP

- Please refer to this doc to provide access to Merchandising Cloud via Neto Partnership.  - Once you have submitted Merchandising Cloud's Partnership or if you have any questions, please contact us at [support@maropost.com](mailto:support@maropost.com).
