# Case Studies

You can learn from real merchant success stories demonstrating how Merchandising Cloud solutions help improve conversion rates, revenue, and customer experience across different industries and platforms. These case studies showcase measurable results ranging from increased conversion rates to improved average order values, providing practical benchmarks for what merchants can expect when implementing AI-powered search, recommendations, and smart collections.

Reviewing these examples helps inform your merchandising strategy by showing proven outcomes across various business models, from automotive to fashion to home goods. The documented results-such as conversion improvements, ROI returns, and enhanced user experience metrics-can guide your implementation priorities and help set realistic performance expectations for your own ecommerce store optimization efforts.

*See how these merchants increased revenue, boosted conversion, and improved UX with Merchandising Cloud solutions.*
