# Stamped.io

We've partnered with Stamped.io to help you leverage the power of User Generated Content - utilizing customer ratings and reviews to increase your store conversion.

### Steps to integrating Stamped.io into Merchandising Cloud:

1. Private and Public Keys from Stamped.io
2. Login to [Merchant Dashboard](https://dashboard.findify.io/)
3. Enable Stamped.io on Merchant Dashboard
4. Sync product feed

## Step 1: Private and Public Keys

Access your Private Key and Public Key from your Stamped.io account.
If you don't have them, go to [https://go.stamped.io/#/app/settings](https://go.stamped.io/#/app/settings), on the "API Keys" tab and click on the "Show" button. The client ID is the public key.

## Step 2: Login to Merchant Dashboard

Log in to [Merchant Dashboard](https://dashboard.findify.io/). For further information please visit [Merchant Dashboard](https://stamped.io/account/signin)

## Step 3: Enable Stamped.io

- Go to [Plugins](https://dashboard.findify.io/plugins/stamped),
- Enable Stamped.io
- Enter your Public and Private Keys
- Click save

![1302e7a-Stamped.gif](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/CNKPQ7PY9A4K/1302e7a-stamped.gif)

## Step 4: Contact Support

- Contact our support team: [support@maropost.com](mailto:support@maropost.com) with the request to setup a Reviews Puller job.
- Our support team will get back to you once everything is setup and ready to use.

## Step 5: Add the "customer rating" filter

Add the "customer rating" filter to your live filter list, via the [facets menu](https://dashboard.findify.io/setup/primary/filters) in your dashboard
