# Yotpo

**The integration between Yotpo and Maropost Merchandising Cloud (formerly Findify) helps shorten the time it takes customers to complete a purchase. Not only do customers quickly see the star rating for each product, but they can also filter products based on their ratings, making sure your reviews have an even bigger impact.**

Integrating Yotpo into the Merchandising Cloud solution does not require any coding.

### Steps to integrating Yotpo into Merchandising Cloud:

1. Integrate Yotpo in your Store
2. Access Your App-Key
3. Enable Yotpo in Merchandising Cloud
4. Add the "avg. customer rating" filter

## Step 1: Yotpo Integration

Make sure you have completed your Yotpo integration. If you have not, head to [Yotpo.com](https://www.yotpo.com/?utm_source=Findify_github&utm_medium=documentation&utm_campaign=Findify_documentation) to sign up or get in touch with the Yotpo team.

## Step 2: Access your App Key

Your app-key can be accessed in your Yotpo Dashboard

**i) Go to **[**Yotpo.com**](https://reviews.yotpo.com/?utm_source=Findify_github&utm_medium=documentation&utm_campaign=Findify_documentation)** and log in**

**ii) Click Store Settings**

![12d1563-Yotpo_Crendetials_Store-Settings.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/NN48YGV8JKDH/12d1563-yotpo-crendetials-store-settings.png)
*Store Settings in the Yotpo Dashboard*

**iii) Copy the App Key in the API Credentials section**
*Note: We do NOT require your secret key*

![8f2fa64-Yotpo_Crendetials_APP-Key.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/R5OVN3AB3AIK/8f2fa64-yotpo-crendetials-app-key.png)
*App Key in the API Credentials section*

## Step 3: Enable Yotpo

1. Enable the Yotpo integration from your [Merchant Dashboard](https://dashboard.findify.io/plugins) by simply sliding the toggle.
2. Add the Yotpo Public API Key to the key input in our Dashboard and hit Save.
3. Contact our support: [support@maropost .com](mailto:support@maropost.com) to setup a Reviews puller.
4. Our support will get back to you once the Puller job is setup.
  ![cdb7434-yotpo.gif](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/QJ4JZN5X3YXE/cdb7434-yotpo.gif)

*Paste your App Key in Findify's Dashboard*

## Step 4: Add the "customer rating" filter

Add the "customer rating" filter to your live filter list, via the [facets menu](https://dashboard.findify.io/setup/primary/filters) in your dashboard

![22b37b8-Customer_rating_filter.gif](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/GGSX9BHRXYPB/22b37b8-customer-rating-filter.gif)
*Enable customer ratings as a filter*
