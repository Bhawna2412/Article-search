# Promo Card Customization

You can customize promo cards to promote campaigns, highlight new collections, or feature special offers within your search results and collection pages. These versatile promotional elements enable you to intersperse marketing content among product listings, driving attention to strategic initiatives.

Promo cards provide opportunities to showcase brand narratives, drive traffic to campaign pages, or incentivize specific customer behaviors without relying solely on product listings. By strategically placing customized promotional content within search results, you can influence browsing behavior, highlight key messaging, and support merchandising campaigns that help improve overall engagement and conversion.

Promo cards serve as versatile assets for promoting various content alongside product listings within your Shopify store. They offer opportunities to highlight new assortments, drive traffic to campaign pages, convey product narratives, and incentivize customer behavior through promotions. Promo Card Campaigns feature provides a customizable and intuitive solution to enhance brand engagement and drive sales.

## Additional Information

**What Are Promo Cards?**

Promo cards are non-product assets displayed alongside products within the grid. They can include images, text, calls-to-action (CTAs), links, and even GIFs to provide a dynamic experience. Use cases range from highlighting new assortments and driving traffic to campaign pages to promoting special events or offers.

**Benefits of Promo Cards**

Promoting content directly within product listings enhances brand engagement and increases sales. With Promo Card Campaigns feature, you can easily drive brand collaborations, sell specific products, or promote events while maintaining control over the content's visibility and focus.

**Conclusion**

Customizing promo cards within our Shopify app offers a powerful way to engage customers and drive sales by strategically promoting content alongside product listings.
