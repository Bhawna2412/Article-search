# Control Promo Card Appearance

You can control the visual appearance and behavior of promo cards to ensure they align with your brand identity and merchandising strategy. This customization capability enables you to adjust styling, layout, and content display to create promotional elements that integrate seamlessly with your product listings.

Customizing promo card appearance helps ensure promotional content feels like a natural extension of your store rather than intrusive advertising. By controlling visual elements like sizing, colors, typography, and positioning, you create cohesive promotional experiences that capture attention while maintaining your overall design aesthetic and brand consistency.

## FAQ

### Q1: How many Promo Card ‘Profiles’ can I use?

Currently there are two profiles - Default and Secondary. However, additional profiles can be added over time, if deemed valuable by the client base.

### Q2: Can two different styles be used within one Promo Card campaign?

Yes, two different styles can be used per campaign, and they can be managed independently for search and collection components.

### Q3: Can merchants rename the app blocks in Shopify?

No, the app blocks are named Promo Default Profile and Promo Secondary Profile, aligning with the two profile options in the dashboard. These names are only visible in the Merchant Dashboard and in the Shopify visual editor and are not visible to clients.

### Q4: Is the process the same for creating and controlling Promo Cards within Collections?

Yes, the process is the same, but feature different components. You can either copy the style from Search to Smart Collections or choose to style them differently, as desired.

### Q5: Are there any prerequisites I need to meet before using this functionality?

Yes, merchant stores need to be running front-end liquid framework on version 2024-06.01 or later.
