# Image Change on Hover Component

You can enable image change on hover to display alternative product images when customers hover over product cards, providing a quick preview of additional product details or variations. 

This interactive feature enhances the browsing experience by allowing customers to see multiple product views without clicking through to product pages.

Implementing hover-based image changes helps showcase product features more effectively, whether you're highlighting different angles, colors, or style variations. 

By enabling the image_2_url field in your Merchant Dashboard, you provide customers with richer product information directly in search results and collections, which can help improve engagement and support more informed purchasing decisions.

The Image Hover feature in the Merchandising Cloud (formerly Findify) Native Shopify App enhances the visual presentation of product cards by enabling the display of alternative images when hovering over the primary product image. 

This feature provides merchants with a simple yet effective way to showcase additional product details and variations, improving the overall browsing experience for customers.

## Enabling Image Change on Hover

To enable the Image Hover feature, go to your Merchant Dashboard. Navigate to Settings --> Primary Setup --> Filtering. From the list that appears on screen, locate the image_2_url field. Toggle the corresponding button to enable it. 

Once enabled, hovering over a product image will automatically trigger the display of the second image added to the product in question. To change which image appears on hover, go to your Shopify dashboard and reorder the product images accordingly.

## Usage

Once the Image Hover feature is enabled, it seamlessly integrates into your Shopify store's product browsing interface. Customers can simply hover their cursor over a product image to view the alternative image associated with it, providing them with a quick glimpse of additional product details or variations.
