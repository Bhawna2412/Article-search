# Loox

You can integrate Loox photo reviews with Merchandising Cloud to display customer-submitted product photos and ratings on product cards. This visual social proof helps customers see real-world product usage, which can significantly influence purchasing decisions by providing authentic product representations.

Loox integration enhances product cards with user-generated visual content that helps build trust and provides realistic product expectations. By showing actual customer photos alongside product listings, you create more authentic product presentations that can help improve conversion rates through increased buyer confidence and reduced post-purchase dissatisfaction.

We offer a number of integrations with complementing services and plugins. In this guide, we will walk you through how to set up the Loox product reviews app on your Shopify store.

To start, you must have already downloaded the Loox app from the [Shopify App Store.](https://apps.shopify.com/loox)

![3f5b6c0-Loox.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/CRRBRPMJOKND/3f5b6c0-loox.png)

## Step 1: Enable Loox Core App in Your Shopify Theme

**Enable Loox Core App**: Ensure that the Loox Core app is enabled and integrated into your Shopify theme.

## Step 2: Insert Loox Widget Code in `findify-product-card.liquid`

**Copy and Paste Widget Code**: Insert the following Loox Widget code into your `findify-product-card.liquid` file. This will display the Loox rating on your product cards.

JSX

```
<div class="loox-rating" data-fetch data-id="{{ id }}"></div>
```

> **Note:** **📘Note: **Replace `{{ id }}` with the Liquid variable that contains the unique identifier (ID) of each product.

## Step 3: Additional Configurations

**Whitelist External Domains**: If necessary, whitelist external domains to display the Loox iframe. More information can be found [here](https://help.loox.io/article/416-how-do-i-white-list-an-external-domain-to-display-the-loox-iframe).

## Step 4: Customize Integration (if required)

**Customize Widget**: Modify the Loox Widget code or styling as per your design requirements.

By following these steps, you can seamlessly integrate Loox Reviews into your Findify-powered Shopify store. Loox Reviews provide valuable social proof and enhance customer trust directly on your product pages.
