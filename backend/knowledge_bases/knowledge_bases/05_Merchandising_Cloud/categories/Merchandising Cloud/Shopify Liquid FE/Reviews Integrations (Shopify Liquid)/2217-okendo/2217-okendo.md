# Okendo

You can integrate Okendo reviews and ratings with Merchandising Cloud to display comprehensive customer feedback on product cards throughout your search and collection pages. This integration brings detailed review data into the product discovery experience, helping customers evaluate products based on verified customer experiences.

Okendo integration provides rich review data that supports more informed purchasing decisions by surfacing customer feedback during product browsing. By displaying ratings, review counts, and review highlights on product cards, you help customers assess product quality and suitability more quickly, which can help improve conversion rates and reduce returns through better purchase alignment.

Our Native Shopify App, built using the Liquid language, allows for seamless integration with various review services, including Okendo. This guide will walk you through the steps to integrate Okendo into your Maropost Merchandising Cloud (formerly Findify) setup on Shopify.

To start, you must have already downloaded the Okendo app from the [Shopify App Store.](https://apps.shopify.com/okendo-reviews)
