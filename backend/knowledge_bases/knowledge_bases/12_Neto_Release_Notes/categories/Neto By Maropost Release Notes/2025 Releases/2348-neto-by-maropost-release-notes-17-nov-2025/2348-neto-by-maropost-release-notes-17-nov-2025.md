# Neto by Maropost Release Notes – 17 Nov 2025

## OVERVIEW

In this release, we are excited to announce an update to our purchase-related rewards system.

## EARN REWARDS FEATURE

Shoppers can now earn lucrative reward points on their recent in-store and online purchases. Merchants can enable this feature on their Neto POS account and incentivize shoppers to spend more on products to earn greater reward deals.

This, in turn, enables merchants to drive sales promotions across multiple channels and attract more traffic to their physical and online stores.
