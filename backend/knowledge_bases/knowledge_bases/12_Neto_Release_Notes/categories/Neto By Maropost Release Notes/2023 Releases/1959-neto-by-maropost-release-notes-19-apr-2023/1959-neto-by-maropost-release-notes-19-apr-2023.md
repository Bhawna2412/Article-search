# Neto by Maropost Release Notes – 19 Apr 2023

## **Improvements**

- PayPal Checkout and PayPal Advanced Credit and Debit Cards payments initiated at Neto Checkout pass the Neto Order ID to the PayPal transaction’s **Invoice ID** and **Description** fields. This is reflected in the Payment Details in Neto.
- PayPal Checkout and PayPal Advanced Credit and Debit Cards payments initiated from a customer’s My Account page pass the Neto Order ID to **only **the PayPal transaction’s **Description** field. This is reflected in the Payment Details in Neto.

## **Fixes**

- 
- Fixed an issue with Allied Express shipping consignments where the tracking details on the order page were not working.
