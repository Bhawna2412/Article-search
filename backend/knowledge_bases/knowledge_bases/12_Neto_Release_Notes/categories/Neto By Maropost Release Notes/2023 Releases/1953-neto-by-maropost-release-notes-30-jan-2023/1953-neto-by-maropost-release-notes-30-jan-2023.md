# Neto by Maropost Release Notes – 30 Jan 2023

## **Improvements and Updates**

- Added an eligibility check to the PayPal Advanced Debit and Credit payment method during setup and editing to check whether the merchant’s PayPal account is eligible to offer this option.
- Added an eligibility check to the My Account page of the webstore so that PayPal Cards does not appear as an available payment method if the method is not available to the merchant’s PayPal account.

## **Deprecated**

Removed non-supported and legacy carriers from the available carriers in the new Carrier & Labels configuration page. Note: existing setups can continue to be used, though they will not be maintained or updated.
