# Add a Business Logo to your Website

Upload different logos for different print docs and website locations.

[▶ YouTube Video](https://youtu.be/RW_p1nOOBbM)
To add logos to your Maropost Commerce control panel:

1. 
  In your Maropost Commerce control panel, navigate to **Settings & Tools** > **Logo**.
2.   - Invoices
  - Web shop
  - Managed checkout
  - Mobile shop
  - Facebook shop
  - Control panel
  - eBay store
  - Favicon
  - POS
  - .png
  - .jpg
  - .gif and
  - .bmp.

  Click on the **Browse **button next to the relevant image to upload.

  The **Logos and Branding** page displays options for uploading logos for:

  The following file formats are used:

  ![image1](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/AddaBusinessLogotoyourWebsite.05T5g00000xpdU5EAI.png)
3. 
  Test your logo size by generating the print doc on a test order, or browsing to your website's home page.

  When adding a logo, you have the option to resize it from the original dimensions. If you upload a logo and find it's too big, upload it again and reduce the size by percentage (e.g. 50% to shrink it in half) and check the new results. It is not recommended that you increase the size of the image when uploading, as it can cause distortion.
4. Once uploaded, click on the **Save Changes** button.

You have successfully uploaded a business logo to your Maropost Commerce control panel.
