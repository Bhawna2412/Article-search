# Webstore Quickstart Guide

This guide covers the main steps to get your webstore live. It's intended for users who are already familiar with Neto or similar eCommerce platforms.

## Basics

Set up you company information and prepare your control panel.

1. Navigate to **Settings & tools** > **Business details** and enter your company information.

> **Note:** Enter a BCC email address to receive a copy of system emails that your customers receive.

2. Navigate to **Settings & tools** > **Logos** and upload your company logos.

> **Note:** As a minimum add a website logo, invoice logo, and favicon.

3. Navigate to **Settings & tools** > **Staff users** and [create user accounts](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1438-add-a-staff-user-account) so that your staff can log in to Neto.

## Build your Webstore

Create the front end of your website that your customers will see.

1. Navigate to **Webstore** > **Themes**. Install and customise a theme for your webstore.
2. Navigate to **Webstore** > **Web page**. Update the default pages and add new web pages.
3. Navigate to **Webstore** > **Menus**. [Update the existing menus, and add links](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1101-add-or-edit-website-menus) to any new pages you've added.

> **Note:** Remove the "edit menu" link in each menu.

4. Navigate to **Webstore** > **Advertising campaigns**. [Upload a banner](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1114-website-banner-adverts-and-carousels) to the home page of your website.

> **Note:** Create banners for free in [Canva](https://www.canva.com/).

5. Navigate to **Webstore** > **Webstore settings**. Review the settings and [change how your webstore functions](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1106-webstore-settings).
6. Navigate to **Webstore** > **Checkout settings**. Review the settings and [change how your checkout page functions](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1037-checkout-settings).

## Load your data

Add your products, product categories and existing customers.

1. Navigate to **Products** > **Active** and [add your products manually or by importing a file](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/26-add-and-manage-products).

> **Note:** You can also create your product categories at the same time you add products.

2. Navigate to **Products** > **Product categories** and [add your product categories manually or by importing a file](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/685-add-a-product-category)

> **Note:** Upload images to the top level categories so they display on your website.

3. Navigate to **Customers** > **View customers & prospects**. Add existing customers to Neto manually or by importing a file

## Set up Tasks

Set up integrations with your shipping carriers, payment gateways, and accounting system.

1. Navigate to **Shipping** > **Shipping options** and [set up your shipping costs](https://www.netohq.com/support/s/knowledge#category=Support__c:Shipping_carrier_setup__c).

> **Note:** We recommend [Sendle](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/809-sendle-shipping-option-set-up#category=Support__c:Shipping_carrier_setup__c).

2. Navigate to **Settings & tools** > **Payment methods** and add payment methods so customers can pay for their orders.

> **Note:** We recommend [PayPal](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/811-paypal-payment-method), Stripe (credit cards) and either Afterpay or Zip.

3. Navigate to **Addons** and click **Accounting** in the side menu. Install the add-on for your accounting system and set it up in Neto.

> **Note:** Neto Commerce Inventory is compatible with the Xero direct integration.

4. Navigate to **Addons** and click **Marketing**. We recommend SmartrMail or [MailChimp for Neto](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1500-mailchimp-for-neto).

## Optional Features

These modules provide additional functionality and efficiency.

- Navigate to **Stock control** > **Purchase orders** and install Neto Commerce Inventory to manage your stock with purchase orders.
- Click on Analytics studio and set up powerful reports.
