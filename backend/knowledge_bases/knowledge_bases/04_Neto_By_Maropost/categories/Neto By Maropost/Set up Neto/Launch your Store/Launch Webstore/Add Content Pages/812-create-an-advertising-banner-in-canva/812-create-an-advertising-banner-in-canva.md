# Create an Advertising Banner in Canva

Canva is a free tool to design and create media assets for your website. We recommend Canva for creating advertising banners. To create a banner in Canva:

1. [Sign up to Canva](https://www.canva.com/signup). Canva is free for basic accounts, and you can upgrade to Pro for additional features.
2. [Log in to your Canva account](https://www.canva.com/login).
3. In the menu, click on **Templates**.
4. In the search bar, search for **Web Banners**.
  ![image1](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/CreateanAdvertisingBannerinCanva01.05T5g00000sz8QKEAY.png)
5. Pick a template to use as a starting point for your own banner. It will be loaded into the editor for you to customise.

> **Note:** If you want to try other templates it's easy to switch on the **Templates** tab.

6.   - Background image.
  - Main text.
  - Sub text.

  There are three elements you'll be able to change in most templates:
7. Change the background image by pasting (ctrl/cmd + v) a new one on top. Then expand it to fit the size of your banner. [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com/) and [Pexels](https://www.pexels.com/) are great resources for free stock images.
  ![Image](https://www.neto.com.au/assets/images/canva2.gif)
8. Edit the text fields to display your own message. The toolbar at the top lets you adjust font, size, colour, and other elements.
  ![image3](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/CreateanAdvertisingBannerinCanva03.05T5g00000sz82xEAA.png)
9. Once you're happy with your banner, click the **Download** button to save it to your computer.

You can now add the image to your website ad banner space.
