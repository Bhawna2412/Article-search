# Carrier Based Pricing Shipping Option Setup

[https://www.youtube.com/embed/dVU3JlYQZWM](https://www.youtube.com/embed/dVU3JlYQZWM)

> **Note:** Neto partners can set this up for you. [Visit the job request portal](https://www.netohq.com/job-request-portal) for a quote.

The shipping wizard walks you through all the steps required to configure your carrier rates. To set up your shipping options using the wizard:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Shipping** > **Shipping Options**.
2. Click on the green **Add new shipping option** button from the top right hand side.
  ![image1](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/CarrierBasedPricingShippingOptionSetup1.05T5g00000sz5UREAY.png)
3. Click **Set up shipping carrier based pricing**.
  ![image2](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/CarrierBasedPricingShippingOptionSetup2.05T5g00000sz8HwEAI.png)
4. Make sure you have your API credentials handy and click the **Continue** button.
5. You'll now be in **Step 2**. Enter the required account details. The details required change slightly with every carrier, but most include and API key or, username, and password. Once complete, click the **Next Step** button.
6. You'll now be in **Step 3**. Select the services you wish to provide to your customers. Once select, click **Next Step**.
  ![image3](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/CarrierBasedPricingShippingOptionSetup3.05T5g00000sz8IfEAI.png)
7. You'll now be in **Step 4**. Here you can optionally rename the services and add a description. Once complete, click **Next Step**.
  ![image4](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/CarrierBasedPricingShippingOptionSetup4.05T5g00000sz6hPEAQ.png)
8. You'll now be in **Step 5**. Here you can toggle the option to show visibility to customers and staff and set PO BOX availability. Once complete, click **Next Step**.
  ![image5](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/CarrierBasedPricingShippingOptionSetup5.05T5g00000sz6XUEAY.png)
9. You'll now be in **Step 6**. Here you can optionally provide a discount based on the customers overall spend amount, eg. If a customer spends over $100, they get free shipping. Once complete, click **Next Step**.
  ![image6](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/CarrierBasedPricingShippingOptionSetup6.05T5g00000sz89UEAQ.png)
10. You'll now be in **Step 7**. Choose whether you'll be using a standard printer to print consignment labels or a thermal printer. Once complete, click **Next Step**.
  ![image7](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/CarrierBasedPricingShippingOptionSetup7.05T5g00000sz8L5EAI.png)
11. You'll now be in **Step 8**. Review your final shipping setup. If you need to make any changes, click on the **Previous Step** button, alternatively click **Complete** to activate your new shipping option.
  ![image8](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/CarrierBasedPricingShippingOptionSetup8.05T5g00000sz8LyEAI.png)

Your shipping option is now set up. We recommend you test it on your webstore with different postcodes to ensure it works as expected.
