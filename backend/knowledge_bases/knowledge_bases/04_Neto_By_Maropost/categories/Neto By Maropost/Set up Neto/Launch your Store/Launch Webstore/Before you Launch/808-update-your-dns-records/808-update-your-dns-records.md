# Update your DNS Records

Your DNS (domain name server) must be hosted by a third party, external to Neto. Once you have requested that your site go live we will send you the IP address to change the A and WWW records in your DNS. Neto provides basic support only with this, they are unable to make the changes to a third party system on your behalf.

You do not need to notify Neto if you transfer the domain name between third party servers.

**Please Note**: The instructions below all require information provided to you by the Neto launch specialists. If you have not yet requested that your site go live, please do so [here](https://netoecommerce.wufoo.com/forms/maropost-commerce-cloud-go-live/).

If you are not familiar with DNS, GoDaddy have a great guide and video [here](https://au.godaddy.com/help/what-is-dns-665).

If your DNS provider is not in this list consult their website or support team for assistance.
