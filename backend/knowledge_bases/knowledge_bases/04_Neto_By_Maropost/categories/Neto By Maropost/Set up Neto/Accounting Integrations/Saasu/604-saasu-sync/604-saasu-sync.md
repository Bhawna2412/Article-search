## Overview

Saasu Sync is designed for sole traders, contractors, startups and small businesses. Thousands of product and service businesses use cloud accounting to get their BAS and Single Touch Payroll done.

This add-on is built and maintained by mysync, and requires a Saasu account. Pricing can be found [here](https://www.saasu.com/pricing/).

## Benefits & Features

- 
  **Sync Orders and Inventory:** Update your Maropost products from your Saasu inventory stock levels. Sync orders, customers and payments from Neto.
- 
  **Custom Data Flows:** Customise and add additional functionality for users when requested.

These data transfers can be configured in mysync.

## Set up Saasu

To set up the Saasu Sync add-on in your Neto webstore:

1. 
  In your Neto control panel, click the **Addons** menu.
2. 
  Find the Saasu Sync add-on and click the **Install** button.
3. 
  The Saasu add-on will be installed in your control panel.
4. 
  In the pop-up window, click the **Configure Saasu Sync** button.
5. 
  Follow the steps on the [mysync help site](https://help.mysync.io/hc/en-us/articles/4404104762905-SAASU-Sync-Neto-Guide) to set up your SAASU Sync connection.
