# MYOB Account Right Live by Novel Integrations

#### This add-on was built by Novel Integrations

[Visit add-on website!](https://novelintegrations.com.au/)

For support with this add-on [contact Novel Integrations](https://novelintegrations.com.au/contact/).

## Overview

Sync and track the status of your Neto by Maropost orders and RMAs in MYOB. Novel Integration allows mapping of locations in MYOB with warehouse in Neto. This integration offers new features that will save hours in correcting stock levels in Neto or creating credits in MYOB for RMAs generated in Neto.

This connector syncs orders live based on selected order statuses. It has the ability to sync Neto orders as a Sales Order in MYOB and then convert it to a Sales Invoices once the order is dispatched in Neto.

Duplication of customers does not occur with this integration. Both customers and Payments are matched from Neto to MYOB and MYOB to Neto.

## Set up MYOB Account Right Live by Novel Integrations

To set up the MYOB Account Right Live by Novel Integrations add-on in your Neto webstore:

1. In your Neto control panel, click on the **Addons** menu.
2. 
  Find the MYOB Account Right by Novel Integrations add-on and click the **Install** button.
3. 
  The MYOB Account Right by Novel Integrations add-on will be installed in your control panel.
4. In the pop-up window, click the **Configure MYOB AccountRight Live by Novel Integrations** button to connect your Neto account…
5. 
  Follow the steps on the Novel Integrations website [here](https://novelintegrations.com.au/maropost-myob-integration-guide/) to set up the add-on.
