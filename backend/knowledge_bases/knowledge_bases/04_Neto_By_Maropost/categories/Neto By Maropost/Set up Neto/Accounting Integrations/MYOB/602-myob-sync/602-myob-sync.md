## Overview

MYOB AccountRight is powerful accounting with business management capabilities so your client’s business can stay competitive and get ahead. Within the dashboard, you get a powerful single view to interact with your clients online.

Gain timely, actionable insights into your clients’ businesses with Power BI, a powerful analytics tool that allows you to give even better advice to your AccountRight Live clients.

Richer data allows you to have better conversations with your clients, helping them identify opportunities and threats in advance to maximise their business performance.

## Set up MYOB

To set up the MYOB add-on in your Neto webstore:

1. 
  In your Neto control panel, click on the **Addons** menu.
2. 
  Find the MYOB Sync add-on and click the **Install** button.
3. 
  The MYOB Sync add-on will be installed in your control panel.
4. 
  In the pop-up window, click the **Configure MYOB by Sync** button.
5. 
  Follow the steps on the [mysync help site](https://help.mysync.io/hc/en-us/articles/900006148026-MYOB-Sync-Neto-Guide) to set up your MYOB Sync connection.
