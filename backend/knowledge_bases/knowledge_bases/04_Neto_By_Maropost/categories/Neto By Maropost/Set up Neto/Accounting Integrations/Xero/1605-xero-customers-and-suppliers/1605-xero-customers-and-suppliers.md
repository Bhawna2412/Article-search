# Xero Customers and Suppliers

## Import Customers and Suppliers from Xero

Customers and suppliers can be imported from Xero into Neto by Maropost using the **Import Customer Settings** in the Xero workflow. This will import any contacts from Xero that are set as a customer or supplier into Neto. Ensure your Xero contacts are correctly set as either customers or suppliers to ensure smooth operation.

> **Tip**: We recommend consulting your accountant or Xero specialist on the best way to flag contacts as Suppliers in Xero as part of your business processes.

These settings will import both customers and suppliers. You can turn either of the above imports off in the **Xero** > **Tasks** menu. Click on either the **Import customer from Xero** or **Import Suppliers from Xero** task IDs, and change their status to **Inactive**.

> **Important**: Individual contacts or suppliers can’t be excluded from the import, and will be re-imported if deleted from Neto.

## Supplier Matching

Suppliers match differently with customers. In Xero, the contact name of the supplier must match the supplier’s **Company** field in Neto.

## Xero Contacts that are both Customers and Suppliers

When you have an Xero contact that is both a supplier and customer, extra steps need to be taken to ensure smooth operation. In these cases, update the customer in Neto so that the username matches the supplier ID.
