# Currency and Tax Settings

Product prices have three options related to tax:

- Tax inclusive - the price of the product includes tax.
- Tax exclusive - the product price does not include tax.
- Tax free - tax is not charged for this product.

The tax rate and defaults are set in the currency and tax settings. To change your currency and tax settings:

1. In your control panel navigate to **Settings & Tools** > **Currency & tax**.
2. Modify your settings as required. Use the table below as a reference for the settings available.
3. Click the **Save** button.
