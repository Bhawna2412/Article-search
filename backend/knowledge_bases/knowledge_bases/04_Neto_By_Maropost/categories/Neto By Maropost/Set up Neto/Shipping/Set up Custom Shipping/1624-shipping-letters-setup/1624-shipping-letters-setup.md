# Shipping Letters Setup

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Shipping** > **Shipping Option**.
2. Click on **Add New Shipping Option**.
3. Click **Set My Own Pricing**.
4. In the advanced options select **Weight or size** of order.
5. Choose **Select Countries** and tick **Australia**, then move on to the **Next Step**.
6. Name your pricing table and select your weight range and cost for your 1st parcel and subsequent parcel when it exceeds this weight. At the time writing this article, the weight range is 0kg to 0.25kg, and the cost of a letter is $1.00. Select the shipping categories to apply this shipping option to, and once complete, click **Next Step**.
7. Set the name to **Letters** and set **Available to PO Box Addresses** to **Available**. Click **Next Step**.
8. Choose how you will print the shipping labels and click **Next Step**.
9. You can now optionally tick **Add Discount Incentive** if you want to make this option free over an certain spending amount. Click **Next Step**.
10. Review and click **Complete**.
11. Now that we’ve completed the shipping option setup, we need to create a **Shipping Category** which will funnel products to be recognised by this option.
12. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Settings & Tools** > **Shipping**.
13. Click **Shipping Categories**. Click the green **Add New** button on the top right hand side.
14. Name the new category **Letters** and **Save & Close**.
15. To add our shipping category, navigate to **Shipping** > **Shipping Options**.
16. Click on **Letters **to edit the shipping option.
17. Scroll down to the **Map your shipping categories to Shipping Services**. Delete all of the categories (such as Default, Dangerous Goods, etc) showing in this section.
18. Click **Add Shipping Category** and add **Letters** for both drop-down menu options. Click **Save & Close**.
19. Now go back to the **Shipping Options** page and edit your remaining shipping setups. Simply add **Letters** as a shipping category and populate the drop-down menu's as above. This will allow letters to be posted with other bigger items.
20. Now that your shipping options and categories are setup, you need to identify products which are only to be shipped by letter. To do this, navigate to **Products** > **View Products** and edit product you wish to make changes to.
21. Click the **Shipping** tab section and select **Letters** as the **Shipping Category** and save changes. Repeat for any other products you wish to send as letters.
22. Now that this is complete, your product will be marked as a ‘letter’ shipping option during the pick, pack and dispatch process.
