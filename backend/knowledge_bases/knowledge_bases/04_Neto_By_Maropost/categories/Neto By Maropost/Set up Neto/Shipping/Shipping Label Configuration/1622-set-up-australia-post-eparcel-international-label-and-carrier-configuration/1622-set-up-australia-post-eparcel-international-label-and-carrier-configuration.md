# Set up Australia Post eParcel International Label and Carrier Configuration

## What is eParcel International?

**Important**: Setting up eParcel International as a shipping option is a manual process based on the rates provided to you from Australia Post. Please contact Neto support to complete this section of the setup.

eParcel International is a service provided by Australia Post. eParcel International facilitates the use of the following carrier services:

| Service | Weight Limit | Delivery Time | Included Features | Extra Cover up to |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Express Courier International | 20kg | 2-4 business days* | Tracking^ Signature on delivery# | $5000† |
| Pack and Track International | 2kg | 3-10 business days* | Tracking^ | $500† |
| Registered Post International | 2kg | 3-10 business days* | Signature on delivery# Compensation (up to $100) | N/A |
| Airmail | 20kg | 3-10 business days* | N/A | $5,000† |

For further information on the services above go here: [eParcel International](http://auspost.com.au/business-solutions/international-contract-services.html).

## Does eParcel International have any requirements?

In order to use Neto with eParcel International label generation and manifest submission, you will need:

- An existing eParcel Account.
- eParcel API Details.
