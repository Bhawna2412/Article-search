# Retrieve UPS Shipping Label Details

**Please Note**: UPS shipping labels are only available US merchants.

Neto’s UPS integration allows you to create consignment labels for sales orders and submit them directly to UPS. Neto sets up the label for you free of charge, but requires some of your account details. To retrieve this information from UPS:

1. Log in to your UPS account at [https://www.ups.com/us/en/Home.page](https://www.ups.com/us/en/Home.page).
2. Click the **Services** option in the main menu, and choose **Integrating UPS Technology**.
3. Click on **UPS Developer Kit** link.
4. Click on **Shipping API** link.
5. Click the **Developers: Download APIs** button.
6. Click the **Manage Access Keys** link.
7. Click the **Request New Access Key** link.
8. Confirm your details and click **Request Key**.
9. Copy the key provided to you, and paste it into the [label setup from](https://forms.neto.com.au/services/shippingcarrierlabelsetup.html), along with the rest of the information required.

Neto staff will notify you once you label set up is complete.
