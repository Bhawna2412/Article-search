# Create and Retrieve eParcel or StarTrack Shipping, Tracking and Label Details

## API Details

1. Log into the [Australia Post Development portal](https://developers.auspost.com.au/). Your eParcel account manager can provide you access to the portal.
2. If you haven't already you will need to register for an API key. Click **Register now for an API key** under the **Shipping and Tracking** section. You will need to do this for each eParcel account you have.
  ![Create and Retrieve eParcel or StarTrack Shipping, Tracking and Label Details 01.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000OP9WdMAL.jpg)
3. On the next page click **Complete Registration**.
  ![Create and Retrieve eParcel or StarTrack Shipping, Tracking and Label Details 02.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000OP9pYMAT.jpg)
4. Enter you Australia Post **Account ID** and check your **Email address** is correct. Leave all the options ticked as shown below and click **Next**.
  ![Create and Retrieve eParcel or StarTrack Shipping, Tracking and Label Details 03.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000OP9piMAD.jpg)
5. Select **Yes** and choose Neto from the drop down list. Check the confirmation box and click **Next**.
  ![Create and Retrieve eParcel or StarTrack Shipping, Tracking and Label Details 04.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000OP9pxMAD.jpg)
6. Choose your state from the list and click send.
  ![Create and Retrieve eParcel or StarTrack Shipping, Tracking and Label Details 05.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000OP9qbMAD.jpg)

You will need to wait for Australia Post to process your request. You will receive a notification to the email address you provided. Once this has been received:

1. Navigate to the **Projects** tab.
  ![Create and Retrieve eParcel or StarTrack Shipping, Tracking and Label Details 06.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000OP9qvMAD.jpg)
2. Click create a new **Project**. and enter and **Name**.
  ![Create and Retrieve eParcel or StarTrack Shipping, Tracking and Label Details 07.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000OP9rKMAT.jpg)
3. Generate an API Key by clicking **Manage Access**.
  ![Create and Retrieve eParcel or StarTrack Shipping, Tracking and Label Details 08.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000OP7xgMAD.jpg)
4. Click on **Create a key**.
  ![Create and Retrieve eParcel or StarTrack Shipping, Tracking and Label Details 09.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000OP9sSMAT.jpg)
5. Nickname your key and **Secret** (password), and keep note of what it is. Also keep note of your API key that is generated on the next page.
  ![Create and Retrieve eParcel or StarTrack Shipping, Tracking and Label Details 10.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000OP9t1MAD.jpg)
6. Activate API Services by going to the **Services** menu, and clicking on **Manage services**.
  ![Create and Retrieve eParcel or StarTrack Shipping, Tracking and Label Details 11.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000OP9tfMAD.jpg)
7. Enable all services and click the **Update** button.
  ![Create and Retrieve eParcel or StarTrack Shipping, Tracking and Label Details 12.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000OP9unMAD.jpg)

You will now be able to see your API key from the project page.

## MLID, Charge Codes and Account Number

Neto's eParcel label set up also requires some account information that can be accessed through eParcel:

1. Log in to your [eParcel account](https://eparcel.auspost.com.au/eParcel/common/auth/login.do).
2. Click on the **Administration** tab and choose **Contract Information**.
3. Copy the required information from this page into the label set up form.

You can now complete and submit the label set up form.
