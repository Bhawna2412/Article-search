# Australia Post eParcel International Shipping Manual Setup

**Please Note**: This support article requires the Neto Commerce Ship add-on.

eParcel International services can also use your live rates provided by Australia Post. Follow the steps in this article to add international shipping using your eParcel account details.

> **Note:** Neto partners can set this up for you. [Visit the job request portal](https://www.netohq.com/job-request-portal) for a quote.

## Shipping Zones

Zones for eParcel International come pre-installed in Neto. They are under the name **AP International**, and the rates provided to you will match these zones accordingly.

## Prepare your Data

eParcel International will provide your rates in a PDF which isn't able to be imported into Neto. Rather than transpose it into a spreadsheet and import, it is quicker to populate the rates through the shipping wizard.

## Add the Shipping Option

eParcel International shipping is best created through the shipping wizard.:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Shipping** > **Shipping Options**.
2. Select **Add New Shipping Option**.
  ![image1](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/AustraliaPosteParcelInternationalShippingManualSetup1.05T5g00000xrevaEAA.png)
3. Choose **Set My Own Pricing**.
  ![image2](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/AustraliaPosteParcelInternationalShippingManualSetup2.05T5g00000xrfEMEAY.png)
4. In the advanced options click **Weight Or Size**.
  ![image3](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/AustraliaPosteParcelInternationalShippingManualSetup3.05T5g00000xrdV3EAI.png)
5. Select **Preset Carrier Locations**, pick **Auspost International** and **Select All** locations, then click **Next Step**.
  ![image4](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/AustraliaPosteParcelInternationalShippingManualSetup4.05T5g00000xrfFaEAI.png)
6. You now need to transpose the pricing data from the spreadsheet into the fields provided, depending on which service you're setting up. In this example, we're setting up **Airmail**.
  ![image5](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/AustraliaPosteParcelInternationalShippingManualSetup5.05T5g00000xrb5qEAA.png)
7. eParcel International has weight limits on its services. With Airmail, there's two bands of rates, for packages under 2kg, and packages over 2kg up to 20kg. Enter a **Name**, and check the **Set Different Pricing For Certain Shipping Locations** box and check all locations you have pricing information for .
  ![image6](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/AustraliaPosteParcelInternationalShippingManualSetup6.05T5g00000xrdFYEAY.png)
8. Enter the pricing information provided by Australia Post into the fields, adding a new band for the 2kg to 20kg rates. The **Per Item Plus** charge needs to go into the **1st Parcel Override** and **Per Subsequent Parcel Override**. Enter the **Per KG** rate into the **Per Kg Override**
  ![image7](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/AustraliaPosteParcelInternationalShippingManualSetup7.05T5g00000xrd9pEAA.png)
9. Check the shipping categories this option will apply to, and then click **Next Step**.
  ![image8](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/AustraliaPosteParcelInternationalShippingManualSetup8.05T5g00000xrfHQEAY.png)
10. Enter a **Name** and **Description** for the shipping option, and set the **Visibility** and **PO Box** setting.
  ![image9](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/AustraliaPosteParcelInternationalShippingManualSetup9.05T5g00000xreIxEAI.png)
11. Choose how you will print labels and move on to the **Next Step**.
  ![image10](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/AustraliaPosteParcelInternationalShippingManualSetup10.05T5g00000xrfHjEAI.png)
12. Configure pricing discounts based on the order total if desired (optional) and move on the **Next Step**.
  ![image11](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/AustraliaPosteParcelInternationalShippingManualSetup11.05T5g00000xrb8LEAQ.png)
13. Review the details and **Complete Setup**. At this stage it is highly recommended that you test the shipping option to ensure accurate costs are returned.
  ![image12](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/AustraliaPosteParcelInternationalShippingManualSetup12.05T5g00000xrfJ2EAI.png)
14. Complete the [Shipping Carrier Label Setup](http://forms.neto.com.au/services/shippingcarrierlabelsetup.html) form and Neto staff will configure your labels as a free service.
