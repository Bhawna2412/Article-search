# Control Which Countries are Available in your Checkout

In your Neto by Maropost control panel navigate to **Settings & Tools** > **Shipping**. Click **Available Countries**.

From here you can choose your sort order or set particular countries to inactive.

Select the countries you wish to make inactive, scroll down to the bottom of the page and click **With x Selected** button and then click **Mark Selected as Inactive**.
