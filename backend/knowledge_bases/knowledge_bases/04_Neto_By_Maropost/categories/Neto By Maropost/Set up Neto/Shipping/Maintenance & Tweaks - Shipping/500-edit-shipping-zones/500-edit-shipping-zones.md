# Edit Shipping Zones

## View and Edit Shipping Zones

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Settings & Tools** > **Shipping**.
2. Click on **Shipping Zones**.
  ![Edit Shipping Zones 1.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000PMaP1MAL.jpg)
3. You can filter the results by courier (carrier), preset (default), country, zone name, zone code, postcode or suburb. To view the preset shipping zones for all the major carriers already in the system click **View Shipping Zone Presets**.
4. Select the carrier zones you wish to view or update and apply the filter.
5. To delete the zone check the box next to it and click **Delete Selected**.
  ![Edit Shipping Zones 2.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000PMaRHMA1.jpg)
6. To update the zone, click on the zone code. In this screen you can edit the zone code, zone name or courier and add new post codes to the zone.
7. You can also download the zones table for the carrier in csv format, make changes on the table and then bulk upload the changes in the main shipping zones page.
  ![Edit Shipping Zones 3.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000PMaTNMA1.jpg)
8. **Save** your changes.

## Import Shipping Zones

To import your shipping zones please refer to the steps in [this article](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/601-custom-shipping-overview-and-setup#shipping-zones).

## Download Shipping Zones

You can download carrier shipping zones as a CSV file to make changes in bulk and upload back into Neto. To download shipping zones:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Settings & Tools** > **Shipping**.
2. Click on **Shipping Zones**.
  ![Edit Shipping Zones 4.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000PMa2oMAD.jpg)
3. Click on a zone for the courier that you want to download.
4. Click the **Download Zones Table For This Courier** button.
  ![Edit Shipping Zones 5.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000PMaTXMA1.jpg)

The courier's zones will download to your computer as a CSV file.

## Delete Shipping Zones

To delete shipping zones:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Settings & Tools** > **Shipping**.
2. Click on **Shipping Zones**.
  ![Edit Shipping Zones 6.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000PMVY0MAP.jpg)
3. Filter the results by courier (carrier), preset (default), country, zone name, zone code, postcode or suburb. To view the preset shipping zones for all the major carriers already in the system click **view shipping zone presets**.
  ![Edit Shipping Zones 7.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000PMUBYMA5.jpg)
4. Select the carrier whose zones you wish to delete and press **Delete Selected**.
  ![Edit Shipping Zones 8.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000PMY30MAH.jpg)
