# Paymark Credit Card Payment Method

This addon is only available for New Zealand customers where NZD$ is set as the currency in **Settings & Tools** > **Currency & Tax Settings**.

[Image: image1]

**Important**: The Paymark credit card gateway can no longer be installed. Please speak with our customer success team on 1300 730 300 to discuss our available payment gateway options.

With Click, the Paymark eCommerce gateway, they’ve made it easy for everyone to accept digital payments from their website or smartphone apps in New Zealand.

[https://www.youtube.com/embed/BVXRFR3uwko?rel=0&controls=0&showinfo=0](https://www.youtube.com/embed/BVXRFR3uwko?rel=0&controls=0&showinfo=0)

1. In your Neto by Maropost control panel navigate to **Settings & Tools** > **Payment Methods**.
2. Click the **Complete Setup** button on the recommended providers where the Paymark logo appears. A popup will appear, scroll down and select **Paymark** from the available options.
3. Under configuration, enter your** account ID**, **Username **and **Password **(all provided by Paymark).
4. Set any surcharges, minimum or maximum spending’s.
5. Press the **Complete** button.
