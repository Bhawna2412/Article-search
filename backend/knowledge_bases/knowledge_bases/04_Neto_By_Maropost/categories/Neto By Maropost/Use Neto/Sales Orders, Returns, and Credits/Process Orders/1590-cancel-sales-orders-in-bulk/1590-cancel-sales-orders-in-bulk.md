# Cancel Sales Orders in Bulk

## Steps

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Sales Orders** > **View Orders**.
2. Click the checkbox next to each order (within the **Order ID** column) to be cancelled.
3. Scroll to the bottom of the page to locate the **Bulk Actions** button.
4. Select **Cancelled** from the **Change Order Status** dropdown.
5. Click the **Apply ** button. Neto will display a dialog box, asking you to confirm the cancellation.
6. Click the **OK** button.

## Result

The selected orders will now be cancelled, and the items will be returned to stock.
