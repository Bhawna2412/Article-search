# Manage your Neto Subscription

Your subscription (or plan) determines how much you're billed every month, based on your plan and any additional features. To view pricing, visit [https://www.netohq.com/au/pricing](https://www.netohq.com/pricing/plans).

## View Subscription Details

Your Neto subscription can be accessed through your control panel. To view your subscription details:

1. In your Neto control panel click on your name in the top right hand corner and select **Manage Subscription** from the drop down menu.
  ![Image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/ManageyourMaropostCommerceSubscription1.05T5g00000hxC9kEAE.png)
2. Click on the **Subscriptions** tile.
  ![Image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/ManageyourMaropostCommerceSubscription2.05T5g00000hxDqZEAU.png)
3. Your plan name will appear. Click the **View** link on the right hand side.
4. An overview of your subscription appears. In the **Items** section, click on tile that displays the amount recurring/pending.
  ![Image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/ManageyourMaropostCommerceSubscription3.05T5g00000hxDr3EAE.png)

Your plan details page displays listing all of the included modules you're subscribed to.

> **Note:** Annual revenue is calculated from the Dispatched Orders Report. Refer to [this article](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1520-dispatched-orders-report) to see how the totals are calculated.

## Add or Edit a Payment Method

You are invoiced for your subscription fees in advance (monthly from the date of sign up) with fees covering the subsequent month. To change which how your account is billed, or update an existing payment method:

1. In your Neto control panel click on your name in the top right hand corner and select **Manage Subscription** from the drop down menu.
2. In the payment method section, click the **Manage** link.
  ![Image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/ManageyourMaropostCommerceSubscription4.05T5g00000hxDrhEAE.png)
3.   - Select credit card from the dropdown menu and click the **Add** button to enter your credit card details.
  - Click the **Edit** link on an existing card to update the details.
  1. Select PayPal from the dropdown menu and click the **Add** button.
    ![Image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/ManageyourMaropostCommerceSubscription5.05T5g00000hxCsuEAE.png)
  2. Confirm payment of any outstanding fees and click the **Add** button.
  3. You'll be directed to PayPal to log in with your credentials.
  4. Choose from your linked payment methods and click the **Continue** button.
  5. Click the **Agree and Continue** button.

  You can now add or edit a credit card or PayPal account

  To add or edit your credit card details:

  **Please Note**: Ensure the postcode you've entered doesn't match the one your bank has on file.

  To add your PayPal account:

  You'll be returned to the payment methods page and PayPal will be used to pay your Neto subscription fees.
4. Click the **Save** button.

Your credit card is now added/updated, and will be billed automatically for your subscription fees.

If you have any questions about Neto fees, please [contact us](https://www.netohq.com/support/s/ask-us).

## Pay an Invoice

Your credit card or PayPal account is billed automatically for your Neto fees. If a payment has failed you can pay manually by following this process:

1. In your Neto control panel click on your name in the top right hand corner and select **Manage Subscription** from the drop down menu.
2. In the summary section, click **Invoices**.
3. On the invoice you wish to pay, click **View**.
4. On the invoice details screen, click **Make payment**.
5. Select the stored payment method you wish to use and the amount you wish to pay, and then click **Pay**.

## Generate a Statement

You can generate a statement whenever you need through your control panel.

### Neto Statement

To generate a statement:

1. In your Neto control panel click on your name in the top right hand corner and select **Manage Subscription** from the drop down menu.
2. Click the **Statements** tile.
  ![Image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/ManageyourMaropostCommerceSubscription6.05T5g00000hxBi2EAE.png)
3. Click the **Generate** button.
  ![Image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/ManageyourMaropostCommerceSubscription7.05T5g00000hxDOREA2.png)
4. Set the date range and filters for your statement and click the **Generate** button.
  ![Image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/ManageyourMaropostCommerceSubscription8.05T5g00000hxDarEAE.png)

Your statement is generated and can be emailed or downloaded as a PDF file.

### Sendle Statements

Sendle invoices are charged weekly (every Tuesday) for the previous week through Neto. If you processed an order directly within the Sendle website, you will be charged separately by Sendle. Sendle statements are located in the legacy billing portal:

1. In your Neto control panel click on your name in the top right hand corner and select **Legacy Billing Portal** from the drop down menu.
2. Click on **View all past invoices**.

You'll see a list of all invoices, with links to view and download them as PDF files. If you want to see the breakdown of shipments, you can access these through the [Sendle Dashboard](https://www.sendle.com/users/sign_in).

You'll need the email address used in Neto to generate consignments, as the Sendle username. You can find the email address in **Settings & Tools** > **Shipping** > **Carriers & Labels**. Click into the ID / Name for the label config that uses Sendle. The email address in use under the **Sender Details** is what Neto uses to generate Sendle consignments with (Do not change this address under any circumstances).

## Cancel your Neto Subscription

> **Important:** You must provide 60 days notice, and settle any outstanding balance, to completely cancel a Neto subscription and close your account.

To cancel your subscription, please email success@maropost.com, or reach out to the Maropost Success team on 1300 730 300 and select option 4 when prompted.

> **Note:** If your account is still on a trial status you do not need to contact us to cancel your subscription. Simply let the trial period lapse.

## Change of Ownership or Business Details

If your business changes ownership or has new details, please complete the [change of ownership form](https://netoecommerce.wufoo.com/forms/change-of-ownership-new-business-details/) so that our records can be updated to match.

To update your primary contact, in your control panel, click on the username in the top right, and select **Manage subscription**. You'll be taken to the billing portal. Click the menu button and choose **Profile**. On your profile, click the **Edit** link and change your contact details.
