# Request Access for a Partner

Neto by Maropost’s approved partners provide professional support, advice, and services. When you contract a partner to work for you, they often need access to your control panel or site template files via SFTP.

To grant access to your control panel and/or site template files via SFTP for an approved Neto partner:

1. Complete the [request partner access](https://netoecommerce.wufoo.com/forms/request-partner-access) form.
2. Upon submission, the request is reviewed by the Neto by Maropost partner team and approved if the relevant verification criteria are met.
3. Once approved, notification will be sent to you and the partner via email.

> **Note**: Requests are processed between 9 am-5 pm, Monday-Friday, and are normally completed within 1 business day. Our team can check who has existing partner access to your site, as well as revoke access if no longer needed.
