## Set up Just Uno

1. In your Neto control panel click the **Addons** menu.
2. Find the Just Uno add-on and click the **Install** button. You’ll see two fields that need data from your Just Uno account.
3. Go to [Just Uno](http://www.justuno.com/) and sign in or create an account.
4. Go to **Settings** > **Embed Code**.
5. Copy and paste the provided **Embed Code** into the **Footer** section in the Neto pop-up window.
