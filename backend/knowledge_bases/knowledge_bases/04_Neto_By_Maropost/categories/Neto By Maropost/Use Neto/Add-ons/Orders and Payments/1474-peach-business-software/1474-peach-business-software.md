# Peach Business Software

[Image: User-added image]

### This add-on was built by Peach Business Software

[Visit Add-on Website!](http://peachv12.com.au/inventory-control-software-neto/)

For support with this add-on [contact Peach Business Software](http://peachv12.com.au/peach-software-inventory-control-support-portal/).

Peach Business Software is a complete ERP package for warehouse, inventory and customer management, order fulfilment, and point of sale. It has a wide range of customisation available which allows for complex workflows and product configurations. Peach Business Software has functions including:

- Warehouse management: Paperless stocktaking and stock adjustments using the online interface.
- Inventory management: Paperless purchases and accounts receivable management.
- Customer management: Manage both customers and suppliers and keep track of interactions with them.

## Add-on Benefits & Features

With the **Peach Business Software** add-on you can:

- Export sales orders from Neto into Peach for fulfilment. Once orders are dispatched in Peach they are uploaded to Neto with all of the tracking information.
- Import all products in Peach into Neto and keep them up to date. Customisable mapping is available between Peach and Neto product fields.
- Export customers from Neto into Peach on sales orders.

## Set up the Peach Business Software Add-on

To install and set up the Peach Business Software add-on:

1. In your Neto control panel, click the **Addons** menu.
2. Find the Peach Business Software add-on and click the **Get App** button.
3. Click the **Get App** button in the pop-up window and you’ll be directed to the Peach website to complete the process.
