# Bulk Split Orders

## This add-on was built by Maropost

Add-ons and Integrations built in-house by Maropost are documented and supported by our customer service team. If you're having issues with this integration, [let us know](https://www.netohq.com/support/s/ask-us)!

To install the Bulk Split Orders add-on:

1. In your Neto by Maropost control panel select the **Addons** menu.
2. In the order management section, locate the Bulk Split Orders add-on and click the **Install** button.
3. Confirm by clicking the **Install** button in the po-up window.

Once activated, the menu option **Split Orders** will appear under the **Orders** menu in the control panel. The report will list orders where some of the product in the order are out of stock:

You can split these orders into two separate orders, one with products that are in stock and the second with product where they are out of stock. The order with the out of stock products will have the status of **Backorder Approved**. You will find these orders under **Sales orders** > **Backorder approved**.

A credit will be applied to the first order for the value of the products that are out of stock. This credit will be applied to the second order.
