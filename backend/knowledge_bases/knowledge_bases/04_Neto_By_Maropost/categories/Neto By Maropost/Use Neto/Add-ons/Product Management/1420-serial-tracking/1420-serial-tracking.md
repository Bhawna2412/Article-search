# Serial Tracking

[Image: User-added image]

## This add-on was built by Maropost

Add-ons and Integrations built in-house by Maropost are documented and supported by our customer service team. If you’re having issues with this integration, [let us know](https://www.netohq.com/support/s/ask-us)!

To install the Serial Tracking add-on:

1. In your Neto by Maropost control panel select the **Addons** menu.
2. In the product management section, find the Serial Tracking add-on and click the **Install** button.
3. Confirm by clicking the **Install** button in the pop-up that appears.

Once installed, the **Serial Tracking** option will appear in the **Naming & Visibility** section of the product detail in the control panel:

When processing an order for a serial tracked product, there will be an option to enter the serial number of the product for warranty purposes.

Once a serial number is saved against an orderline, you can view it by clicking the + icon on the left hand side of the orderline. You can also turn on the advanced config `SHOW_ITEM_SERIAL_ON_OPT`, to display any serial number that has been saved by default.

This can be found in **Settings & Tools** > **All Settings & Tools**. Click on **Advanced Configuration** and search in the name field. Change this to **Yes**.
