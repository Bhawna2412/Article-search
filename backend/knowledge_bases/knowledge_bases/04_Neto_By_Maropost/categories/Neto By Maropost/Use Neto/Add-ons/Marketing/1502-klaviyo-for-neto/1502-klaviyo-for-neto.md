## Overview

With Klaviyo For Neto you can:

- **Send truly personalized automations**: Trigger Flows based on dates, events, list membership, or segment membership and use splits, filters, A/B tests and more to target and optimize.
- **Define segments without limitations**: Use any combination of events, profile properties, location, predicted values, and more — over any time range.
- **Recapture lost sales**: Sell more with pre-built email flows, including abandoned cart and win back emails. Create custom flows using virtually any trigger.
- **Cut through the clutter**: Combine data from Neto and other leading apps to segment your audience and send super-targeted, personalized email campaigns.
- **Make better decisions**: See more than just opens and clicks. Klaviyo puts the results that matter front and centre so you can measure what really matters - sales.

## Install and Set up the Klaviyo for Neto Add-on

To set up the Klaviyo for Neto add-on:

1. In your Neto control panel click on the **Addons** menu.
2. Find the Klaviyo for Neto add-on (in the marketing section) and click the **Install** button.
  You’ll be redirected to the Klaviyo for Neto site to authenticate your account.
3. Enter your Klaviyo API keys or to create a Klaviyo account.
4. Select the list in Klaviyo that you want your Neto customers added to.
5. Click the **Proceed** button and enter your payment information.
6. Complete the wizard to finalise your Klaviyo for Neto integration.
