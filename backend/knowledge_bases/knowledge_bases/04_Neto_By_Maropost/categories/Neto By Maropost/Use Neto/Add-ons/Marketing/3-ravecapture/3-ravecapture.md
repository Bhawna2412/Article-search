# RaveCapture

You can integrate RaveCapture to collect verified customer reviews, photos, and user-generated content that helps strengthen customer relationships and improve B2B ecommerce conversion rates. This enables you to showcase authentic customer experiences through product reviews and visual content, with properly configured review systems typically improving customer order experience by building trust and providing social proof that influences purchasing decisions.

Implementing RaveCapture integration provides B2B merchants with automated review collection and moderation capabilities that help build credibility across your product catalog. This helps optimize your online presence through SEO-enhanced review content, rich snippets in search results, and visual marketing opportunities, while enabling data-driven product improvements based on verified customer feedback and engagement patterns.

## Benefits & Features

### Intelligent Review Requests

Easily collect more reviews, photos, and Q&A with RaveCapture AI powered requests. The RaveCapture system is able to determine the best time to send a request to your customers, ensuring a high open and response rate.

### Visual Marketing

Showcase your customer’s photos directly on the product page and throughout your site. Social proof is the most powerful trust signal that helps site visitors see how well your customers love your products.

### In-email Review Requests

Collect more reviews, photos, videos, and Q&A with RaveCapture. The in-email request form boosts review request conversion. Customers do not need to click a link to leave a review like with our competitors. 

### User-generated Content (UGC)

Showcase your customer’s photos and videos across your site. Social proof is the most powerful trust signal that helps site visitors see how well your customers love your products.

### Add Stars to your Search Engine Results Page (Rich Snippets)

Stand out from your competition and increase organic traffic to your website by displaying your reviews & ratings.
