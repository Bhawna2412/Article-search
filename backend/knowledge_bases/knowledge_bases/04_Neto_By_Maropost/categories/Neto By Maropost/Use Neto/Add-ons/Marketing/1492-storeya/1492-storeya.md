## Overview

StoreYa marketing experts will create personalised ad campaigns and their algorithm will automatically optimise them. StoreYa’s AI is designed to drive the most relevant customers to the most relevant product page on your site at the lowest cost possible. StoreYa uses ads that are relevant for your store, including Google Search and Shopping, YouTube, Smart Display, Facebook and Instagram acquisition, and retargeting ads. StoreYa’s algorithm optimises the campaigns in real-time according to 300+ different parameters analysed to display the most suitable ad to the most relevant potential customer.

StoreYa’s cross-platform dashboard reflects your visitors, orders, revenues, and return on advertising spend per channel in a digestible format and scales according to the results.

## Benefits & Features

**Drive traffic to your store**
Personalised ad campaigns will advertise products from your store’s catalog to the customers most likely to make a purchase.

**Convert visitors into customers**
StoreYa’s algorithm optimises campaigns, targeting visitors in the locations where you ship.

**Manage your ads from one simplified dashboard**
View and monitor your visitors, orders, revenues, and more in a clear format that will grow along with your sales.

**Automatic bid optimiser**
Real-time bidding helps us to get the most cost-effective bid for your campaigns at any given time.

**Geo-targeted visitors**
Drive traffic targeted to the countries you ship to.

**Dynamic keywords & ads**
Real-time adjustments of the keywords, ads and the most relevant category or product page at your store to the search queries seek on Google.

**Machine learning**
Constantly uses the most suitable advertising method for your store.

## Set up the StoreYa Add-on

To set up the StoreYa add-on in your Neto webstore:

1. In your Neto control panel click on the **Addons** menu.
2. Find the StoreYa add-on in the Marketing section, and click the **Install** button.
3. Click **Install** in the pop-up window to confirm.
4. The add-on will install. When complete, click the **Configure StoreYa** button.
5. Follow the steps [provided by StoreYa](https://storeya.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/4405238245906-Maropost-Neto-How-to-install-Google-Ads-conversion-pixel) to set up your StoreYa integration.
