## Install and Set up the Hubbed Click & Collect Add-on​

> **Important:** You must register for a Hubbed Account [here](https://hubbed.com/click-collect/) and receive a Hubbed Account API Key to use the Hubbed Click & Collect Neto add-on.

​

To install and set up the Hubbed add-on:

1. In your Neto control panel click the **Addons** menu.
2. Find the Hubbed add-on and click the **Install** button.
3. Click **Configure** and enter your Neto store credentials to access the Hubbed dashboard.
4. In the Hubbed dashboard, navigate to **Settings**.
5. Enter your Hubbed API Key in the **API Key** field.
6. Toggle the **Click & Collect Active** setting to **Yes**.

​You have now successfully installed the Hubbed Click & Collect add-on for your Neto store

## Test the Hubbed Add-on

​Once installed, check that the add-on is working correctly:

1. On your webstore add a product to your shopping cart. A Hubbed Click & Collect button will display on the cart page.
2. Click on the Hubbed Click & Collect button. A popup displays, enabling you to search for Hubbed collection points
3. Enter a postcode or suburb in the search bar to return a list of Hubbed Collection points for the user to select.
4. Select a location from the list to update the delivery address to the Hubbed Collection point.

​

## Process Hubbed Click & Collect Orders

​To process orders using Hubbed Click & Collect:

1. In your Neto control panel, navigate to **Sales Orders** > **View Orders** and open the sales order to be fulfilled.
2. Move the order to the **Pack** status and generate a shipping consignment for the order, ensuring that a tracking number exists for the consignment. Further details for generating shipping consignments can be found [here](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1387-consignment-labels-and-manifests#2).
3. Mark the order as **Dispatched**. The order details and fulfillment status will then be visible within the Hubbed dashboard.

​

## Optional - Add a Click & Collect Parcel Handling Fee

To add the Hubbed Click & Collect parcel handling fee charge to you customer:​

1. In the Hubbed dashboard, navigate to **Settings** and scroll down to **How to pass on the Hubbed Click & Collect cost**.
2. Toggle the **Passing on the Click & Collect cost to customer** field to yes.
3. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Shipping** > **Shipping Options**.
4. Click the **Create shipping option** button.
5. Click the **Set my own pricing** button. The Create Shipping option page will update with a range of new options.
6. Click the **Flat-rate** button.
7. On **Step 1 - Where to Ship** select **Australia**.
8. On **Step 2 - Pricing** Enter your pricing for the new shipping option.
9. On **Step 3 - Naming & Visibility** Set the name for new shipping option to **Hubbed Fees** and enter the description as **Hubbed Click & Collect fees.**
10. Click on **Complete** to create the new shipping option.
