# Neto Commerce Ship

You can streamline multi-carrier shipping management through Neto Ship, which provides centralized access to multiple shipping carriers, rate comparison, and label printing from a single integrated platform. This unified shipping solution helps improve fulfillment efficiency by enabling you to compare rates across carriers, select the best option for each shipment, and print labels without switching between multiple carrier websites or systems. Properly configured Neto Ship typically improves operational efficiency by reducing the time spent on shipping tasks while enabling more strategic carrier selection based on real-time rate comparisons.

Neto Ship gives you control over shipping cost optimization and carrier management, enabling you to consistently select the most cost-effective shipping option for each order while maintaining fast fulfillment workflows. This supports business profitability by reducing shipping expenses and improving fulfillment speed through streamlined, centralized shipping operations.

Neto Ship allows you to drastically streamline order fulfilment and dispatch by cutting out the need to use third party freight software.

Automate your shipping quoting, labelling and manifesting with our multi-carrier shipping system.

## Install Neto Commerce Ship

1. In your Neto control panel click the **Addons** menu.
2. On the Addons page, locate the **Neto Ship** add-on tile and install it.**Please Note**

Please Note

## Install a Carrier Shipping Label

1. In your Neto control panel, navigate to**Shipping**>**Carrier Accounts**to be taken to the**Carrier Label**page.
2. Select a carrier from the list by clicking on the**Install**button. This will take you to the**Shipping Carrier Label Setup**form.
3. Complete and submit this form for Neto to process on your behalf.

## Set up a Shipping Option

Neto shipping is split up into two sections, the **Shipping Option** and the **Carrier Account (Label Configuration)**:

- The Shipping Option includes the shipping different methods that display when the customer places an order and the rates charged to the customer for shipping.
- The Carrier Account is the settings to submit the consignment details to the carrier.

You can also set up the Shipping Options yourself using our shipping set up wizard. In your Neto control panel, select **Shipping** > **Shipping Options.**

## Generate Labels

Once activated, the buttons **Auto Generate Shipping Consignment** and **Manually Add Shipping Consignment** will appear when viewing order details in the control panel:

The buttons will display for orders with the status of **Pack** or **Dispatched**.

You can also create consignments in bulk by using the **Bulk Create Consignments & Dispatch** feature. To turn on bulk order processing, checkout the [Batch Order Processing](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1365-batch-order-processing) add-on.

## Label Printers

A dedicated thermal printer for your shipping labels will save you a lot of money in the long run. Thermal printers don't use toner or ink, and there's less wastage compared to printing 4 labels on an A4 page. You also have a lot of flexibility with the brand of label printer. If you're looking for recommendations, check out our Thermal Label Printer article.
