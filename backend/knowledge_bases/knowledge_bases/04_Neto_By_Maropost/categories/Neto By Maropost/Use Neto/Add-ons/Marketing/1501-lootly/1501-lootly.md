# Lootly

[Image: User-added image]

This add-on was built by **Lootly**.
[Visit add-on website!](https://lootly.io/)
For support with this add-on [contact Lootly](https://support.lootly.io/en/).

## Overview

Lootly helps brands launch a personalised rewards program to better connect with existing customers while growing new customers with points, referrals and special VIP tiers.

Loyalty and referrals are some of the greatest tools in growing an ecommerce business. Repeat sales are absolutely critical to the long term health of a business, and there’s no better way to accomplish this than with Lootly’s points and referrals system.

Referrals are essentially a free traffic source, you are gaining an army of commission-only sales people who go out into the world and sell your products for you. Your brand in turn, rewards these customers with points or a special coupon every time they drive a new sale to your business.

Combining loyalty and referrals is a one-two punch marketing boost with directly measurable ROI.

The Lootly add-on uses the below data from Neto:

- Orders
- Customers
- RMAs

## Benefits & Features

**Incentivise Actions with Points**

With over a dozen ways to Earn & Spend Points, you can build exciting ways for customers to interact with your Neto store.

**Reward Your Customers**

Offer a variety of ways to reward your loyal customers such as:

- $ Off Coupon
- % Off Coupon
- Free Product
- Free Shipping
- Variable $ Off Coupon
- Custom Actions via API

**Referral Program**

Give your existing customers a great way to be rewarded for sharing your store with their friends and family.

When a referred user visits your site, they are immediately shown a coupon to use on their first purchase. Lootly makes it easy for everybody to obtain their discount and start shopping on your store.

**VIP Program**

Drive repeat purchases, customer loyalty and happiness by introducing a VIP Program.

Reward your best customers with exclusive discounts and special promotions.

**Discover what your Customers Love**

Optimise your loyalty program overtime by understanding which actions & rewards are being utilised the most and adjust your items in just a few clicks.

Lootly also allows you to examine individual customers to get a complete understanding of how a customer is using your program.

## Install and Set up the Lootly Add-on

To install and set up the Lootly add-on:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to the **Addons** menu.
2. Find the **Lootly** add-on and click the **Install** button.
  You’ll be redirected to the Lootly dashboard to authenticate or create your account.
3. Follow the set up steps in the Lootly [help guide](https://support.lootly.io/en/articles/4602552-neto-implementation-guide).
