## Benefits & Features

With **Algolia Search for Neto** you can:

- Bring search autocomplete, typo tolerance, synonyms management, filters and facets and many more to your customers with minimal work.
- Create merchandising rules and fine-tune the products ranking based on their popularity, profit margins or any data that matters to you.
- Discover what your shoppers are searching for, use those insights to improve your product catalogue, adapt your SEO, and fuel merchandising.
- Users find the products they’re looking for faster, increasing sales.
- Prioritise your most popular items right from the dashboard.
- Optimise listings in your store with insights into what your users are searching for.

## Set up Algolia Search

To set up Algolia Search for Neto:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to the **Addons** menu.
2. Find the **Algolia Search for Neto** add-on (in the marketing section) and click the **Get App** button.
3. Click the **Get App** button.
4. You'll be redirected to the Algolia Search for Neto form. Fill in the fields and click the **Submit** button.

A member of the Algolia Search for Neto team will contact you for consultation about your needs.
