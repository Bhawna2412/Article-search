## Overview

PriceMe is a shopping comparison website which allows customers to find and compare products in one location. Neto uses an easy to use data feed to send information to PriceMe to update your products through the shopping comparison website.

## Set up the Priceme Add-on

1. Sign up for a PriceMe account through their website – [promote.priceme.com](https://promote.priceme.com/).
2. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Other** > **Shopping feeds**.
3. Locate PriceMe, and click the **Install** button.
4. The data feed will install, then the option will appear to **Configure**. Click the button. Scroll down to **File Delivery** and you’ll be provided with the option to **Send File To** **Local Server**.
5. Copy the **File Location (HTTPS URL)** which is to be provided to PriceMe. This is a link to a file that contains a list of your products in the required format. Set an **Export Schedule** which we recommend sending once daily.
6. You can modify the Feed to filter products that you don’t wish to push to PriceMe. To do this, scroll down to the **File Builder** and click the **Filter** tab to make products, categories, pricing and other changes. Scroll down and click **Save Changes**.
7. To make changes to the feed, simply scroll back up the top and click the **Generate Data File** button and click **Download Data File** button to review the results of your changes.
8. Log into your [PriceMe account](https://merchant.priceme.com/) and set the import schedule and complete your setup.
