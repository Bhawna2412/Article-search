## Overview

By capturing real conversions taking place on your site and showing those to others, Fomo notifications make social proof simple.

- 
- 
- 

Fomo integrates with Neto by Maropost and 50+ other marketing tools to pull in new user actions and show those off to visitors. When a purchase is made, Fomo turns it into social proof.

## Benefits & Features

**True design control**

Like no other social proof tool, Fomo lets marketers design social proof for their own brand. Whether through custom notification images or custom CSS, match the look of Fomo notifications to your brand’s fonts, colors, and more.

**Your rules rule**

Keep it simple or take control. With Fomo, you decide how and where social proof is used. Whether site-wide, only on certain category and product pages, or with custom pacing and click-through rules, Fomo works how you want it to.

**Track ROI**

Monitor user engagement and more through Fomo’s powerful admin dashboard. Connect Google Analytics and track ROI directly from notification click-through conversions and know exactly how much new revenue social proof is generating.

**Combine**

Create even more effective social proof by combining recent Neto purchases with reviews, form fills, and more.

**Design**

Select from Fomo’s design themes and edit notification styles and text to perfectly match your brand.

**Improve**

Set page rules and timing or use Fomo’s AI-powered recommendations to adjust settings and improve conversion power even more.

## Set up Fomo

1. 
  Visit [Fomo.com](https://fomo.com) to set up your account.
2. Embed the [Fomo snippet](https://help.fomo.com/article/278-how-to-embed-the-fomo-snippet-on-neto) into Neto.
3. [Integrate Fomo with Neto](https://help.fomo.com/article/298-how-to-connect-neto). The entire set up process takes just minutes.
