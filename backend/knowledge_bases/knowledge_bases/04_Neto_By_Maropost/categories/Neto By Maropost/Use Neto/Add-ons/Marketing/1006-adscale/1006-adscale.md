# AdScale

![User-added image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/AdScale.05T5g00000jCytCEAS.png)
This add-on was built by **AdScale**.[Visit add-on website](https://adscale.com/maropost/)

For support with this add-on [contact AdScale](https://adscale.com/contact-us/).

## Overview

AdScale uses your sales order and product data to optimise your advertising on Google and social media platforms. To do this, Neto will send AdScale:

- Sales orders (conversion tracking)
- Products (advertising optimisation)
- Product categories (advertising optimisation)

The links below provide more information about how AdScale works.

- [Create a Campaign](https://help.adscale.com/en/articles/4361943-creating-a-new-campaign)
- [AdScale Help Centre](https://help.adscale.com/en/)

## Install and Set up the AdScale Add-on

To set up the AdScale add-on for your Neto control panel:

1. In your Neto control panel click on the **Addons** menu.
2. Find the AdScale add-on in the Marketing section, and click the **Install** button.
3. Click **Install** in the pop-up window to confirm.
4. The add-on will be installed. When complete, click the **Configure AdScale** button.
5. Follow the steps to create an AdScale account. Setup documentation provided by AdScale is available [here](https://help.adscale.com/en/collections/3674974-creating-ads).
