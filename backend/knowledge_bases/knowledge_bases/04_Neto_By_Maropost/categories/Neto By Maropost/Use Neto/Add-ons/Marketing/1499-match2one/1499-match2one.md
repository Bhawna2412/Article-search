## Overview

By connecting Match2One to your website, the platform will automatically track and learn about your online audience - where to find new customers online, and target people who are most likely to become a new customer.

Generally 2-3% of shoppers convert on the first visit to an online store. Retargeting brings back the other 97%. Retargeting works by tracking people who visit your site and showing your ads to them as they visit other sites online.

## Benefits & Features

- Easy to use, self-serve platform.
- Create stunning ads in Match2One’s banner maker tool.
- Prospecting & Retargeting.
- Transparent & detailed reports.
- Auto-pilot delivering high ROI.
- Simplify modern advertising, premium inventory access.
