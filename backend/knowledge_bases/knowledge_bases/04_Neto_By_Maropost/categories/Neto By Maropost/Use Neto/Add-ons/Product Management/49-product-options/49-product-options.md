## Install the Add-on

To install the Product Options add-on:

1. In your Neto control panel select the **Addons** menu.
2. In the product management section, find the Product Options add-on and click the **Install** button.
3. Confirm by clicking the **Install** button in the pop-up that appears.

Once installed the **Extra Options** section will appear on all products in your control panel:

![Product Options2.jpg](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000TRFM3MAP.jpg)

> **Note:** : If the 'Extra Options' is not showing up by default on the product level, check the 'Customise Layout' section on the top right of the product page and make sure 'Extra Options' is selected.

## Add Extra Options

To add extra options to a product:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Products** > **View Products**.
2. Select the product you want to add extra options to.
3. Scroll down to the **Extra Options** section.
4. ![Product Options3.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000SQGUHMA5.jpg)
  Add a new option by clicking the **Add Product Option** button
5.   - **Selection Box** - a drop down menu.
  - **Text Field** - standard text field.
  - **Integer Field** - enter only allows whole numbers.
  - **Decimal Field** - enter both whole and decimal numbers.

  Select the fields type for your product option. Below is a list of the available field types:

  ![Product Options4.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000TRFmUMAX.jpg)
6. Enter the details of your extra options, and a price (if applicable).
7. Click the **Save** button.

Your product will now display the configured extra options on your website.

## Field Types

If you're unsure what type of field you want, consult the available options and examples below:

### Selection Box

For an option with multiple options, such as gift wrapping, select **Selection Box** under **Allow Customer To Enter Option From**.

Click on the **Add attribute** button to add an option. Enter the option name under **Option Attribute** and if there is an additional cost, enter it under **Additional Price**.

Select the default option by entering the option name under **Default**.

![Product Options5.jpg](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000TRFn3MAH.jpg)
Here's how it will display to customers on your website.

![Product Options6.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000TRFnXMAX.jpg)

### Text Field

To display a field to enter text, select **Text Field** under **Allow Customer To Enter Option From**.

If you want to limit the number of characters that the customer can enter into the text field, click on **Add attribute** and under **Option Attribute** enter **LENGTH**. Then enter the maximum number of characters under **Attribute Value**.

![Product Options7.jpg](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000TRFoaMAH.jpg)

### Integer Field

Use this option to allow customers to only enter whole numbers into the field. Select **Integer Field** under **Allow Customer To Enter Option From**.

- If you want a minimum quantity, click on **Add attribute** and enter **MIN** under **Option Attribute**. Then enter the minimum value under **Attribute Value**.
- If you want a maximum quantity, click on **Add attribute** and enter **MAX** under **Option Attribute**. Then enter the maximum value under **Attribute Value**.
- If you want to multiply the entered number by a value, click on **Add attribute** and enter **MULTIPLIER** under **Option Attribute**. Then enter the multiplier value under **Attribute Value**.
  ![Product Options8.jpg](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000TRFp9MAH.jpg)

### Decimal Field

Use this option if you want the customer to enter a decimal number. Select **Decimal Field** under **Allow Customer To Enter Option From**.

- If you want a minimum quantity, click on **Add attribute** and enter **MIN** under **Option Attribute**. Then enter the minimum value under **Attribute Value**.
- If you want a maximum quantity, click on **Add attribute** and enter **MAX** under **Option Attribute**. Then enter the maximum value under **Attribute Value**.

![Product Options9.jpg](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000TRFpOMAX.jpg)
