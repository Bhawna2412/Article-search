## Overview

**#SellMore** and** #StressLess** with the Optiseller Maropost add-on.

Created bespoke for Maropost users, the Optiseller Maropost add-on integrates directly into the Maropost ecosystem, providing you data-rich listings without having to leave Maropost. Built with eBay sellers in mind, Optiseller is designed to increase your eBay sales and revenue by driving improved SEO directly from eBay suggestions. Optiseller Essential tools feature even more ways to drive your listing visibility on eBay, saving you precious time!

## **Benefits & Feature List:**

- 
  Use your existing data on Maropost and suggest the most appropriate categories, item specifics and data points for creating your listings.
- 
  Improved data and guidance on how to maximise your listings will give you the best chance to drive your visibility and sales.
- 
  Access to the full range of Optiseller Essential tools as part of your package.
