## Overview

iCapeesh offers a cost effective, complete solution, tailor-made to your specific requirements that provides instant / real time customer feedback.

## Benefits & Features

Engage with your customers immediately after they have purchased a product through your webstore. iCapeesh contacts customers via email with a questionnaire for feedback on their purchase. As soon as the feedback is received it is immediately available to you in the [iCapeesh dashboard](https://www.icapeesh.com/your-dashboard.html).

Customer service is one of the quickest growing job vacancies in the market place today. Make your life easier, save money, let the program do the work for you.
