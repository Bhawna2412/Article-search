# FeedOps

[Image: User-added image]

### This add-on was built by FeedOps

[Visit add-on website!](https://www.feedops.com/)

For support with this add-on [contact FeedOps](https://www.feedops.com/).

## Overview

Include keywords in your product titles popular with shoppers searching for the products you sell. Get free clicks from the Google shopping tab and set up a traffic report. Get ongoing support for Merchant Center - including help with any errors or warnings with your feed.

## Benefits and Features

- Sell more products in Google and Facebook without the complexity.
- Run your own Google Smart Shopping Campaign. Get automated budget and goal recommendations.
- Create and manage your own Facebook Catalog Feed. Use this for Facebook and Instagram ads and shopping channels.
- Create and manage your own Google Shopping Feed. Get ongoing help with Google Merchant Center
- Include keywords in your product titles popular with shoppers searching for the products you sell.
- Get free clicks from the Google shopping tab and set up a traffic report.
- Get support with Merchant Center errors and warnings.
- Get customer success management support.
- Channel specific product title optimizations. Optimize product titles using product data attributes in bulk at a website level or at a category level.
- Get notifications on how the Google Smart Shopping Campaign is performing.
- Add URL Tags to your Google Shopping feed to track performance of Google’s Free Product Listings.
- Select and order which categories to advertise on. Rewrite these product categories in bulk for product title optimizations and reconfigure the Google Product Category.
- Rewrite product attributes in bulk for product title optimizations.
- Edit and optimize any attribute in the feed at a product level.

## Set up the FeedOps Add-on

To set up the FeedOps add-on:

1. In your Neto by Maropost control panel click on the **Addons** menu.
2. Find the FeedOps add-on and click the **Install** button.
3. The FeedOps add-on will be installed in your control panel.
4. Follow the steps on the [FeedOps help site](https://www.feedops.com/integrations/feedops-neto) to set up your FeedOps connection.
