# Google Analytics 4

## Overview

Google Analytics collects customer metrics through a number of tracking events, such as add to cart, category/product list views, and order/product details to track conversions.

This add-on replaces Maropost’s Google Tag Manager for eCommerce tracking, but can be run along side your existing Tag Manager/Universal Analytics if already set up.

## Benefits & Features

Google Analytics 4 is the new default analytics offered by Google, with several advantages over the previous version:

- Privacy-focused and durable for the future.
- Enhanced ecommerce tracking built in! No additional setup required.
- Intelligent, using machine learning to unearth insights about the customer journey across platforms and devices.
- Enhanced, seamless integrations with Google’s advertising platforms to optimise campaign performance and drive greater marketing ROI.

## Set up the Google Analytics 4 Add-on

1. Set up a [Google Analytics 4 Property](https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/9304153#property). When configuring your Property ensure the “data stream” is set to **Web** and you have entered your Maropost webstore URL.
2. Once your data stream is active, copy its **Measurement ID **(including the “G-” prefix) as you will need this later.
  **Please Note**: Data collection for your Google Analytics 4 Property is handled by the add-on, you do not need any code supplied by Google.
3. In your Neto by Maropost control panel click on the **Addons** menu.
4. Locate the **Google Analytics 4** add-on under Analytics & Optimisation and click install.
5. In the pop-up window, enter the **Measurement ID** you copied from your Analytics Dashboard and click the **Install** button.

Your webstore will now start sending ecommerce tracking events such as add to carts, cart/checkout view, and purchases (conversions) to your Google Analytics 4 Dashboard, general page view, and search events.

**Please note**: While GA4 has some live data, most of the tracking information will take 24 hrs to appear in any reports.

**Tip**: Looking to expand your Google Analytics 4 setup? Your Google Analytics 4 Admin Dashboard allows you to link your [Google Ads account](https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/9379420?hl=en#zippy=,in-this-article) or turn on [enhanced measurement](https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/9216061?hl=en) events (such as form submissions, file downloads, and more) for your Analytics Property.
