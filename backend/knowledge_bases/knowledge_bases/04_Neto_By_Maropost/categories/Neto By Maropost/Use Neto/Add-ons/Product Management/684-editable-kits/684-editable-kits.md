# Editable Kits

## This add-on was built by Maropost

**Warning**: This feature is currently not available to all customers. Please contact Neto by Maropost support if you require this feature.

Add-ons and Integrations built in-house by Maropost are documented and supported by Neto by Maropost customer service team. If you're having issues with this integration, [let us know](https://www.netohq.com/support/s/ask-us)!

When activated, the **Editable Kit** option will appear in the **Kitting** section of the product details in the control panel:

When selected, you can offer your customers a list of items that can be bundled together and they can pick and choose what goes in the kit. You can add more than 100 component skus to the kit and enable minimums and maximums per each component.

**Please Note**: Editable Kits are not compatible with Neto POS.
