# Advanced Product Descriptions

[https://www.netohq.com/support/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka12x00000000CH&feoid=00N2800000JL2j1&refid=0EM2x000000L17y](https://www.netohq.com/support/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka12x00000000CH&feoid=00N2800000JL2j1&refid=0EM2x000000L17y)

### This add-on was built by Maropost

**Please Note**: Not all themes support these fields by default, and may require a tweak to achieve your desired outcome.

Add-ons and Integrations built in-house by Maropost are documented and supported by our customer service team. If you're having issues with this integration, [let us know](https://www.netohq.com/support/s/ask-us)!

This add-on adds nine additional fields to your products, including:

- Short Description
- Specifications
- Warranty
- Terms and Conditions
- Internal Notes
- Author/Artists
- Type
- Format
- Manufacturer Model Number

These fields can display addition product information on your webstore, or be mapped to specific Amazon, Catch, or eBay fields to supply required data.

**Tip**: Neto partners can customise your theme to display these fields. [Contact a design partner](http://forms.neto.com.au/services/designtweak.html?) for a consultation.

## Set up the Advanced Product Descriptions Add-on

To install the Advanced Product Descriptions add-on:

1. In your Neto control panel, navigate to the **Addons** menu.
2. Find the **Advanced Product Descriptions** add-on in the Product Management section.
3. Click the **Install** button.
4. Confirm by clicking the **Install** button in the pop-up that appears.

The add-on is now installed. You can populate the information in these fields [individually, or by the ](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/26-add-and-manage-products)[import wizard](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/668-product-import-wizard).
