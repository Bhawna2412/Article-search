# Oktium

[Image: User-added image]

### This add-on was built by Oktium.

[Visit add-on website!](https://oktium.com/)

For support with this add-on [contact Oktium](mailto:support@oktium.com).

## Overview

Oktium is an instant live video connection for online customers to real salespeople and service providers at brick-and-mortar locations. It adds a video call button to your website for customers to connect directly to you.

## Benefits & Features

**In-store like experience**

Connect with your customers, showcase your products, answer customers` questions and sell your products right during the Oktium video call.

Oktium call also enables contact information sharing, appointment scheduling and most importantly creating orders.

## Set up Oktium

1. Download Oktium 4B app from [AppStore](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/oktium-for-business/id974934460).
2. [Register](https://oktium.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/360009406114-Business-profile-setup) for an Oktium account.
3. Oktium will contact you and implement the Face2 button to your Neto by Maropost store.

You can learn more about the Oktium platform [here](https://oktium.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/categories/201790985-Business).

For enquiries or questions e-mail [Oktium](mailto:marketing@oktium.com).
