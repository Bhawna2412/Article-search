# PDF Brochure Upload

## This add-on was built by Maropost

Add-ons and Integrations built in-house by Maropost are documented and supported by our customer service team. If you're having issues with this integration, [let us know](https://www.netohq.com/support/s/ask-us)!

To install the PDF Brochure Upload add-on:

1. In your Neto by Maropost control panel select the **Addons** menu.
2. In the product management section, find the PDF Brochure Upload add-on and click the **Install** button.
3. Confirm by clicking the **Install** button in the pop-up that appears.

The PDF brochure upload add-on will be installed, and a new section will appear on your products to upload a brochure for them.

Click on the **Browse** or **Choose File** button and select the PDF from your computer. The product page will display a PDF icon in the images section:

**Note:** The placement of the PDF icon will depend on the design of your website theme.
