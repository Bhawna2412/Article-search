## Set up Allied Express Shipping Labels

Shipping label and manifest features require the **Neto Commerce Ship** module. If you have not activated this paid module, in your control panel, click on the puzzle icon and select **Shipping** in the menu (with the grey background). Scroll down to **Print & Post** and click on **Install**.

On the Add-On page select **Shipping** in the menu. Scroll down to **Allied Express** and click on **Install**. You will be taken to setup the Label & Carrier Configuration to setup the carrier details.

You can either follow the instructions in the article below to set it up, or use Neto's free setup service. To request the free setup use the [Shipping Carrier Label Setup Form](https://forms.neto.com.au/services/shippingcarrierlabelsetup.html).
