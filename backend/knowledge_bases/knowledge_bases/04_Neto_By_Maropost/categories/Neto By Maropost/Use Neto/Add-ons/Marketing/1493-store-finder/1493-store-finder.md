# Store Finder

**Please Note**: The store finder add-on requires either the Online Store, or B2B Ecommerce add-ons.

## Install the Store Finder Add-on

1. In your Neto control panel click on the **Addons** menu.
2. Find the **Store Finder** add-on and click the **Install** button.

Once activated, a **Store Finder** menu option will appear under the **Products** menu.

## Set up the a Store Finder

With the add-on installed, you now need to add your store locations:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Products** > **Store Finder**.
2. Click the **Add new** button.
3. Enter a **Name** for the store.
4. If the store is also one of your warehouses, select it next to **Warehouse store**.
5. If the store has a customer account on your site, you can link the customer to this store. To do this check **Change user?** and search for a customer and click on the username in the search results.
6. Enter a **Description**.
  ![image2](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/StoreFinder2.05T5g00000sBUE1EAO.jfif)
7. Enter the stores address. The *Latitude* and *Longitude* will be generated based on the address.
8. You can also relate the store to product categories on the **Store Categories** tab.
9. On the **Store Logos** tab you can upload a logo for this store.
  ![image3](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/StoreFinder3.05T5g00000sBUEfEAO.jfif)

  You can also create a store from a customer card by scrolling down to the **Store Locations** section and clicking on the **Add New Store** button.

  ![image4](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/StoreFinder4.05T5g00000sBUEzEAO.jfif)

## Add a Store Finder Page

So that your customers can use the store finder, you will need to create a page for it on your site. To create the page:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Webstore** > **Web Page** (in some control panels the menu is **Webstore** > **Information Page**).
2. Click the **Add New** button.
3. Enter a page **Name** and click on **Continue**.
4. Scroll down to the **Template & Design** section and under **Body Template** select **store_finder**.
  If your template is not in the list, contact your web designer or [a design partner](https://forms.neto.com.au/services/designtweak.html?) to create it for you.
5. Click the **Save** button to finish adding the page.
