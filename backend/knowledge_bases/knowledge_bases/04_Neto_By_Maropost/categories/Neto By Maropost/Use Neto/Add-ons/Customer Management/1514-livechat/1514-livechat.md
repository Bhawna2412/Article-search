# LiveChat

### This add-on was built by **LiveChat**.

[Visit add-on Website!](https://www.livechatinc.com/)

## Overview

LiveChat is an online customer service software with live support, help desk software, and analytics functions. LiveChat uses a website chat widget, embedded in your Neto webstore, so that customers can engage directly with you while browsing.

## Benefits & Features

With **LiveChat** you can:

- Chat directly with customers on your website.
- Send canned responses to common questions.
- Monitor your website traffic.
- Analyse the efficiency of your staff.
- And more.

## Setup Instructions

To set up the LiveChat add-on on your Neto webstore:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to the **Addons** menu.
2. In the Support & Live Chat section, find the LiveChat add-on and click the **Get App** button.
3. In the pop-up window, click the **Get App** button, and you'll be taken to the LiveChat sign up page.
4. Create a free trial account with LiveChat by entering your email address. If you already have a LiveChat account, [log in to your account](https://my.livechatinc.com/).
5. Click the **Install LiveChat code manually** option, and copy the code that appears.
6. In your Neto control panel, navigate to **Settings & tools** > **All settings & tools**.
7. Search for **Custom Scripts** and click on it.
8. Click the **Add New** button.
9. Enter a **Name** (e.g. LiveChat) for your script.
10. Paste the code you copied earlier into the **Page Footer** tab of the scripts section.
11. Click the **Save** button. A pop-up appears to verify your identity. Follow the steps to confirm your actions.
