## Overview

With the Smartarget WhatsApp - Contact Us add-on you can add a small icon on the bottom-right corner of your website with your phone number and short message, such as Contact us or Message us. When the user clicks on the icon a WhatsApp tab will open for web and chat with you.

## Benefits & Features

- Help customers to approach the seller in the most convenient way
- Allow sellers to communicate special deals, shipping information and more
- Exit popup - prevent users from leaving the website
- FAQ - show FAQ on every page so users gain more trust in the website

## Install and Set up the Smartarget WhatsApp - Contact Us Add-on

1. In your Neto by Maropost control panel navigate to the **Addons** menu.
2. In the Support & Live Chat section find the **Smartarget WhatsApp - Contact Us** add-on and click the **Install** button.
  You’ll be redirected to the Smartarget dashboard to authenticate or create your account.
3. Follow the steps in the [Smartarget guide](https://smartarget.online/neto.php) to configure the add-on settings..
4. Click on **Save settings** to apply your changes.
