## Install and Set up the Sales Ticker Add-on

The sales ticker will display purchases for sales order in these statuses:

- New
- Pick
- Pack
- Pending Dispatch
- Pending Pickup
- Dispatched

In addition, products must also be **Active** and **Approved** for webstore to appear in the sales ticker.

> **Note:** Sales orders made in Neto POS will not display in the sales ticker.

To set up the Sales Ticker add-on for your Neto webstore:

1. In your Neto control panel click on the **Addons** menu.
2. Find the Sales Ticker add-on in the Marketing section, and click the **Install** button.
3. Enter the number of days that you want to display orders from. For example, if you set it to 1 (the default), the sales ticker will display orders placed in the last 24 hours.
4. Click the **Install** button, and then the **Close** button.

The Sales Ticker add-on is now installed.

> **Note:** The Sales Ticker is automatically hidden on mobile to prevent it covering too much of the page. If you need to make changes to the design, click on the **Script** button in the add-on to be taken to the 3rd party scripts, where the add-on code is installed.
