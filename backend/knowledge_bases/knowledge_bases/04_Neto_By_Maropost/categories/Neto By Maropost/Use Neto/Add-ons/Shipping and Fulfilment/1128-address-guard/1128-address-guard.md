# Address Guard

![User-added image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/addressguardlogo.05T5g00000qZurEEAS.png)
This add-on was built by **Address Guard**.[Visit add-on website](https://addressguard.io/)

For support with this add-on, contact [support@addressguard.io](http://support@addressguard.io).

## **Overview**

Customers often mistype shipping addresses, which leads to failed deliveries and ultimately, upset customers. Inaccurate shipping addresses may result in returned shipments and expensive redelivery fees. Address Guard verifies, standardize, cleanse, and format address data for 240+ countries, all via an easy-to-integrate solution with your Neto web store. When customers complete their order and land on the thank you page, an address popup will appear indicating any issues with the shipping address. From here, customers can accept our suggestions or edit their address to comply with your shipping requirements.

## **Benefits & Feature List**

- Get prompted with address suggestions and notified when issues are detected on your order thank you page.
- Prevents shipment to undeliverable addresses with custom rules (for example, PO Boxes, Missing house #, Missing Apt #, and so on).
- Get a customized design of the address prompt to match your brand design in a few clicks.
- Automatic email notifications and follow-ups when suggestions are declined.
- Works seamlessly across Mobile, Tablet, and Desktop with blazing-fast performance.

## **Setup Instructions**

To set up the Address Guard add-on for your Neto web store:

1. In your Neto control panel, click on the **Addons** menu.
2. Find the **Address Guard** add-on under **Shipping,** and then click the **Install** button.
3. In the popup window, click **Install** to confirm and begin the installation.
4. Once the add-on has been installed, click the **Configure Address Guard** button.
5. Complete or tweak your setup from your Address Guard dashboard.
6. Further documentation can be found on the [Address Guard website](https://wisdomkb.help/addressguard/integrations/getting-started-with-maropost).
