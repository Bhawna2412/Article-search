## Overview

Shopbot have, since their inception, developed into a global leader on the price comparison market, backed by the decades of experience of their founders. Shopbot's role is to efficiently link customers and merchants in the rapidly expanding online retail market.

[Visit Shopbot Australia](http://www.shopbot.com.au/)
[Visit Shopbot New Zealand](http://www.shopbot.co.nz/)
[Visit Shopbot Singapore](http://www.shopbot.com.sg/)
[Visit Shopbot Malaysia](http://www.shopbot.com.my/)

Shopbot is a shopping comparison website which allows customers to review products and find the best deals online. If the customer likes what they see, they’ll be redirected to your website to purchase.

Neto uses a data feed to send information to Shopbot to update your products through the shopping comparison website.

## Set up the Shopbot Add-on

To install and set up the Shopbot add-on:

1. Sign up for an account through the [Shopbot](http://shopbot.business/) website.
2. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Other** > **Shopping feeds**.
3. Locate Shopbot and click the **Install** button.
4. You’ll be provided with a Feed URL. Provide this URL to Shopbot. This is a link to a file that contains a list of your products in the required format.
5. You can modify the Feed URL, filtering products that you don’t wish to push to Shopbot. To do this, click the **Edit** button.
6. Scroll down to the **File Builder** section and click the **Filter** tab to make products, categories, pricing and other changes. Scroll down and click **Save Changes**.
7. To make changes to the feed, simply scroll back up the top and click the **Generate Data File** button and click **Download Data File** button to review the results of your changes.
