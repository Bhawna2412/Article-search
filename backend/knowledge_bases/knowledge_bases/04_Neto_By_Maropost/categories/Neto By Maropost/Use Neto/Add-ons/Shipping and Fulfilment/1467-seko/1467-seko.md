# Seko

### This addon was built by **Maropost**

Add-ons and Integrations built in-house by Maropost are documented and supported by our customer service team. If you're having issues with this integration, [let us know](https://www.netohq.com/support/s/ask-us)!

Seko is a global fulfilment and logistics solution able to optimise delivery channels that are right for you and your situation. Thanks to close connections with every major integrator and mail provider in larger eCommerce markets, and contracts with domestic and regional parcel carriers, Seko are able to give retailers access to the ideal solution for each delivery.

This add-on integrates your Seko account to Neto's powerful order management system, automating consignment creation, manifest submission, and label generation with Seko's carriers.

## Set up Seko Shipping Labels

**Please Note**: This shipping carrier requires the **Neto Commerce Ship** add-on to print labels and submit manifests.

1. On the Add-On page select **Shipping** in the menu.
2. Scroll down to **Seko/Omni Parcel** and click on the **Install** button.
3. You will be taken to the **Shipping Carrier Label Setup** form. Complete the form, and Neto staff will set up your label service free of charge.
