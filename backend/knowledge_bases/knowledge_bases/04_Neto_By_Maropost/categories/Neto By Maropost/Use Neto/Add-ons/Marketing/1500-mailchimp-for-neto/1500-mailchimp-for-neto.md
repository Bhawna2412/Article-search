## Overview

Connect your Neto store to Mailchimp. Sync your store data, send targeted campaigns to your customers, and sell more stuff! Get Mailchimp for Neto, your best business resource in just a few clicks. And best of all? No coding necessary—just a simple connection for a booming business.

## Benefits & Features

With **Mailchimp for Neto** you can:

- Send abandoned cart recovery emails and follow-ups.
- Add personalised product recommendations to your emails.
- Send your targeted sales to customers based on their past purchases.
- Add drag-and-drop product blocks to your EDMs.
- Automate your product follow-ups, review requests and welcome series.
- Segment your mailing list based on Neto customer groups and sales channels.
- Always have access to the latest Mailchimp features.
- ...and much more!

## Set up Mailchimp for Neto

Sign up to Mailchimp for Neto [here](https://mailchimpforneto.com/). Once you have an account:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to the **Addons** menu.
2. Find the **Mailchimp for Neto** add-on (in the marketing section) and click the **Install** button.
3. Click the **Install** button.
4. The Mailchimp for Neto add-on will be installed. Click the **Configure Mailchimp for Neto** button.
5. You'll be redirected to the Mailchimp for Neto site to authenticate your account. Click the **Connect To MailChimp** button.
6. Enter your Mailchimp username and password.
7. Select the list in Mailchimp that you want your Neto customers added to.
8. Click the **Proceed** button and enter your payment information.
9. Complete the wizard to finalise your Mailchimp for Neto integration.
