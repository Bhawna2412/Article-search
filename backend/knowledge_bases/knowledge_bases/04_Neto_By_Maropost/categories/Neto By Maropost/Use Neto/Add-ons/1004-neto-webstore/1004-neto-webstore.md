# Neto Webstore

[Image: User-added image]

### This add-on was built by Maropost

Add-ons and Integrations built in-house by Maropost are documented and supported by our customer service team. If you’re having issues with this integration, [let us know](https://www.netohq.com/support/s/ask-us)!

## Set up the Neto Webstore Add-on

This is located under **Neto Modules** in the add-on store menu. Click on the **Install** button. The Neto module is a sales channel and may incur an additional cost.

This will appear on your invoice as **Neto A-6**.

## Benefits & Features

### A store that never closes

Operate 24/7 with Neto’s cloud-based eCommerce solution. Manage all your sales channels from one platform, wherever you are.

- **Build a beautiful online store**
	Choose from over 70 free, editable, and fully-responsive web store themes designed by some of Australia’s best web designers.
- **Maintain complete control**
	You have full access to the HTML, CSS, and JS of your store, making it easy to customise every aspect of your website and shopping cart.
- **Enjoy flexible navigation**
	Add products to an unlimited number of product categories. Allow customers to filter results by pre-defined specifics (eg. size or colour).
- **Showcase your products**
	Your products are displayed in an online catalogue that you can organise by category, specific, or variant.
- **Keep stock in multiple locations**
	Keep track of your inventory across multiple warehouses/locations. Display real-time stock availability per location to your customers.
- **Sell anything**
	Neto does not limit the number or type of products you can have in your catalogue. If you can ship it, you can sell it with Neto.
- **Secure checkout**
	All account pages, including your checkout and payment pages, are secured by 256-bit secure socket layer technology.
- **Seamless one-page checkout**
	You can expect lower cart abandonment rates with our one-page Ajax checkout. Quick, easy, and secure.
- **Upsell at cart**
	Increase your average order value with cart upsells. Provide product recommendations based on a customer’s cart contents.

### Run your business from anywhere

With Neto’s cloud-based platform, you can keep track of exactly what is happening across all your locations from a central dashboard.

- **Inbuilt order management**
	Manage sales orders across multiple sales channels and locations from a central control panel.
- **Inbuilt pick, pack, and dispatch**
	Pick, pack, and print shipping labels directly from orders in Neto. No need to use clunky third-party shipping apps.
- **Inbuilt inventory management**
	Manage your purchase orders, stocktakes, and adjustments for multiple locations from a central control panel.
- **Google Analytics**
	Track your website traffic, transactions, and conversion rates in real time with the world’s leading analytics platform.
- **Over 40 inbuilt reports**
	Run order, payment, customer, inventory, and other reports in real time. Export all reports to Excel for further analysis.
- **Real-time dashboards**
	Track the progress of your business in real time with your own customisable dashboard. Drag and drop widgets to make it your own.
- **Direct integrations**
	Extend Neto with direct integrations to popular accounting, inventory, and marketing apps.
- **Powerful API**
	The Neto application programmer interface (API) enables third-party software to interact with Neto.
- **Over 100 add-ons**
	Turn on hundreds of advanced features such as loyalty and customer groups from within the add-on store.

### A feature-rich eCommerce platform

- **Product variations**
	If you sell products with varying buying options such as colour and size, we have you covered! Each variation can have its own SKU, name, price, image, stock, and description.
- **Secure shopping cart**
	All account pages including your checkout pages are secured by 256-bit secure socket layer (SSL) technology.
- **Sync with accounting**
	Direct two-way integrations with popular accounting programs such as Xero, MYOB, and Saasu.
- **Invoice and email notifications**
	Automated receipt, invoice, and shipping tracking emails and PDF documents. Customise the look and layout of all systems-generated emails and documents.
- **Promotions and discount coupons**
	Configure discount coupons and promotions. Trigger discounts automatically based on a pre-defined set of rules.
- **Reward loyal customers**
	Reward your loyal customers with a loyalty points program. Customers earn points for purchases that can be redeemed as credit towards future purchases.
