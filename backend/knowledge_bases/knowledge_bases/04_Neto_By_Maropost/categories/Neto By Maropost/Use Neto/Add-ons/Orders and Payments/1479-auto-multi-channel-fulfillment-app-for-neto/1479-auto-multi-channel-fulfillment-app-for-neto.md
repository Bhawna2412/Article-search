## Benefits and Features

With the Auto Multi-Channel Fulfillment App for Neto you can:

- Automatically export Neto orders to Amazon FBA for faster and efficient fulfillment and shipping.
- Sync inventory from Amazon FBA for mapping of SKUs and automated fulfillment of orders
- Track the status of shipments by Amazon and inform a customer about it
- Inventory and order management to assess items stocks at the Amazon fulfillment centre.
- More control over shipping speeds by adding custom Amazon custom shipping methods.
- An easy to use dashboard offering complete overview of inventory, order and tracking from a single day to entire month.
- Import order details from Neto to be shared with Amazon FBA for fulfillment and shipping.
- Bundle mapping of items with SKU for discount offers and seasons sales while increasing the cart value.
- Offers access to Amazon market places across the world integrating them with the app.

## Install and Set up the Auto Multi-Channel Fulfillment App for Neto Add-on

To set up the Auto Multi-Channel Fulfillment App for Neto add-on:

1. In your Neto control panel click on the **Addons** menu.
2. Find the Auto Multi-Channel Fulfillment App for Neto add-on and click the **Install** button.
  You’ll be redirected to the Auto Multi-Channel Fulfillment App for Neto site to authenticate your account.
3. Complete the set up by following [the steps on the WebBee website](https://www.webbeeglobal.com/published-apps/neto-multi-channel-fulfillment-app/).
