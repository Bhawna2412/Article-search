## Overview

Outsmart is a suite of conversion apps, featuring One Click Upsells. One Click Upsells lets you offer relevant upsells and cross sells at the right time and place to your customers.

Outsmart uses the below data from Neto:

- Products
- Categories
- Brands
- Sales order orderlines

## Install and Set up the Outsmart Apps Add-on

To install and set up the Outsmart Apps add-on:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to the **Addons** menu.
2. Find the **Outsmart Apps** add-on and click the **Install** button.
  You’ll be redirected to the Outsmart dashboard to authenticate or create your account.
3. Follow the set up steps in the Outsmart [help guide](https://docs.outsmart.digital/).
