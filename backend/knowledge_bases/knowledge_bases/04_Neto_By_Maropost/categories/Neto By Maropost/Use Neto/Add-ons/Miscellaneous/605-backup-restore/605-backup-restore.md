## Overview

Backup & Restore app is the easiest way to back up your Maropost store ecommerce data and undo unwanted changes. Get peace of mind knowing that your most important data is automatically backed up.

All customers and orders are stored continuously. A daily snapshot of your product data is created and can be accessed at that point in time. Data is stored for 7 days and monthly for a year. Extended periods are also available.

## Benefits & Features

**Full Product Backup Daily**
A snapshot of your products is created daily so you can refer back to what your data looked like at different points in time.

You can retrieve a backup of your data for a specific date and export some or all the data. Use a csv export or connect your store via our API integration to transfer data back.

**Customer and Order Backups**
All your customers and order history is backed up initially and then all new entries are added.
The app keeps the original information so if you edit it within Maropost you will be able to refer back to the original data saved in the app.

**List of data that is transferred between systems:**

- Products
- Orders
- Customers
- Content/Categories
