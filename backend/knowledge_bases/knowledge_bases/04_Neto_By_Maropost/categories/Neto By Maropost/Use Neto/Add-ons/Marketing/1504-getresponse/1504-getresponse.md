## Overview

The GetResponse email marketing and online campaign management platform helps marketers build targeted subscriber lists and send high-impact newsletters and follow-up campaigns. It offers marketing automation, responsive email design, hundreds of ready-to-use email templates, landing page creator, and detailed statistics.

GetResponse are also proud to be a spam-free solution, achieving a consistent deliverability rate of over 99%, the highest deliverability rate in the industry.
