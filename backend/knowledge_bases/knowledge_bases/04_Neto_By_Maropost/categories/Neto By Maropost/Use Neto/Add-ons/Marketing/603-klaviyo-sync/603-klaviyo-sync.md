## Overview

The Klaviyo Sync add-on collates and syncs an enormous amount of data from Neto to your Klaviyo account. This includes events for abandoned carts, fulfilled orders, ordered products, and orders placed. The app also lets you select a Klaviyo list to automatically add Neto newsletter subscribers.

## Install and Set up the Klaviyo Sync Add-on

To set up the Klaviyo Sync add-on in your Neto webstore:

1. In your Neto control panel click on the **Addons** menu.
2. Find the Klaviyo Sync add-on and click the **Install** button.
  You'll be redirected to the Klaviyo site to authenticate your account.
3. Follow the steps on the [mysync help site](https://help.mysync.io/hc/en-us/articles/360061443713-Klaviyo-and-Neto-Guide) to complete your setup.
