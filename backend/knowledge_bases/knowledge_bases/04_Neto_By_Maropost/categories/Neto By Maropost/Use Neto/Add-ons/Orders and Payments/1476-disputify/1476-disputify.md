# Disputify

[Image: User-added image]

This add-on was built by **Disputify**.
[Visit add-on website](https://www.disputify.com/)
For support with this add-on [contact Disputify](https://disputify.atlassian.net/servicedesk/customer/portal/2/group/3/create/12).

## Overview

Disputify sits invisibly on your Neto webstore checkout to track your Stripe transactions in real-time. Disputify leverages a network of data points to calculate the probability of any given transaction resulting in a fraudulent chargeback.

Dispute scores are added to each of your transactions allowing you to view your customers fraud history before dispatching orders, essentially creating a virtual “blocklist” of high risk customers.

## Install and Set up the Disputify Add-on

To set up the Disputify add-on in your Neto webstore:

1. In your Neto control panel click on the **Addons** menu.
2. Find the Disputify add-on and click the **Install** button.
  The Disputify add-on will be installed in your control panel.
3. In the pop-up window, click the **Configure Disputify** button.
4. Follow the steps on the [Disputify help site](https://disputify.atlassian.net/servicedesk/customer/portal/2/article/524291) to configure your Disputify connection.
