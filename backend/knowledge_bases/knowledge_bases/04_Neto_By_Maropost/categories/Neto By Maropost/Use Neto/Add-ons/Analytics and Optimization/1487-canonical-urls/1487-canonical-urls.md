## About Canonical URLs

Canonical URLs are like a redirect for search engines when you have the same content with a different URL. A simple example is your home page, which may be found at www.mysite.com.au, but also mysite.com.au or www.mysite.com.au/index.html. All URLs display the same content, but can cause problems when search engines crawl your site. So to improve your SEO, you can set a **Canonical URL** to tell search engines (like Google) which URL you prefer to display to visitors to your site.

## Set up the Canonical URLs Add-on

To install the Canonical URLs add-on:

1. In your Neto by Maropost control panel, click the **Addons** menu.
2. In the SEO category, find the Canonical URLs add-on and click the **Install** button.
3. Confirm installation by clicking the **Install** button in the pop-up window.

The add-on will be installed. Click the **Close** button when complete.

Once active, the **SEO Canonical URL** field will be added to the **SEO** section of your product pages.
