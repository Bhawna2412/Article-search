## Set up the Sales Follow Up Email Add-on

1. In your Neto by Maropost control panel click the **Addons** menu.
2. In the marketing section find the Sales Follow Up Email add-on and click the **Install** button.
3.   - **Default order date field used in follow up email**- Choose the date to use for the follow up email. Must be one of Date Paid, Date Placed, Date Required, Date Invoiced or Default.
  - **Number of days to send order follow up email**- Enter the number of days from when the order was shipped what you want the followup email to be sent.
  - **Send follow up email for first order only**- Prevents follow up emails for each order a customer places.
  - **Order type allowed to send follow up email**- The order types you wish to send follow up emails to. Select all to send to all order types.
  - **Customer group allowed to send follow up email**- The customer groups you want to send follow up emails to. Select all to send to all customers (Requires the[Customer Groups add-on](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/693-customer-groups)).

  Set when a follow up email is sent, and who they go to:

  ![Sales Follow Up Email2.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000ERebCMAT.jpg)
4. Click the **Install** button to complete the set up.

**Tip**: To change how the email looks see [this article](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1107-customise-email-and-print-templates#10). The template for this email is stored on the file server here: **/private/www/netosuite/SysDoc/emails/order/followup/**, or in the control panel under **Settings & tools** > **All Settings & tools** > **Email Templates** > **Order Emails** > **Order Follow Up Email**.
