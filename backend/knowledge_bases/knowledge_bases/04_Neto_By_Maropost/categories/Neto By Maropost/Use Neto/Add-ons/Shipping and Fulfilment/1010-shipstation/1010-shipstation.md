## Install and Set up the ShipStation Add-on

To set up the ShipStation add-on in your Neto webstore:

1. In your Neto control panel, click on the **Addons** menu item.
2. In the Add-ons page submenu, scroll to the **Categories** section and click **Shipping** to find the Shipstation add-on tile.
3. Click the **Install** button in the ShipStation add-on tile to install the add-on.
4. When the add-on has been installed, click the **Configure ShipStation** button.
5. Complete or tweak your setup from your ShipStation dashboard.
