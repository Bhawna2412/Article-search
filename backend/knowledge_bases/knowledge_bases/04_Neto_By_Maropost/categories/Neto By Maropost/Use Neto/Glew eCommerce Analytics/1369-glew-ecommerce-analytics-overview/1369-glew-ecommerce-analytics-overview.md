### Benefits

#### Store Performance Management

- Powerful filters let you segment performance data by device type, geography, day of the week, or channel.
- Get insights into seasonal trends and patterns by using the calendar picker to quickly and efficiently adjust your report's date range.
- Upload your Cost of Goods Sold data and Neto will automatically generate valuable information about channel profitability, return on ad spend, and product profitability.
- Incorporate administrative metrics such as shipping revenue, refunds, and sales tax to get an even more comprehensive view of your performance.

#### Customer Analytics

- Discover which channels and campaigns generate the highest customer lifetime value and have the lowest customer acquisition cost.
- Receive and act on valuable insights regarding customer purchase behaviour, lifetime value, and more.
- Optimise marketing spend for profitability with advanced customer analytics at multiple levels of granularity.
- Leverage more than 20 unique customer segments in your email campaigns and advertising efforts by easily exporting segments as lists.

#### Product Analytics

- Measure and analyse performance across product categories, segments, and individual SKUs.
- Increase average order value with product bundling recommendations.
- Get a detailed picture of which products are most profitable, and which are hurting your bottom line.
- Maximise the value of your product data with advanced analytics and reporting tools.

#### Scheduled Reports

- Automatically compile and send daily performance snapshots,weekly updates, and monthly reports.
- Heighten communication within your team and save time.
- Create high-quality custom reports using multiple data sources.
- Deliver interactive and printer-friendly reports in just a few clicks.

#### Cost Of Goods Sold Reporting

- Monitor gross margin and a variety of KPIs that rely on Cost of Goods Sold data.
- Easily determine if you are spending too much money marketing low gross margin items.
- Quickly act on opportunities to allocate more ad spend to high margin products.
- Get a clearer picture of how to improve performance and increase gross profit.

### Overview

Glew Analytics it will sync historical data for any sales channel that has orders in Neto. Sales are reported based on the invoiced date, and there is no limit to the amount of data that can be imported. The only exception is cancelled orders will not be synced.

This data is updated every hour for abandoned carts (if installed), customers, orders, and returns. Categories, products, suppliers and warehouses are updated daily at midnight. The initial sync may take longer, depending on how many orders, customers, and products you have.

#### General features

Throughout Glew eCommerce Analytics you're able to filter the metric, chart or table using the three dot button in the top right hand corner.

Here you can change the date ranges, apply filters for specific advertising campaigns or sales channels, add the metric you've selected to a report, or even create a brand new report with the filters you've applied.

You'll also see information icons to help you understand what information is being displayed, and how it is calculated.

Many metrics will display the **Group By** button, allowing you to change how the data is represented. For example, when viewing a years worth of revenue, you can group it by month (rather than day) to get a clearer view of trends and changes.

Lastly, you'll find additional filters, columns, and export options in the **Orders**, **Customers** and **Products** sections. Use these to drill down to the specific data you need, and export it for use in a CRM, email marketing campaign, or other third party application.

#### Highlights

Highlights represents a real-time view of your store so you can quickly understand how key metrics are performing Today, This Week, This Month and This Year. Glew eCommerce Analytics also provides historical comparisons for each time period so you can benchmark your recent performance versus historical performance.

By default, the highlights page displays Revenue, Gross Profit, Orders, Products Sold, Average Order Value, Refunded, Visits and Conversion Rate. The date range, and data can be filtered using the three dot button in each window.

#### Performance

The Performance tab combines data from your Neto account with data from major advertising channels to provide insights into the effectiveness of sales channels and marketing campaigns. Previously, reaching this data meant cross-checking multiple dashboards and tediously combining data into Excel spreadsheets.

When evaluating data, you never want to compare apples to oranges. Monday trends are different than Sunday trends. December sales are higher than October sales. To help you understand benchmarks of performance, each metric in the Highlights page has a percentage up or down from each time frame.

#### Orders

The Orders page is where you can keep a close eye on incoming revenue, products discounted, coupons and refunds that have been issued. Use this data to create accurate real time reports and track down issues with returns and gauge the effectiveness of discounts and promotions.

Analyse your most profitable sales channels and how your customers are browsing them to get the maximum return on your advertising budget. Gauge the effectiveness of new sales channels by generating reports that can be automatically delivered to your inbox.

#### Customers

With the Customer Profile and List pages you can enhance your service level by equipping your team with important information such as a customer's order history, channel of attribution, and abandoned cart info. See how long they have been shopping with you, their Average Order Value, and whether you're at risk of losing this customer. Use this information to tailor communication that your customers will perceive as professional, informed and courteous. In turn, they'll deem you worthy of their continued business.

Personalised, targeted email can generate massive increases in revenue over generic blast email campaigns. Our Customer Segmentation feature helps you realise that increase in revenue in countless ways.

Customer Segmentation includes over 20 unique segments that can be exported and uploaded into your preferred email management platform. For each segment, see insights including Lifetime Value, Revenue, Average Order Value and the Most Commonly Purchased Products.

#### Products

The Products Dashboard displays key performance indicators for each product. It includes a breakdown of Sales Margin, Views, Orders, Conversion, Quantity Sold, Quantity Left, Revenue, Cost of Goods Sold, Gross Profit and Refunded.

A great way to use this data would be to sort your Refunded Products and look for products with a high Refund Rate. Then, evaluate what could be the issue. Does the product have a misleading description? Are there issues with sizing? Does the quality of the product not meet high standards? Fixing these issues would put money directly back into your pocket.

At any point in the Product section, if you click on the image or name of a product, you'll go to an individual product page. On this page, all of the data will be shown as it relates ONLY to this individual product.

#### Reports

Save time compiling, cleansing, and visualising your data, as Glew eCommerce Analytics transforms silos of eCommerce data into beautiful reports that help you grow your business. Reports can be quickly and easily generated from any of the metrics in Highlights, Performance, Customers or Products, filtered to suit your individual business needs.

Smart scheduling lets you have this data at your fingertips so you can make informed business decisions wherever you are.

#### Store Settings

Power up your data and take reporting to the next level by integrating your Google Analytics, Adwords and more with Glew eCommerce Analytics. Connect your accounts with just a few clicks to cut out hours of cross referencing and managing spreadsheets.

Link up Mail Chimp and you can automatically create segmented mailing lists from your Glew eCommerce Analytics segments. Use these segments for targeted marketing to turn one of purchasers into regular repeat customers.

## Set up Analytics Studio

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to the **Addons** menu.
2. In the **Analytics & Optimisation** section click **Install** on Glew eCommerce Analytics Studio.
3. Once installed you will have a new menu on the left hand side called **Analytics Studio**.

### Log in to Analytics Studio

1. In your cotnrol panel click the **Analytics studio** link.
2. When logging in for the first time the Analytics Studio **Getting Started** page displays in a new tab. Follow our Getting Started Guide to set up your Analytics Studio account.
3. When logging in any time after the first the Analytics studio dashboard displays in a new tab.
