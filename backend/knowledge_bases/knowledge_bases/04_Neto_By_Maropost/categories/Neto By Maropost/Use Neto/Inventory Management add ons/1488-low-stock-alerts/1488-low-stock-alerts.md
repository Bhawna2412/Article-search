# Low Stock Alerts

### This add-on was built by Maropost

Add-ons and Integrations built in-house by Maropost are documented and supported by our customer service team. If you're having issues with this integration, [let us know](https://www.netohq.com/support/s/ask-us)!

Always stay abreast of stock that is getting low by receiving timely notifications when product inventory reaches a defined level.

## Set up Low Stock Alerts

To install and set up the Low Stock Alerts add-on:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Addons**.
2. Find the **Low Stock Alerts** add-on and click the **Install** button.
3.   - Restock Qty
  - Reorder Qty
  - Restock Warning Level

  Products will now have additional fields to configure the stock levels when alerts occur:

  Populate these fields for individual products or in bulk using the import wizard.
4. Once the stock on hand reaches these levels, the product will appear on the Inventory Restock Report which is accessed from the **Reports** menu.
5. Navigate to the **Webstore** > **Webstore Settings** page.
6. In the out of stock warnings section, enable the alerts you would like to receive.
7. Check that the system email address to receive these emails is correct in the **Settings & Tools** > **Business Details** page.
