# Create Custom Links in your Control Panel

You can add commonly used control panel pages (or even pages from the front end of your site) to a custom section in the left-hand menu of your control panel.

## Add a Custom Link

To add a custom link in your control panel:

1. In your Neto control panel, navigate to **Settings & Tools** » **All Settings & Tools**.
  ![shiprate25.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/QWK959ZG0E29/shiprate25.png)
2. Click on **Custom Control Panel Links** in the **Control Panel section**.
  ![customlinks0.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/O3DDXFZ8FJ05/customlinks0.png)
3. Click on the **Add New Menu Item** button.
  ![customlinks1.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/9B5SL53C5LM2/customlinks1.png)
4. A new gray bar will appear. Click on **Menu Item Settings** (cog icon) in the bar to edit the menu item**.**
  ![customlinks2.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/EG7A2DEDRX6W/customlinks2.png)
5. Enter the name of the new menu option under **Item Name**. This will be the name that appears in the menu.
  ![customlinks3.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/FC8LHXBLXLGV/customlinks3.png)
6. Under Select URL for menu item, choose one of the following:
**
*****Link to a URL**** - to enter a web address for an external website. Paste the web address in the field below. This can be a web address of a page in your control panel.

****Link to an existing product**** - link to product page. Enter the product's SKU in the field below. Click on ****Accept**** to save the new menu link.

****Link to an existing stockist**** - links to a stockist or store location. Select a store from the drop-down list below.

****Blog Post, Brand, Buying Guide, Form, Information Page, or Product Category**** - links to a page with that content type. Select a page from the drop-down menu below.*
  ![customlinks4.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/4NHL8YGXR9WH/customlinks4.png)
7. Click on **Accept** to save the new menu link. Refresh your control panel to see the new custom links menu you have just created.
  ![customlinks5.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/QRYNMHFR1TLY/customlinks5.png)

## Edit the Menu Layout

To change the order of the menus, remove menus, and create submenus:

1. Click and drag the menu option up or down to adjust its position. Release the mouse button when it's in the correct position. To create a sub-menu option, drag the option to the right so it is indented.
  ![customlinks6(1).gif](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/50FBL3VX2RWB/customlinks6-281-29.gif)
2. To add a sub-menu option, click on the **+** icon. To remove a menu option, click on the **x** icon.
  ![customlinks7.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/TI48Q6JEGHV7/customlinks7.png)
3. Click on the **Save **button. Refresh your control panel to see the changes to the custom links menu you have performed.
  ![customlinks8.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/1AYWA178KRNA/customlinks8.png)
