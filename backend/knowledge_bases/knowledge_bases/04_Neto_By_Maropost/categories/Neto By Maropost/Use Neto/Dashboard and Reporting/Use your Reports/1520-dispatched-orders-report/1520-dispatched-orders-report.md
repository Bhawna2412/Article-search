# Dispatched Orders Report

The dispatched orders report adds the totals of all sales orders that have been dispatched. This includes any orders with a return (RMA) or cancelled orders.

| Report Column | Formula |
| --- | --- |
| Revenue | Total value of all orders in dispatched status for that month and year. Includes tax and shipping amounts. |
| Tax | Total tax amount of all orders in dispatched status for that month and year. |
| Shipping | Total shipping amount of all orders in dispatched status for that month and year. |
| Revenue, excluding Shipping and Taxes (AUD) | Total value of all orderlines in dispatched status for that month and year. |
| Orders | Number of dispatched orders for that month and year. |
