# Add Widgets to the Dashboard

## Overview

Add widgets to your Neto Dashboard and make your dashboard a powerful tool in providing a snapshot of up-to-date store related information. This information can include:

- Sales data
- Order data
- New customer data.

Widgets are larger pieces of information that display in the centre of the dashboard. There are also sidebar widgets which are smaller pieces of information that display in the side bar of the dashboard.

## Add a Widget to your Dashboard

1. In your Neto control panel, select the **Dashboard** menu.
2.   - Click in the **Add widgets to your dashboard...** field or
  - Click the **Search** button.

  Locate the **Widgets** section. There are two ways to locate the widget you want to add:
3. Select a widget from the displayed list.
4. Click the **Okay** button. The selected widget will display on the dashboard.
5. Once added, you can change the layout of the dashboard by dragging and dropping any of the widgets you wish the use.
