# Neto by Maropost Pick'n Pack

Mobile order fulfillment technology transforms your warehouse operations by combining barcode scanning, real-time inventory management, and wireless printing capabilities. This comprehensive solution helps streamline order processing, reduce picking errors, and increase fulfillment efficiency while providing the flexibility to scale operations across multiple warehouse locations.

You can implement Pick'n Pack to enable warehouse staff to fulfill orders against real-time inventory information using mobile devices with integrated barcode scanners. This typically reduces order processing time and improves accuracy by eliminating manual data entry, while giving you complete visibility across warehouse operations that supports efficient B2B fulfillment workflows.

## How Pick'n Pack Works

Neto by Maropost Pick'n Pack is an iOS based app which works on iPad and iPhone encased with Linea Pro barcode scanners, with the latest version of the Pick'n Pack app being designed to use the iOS device camera without requiring the external scanning hardware. Warehouse operators can start to fulfil orders against real-time inventory information from multiple warehouse locations. Orders can be processed on the spot or be sent to a packing station using the web-based version of Pick'n Pack to quickly process and ship orders quickly.

For all your hardware requirements, visit our partners at [POS99](https://neto.pos99.com.au/neto-hardware/neto-mobile-printers/).

## Benefits of Pick'n Pack

- **Scan Pick Orders**: Save time and improve accuracy with barcode picking or use our "touch to pick" function. Incorrect picks signalled by an error tone or vibration.
- **Print Wirelessly**: Generate real time shipping cost estimates and print shipping labels & manifests to compatible mobile or desktop printers.
- **Increase Efficiency**: Sort order by zone, SKU or shipping method. Increase your warehouse efficiency by assigning users to zones and having them pick by zone.
- **Save Money**: With the cost of paper, incorrect picks and data entry errors, Neto by Maropost Pick'n Pack pays for itself.
- **Flexible Use**: Combine mobile Pick'n Pack with the web version of Pick'n Pack for desktop based operations. Useful for customers with packing stations.
- **Barcodes not required**: Simply touch an order line to mark it as picked with no barcodes required.
