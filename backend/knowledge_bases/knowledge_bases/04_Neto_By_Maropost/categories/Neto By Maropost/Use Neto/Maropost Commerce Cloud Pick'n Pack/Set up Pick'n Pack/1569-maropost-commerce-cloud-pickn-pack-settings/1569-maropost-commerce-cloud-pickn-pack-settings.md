# Maropost Commerce Cloud Pick'n Pack Settings

You can configure Pick'n Pack settings to optimize your warehouse operations and improve order fulfillment speed, enabling your team to process orders more efficiently with mobile picking workflows. This helps streamline fulfillment operations by customizing pick and pack filters, barcode scanning options, automatic order routing, and printer configurations to match your warehouse processes, typically improving order processing speed and reducing picking errors. Properly configured Pick'n Pack settings enable you to automate order status transitions and support both mobile and desktop barcode scanning for flexible fulfillment workflows.

Setting up Pick'n Pack with appropriate global settings provides centralized management of warehouse operation preferences, helping ensure your fulfillment team works efficiently while maintaining accuracy in order processing and shipping label generation across all fulfillment activities.

Pick'n Pack has many useful settings to help your process orders faster and more efficiently. To change your Pick'n Pack settings:

1. In your Neto control panel, navigate to **Settings & Tools **> **All settings & Tools**.
2. Click on the **Mobile Pick'n Pack** tab and select **Pick'n Pack Settings**.
3. On the settings screen you'll see all the available global settings available for Pick'n Pack.
  Once you've made your changes, click **Save Changes**.
