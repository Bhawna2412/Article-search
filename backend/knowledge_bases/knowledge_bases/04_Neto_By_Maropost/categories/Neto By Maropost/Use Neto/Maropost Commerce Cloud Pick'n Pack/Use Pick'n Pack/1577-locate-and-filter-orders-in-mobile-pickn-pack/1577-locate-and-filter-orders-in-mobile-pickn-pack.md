# Locate and Filter Orders in Mobile Pick'n Pack

You can improve warehouse productivity by leveraging advanced order filtering capabilities that help pickers focus on the most relevant orders based on zone assignments, shipping methods, or specific customer requirements. This targeted approach to order picking helps reduce unnecessary warehouse travel and enables more efficient batch picking workflows, improving operational efficiency and reducing order processing time.

Configuring zone-based picking with staff assignments provides complete visibility into warehouse operations and helps ensure pickers work on orders within their designated areas. This systematic approach typically improves picking accuracy and enables better warehouse resource utilization, particularly beneficial for larger warehouses with multiple picking zones.

## To locate a list of orders to pick

click on the **Pick** button at the top of any screen. The **Pick** order screen will display a list of all orders that have not yet been picked.

## Filtering Orders to Pick

You can filter your orders on the order list page using the filter options at the top of the page.

**Pick by Zone**
Orders are filtered by picking zone. You can assign users to picking zones so that orders in their zone appear first automatically. You can do this from within the "staff user manager" section of your desktop control panel.  (See: Assigning Staff Members to Picking Zones)

**Pick by Shipping Method**
Orders are filtered by shipping option. You can select from a list of available options.

**Search Orders**
You can search for specific orders by customer name, order number or purchase order number.

Advanced filters are also available by clicking the "advanced filter" link. This will display additional filters over and above those displayed above. You can also use the advanced filter list to filter by multiple filters at the same time. For example if you would like to filter by zone and shipping method.

For large warehouses / pick areas we recommend using Zone as your default pick filter. You can assign warehouse users (pickers) to zones so that by default order lines for product in their zone are displayed first.

### How do I assign a product to a picking zone?

You can assign a product to a zone from within a products edit screen, using a file import or using the API. When setting up your warehouse it is important to group like products together into well thought out zones.

### How do I  assign a warehouse user / picker to a warehouse zone?

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Settings & Tools** > **Staff Users**.
2. Select the staff member you want to edit.
3. Click on the **Picking Zones** tab.
4. Add picking zones to the user (you can assign multiple zones to a single user).

## To locate a list of orders to pack

To locate a list of orders to pack, click on the **Pack** button at the top of any screen. The **Pack** order screen will display a list of all orders that have not yet been dispatched.

## Filtering Orders to Pack

You can filter your orders on the order list page using the filter options at the top of the page.

**Pack By Order**

Lists all order by last picked

**Pack By Bin Location**

Gives you the option to scan your pick bin barcode to locate the order to pack

**Pack By Shipping Method**

Gives you the ability to select orders to pack by shipping option

## Search Orders

You can search for specific orders by customer name, order number or purchase order number.
