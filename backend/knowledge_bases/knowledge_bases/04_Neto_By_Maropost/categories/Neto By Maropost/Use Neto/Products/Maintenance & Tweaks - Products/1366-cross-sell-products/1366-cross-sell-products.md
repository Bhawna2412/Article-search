**Cross selling** can increase basket size by interesting the customer with more products from other categories.

**Upselling** is a way to increase money spent on a particular item; buy a more expensive version of the same product type or add warranties/features that relate to the product the customer is interested in.

**Please Note**: Cross and upsells are standard on most Neto website themes. Custom or premium themes may not support cross and upsells without a design tweak

## Set up Cross or Up sell Products

1. Install the free **Cross Sell Products** add-on from the Add-on Store (under **Product Management** section). The **Cross Sell Products** section all appear in the product details in the control panel:
2. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Products** > **View Products** and select a product you wish to edit.
3. Scroll down to either the **Cross-sell Products** tab or **Upsell Products** tab.
  [https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/CrossSellProducts3.05T5g00000gPvy2EAC.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/CrossSellProducts3.05T5g00000gPvy2EAC.png)

  **Tip**: If you don't see the cross-sell or upsell sections, click the **Customise Layout** button at the top of the page and enable them.
4. Add products to the search field. You can use parent or kitted products in this section as well if you wish.
5. Press **Save**.

Here is an example of how it looks on your webstore. This will change depending on the theme you are using.

[https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/CrossSellProducts4.05T5g00000gQ2erEAC.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/CrossSellProducts4.05T5g00000gQ2erEAC.png)

Upsell products can also be shown at checkout by turning on the configuration in your Web store settings.

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Settings & Tools** > **All Settings & Tools**.
2. Select **Web Store** from the left side menu, and choose **Webstore Settings**.
3. Scroll to the **Upsell Settings** and toggle the option **Display Upsell Page Before Checkout** and **Save**.
