# Set up SmartrMail

### This add-on was built by SmartrMail

Visit add-on website

For support with this add-on [contact SmartrMail](https://docs.smartrmail.com/en/).

## SmartrMail Overview

SmartrMail

is an email marketing application tool that automatically sends personalised shopping-related emails and integrates directly with Neto.

Different customer groups can be identified by categories such as:

- Email engagement
- Order recency
- Purchase history
- Date/day added to your list
- Total orders
- Total spend

Targeted emails can then be sent to a different cross sections of customers in your email list, including:

- new sign ups
- valued customers
- inactive subscribers and
- customers who have purchased specific items.

### Features:

- Automatically send personalised, curated product emails
- Compose email newsletter five times faster with in-app product lookup.
- Automate welcome emails, win-back campaigns, cross-sell recommendations etc.

## Set up SmartrMail

1. In your Neto control panel click on **Addons**.
2. Find the SmartrMail addon and click the **Install** button.
3. Confirm by clicking **Install** again.

SmartrMail will now be installed and linked to your Neto control panel. To access SmartrMail, navigate to **Marketing** > **Email Marketing**.

## Turn off Abandoned Cart Emails in Neto

SmartrMail can handle abandoned cart emails automatically using the Abandoned Cart Saver add-on. This add-on needs to be installed, with the **Send abandoned cart customer email **setting inactive.

**Please Note**: If this add-on is installed already, you will still need to follow these steps to ensure Neto is not sending abandoned cart emails.

1. In your Neto control panel, click the **Addons** link from the menu.
2. Click on the **Marketing** link within the **Add-on Store** menu.
3. Click the **Install** button beneath the **Abandoned Cart Saver** option.
  **Please Note**: If you already have this module installed, continue to the next step.
4. Clear the **Send abandoned cart customer email** checkbox to ensure Smartr Mail sends emails and not Neto.
5. Click the **Install** button.
