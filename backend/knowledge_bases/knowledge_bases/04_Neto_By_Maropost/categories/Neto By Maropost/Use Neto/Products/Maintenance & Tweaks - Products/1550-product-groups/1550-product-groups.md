# Product Groups

Unlike [order quantity limits](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1355-order-quantity-limits) (which is a per product feature), you can set purchase limits that apply to multiple products. Product groups can also be assigned a discount coupon to add purchase incentives.

> **Important:** Product Groups requires the [Advanced Product Descriptons](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1473-advanced-product-descriptions) add-on so that products can be assigned to groups.

## Add a Product Group

To add a new product group:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Settings & tools** > **All settings & tools**.
2. Find and click on **Product Groups**.
3. Click the **Add New** button and then click **Add New Product Group**.
4. Enter the details of your product group.
5. Click the **Create / Update Item Group** button.

Your product group is now created and can be assigned to products.

## Assign a Product to a Product Group

Product groups can be assigned to product manually in your control panel or by using the [Complex Import Wizard](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/668-product-import-wizard#complex-import).

To assign a product group to a product manually:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Products** > **View products**.
2. Click on the product you want to assign a product group to.
  **Please Note**: Assign groups to child products rather than the variation parent.
3. In the Categorisation section of the page click on the **Group** dropdown menu and select a product group.
  **Please Note**: Each product can only belong to one Product Group at any time.
4. Click the **Save** button.

Repeat these steps for any additional products you want assigned to the product group.

## Delete a Product Group

To delete a product group:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Settings & tools** > **All settings & tools**.
2. Find and click on **Product Groups**.
3. Use the checkbox to select the group(s) that you want to delete.
4. Click the **With x selected** button and then click **Delete Group**.

Your product group is now deleted.

## Product Groups and Discount Coupons

Product groups can be combined with discount coupons using the product group condition or product group type discounts. To set up discounts using these follow the steps [to Add a Discount Coupon](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1360-add-a-discount-coupon).
