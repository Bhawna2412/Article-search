# Automatically Apply Discounts to Customers

## Set a permanent fixed percentage discount on the customer card

You can define a set percentage off discount amount per customer that automatically applies to a customers order. This discount percentage will be in addition to any other discounts and will apply to all products.

1. In your Neto by Maropost control panel navigate to **Customers** > **View Customers & prospects**.
2. Search for the customer and open the customer card
3. Click on the **Payment Details** ;tab.
4. Enter a discount percentage amount in the **Default Discount Percentage** field.
  **Note**: This discount will only be applied if the customer is logged in. In this example, the customer will receive 10% off all prices site-wide.
5. **Save **the changes.
