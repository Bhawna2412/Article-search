# Assembled Dimensions

## [Image: Assembled-Dimensions.png]
This add-on was built by Maropost

Add-ons and Integrations built in-house by Maropost are documented and supported by our customer service team. If you're having issues with this integration, please [let us know](https://www.netohq.com/support/s/ask-us)!

Add fields to display the assembled dimensions of your products to your customers. Ideal for furniture retailers.

To install the assembled dimensions add-on:

1. In your Neto by Maropost control panel click on the **Addons** menu.
2. In the Product Management section, find the Assembled Dimensions add-on and click the **Install** button.
3. Click the **Install** button in the pop-up that appears.

The **Assembled Dimensions** section will appear in the product details page in the control panel:

On the product page, the dimensions and unit of measure appear under the **Specifications** tab:

Shipping dimensions are used in shipping cost calculations and the shipping consignment / label generation. Assembled dimensions are for descriptive purposes only and are used to inform customers about the assembled dimensions of a product.

The difference between the two is easily demonstrated with flat pack furniture. A shelving unit will ship disassembled, but the assembled dimensions are important for customers to decide if the product is the right fit for them.
