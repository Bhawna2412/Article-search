# Manage Email Subscribers and Contact Lists

## Add a Contact List

Contact lists help you organise your subscribers into specific groups, for different purposes.

**Please Note**: Contact lists do not sync with email marketing integrations, but can be exported in a CSV file.

To add a new contact list:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Marketing** > **Contact lists**.
2. Click the **Add new list** button.
3. Fill in the fields for your contact list:
4. Click the **Save & close** button.

Your contact list is added, and you can now add subscribers to it.

## Add a New Subscriber

Email subscribers can be added even if the recipient doesn't have a customer card in your control panel. To add a subscriber:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Marketing** > **View Subscribers**.
2. Click the **Add new subscriber** button.
3. Add the subscribers email address, and opt them in to the emails you want them to receive.
4. Click on **Fields**. Here you can enter more information about the subscriber. If they make an account on your site, these details will automatically update from the customer card.
5. Click on **Member of lists**. You contact lists display here, and you can add the subscriber to these lists by setting the drop down menus to **Active** and **Opt In**.
6. Click the **Save** button. Your subscriber is created and added to your lists.

> **Note:** Click the **Create User** button and enter a username to make an account for your subscriber.

7. Click the **Generate data** button to queue a process that will update your contact lists.

## Unsubscribe or Remove a Subscriber

To stop a subscriber from receiving your emails:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Marketing** > **View Subscribers**.
2. Click the checkboxes next to the subscribers you want to unsubscribe or remove.
3. At the bottom of the page, click the **With # Selected** button.
4. To remove from a specific list, hover over **Unsubscribe from** and click the specific list. Alternatively, click **Delete** to remove the subscriber completely.

## Export Subscribers and Contact Lists

Subscribers are not automatically synced with your email marking integrations unless they are a customer or prospect. Instead, you will need to export your subscribers regularly and import them into your email marketing system.

There's two ways to perform the export, on the subscribers page, and contact list page.

- Subscribers
- Contact List

### Subscribers

To export a list of your email subscribers:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Marketing** > **View Subscribers**.
2. At the top of the page, click the **Export** button, and then click **Export results as .CSV file**.

The file will be downloaded to your computer automatically.

### Contact List

To export the subscribers on a specific contact list:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Marketing** > **Contact lists**.
2. Click on the contact list you want to export.
3. At the top of the page, click the **Export** button.

> **Note:** You can also set filters on this page to only export specific subscribers on the list.

The file will be downloaded to your computer automatically.
