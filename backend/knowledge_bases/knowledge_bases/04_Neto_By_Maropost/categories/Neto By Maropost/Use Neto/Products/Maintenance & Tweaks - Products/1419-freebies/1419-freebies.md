# Freebies

[Image: Freebies]

### This add-on was built by Maropost

Add-ons and Integrations built in-house by Maropost are documented and supported by our customer service team. If you're having issues with this integration, [let us know](https://www.netohq.com/support/s/ask-us)!

## Overview

Freebies are products that are given away for free when another product is purchased. Customers who purchase the product will also get the free products listed.

**Please Note**: Parent products and gift vouchers are not supported as freebies. The individual variations should be added instead.

## Install the Freebies Add-on

To install the add-on:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to the **Addons** menu.
2. In the product management section, find the **Freebies** add-on and click the Install button.
3. Confirm by clicking the **Install** button in the pop-up that appears.

Once installed the Freebies section will appear on all products in your control panel:

## Add Freebies to Products

Freebies has the benefit of displaying the free product(s) the customer will choose from when on the shopping cart page.

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Products** > **View Products**.
2. Edit the product you wish to make changes to.
3. Scroll down to the **Freebies** section.
4. Click on the field under **Add Related Product** and enter either the product SKU or part of the product name. A list of the products that match what you have entered.
5. Select the product from the list. You can add more than one product.
6. **Save changes**. When the product is added to the shopping cart, the customer can choose which of the free products they want to add to their order.
