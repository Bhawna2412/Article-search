# Integrate Google Tag Manager

Google Tag Manager is a great way to manage and control all google scripts in one location.

**Tip**: Looking for our in-depth set up guide? See this article instead.

**Important note**: Adding too many scripts through Google Tag Manager can significantly slow your website.

1. Before you get started, sign up for your free [tag manager account](https://tagmanager.google.com/#/admin/accounts/create).
2. Within the **Setup Account** page, add your account name (eg. your company name).
3. Setup your container (ie. your website address) and select **Web** as the use and click **Create**.
  [Image: User-added image]
4. Agree to the terms and conditions, and you’ll be given two scripts to embed into your website. Keep this page open so you can copy and paste the code in step 7.
5. In your Neto by Maropost control panel navigate to **Setup & Tools** > **All Settings & Tools**.
6. Click on **Custom Scripts** and then **Add New**.
7. Name the script **Google Tag Manager**. Paste the first code into the **Page Header**, and the second into the **Page Footer** tab, then **Save**.
  **Please Note**: Changes to a custom script require you to authenticate your access. Click the **Send Token Now** button and an email will be sent to you with a security token. Copy the token and paste it into the verification field. Click the **Verify Token** button and your changes can be saved. The token will last two hours in case you need to change multiple scripts.
