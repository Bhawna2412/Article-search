# Add, View, Edit, and Process Neto Commerce Inventory Stocktakes

> **Important:** This article requires the **Neto Commerce Inventory** add-on. If you are not using **Neto Commerce Inventory**, please refer to the [standard instructions](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1549-product-stock-level-quantity-management).

A stocktake is a count of the business inventory on hand, typically done at the end of the financial year, though some businesses may do it more regularly.

Having an accurate idea of the number of stock items your business owns allows your accountant to reconcile physical stock to the inventory records, highlight variances, and identify issues with stock management and control.

With accurate knowledge about stock movements and stock on hand, you can make informed decisions about theft, slow-moving items, damaged stock, technology obsolescence and warehouse processes.

## Create a Stocktake from the Stocktake Page

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Stock Control** > **Stocktake**.
2. If this is your first time, click the **Perform first stocktake** button. Alternatively, if you're in the Stocktake page click the **Add** button.
3. Select the **warehouse** you wish to create a stocktake for, then enter a **stocktake name**. Choose either **todays date or a date in the future** for your stocktake

> **Note:** You cannot create a stock take with a due date in the past.

4. **Add products** to your stock take by typing or scanning the product SKU/Name or UPC.
5. Continue to do this for all the products that you would like to add to your stock take.
6. The **On Hand QTY** will be updated with you current expected stock level for the warehouse chosen. (This will continue to update until you put the stocktake in progress, this allows you to plan stocktakes in advance).
7. Once you hit save, the stocktake will be put into a **pending status** ready for you to put it in progress.

## Create a Stocktake from the Product Page

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Products** > **View products**
2. Filter your product selection to the products that you would like to stocktake, bulk select products and click **With selected > Add to new stocktake**.
3. This will load the create stock take page with the products you have selected pre-populated.
4. Once you hit save, the stocktake will be put into a pending status ready for you to put it in progress

## View Stocktakes

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Stock Control** > **Stocktake**.
2. From this page you can see all your stocktakes (Pending, in progress and completed)

You can see when the stocktake is due, where a stocktake has been put in progress the start date will be populated.

## Edit, Start or Cancel a Stocktake

1. To view a stocktake,click on the **Stocktake ID**.
2. This will show your stocktake in **view mode** (except when in **progress**, this will take you to the count page)
3. When the stocktake is **pending**, clicking the link will put you on the view screen and you will be able to edit, start or cancel the stocktake.

## Process a Stocktake

1. Starting a stocktake will lock any products that appear on the stocktake by warehouse. You will not be able to perform any of the following actions for those products:  - Receive purchase order
  - Perform a stock adjustment
  - Start a stocktake
  - Pick orders

> **Note:** Locking only occurs when the stocktake is in progress, Canceling or completing a stocktake will release the above functionality.
