# Neto POS

![CC-POS.jpg](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000e7t21EAA.jpg)

### This add-on was built by Maropost

Add-ons and Integrations built in-house by Maropost are documented and supported by our customer service team. If you're having issues with this integration, [let us know](https://www.netohq.com/support/s/ask-us)!

**Please Note**: Neto POS is not available outside of Australia and New Zealand.

## Set up Maropost POS

Navigate to the **Addon** store menu. In the Maropost modules section, find the Maropost POS module and click the **Install** button. Maropost POS is an additional cost.

Check out Set up Neto POS to get started.

## Features

- 
  Maropost POS works on PC, Mac, and iPad, and syncs to a central database in the cloud. Use any supported device or multiple.
- 
  You can sell even when the internet goes down. Maropost POS will automatically sync your sales back to the cloud when you’re back online.
- 
  Apply discounts to line items or an entire sale as a % or $ amount.
- 
  Add notes to line items to record additional information related to the sale of that item. Notes can appear on receipts.
- 
  Create customer accounts to track history and loyalty. Sales taken in Maropost POS will sync with your online customer accounts.
- 
  Print receipts for your customers or email them directly.
- 
  Add serial tracking to specific products and the system will automatically prompt your staff to enter or scan a serial number at the point of sale.
- 
  Part way through a sale and need to serve another customer in the meantime? Simply park sales for future access at the touch of a button.
- 
  Quickly add items to a sale with USB or Bluetooth barcode scanner support. Store multiple barcodes against a single SKU to support supplier changes.
- 
  Quickly find what you are looking for by keyword or UPC.
- 
  Organise your inventory into an easy to navigate catalogue. Coming soon: Pin popular products to your home page for quick and easy access.
- 
  Maropost POS is fast. Using the latest in user interface technology, we have designed Maropost POS to be responsive to your touch inputs.
- 
  Refund past orders to store credit, or back to the customer using the original payment method.
- 
  Reward your customers with loyalty points each time they purchase. Each point has a monetary value, which can be applied to future sales.

## Payments

- 
  Securely accept credit or debit cards through leading payment processors or your own bank issued terminal. Integrated credit, debit, and Alipay payments with Tyro terminals.
- 
  Accepting cash is quick and easy. Maropost POS will calculate how much change is due and automatically round transactions where necessary.
- 
  Accept multiple payments using different methods for a single sale.
- 
  Issue gift certificates to your customers that can be redeemed against sales.
- 
  Customers can pay on terms either in store our through your Neto webstore.
- 
  Supported Tyro EFTPOS terminals can accept Alipay payments.

## For Growing Businesses

- 
  Create user accounts for your staff members to track sales, manage permissions and control security. Simple PIN access makes it quick and easy for staff to switch between users.
- 
  Create and control additional locations / outlets from a central control panel. Allocate stock, staff and registers to outlets and run reports on the performance of each separately.
- 
  Lock registers to specific hardware devices for complete control over your sales architecture.
- 
  Maropost POS is part of Neto, an all-in-one commerce platform that allows you to sell online, offline and on eBay from one centralised solution.

## Data Management

- 
  Your products, customers, staff, sales, reporting and more is all managed in one central online database. Secure and accessible from anywhere, at anytime.
- 
  Use our product and customer import templates to bulk import your existing data. If you have an existing POS solution, we can import your products in a few simple steps. Data import tools can also be used to adjust stock, change pricing and re-organise your catalogue in bulk.
- 
  Use our data export tools to export all of your data at anytime.
- 
  Over 40 inbuilt reports including product and sales reports, a real-time dashboard and employee performance reports.
- 
  All your data is automatically backed up daily and stored off-site for added security.
- 
  Create a printable summary of your daily totals per register.
