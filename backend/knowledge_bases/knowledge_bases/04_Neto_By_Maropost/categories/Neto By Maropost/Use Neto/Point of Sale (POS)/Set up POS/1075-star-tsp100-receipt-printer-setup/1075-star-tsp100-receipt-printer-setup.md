# Star TSP100 Receipt Printer Setup

**Please Note**: Neto POS is not available outside of Australia and New Zealand.

Maropost POS supports two different models of the Star TSP100 thermal printers, the TSP100LAN and TSP100ECO. Manuals for each model can be found on the Star Micronics website using the links below.

[TSP100LAN](http://www.starmicronics.com/support/Mannualfolder/tsp100lan_hm_en.pdf)
[TSP100ECO](http://www.starmicronics.com/support/Mannualfolder/tsp100iiu_hm_en.pdf)

## Printer Setup

To install and set up your printer please follow these steps:

1. Connect the power cord to your printer and the wall outlet.
2. Connect USB data cable from your printer to your PC.
3. Connect the communications cable from your printer to your cash drawer.
4. For the **TSP100LAN** - Hold down the feed button and power up the printer. This will cause it to print a settings page with and IP address on it, which we will use during configuration.
  If the IP address appears as 0.0.0.0 please contact your system administrator for assistance adding the printer to your network.
5. Download the drivers for your printer on the [Star website](http://www.starmicronics.com/support/manual.aspx?printerCode=TSP100). Set the product type to TSP100futurePRNT.
6. Select the **Drivers** tab and pick **futurePRNT V7.3 Lite ** (under **Recommended**) to download the driver.
7. Once the drives have downloaded, extract the files.
8. Open the folder and double click the **Autorun.exe** installer.
9. Click **Installation** and choose **I Accept**.
10. Follow the steps in the install wizard to install the software.
  If you have the ECO model skip the next steps, as they only apply to the LAN model.
11. Once the software has finished installing, run it by going to your `C:\Program Files (x86)\StarMicronics\TSP100\Software\` folder.
12. Open the folder here and double click the **TSP100LAN_BT_Setting** file. It will look for the printer on your network.
13. Click **Printer Queue** and set it as your default printer if you wish. Then click **Apply**.

If you're using POS in a browser you can now print receipts like you would any other browser page. For iPad instructions refer to the next section.

## Connect an iPad POS Register to your Printer

1. In your POS register, open the menu button and click/tap **Settings**.
2. Click/tap **Printer and Cash Drawer**.
3. Click/tap the Star TSP printer in your list of network printers.
4. Click/tap **Done**.

Your printer is now setup and ready to print through Maropost POS.
