# Wishlists Overview

Customers who have an account on your webstore are able to make wish lists for items they wish to purchase in the future. This feature is available in most webstore themes by default.

Customers can access their wish lists in the future on the webstore by going to their **My Account** section and selecting **My Wish lists**.

## Wish List Reminder Emails

After a wish list is created, an email reminder is sent to the customer the following day. To change the email time:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Settings & tools** > **All settings & tools**.
2. Click on the **Processes & Scheduled Jobs** tab from the left hand side navigation menu and click **System Scheduled Tasks**.
3. In the Scheduled Tasks, scroll down and edit **Wishlist Reminder**. Here you can change when customers receive their wish lists.

## Wish List In-Stock Notification

Customer's who add an out of stock product to their wishlist can be notified when it comes back into stock. To turn on this notification:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Settings & tools** > **All settings & tools**.
2. Find and click on **Advanced configuration**.
3. In the Name field, search for `WISHLIST_RESTOCK_EMAIL_NOTIFY` and click on it in the results.
4. Change the value to **Yes** and click the **Save changes** button.

## Wish List Email Template

To change the wish list email template:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Settings & tools** > **All settings & tools**.
2. In the System Templates section, and click **Email Templates**.
3. Scroll down to ID 21 **Wishlist Notification Emails** and edit the template as required.

## View Customer Wish Lists

To access customer wish lists:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Customers** > **Wish lists**.
2. To access a wish lists for an individual customer, navigate to **Customers** > **View customers & prospects**.
3. Click on a customer you wish to view.
4. In the top right hand corner, click on the **History** button and click on the wish list.
