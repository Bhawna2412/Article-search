# Customer Emails

## Transactional Emails

These emails relate to orders and account management, and customers cannot be unsubscribed from these.

- Order Tracking Email
- Order Receipt Email
- Final Payment Reminder Email
- First Payment Reminder Email
- Customer Statement Template Email > Default
- Dispute Message Email (To Customer)
- Close Dispute Notification Email (To Company)
- Raise Dispute Confirmation Email (To Customer)
- Dispute Message Email (To Company)
- Dispute Status Change Notification Email (To Customer)
- Raise Dispute Notification Email (To Company)
- Order Emails > Order Quote Email
- Customer Emails > Customer Change Password Notification Email
- Customer Emails > Customer Recover Password Email
- Customer Emails > Customer Recover Username Email
- Customer Registration Emails > Customer Registration Notification Email (To Company)
- Customer Registration Emails > Non-Registered Customer Account Activation Email
- Customer Registration Emails > wholesale Customer Registration Notification Email (To Company)
- RMA Emails > RMA Message Email
- Gift Voucher Emails > Default
- Customer Statement Template PDF > Default

## Newsletter Emails

These emails are controlled by the **Newsletter Subscriber** checkbox on the customer account page.

- Order Emails > Order Follow Up Email
- Wishlist Notification Emails > Wishlist Reminder Email
- Abandoned Cart Customer Email > Default

## Notification Emails

These emails are controlled by the **Notification Subscriber** checkbox on the customer account page.

- Customer Emails > First Purchase Email
- Customer Registration Emails > Customer Registration Welcome Email (To Customer)
- Back In Stock Notifications > Back In Stock Notification
- Back In Stock Notifications > Stock Arrival Notification
- Stock Warning Notification Email > Stock Warning Email
- Stock Warning Notification Email > Restock Report Email
