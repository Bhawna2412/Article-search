# Create a TradeMe Account

![image1](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/CreateaTradeMeAccount.05T5g00000w6yhtEAA.jpg)
Trade Me is New Zealand's most popular online platform to buy and sell. You can connect through an automatic export data feed of your products into a designated FTP server where it's converted and pushed through to Trade Me.

Any orders received by Trade Me can then be imported from the FTP location on a regular basis.

> **Note:** Neto partners can set this up for you. [Request a data service job](https://www.netohq.com/job-request-portal) for a quote.

## Create a Trademe Account

1. Navigate to [www.trademe.co.nz](https://www.trademe.co.nz) and register a Trade Me account.
2. You'll be asked to become an authenticated seller. If you need assistance, see the [Trade Me documentation](http://www.trademe.co.nz/help/170/becoming-authenticated) for help.
3. Update your TradeMe account to show that you're "In Trade". Steps are found on the TradeMe website [here](https://help.trademe.co.nz/hc/en-us/articles/360011642731).
4. Set up a [TradeMe Pay Now](https://help.trademe.co.nz/hc/en-us/articles/360007264451) account (unless you are an approved international seller).
5. [Request FTP details](https://help.trademe.co.nz/hc/en-us/requests/new?ticket_form_id=360000518892&id=undefined) from TradeMe.
6. Once you've received this information, [contact a Neto partner](https://www.netohq.com/job-request-portal) for a quote to complete the set upon your behalf or alternatively, read on and complete this integration yourself.

Next step: Prepare Products for Data Export.
