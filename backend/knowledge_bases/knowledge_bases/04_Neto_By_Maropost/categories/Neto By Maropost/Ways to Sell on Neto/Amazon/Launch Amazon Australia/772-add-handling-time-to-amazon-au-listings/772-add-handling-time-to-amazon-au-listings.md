# Add Handling Time to Amazon AU Listings

Handling time is used by Amazon to determine the time it takes for a purchase to be marked as dispatched. Handling time is used in combination with transit time (in Seller Central) to calculate the estimated day of delivery for customers, and Amazon use it as a metric to measure merchant's performance.

`Total Delivery Time = Handling Time + Transit Time (+ any holidays)`

> **Note:** You need to meet or exceed the Handling times you commit to or Amazon may take action on your account.

Neto allows you to set up your handling time one of two ways. The simplest is to set one handling time for all products in Neto. Alternatively, you can enter the handling time on each product, individually or in bulk.

This article contains the following:

- [Set the handling time for your Amazon Australia listings]()
- [Add handling time to individual products]()
- [Add handling time to products in bulk]()

## Steps

### Set the handling time for your Amazon Australia listings

Click the **Shipping & Handling** section to expand the available settings.

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Amazon AU** > **Settings**.
2. Enter the number of days to dispatch an order in the **Handling Time** field.
  ![image1](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/AddHandlingTimetoAmazonAUListings1.05T5g00000tuCtoEAE.png)
3. Click the **Save Changes** button.

The handling time will now be set for all products. If you have certain products that take longer to dispatch, you can also enter a separate handling time for them which will take precedence over this setting. To do this, follow either of the below methods.

### Add Handling Time to Individual Products

1. In your Neto control panel navigate **Products** > **Active**.
  ![image2](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/AddHandlingTimetoAmazonAUListings2.05T5g00000tuCuXEAU.png)
2. Click the relevant **SKU** link to edit the product details.
3. Click the **Shipping** link from the **Product** menu and enter the number of days in the **Handling Time** field.
4. Click the **Save** button.
5. Repeat these steps to add the handling time for each product.

### Add Handling Time to Products in Bulk

1. In your Neto control panel navigate **Settings & Tools** > **Export Data**.
  ![image3](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/AddHandlingTimetoAmazonAUListings3.05T5g00000tuCw4EAE.png)
2. Click the **Perform complex export** button.
3. Select **Start Data Export Wizard** from the dropdown.
4. From the **File Settings** tab, enter a name of your file in the **Name your file** field.
5. Click the **Continue to Next Step** button.
6. Click the **Add New Field To File** button.
7. Select **Product Data** from the dropdown.
8. Select **SKU*** from the second dropdown.
9. Click the **Add New Field To File** button.
10. Select **Product Data** from the dropdown.
11. Select **Handling Time (days)** from the second dropdown.

> **Note:** Add the **Product Data** > **Name** field as well to see your product names in the file.

12. Click the **Continue to Next Step** button.
13. Enter any **Product Filters** into the respective fields.
14. Click the **Continue to Next Step** button.
15. Select **Local URL** from the **Send Export File to** dropdown.
16. Click the **Export Now** button. A page with a link to your file will display. Once your file is created, click the link to download the file.

> **Note:** Click the **Export Process** link to monitor the status of your export to see when it's complete.

17. Open the downloaded file and enter the handling time for each product SKU. Save it when complete.
18. In your Neto control panel navigate **Settings & Tools** > **Import Data**.
19. Click the **Perform complex import** button.
20. Select **Start Data Import Wizard** from the dropdown.
21. Click the **Choose File** button next to the **Upload File** button.
22. Locate the file to upload and click the **Continue to Next Step** button.
23. The fields should map automatically, so click the **Continue to Next Step** button.
24. The update options can stay as they are, so click the **Continue to Next Step** button.
25. Click the **Process File Now** button. Your file will be processed and the handling time for each product will be added from the file automatically.
  ![image4](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/AddHandlingTimetoAmazonAUListings4.05T5g00000tuCwsEAE.png)

You have successfully added handling time to your Amazon listings.
