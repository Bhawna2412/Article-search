## Install the Marketplace Maximizer Add-on

[▶ Embedded Video](https://app.supademo.com/embed/cmhwx777c02ci0a0i1zqx571b?embed_v=2&utm_source=embed)
To set up the Marketplace Maximizer sales channel:

1. 
  In your Neto control panel navigate to the **Addons** page
2. 
  In the Sales Channel section, find the Marketplace Maximizer add-on and click the **Install **button
3. 
  In the popup that appears click the **Install** button
4. 
  Click **Configure Marketplace Maximizer**

The Marketplace Maximizer application is built and maintained by CrescoData. You will be redirected to the Marketplace Maximizer app to accept Marketplace Maximizer’s terms and service costs and to configure your Marketplace Maximizer app.

## Set up the Marketplace Maximizer Integration

Once installed you can access the Marketplace Maximizer application via your Neto control panel. In the Sales Channel section of the sidebar menu, navigate to **Other > Marketplace Maximizer**. Or from the **Addons** page, locate the Marketplace Maximizer add-on and click **Configure**.

To configure your Marketplace Maximizer settings, follow the steps in the [Marketplace Maximizer help centre](https://marketplacemaximizer.com/documentation/).

## Approve Products to sync to Marketplace Maximizer

To list and sync products between Neto and Marketplace Maximizer you need to mark them as 'approved' in your Neto control panel. This will allow Neto to update the price and stock levels on Marketplace Maximizer, keeping them in sync with your other marketplaces.

Note: product approval needs to happen for every marketplace you have connected to Marketplace Maximizer, not just the main Marketplace Maximizer add-on.

Products in Neto can be approved in three ways:

- 
  [Individually Approve Products](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/614-marketplace-maximizer#individually-approve-products)
- 
  [Approve Products in Bulk](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/614-marketplace-maximizer#approve-products-in-bulk)
- 
  [Approve Products via Import](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/614-marketplace-maximizer#approve-products-via-import)

### Individually Approve Products

1. 
  In your Neto control panel navigate to the **Products** > **Active** page.
2. 
  Click on the SKU you want to list and update on Marketplace Maximizer.
3. 
  Select the **Approved for Marketplace Maximizer** checkbox.
4. 
  Click the **Save** button.

Repeat the process for each product to approve. The products will now be synced to Marketplace Maximizer and available to list to any connected marketplaces.

### Approve Products in Bulk

1. 
  In your Neto control panel navigate to **Products** > **Active**.
2. 
  Select the products to approve within Marketplace Maximizer by selecting the checkboxes next to each product you want to approve.
3. 
  Click the **With x Selected **button (beneath the product list) and select **Approve for Marketplace Maximizer** from the pop up menu.
4. 
  In the confirmation pop-up, click the **OK** button.
5. 
  Repeat the process for each group of products to approve.

### Approve Products via Import

**Tip**: [Export a list of products](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/501-product-export-wizard) that you want to approve for Marketplace Maximizer first using the export wizard.

1. 
  In your Neto control panel navigate to **Settings & tools** > **Import data**.
2. 
  Click the **Perform complex import** button and select **Start Data Import Wizard** from the menu.
3. 
  On the **Upload File** page, click the **Browse** button and locate the file to upload.
4. 
  Click the **Continue to Next Step button**.
5. 
  Select ‘SKU*’ in the **Database Field Name** field.
**Tip**: Remove any other fields to ensure other data isn't overwritten, and to speed up the import process.
6. 
  Click the **Add Extra Field** button.
7. 
  Select ‘Approved For Marketplace Maximizer’ from the **Database Field Name**.
8. 
  Change the **Column name** dropdown to ‘Static Value’. A blank field will display beneath.
9. Enter Y in the blank field.
10. You can repeat Steps 6 - 9 to approve products for multiple Marketplace Maximizer connected channels. Simply select a different channel each time until you have covered all channels.
11. Click the **Continue to Next Step** button.
12. Select **Update Existing Products Only** from the **How Do You Want To update Your Database** drop down box.
13. Click the **Continue to Next Step** button.
14. Click the **Process File Now** button.

Neto will start updating all the product SKUs imported as **Approved For Marketplace Maximizer**. Allow up to 10 minutes to complete the import process depending on the number of products you’ve imported.

You have successfully approved products to list and update on Marketplace Maximizer. Neto will now be able to update the prices and stock levels of your products on Marketplace Maximizer. This process occurs approximately every 15 minutes.

## Process Marketplace Maximizer Sales Orders

Marketplace Maximizer sales orders downloaded to Neto are processed the same as other sales orders. You can find more info on how to pick, pack, and dispatch sales orders here.

The order source and original order number is added into the **System Internal Notes**.

## End a Marketplace Maximizer Product Sync

Products synced to Marketplace Maximizer can be removed by unapproving them in Neto. Similar to product approval, the sync can be ended in the same three ways. Depending on your chosen method, perform either of the following:

- 
  For ending an individual product, navigate to **Products** > **Active**. Click the product SKU to go to the product page and then deselect the **Approved for Marketplace Maximizer** checkbox. ([see individual product approval](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/614-marketplace-maximizer#individually-approve-products))
- 
  For ending the product sync in bulk, navigate to **Products** > **Active**. Select the products and then from the **With x Selected **drop-down list (at the bottom of the product list), select **Unapprove for Marketplace Maximizer**. ([see bulk product approval steps](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/614-marketplace-maximizer#approve-products-in-bulk))
- 
  For ending the product sync via import, perform the import with the **Approved For Marketplace Maximizer** column set to N. ([see import steps](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/614-marketplace-maximizer#approve-products-via-import))

## Uninstall the Marketplace Maximizer Integration

Ensure you have uninstalled all connected Marketplace Maximizer channels before uninstalling the main Marketplace Maximizer add-on. Related channels will not be uninstalled automatically.

To remove the Marketplace Maximizer integration:

1. 
2. 
  Click **Cancel Subscription**.
3. 
4. 
  In the Sales Channels section, find the Marketplace Maximizer add-on and click the **Uninstall** button.
5. 
  Confirm that you want to uninstall the integration.

Marketplace Maximizer is now uninstalled from your Neto control panel.
