# Add a JotForm Contact Form to your Webstore

To use a JotForm form in Neto you will need to:

- [Create a Contact Form in JotForm](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1105-add-a-jotform-contact-form-to-your-webstore#create-a-contact-form-in-jotform)
- [Add your Form to your Neto Webstore](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1105-add-a-jotform-contact-form-to-your-webstore#)

## Create a Contact Form in JotForm

To create a contact form in JotForm:

1. Create an account with [JotForm](https://www.jotform.com/). They have both free and paid plans.
2. In JotForm, click the **Create Form** button to start making your form. You can learn more about creating forms on the [JotForm help pages](https://www.jotform.com/help/).

> **Note:** Use a templates and search for contact forms to speed up the creation process. We strongly recommend [adding a captcha to the form](https://www.jotform.com/help/205-how-to-add-a-captcha-field), to help prevent spam enquiries.

3. Once you have finished building your form and reviewing the settings, click on the **Publish** step.
4. Click on **Embed** in the menu on the left.
5. Click the **Copy code** button.

You're now ready to add the form code in Neto.

## Add your Form to your Neto Webstore

With the form created, you can now add it to your webstore:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Settings & tools** > **All settings & tools**.
2. Search for **Content types manager** and click it.
3. Click **Form**.
4. Select the **Show in control panel menu** check box and click the **Save** button.
5. Navigate to **Webstore** > **Contact form**.
6. On the Page **Content tab** click the **Source Code** button.

> **Note:** Check the **Form** tab for any existing content, and delete anything that's already there.

7. Paste the code you copied from JotForm into the text area.
8. Click the **Save** button.

> **Note:** Click the **View on webstore** button to see how your form looks.

Your JotForm contact form is now live on your webstore.
