# Add a Freshdesk Contact Widget to your Webstore

To integrate Freshdesk onto your Neto webstore, there's two parts that need to be set up:

- [Create a Contact Us Widget in Freshdesk](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1104-add-a-freshdesk-contact-widget-to-your-webstore#create-a-contact-us-widget-in-freshdesk)
- [Add the Freshdesk Widget in Neto](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1104-add-a-freshdesk-contact-widget-to-your-webstore#)

## Create a Contact Us Widget in Freshdesk

Freshdesk widgets are straight forward to create and customise to match your webstore branding. Follow the steps [in their help guide](https://support.freshdesk.com/support/solutions/articles/239273-setting-up-your-help-widget) to create a contact us widget.

We recommend that you enable the captcha settings to reduce the amount of spam you receive.

![image1](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/AddaFreshdeskContactWidgettoyourWebstore1.05T5g00000twrumEAA.png)

> **Note:** You can also explore the other features available in the help widget [here](https://support.freshdesk.com/support/solutions/50000000010).

Once your widget is created, copy the embed code so that it can be added into your Neto control panel.

## Add the Freshdesk Widget in Neto

To make the contact us widget appear on your webstore, you need to add a custom script:

1. In your Neto control panel, navigate to **Settings & tools** > **All settings & tools**.
2. Search for **Custom Scripts** and click on it.
3. Click the **Add New** button.
4. Enter a name for your script in the **Name** field (e.g. Freshdesk contact us widget).
5. In the Scripts section, click on the **Page Footer** tab. Paste the embed code from Freshdesk into the text field.
  ![image2](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/AddaFreshdeskContactWidgettoyourWebstore2.05T5g00000twrvGEAQ.png)
6. Click the **Save** button. You'll be prompted to send a verification token to your email address.
7. When you receive the email, copy the security token into your control panel and click the **Verify Token** button.
  ![image3](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/AddaFreshdeskContactWidgettoyourWebstore3.05T5g00000twrw4EAA.png)

Your custom script will be saved, and your Freshdesk widget will appear on your webstore.
