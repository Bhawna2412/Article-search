# Add a Contact Us Form to your Webstore

Contact forms are easily implemented from third party providers into your Neto webstore. We recommend using one of the following solutions, which we provide instructions for:

- Zendesk
- [Freshdesk](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1104-add-a-freshdesk-contact-widget-to-your-webstore)
- JotForm
- [Wufoo](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1009-wufoo-form-builder)

If you don't wish to use one of these providers, you can set up a basic contact from in Neto using the steps below:

- [Add a Form to the Webstore Menu](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1110-add-a-contact-us-form-to-your-webstore#2)
- [Edit the Contact Us Form](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1110-add-a-contact-us-form-to-your-webstore#3)
- [Edit the Thank You Page](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1110-add-a-contact-us-form-to-your-webstore#4)
- [Edit the Contact Form Email Template](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1110-add-a-contact-us-form-to-your-webstore#4)

> **Important:** This option includes basic form functionality only, and does not support CAPTCHA, or a dedicated interface to manage customer interactions.

## Add a Form to the Webstore Menu

**Please Note**: If your Webstore menu already has **Form** or **Contact Form** you can skip this section.

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Settings & Tools** > **All Settings & Tools**.
2. Select **Control Panel** from the side menu and click on **Content Types Manager**.
3. Click **Form**.
  ![image1](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/AddaContactUsFormtoyourWebstore1.05T5g00000twSITEA2.png)
4. Select the **Show In Control Panel Menu** checkbox and click the **Save** button.
  ![image2](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/AddaContactUsFormtoyourWebstore2.05T5g00000twSIiEAM.png)

## Edit the Thank You Page

After a customer submits their contact request, they're presented with a thank you page. To edit this page:

1. In your Neto control panel, navigate to **Webstore** > **Form**.
2. Click on the **Contact Us - Thank You** form.
3. Edit the page, and save your changes.

## Edit the Contact Form Email Template

The email that you receive when the form is submitted can be edited via [SFTP](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1046-request-file-transfer-protocol-ftp-sftp-access) or in your control panel:

1. In your control panel navigate to **Settings & tools** > **All settings & tools**.
2. Search for and click on **Email templates**.
3. Click on **Contact Form Emails**.
4. Click on **contact_us**.

> **Note:** If no templates appear click the **Refresh Template Files** button.

You can now edit the email that is sent to you from the contact form.
