## Set up the Invoice Templates Add-on

To create new template sets you need to install the Invoice Templates add-on:

1. In your Neto control panel click the **Addons** menu.
2. In the content management section, find the Invoice Templates add-on and click the **Install** button.
3. Click the **Install** button in the pop-up window that appears.

Once activated, the **Doc Template** option will appear in the **Billing Address** section for orders and quotes:

![Image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/SetupInvoiceTemplatesforSpecificCustomers2.05T5g00000TlD7VEAV.jpg)

## Custom Invoice Template

Before you can assign a custom template to customers, it first needs to be added to your site files. The easiest way to do this is to [request sftp access](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1046-request-file-transfer-protocol-ftp-sftp-access) to your site, and copy the existing invoice template. You can then edit the HTML/ source code (you will typically require HTML or web design skills to do this) to your requirements. The Neto Developer Documentation contains more information on [Invoices](https://developers.maropost.com/documentation/neto-designer-documentation/emails-printables/system-documents/order/invoice/).

If you're not familiar with HTML, you can have [an approved Neto partner](http://design.neto.com.au/design-tweak) create the templates for you.

## Change the Invoice Template Per Customer

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Customers** > **Customers**.
2. Click on the username of the customer you want to edit.
3. Click the **Payment Details** tab and select the **Default Document Template**.
  ![Image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/SetupInvoiceTemplatesforSpecificCustomers3.05T5g00000TlDYBEA3.png)
4. Click **Save**.

## Change the Default Invoice Template

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Setup & Tools** > **All settings & tools**.
2. Under the **Sales Orders & Invoices** tab, select **Invoice & Statement Settings**.
  ![Image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/SetupInvoiceTemplatesforSpecificCustomers4.05T5g00000TlDYGEA3.png)
3. Select your default from the **Default document template set** dropdown and click **Save Changes**.
  ![Image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/SetupInvoiceTemplatesforSpecificCustomers5.05T5g00000TlDaHEAV.png)

## Edit the Invoice Template Sets

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Settings & Tools** > **All settings & tools**.
2. Under the **System Templates** tab, select **Custom Invoice Templates**.
  ![Image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/SetupInvoiceTemplatesforSpecificCustomers6.05T5g00000TlDaMEAV.png)
3. Click to add a new template or click the ID of the existing template to edit.
  ![Image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/SetupInvoiceTemplatesforSpecificCustomers7.05T5g00000TlEAZEA3.png)
4. You can now edit the details of the document template set.

> **Note:** To edit your email content, see [this article](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1041-edit-system-email-templates).
