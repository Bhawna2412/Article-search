# Manually Approve Website Customer Accounts

Neto automatically approves new customer account registrations. You can change this setting for **Retail** and **Wholesale** customers, allowing staff to manually to verify every registration before they can order from the webstore.

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Settings & Tools** > **All Settings & tools**.
2. Click on the **Customers** tab from the left hand side navigation menu, and click **Customer Settings**.
3. Here you can toggle whether or not you wish customers to be automatically approved when they sign up.

[Image: image1]
4. **Save Changes**.
5. To enable the user, simply navigate to **Customers** > **View customers & prospects**. Find and edit the customer you wish to make changes to.
6. On the top right hand side, click the **Active** button on the top right hand side and save changes.
