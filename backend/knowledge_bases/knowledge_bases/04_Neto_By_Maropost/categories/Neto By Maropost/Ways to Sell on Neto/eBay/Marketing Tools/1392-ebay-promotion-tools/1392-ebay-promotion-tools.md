# eBay Promotion Tools

## eBay Shipping Promotions

eBay's combined payment and promotional shipping options are available directly through their site. To use shipping promotions on your listings you need to set them up on eBay before linking them to your listing rules templates.

- [Flat Postage Rules](https://www.ebay.com.au/help/selling/posting-items/postage-rates/postage-discounts?id=4168#section2)
- [Calculated Postage Rules](https://www.ebay.com.au/help/selling/posting-items/postage-rates/postage-discounts?id=4168#section3)
- [Promotional Postage Discount Rules](https://www.ebay.com.au/help/selling/posting-items/postage-rates/postage-discounts?id=4168#section4)

Your postage rule will now be created and you'll see a **Postage Discount Profile ID** which will be used on your business policies or listing rule templates.

If you're using business policies to manage your postage, you can apply the shipping promotion rule directly to the policy.

Open your postage policy, and check the boxes that relate to the calculated or promotional rules you've created.

If you don't use business policies, you can apply the rules to your Neto listing rules templates:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **eBay** > **Listing Rule Templates**
2. Open the rules template that you wish to apply combined postage to by selecting the **ID**.
3. Select the **Shipping** tab and check the **Apply My eBay Promotional Shipping Rule**.
4. Enter the **Discount Profile ID** from eBay, and click **Save Template & Revise Active Listings**.

### Map Shipping

When using **Promotional Shipping** orders will download to Neto with one of two service names.

- 1599 - Domestic
- 51599 - International

These services need to be mapped to Neto shipping options so that the correct service appears on your order in Neto.

The flat postage rule has been set so that additional items are free, and no more than $10 will be paid for total postage. To use this rules you would need to enter the promo ID in listing template under shipping tab.

## eBay Promotions Manager

To access promotion manager in eBay, [click here](https://www.ebay.com.au/sh/mkt/promotionmanager/dashboard).

**Please Note**: This feature works entirely in eBay and is separate from any promotion or marketing add-ons in Neto. Promotions created in eBay do not carry over to Neto.

## Neto Promotion Pricing

To use your products promotion prices that has been entered on a product:

1. If you haven't already, create your price promotion by following the steps in [this article](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1547-pricing-promotions).
2. Navigate to **eBay** > **Listing Rule Templates**.
3. Open the template that lists the products you want to add promotion prices to.
4. On the field mapping tab, change the start price dropdown menu to **Current Price Group**. This uses the standard price (e.g. Price A) when not in promo period ,and then the promo price (e.g. Promo Price A) when in promo date ranges. This is a more streamlined process as listing price revises automatically.
5. Click the **Save & Revise Active Listings** button.

When your listings revise they will display the promotion price on your products.

## Best Offer

Best Offer is an option available for fixed price listings that allows buyers to make an offer to purchase the item at a price that they select. As a seller, you can accept, decline or counteroffer the Best Offer.

To set Best Offer in Neto:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **eBay** > **Listing Rules Template**.
2. Select the template you wish to add Best Offer to by clicking the **ID**.
3. Select the **Field Mapping** tab and check the **Accept Best Offers?** box.
4. You will now have three fields to set up.
  It is strongly recommended that you base the **Best Offer Auto Accept Price** and **Minimum Best Offer Price** as a percentage of the **Start Price**. For example, in the screenshot below offers will be automatically accepted if they are within 10% of the start price, and rejected if they are 20% or lower than the start price.
5. Once you have set up the fields, **Save Template & Revise Active Listings**.

**Please Note**: Any offers that are not automatically accepted or rejected will need to be [actioned in eBay](http://pages.ebay.com.au/help/sell/best-offer.html).

## Click & Collect

Where available, customers can choose to have their item shipped to their nearest Woolworth's, BIG W or [Parcel Point](https://parcelpoint.com.au/how-it-works/) location instead of having it sent to their home or business. In Neto you can use Click & Collect via a setting in your listing rules template.

### Enable Click & Collect

**Please Note**: These instructions only apply to sellers who are not using business policies. Click & Collect can be enabled on the postage policies in eBay if you use business policies.

Not all categories accept Click and Collect, so it's [recommended confirming](https://neto.com.au/assets/images/docs/7559/ClickandCollectEligibility.xlsx) if your category is available.

If you have Click & Collect enabled on your eBay account you can activate it in our system by following the below steps:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **eBay** > **Setup & Tools**.
2. Click on **Settings**.
3. In the **Listing Settings** section, tick **Show options for Click & Collect on the eBay listing template**.
4. Click **Save**.
5. To set up your rules template, navigate to **eBay** > **Listing Rule Templates**
6. Open a template by selecting the **ID**.

> **Important:** The maximum acceptable parcel weight is 16kg and the maximum parcel dimensions are 41.2cm x 50cm x 36cm, including packaging.

7. Select the **Shipping** tab. Your listing templates will now have a new check box called **Eligible For Click & Collect** where you can then select/unselect the Click and Collect option to be used on the listings associated with template.

Click & Collect will be added to your listings automatically if they meet the following criteria:

- Fast handling time - we recommend 3 days or less
- Listed in an eligible category
- Within maximum parcel weight and dimensions

If you've checked all of the above, but Click & Collect isn't shown on your listings, please [contact eBay](https://signin.ebay.com.au/ws/eBayISAPI.dll?SignIn&ru=http%3A%2F%2Focsnext.ebay.com.au%2Focs%2Fcusr%3FuserQuery%3DClick+%26from_ocs%3D1%26query%3DAbou) for assistance.

For more information on requirements of Click and Collect please visit [eBay seller centre](http://sellercentre.ebay.com.au/click-and-collect-faqs).

## Buy Online Pickup in Store (BOPIS)

eBay offers several different pick-up options  for buyers to get their orders. The simplest form is `Local Pickup`, which is available to all sellers. There is also the [Click & Collect](https://sellercentre.ebay.com.au/click-and-collect) program where orders are collected from the buyers chosen Woolworths or Big W store.

BOPIS combines these two features together to give everyone the best of both worlds. Buyers can choose a local store where they can pick up their order, and merchants get the buyer in their store for up sell and repeat purchase opportunities.

### Get Started

BOPIS features need to be turned on in your eBay seller account by eBay support. Contact eBay on 1800 107 517, Monday-Friday 8:00 AM - 9:00 PM, or Saturday-Sunday 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM (AEST/AEDT) to request BOPIS on your seller account.

While waiting for your eBay account to be upgraded you can start getting everything in Neto ready.

### Enable in Neto

Contact Neto support and ask for BOPIS to be enabled in your control panel. Once it is enabled in your control panel:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **eBay** > **Setup & Tools**.
2. Click on **Store Manager**.
3. Click on the account name that has BOPIS enabled.
4. Scroll to the **eBay Buy Online, Pickup In Store** box, and select the **Allow pick up items in my physical stores** box. Then click the **Save Changes** button.

### Neto Listing Rules Templates

Listings can have pickup enabled or disabled by a setting on their listing rules template, even if the location has stock and BOPIS is enabled.

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **eBay** > **Listing Rule Templates**.
2. Click on the template ID where you want to enable BOPIS.
3. Click on the **Shipping** tab.
4. Select the **Enable BOPIS** checkbox, and click **Save Template & Revise Active Listings**.
5. Repeat these steps for any other rules templates you want to offer BOPIS.

> **Note:** BOPIS orders are processed in Neto just as you would a regular Sales Order. The only difference is an extra stage called **Pending Pickup** which occurs before dispatch.

eBay listings using these templates will be queued to revise automatically, and the pickup option and locations will appear on each listing's **Postage & Payments** tab.

## Markdown Manager

The Neto eBay integration allows you to use Markdown manager to manage promotional pricing instead of our own promo pricing field.

> **Important:** Listings on promotion through markdown manager will not be revised by Neto (except stock level) until markdown manager is switched off or the promotion ends.

Markdown promotions are made in eBay's [promotions manager](https://www.ebay.com.au/sh/mkt/promotionmanager/dashboard). Once they have been created and assigned to your listings in eBay:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **eBay** > **Active Listings**.
2. Click the **Download Active Listings** button so that Neto know which listings are on promotion through markdown manager.

### Force Revisions

This setting lets Neto update the description of listings even when on sale. In most instances this will **remove** the sale price if the sale was setup using eBay's Mark Down Manager. We recommend updating the listing through eBay directly to preserve your promotion. If you still need to overwrite the promotion in Neto:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **eBay** > **Setup & Tools**.
2. Click on **Settings**.
3. Select the **Update description for item on sale** box and click the **Save** button.

Revise your listings, and they will update even though they are on a promotion through markdown manager.

## Guaranteed Delivery Program

eBay's Guaranteed Delivery program gives sellers more visibility on their listings that can be delivered in under three days to a buyer. Eligible listings will be highlighted in search results and buyers can filter results to see items that can be delivered to them using this program.

More information on the criteria, offer, and details can be found [here](https://sellercentre.ebay.com.au/promo/6469).

> **Note:** If at any time you need help from eBay about the Guaranteed Delivery program, please [contact their dedicated team](mailto:DL-eBay-Guaranteed-Delivery-Seller-Support@ebay.com).

**Please Note**: Guaranteed Delivery is only available for domestic shipping in Australia. If you have an eBay US account, please refer to the equivalent program [here](http://pages.ebay.com/seller-center/shipping/guaranteed-delivery.html).

Listings that you want to offer Guaranteed Delivery on must use a domestic rates table in the postage policy, so that you can specify the speed of your delivery to different parts of Australia.

To get started with eBay's Guaranteed Delivery program you need to:

1. Opt in to Guaranteed Delivery [here](https://www.ebay.com.au/ship/optin/egd).
2. Create your domestic postage rate table(s) in eBay.
3. Create/update your [postage policies in eBay](http://www.bizpolicy.ebay.com.au/businesspolicy/manage?&ssPageName=STRK:ME:LNLK) to include the domestic rate tables you've just created as a postage option.
4. [Enable Business Policies in your Neto account](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1079-ebay-business-policies).
5. Assign your returns, payment and postage policies to your listing rules templates. Make sure you've selected the postage policies with the domestic rate table option for any listings that should have Guaranteed Delivery.
6. Revise all listings to update them with the latest postage, returns, and payment policy information.

Once eBay approves your account for Guaranteed Delivery, the Guaranteed Delivery filter will display your eligible listings.
