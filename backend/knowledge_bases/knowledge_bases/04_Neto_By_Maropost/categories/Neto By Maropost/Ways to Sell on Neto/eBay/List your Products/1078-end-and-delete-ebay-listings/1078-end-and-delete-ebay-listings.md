# End and Delete eBay Listings

On occasion you may need to end eBay listings for products you're no longer selling or won't restock. It's best to end the listings through Neto by using the methods in this article.

> **Warning:** Ending a listing is permanent, and cannot be undone. Listings that are ended by mistake must be relisted on eBay.

## Check Relisting Settings

Before you end a listing you need to make sure it won't immediately relist. To check the relisting settings:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **eBay** > **View Listings**.
2. Search for the listing you want to end and click on the red listing ID to open the listing.
3. Check the **Auto Relist Options** field. If it says **Relist item immediately when any listing ends** or **Relist item only when all listing in the same listing template end** you need to make some changes before ending the listing, either on the [rules template](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1078-end-and-delete-ebay-listings#rules-template) or on the [listing](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1078-end-and-delete-ebay-listings#listing-settings).
  ![image1](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/EndandDeleteeBayListing1.05T5g00000v5LK7EAM.png)

### Change the Rules Template

With the listing still open:

1. Click on the link to the rules template at the top of the page. You'll be taken to your listing rules template.
2. Click on the **Categorisation & Listing Rules** tab.
3. Scroll down to the Listing Rules section. Find the **Relist Options** field, and change the setting to **Do not relist any items when listings end, only create new listings once**.
  ![image2](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/EndandDeleteeBayListing2.05T5g00000v5KnJEAU.png)
4. Save your template, and revise your listing.

You can now end the listings using this template without them relisting automatically.

### Change the Listing Settings

With the listing still open:

1. Click on the **Listing Designer** tab.
2. Remove the rules template (select the blank option at the top of the list).
  ![image3](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/EndandDeleteeBayListing3.05T5g00000v5LRwEAM.png)
3. On the first tab, change the relist options to **Do not relist any items when listings end, only create new listings once**.
4. Click the **Save and revise** button.

You can now end the listing without it relisting automatically.

## End Listings when Products are Inactive or Out of Stock

By default Neto won't end listings automatically, including when they are out of stock or marked as inactive. To end listings for out of stock or inactive products you need to change a setting in your control panel.

> **Important:** Deleting the Neto product will never end any eBay listings associated with it.

To change your eBay settings to automatically end listings:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **eBay** > **Setup & Tools**.
2. In the **eBay Settings** page, click the **Settings** option.
3.   - Auto end eBay listing for inactive item
  - Auto end eBay listing for out of stock item

  In the **eBay Config** page, scroll down to the **Listing Settings** section. Here you can toggle either of the settings that will end listings automatically:

  **Please Note**: The 'Auto end eBay listing for out of stock item' requires the Out of Stock Control setting to be turned off.
4. Once you're happy with the settings, click the **Save Changes** button.

## End an Individual Listing in Neto Manually

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **eBay** > **Active Listings**.
2. Select the listing you wish to end by clicking the red **Listing ID**.
3. Check the **Relist Options**. If it is set to relist when a listing ends, you may want to change this in the listing rules template before ending the listing.
  ![image4](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/EndandDeleteeBayListing4.05T5g00000v5LUbEAM.png)
4. Choose a **Reason** and select **End Listing**. Confirm that you want to end the listing in the pop-up box, and the listing will be ended immediately.
  ![image5](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/EndandDeleteeBayListing5.05T5g00000v5KaFEAU.png)

## End Listings in Bulk

To end multiple listings you should first check the **Relist Options** on your listing rules templates.

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **eBay** > **Listing Rule Templates**.
2. Select the template by clicking the **ID**.
3. On the **Categorisation & Listing Rules** tab, scroll down the the **Listing Rules** and check that the **Relist Options** are set to **Do Not Relist**. Change and **Save** if required.
  ![image6](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/EndandDeleteeBayListing6.05T5g00000v5KWhEAM.png)
4. Navigate to **eBay** > **Active Listings**.
5. Select the listings you wish to end by using the **Filters** and check boxes. At the bottom of the page **Select Ending Reason** and click **End Selected**.
  ![image7](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/EndandDeleteeBayListing7.05T5g00000v5LnsEAE.png)
6. Confirm you wish to end the listings in the pop-up box, and Neto will queue the listings to end.

**Please Note**: If you've selected 30 or more listings to end you will need to approve the processes as an additional safety measure.

## Approve Pending Listings

There is limit to the number of listings you can end before requiring approval (the default is 30). To approve a process to end listings:

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **eBay** > **Pending Approval**.
2. Filter by the **eBay Module** field, with the value `EndItem`.
3. Increase number of records per page to **250**.
4. Select all records add click on the **Mark Selected Approved** button at the bottom of the page, so that Neto can end the listings.

Once your listing(s) has been ended you can then mark them **Complete**. Completing a listing will prevent it automatically relisting (though new listings can still be made via rules templates settings.

## Delete eBay Listings

> **Warning:** Deleting a listing in Neto is permanent and not reversible.

Deleting a listing from Neto can only be done if the listing has been **Ended** and marked as **Completed**. It's not necessary to delete listings from your control panel.

### Delete an Individual Listing

1. Make sure that the product has been **Ended** and marked as **Completed**.
2. Once the listing has been marked as **Completed**, the delete option will appear and you can delete the listing.
  ![image9](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/EndandDeleteeBayListing9.05T5g00000v5LTZEA2.jpg)

### Delete Listings in Bulk

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **eBay** > **Ended Listings**.

> **Note:** If the products are already marked as **Completed**, you can go to the Completed page.

2. At the bottom of that page, you will need to mark the ended products as **Completed**.
  ![image10](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/EndandDeleteeBayListing10.05T5g00000v5LpAEAU.jpg)
3. Once the listings are completed, click on the **Delete selected** button to delete the listings.
