# Sell Used and Refurbished Items on eBay

To set up item condition on your products, so that they can be displayed on your eBay listings:

1. Add a [Custom Product Field](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/26-add-and-manage-products#add-product-categories-to-products-via-import) with the details below:
  ![image1](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/SellUsedandRefurbishedItemsoneBay1.05T5g00000v4asrEAA.png)
2. [Update the mapping](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/782-ebay-field-mapping) on your Listing Rule Templates so that your custom product field matchs the **eBay Item Condition** field.
  ![image2](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/SellUsedandRefurbishedItemsoneBay2.05T5g00000v4b4qEAA.png)
3. Use the [Data Import Wizard](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/668-product-import-wizard) to populate your custom product field with their corresponding values from the table above.
4. [Revise the listings](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1082-revise-active-ebay-listings).
