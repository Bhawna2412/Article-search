# Add eBay Item specifics

Specifics can be added to an eBay Listing template however are not mandatory. Specifics are good for items that require additional information, such as ratings (for movies) or materials (for clothing).

## Add Product Specifics on the Rules Template

> **Note:** Item specifics that are required or recommended by eBay do not have to be added to your listing rules template. As long as they are on the product, and the product has a valid value, they will be sent to eBay automatically.

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **eBay** > **Listing Rules Templates**.
2. Click the template link to edit the template details.
  ![image1](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/AddeBayItemspecifics1.05T5g00000sxcwnEAA.png)
3. Click on the **Categorisation & Listing Rules** tab.
  ![image2](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/AddeBayItemspecifics2.05T5g00000sxc2kEAA.png)
4. In the **Specifics** section, click the **Add New Primary Category Specific** button.
5. Add a specific **Type**. To use the value from the specific on the product, set the first dropdown menu to **Static Value**, and leave the value blank. Alternatively, select **Column Name** and select a product field (including [custom product field](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/26-add-and-manage-products#add-product-categories-to-products-via-import)) as the value, and the value of that field on a product will be pushed to eBay when the product lists.
  ![image3](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/AddeBayItemspecifics3.05T5g00000sxeI9EAI.png)
6. Click the **Add New Primary Category Specific** button again to add more specifics to your rules template.
7. In the example below, the Specific/Value is **Material** and **Plastic**. This will appear on all eligible listings. However, if the specific value was blank and on the product page **Metal**, **Metal** will appear instead.
  ![image4](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/AddeBayItemspecifics4.05T5g00000sxcGIEAY.png)

  The specifics listed here will display on the eBay listing above the description (as per the example below).

  ![image5](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/AddeBayItemspecifics5.05T5g00000sxeIxEAI.png)

## Add Specifics to a Product

1. In your Neto control panel, navigate **Products** > **View Products**.
  ![image6](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/AddeBayItemspecifics6.05T5g00000sxeJMEAY.png)
2. Click on the relevant product to edit your product details.
  ![image7](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/AddeBayItemspecifics7.05T5g00000sxeJWEAY.png)
3. Within the menu, click the **Specifics / Filters **link.
4. Add a **Specific Type** or **Specific Value**.
5. Click the **Add Specific** button.
  ![image8](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/AddeBayItemspecifics8.05T5g00000sxeJlEAI.png)

  In the example above, the Rating **MA15** is added. In the eBay listing rules template, the **Specific **will be listed as **Rating** and the **Value** blank.

## Hide a Specific on your Website/eBay

1. In your Neto control panel, navigate **Products** > **Product Specifics**.
  ![image9](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/AddeBayItemspecifics9.05T5g00000sxeJiEAI.png)
2. Click on the relevant **Name** link to hide.
3. Select **Yes** on either the **Hide on Website** or **Hide from eBay** dropdowns.
4. Click the **Save** button to complete.
