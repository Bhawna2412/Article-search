# eBay Listing Compliance

eBay compliance errors give you more visibility over issues with your listings. At a glance you'll be able to see if there are problems with images, listing data, categories, or product identifiers.

For steps on how to resolve these issues, please see our [eBay Errors](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/690-troubleshoot-ebay-error-codes) troubleshooting guide.

## Turn on Compliance Settings

For Neto to display compliance errors on your listings, your account needs to be re-authorised through eBay.

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **eBay** > **Setup & Tools**.
2. Click on **Store Manager** to display the list of eBay accounts connected to Neto.
3. In the **Tokens** column, look for any **eBay Authorisation Token**'s which are invalid. These stores need to be re-authenticated, so click on the store name to see the details page.
  ![image1](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/eBayListingCompliance1.05T5g00000v4aY6EAI.png)
4. Click **Generate New eBay Authorisation Token**.
  ![image2](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/eBayListingCompliance2.05T5g00000v4ZG9EAM.png)
5. Click the **Click Here to Generate Your Unique eBay Token** link and you'll be taken to an eBay page to login.
6. Log in to eBay, and click the button to allow Neto access.

Your account is now authorised and compliance errors will appear on listings.

## Check Compliance Errors

Provided you have completed the authorisation steps above, you will now be able to see any compliance issues with your listings.

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **eBay** > **Active Listings**.
2. Expand the filters, and change the **Compliance** drop down box to **Has Errors**. Now click **Apply Advanced Filter**.
  ![image3](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/eBayListingCompliance3.05T5g00000v4acXEAQ.png)
3. In the **Compliance** column, click **Show Errors** for each listing to see more information about the compliance issues.
  ![image4](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/eBayListingCompliance4.05T5g00000v4a7QEAQ.png)

  Variation products may have an error on one of the child products, so they will instead have a **View Errors on Listing** link. Click this link to open the listing, so you can see which more information about the issue.
4. To resolve the issues, please consult the [eBay - Troubleshoot Error Codes](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/690-troubleshoot-ebay-error-codes) help article for instructions.
