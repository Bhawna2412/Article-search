# Troubleshoot eBay Error Codes

When you add or revise a listing to eBay, you may receive a warning or error message.

- A warning message will not prevent your listing from being added or revised
- An error may prevent a new listing or stop a revision.

[Image: image1]

You can also access these by navigating to **eBay** > **Process log** and filtering by the criteria you are looking for.

[Image: image2]

The easiest way to find a process is to filter by the SKU that you are looking for.

When you click the magnifying glass it expands out all messages/warnings and errors that may have occured for that SKU.

[Image: image3]
