# Go Live With eBay

To get the best out of the Neto eBay integration, you will want your Neto account to be listing, revising and managing the sales orders for your eBay account.

To ensure that you have everything setup from start to finish, we recommend going through our [Launch Guides](https://www.netohq.com/support/s/knowledge#category=Support__c:Launch_eBay_Store__c) to configure the integration.

> **Note:** Neto Partners can take care of the set up for you. Find an approved Neto partner [here](https://www.netohq.com/experts).

## Setup Checklist

Here's a quick checklist of each component you will need to configure, and successfully trade through Neto:

- [Products set up](https://www.netohq.com/support/s/knowledge#category=Support__c:Add_Products__c) in Neto and [linked to any existing listings](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/781-list-products-on-ebay#in-bulk).
- [Listing Rules templates configured](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/783-listing-rules-template-details) (including any [Variation Templates](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/781-list-products-on-ebay#individually)).
- Category mapping (Categories are very important to listing rankings).
- [Shipping options set up](https://www.netohq.com/support/s/knowledge#category=Support__c:Configure_Shipping__c) within Neto, so that orders can be processed.
- [Payment methods set up](https://www.netohq.com/support/s/knowledge#category=Support__c:Configure_Payments__c) in Neto, for reconciliation with your accounting.
- [Payment](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/779-ebay-payment-methods) and shipping mapping configured, for smooth order processing.
- Configure tracking numbers, so that when an order is dispatched the customer can track it through eBay.
- (Optional) Set up [Business Policies](https://sellercentre.ebay.com.au/ebay-for-business/business-policies) in your eBay account and [enable them in Neto](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1087-manage-your-ebay-integration#9).
- (Optional) [eBay listing design setup in Neto](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/785-ebay-listing-design-images#set-up-a-free-ebay-design-templates).

## List & Revise

Once the above is complete, you will need to list your products or revise your active listings. Neto will always revise over the top of what you have in eBay, so if you are using listing rules templates in Neto on your active listings, always remember to make updates to products from within Neto to push them to your eBay listings.

List new products from Neto:

- [List a Single Product](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/781-list-products-on-ebay)
- [List Products in Bulk](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/781-list-products-on-ebay)
- [List Variation Products on eBay](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/781-list-products-on-ebay#individually)

## Update Existing Listings

Update existing listings so they are managed through Neto:

- [Apply rules templates to existing listings](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/781-list-products-on-ebay#rules-template)

## Turn on Order Management

The final steps is to turn on order management from the eBay Settings page in Neto. This can be done by navigating to **eBay** > **Setup & Tools** > **Settings** and clearing the **Enable Manual eBay Order Management** checkbox, then click **Save Changes**.

This will start the processes to import orders from eBay. You will then be able to follow the Pick, Pack and Dispatch order processing method to complete your eBay Orders, which will update their status in eBay.
