# Add Advertisements to Facebook Shop Home Page Carousel

**Tip**: Link your Facebook shop to Neto by following the steps in [this article](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1060-set-up-a-facebook-or-instagram-product-feed).

1. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Webstore** > **Advertising Campaigns**
2. Select **Add New Advertising Campaign**
  [Image: Screen Shot 2024-09-26 at 10.54.13 am.png]
3. Under **Banner, Carousel Slide or Text Advert** select **Add New**.
  [Image: Screen Shot 2024-09-26 at 10.55.17 am.png]
4. In the **Group** field select **Facebook.**
  [Image: Screen Shot 2024-09-26 at 10.58.11 am.png]

  This group will be used for controlling the adverts on your Facebook shop.
5. Add your advert details to the campaign that has now been created. Using the **Ad Builder** upload an image for the advert. You can also add text, however it is not required.
  **Tip**:If you upload an **Image** that is larger than the space provided for the image on your website the system will resize the image to fit the space.
6. In the **Select Location For Ad To Appear** drop down box, choose **Web Page** > **Home** and then click the **+ Add** button, and enter a **Date Start** and **Date End** for your campaign.
  If you don't have the web page option, choose **Information Page** instead.

  [Image: Screen Shot 2024-09-26 at 11.24.29 am.jpg]
7. Click the **Save** button to complete the configuration.
