## Set up the Shopping.com Add-on

Compare millions of products from hundreds of merchants all in one place. Shop, compare and save time and money!

Neto uses data feeds to send product information to Shopping.com to update your products through the shopping comparison website.

**Tip**: Neto partners can set this up for you. [Visit the job request portal](https://www.netohq.com/job-request-portal) for a quote.

[Image: image2]

1. Sign up for an account through the [http://www.shopping.com/](http://www.shopping.com) website.
2. Copy your Account ID. You'll need to add it into Neto shortly.
3. In your Neto control panel navigate to **Other** > **Shopping Comparison Feeds**.
4. Locate Shopping.com and click the **Install** button.
  [Image: image3]
5. Add your Shopping.com **Account ID**.
  [Image: image4]
6. You’ll be provided with a **Feed URL**. Provide this URL to Shopping.com. This is a link to a file that contains a list of your products in the required format.
  [Image: image5]
7. You can modify the Feed URL, filtering products that you don’t wish to push to Shopping.com. To do this, click the **Edit** button.
  [Image: image6]
8. Scroll down to the **File Builder** section and click the **Filter** tab to make products, categories, pricing and other changes. Scroll down and click **Save Changes**.
  [Image: image7]
9. To make changes to the feed, simply scroll back up the top and click the **Generate Data File** button and click **Download Data File** button to review the results of your changes.
  [Image: image8]
