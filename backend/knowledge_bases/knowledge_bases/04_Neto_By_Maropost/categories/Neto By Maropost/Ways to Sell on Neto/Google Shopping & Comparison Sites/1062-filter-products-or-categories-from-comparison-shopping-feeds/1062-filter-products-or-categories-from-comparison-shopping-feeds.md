# Filter Products or Categories from Comparison Shopping Feeds

The search functionality allows you to filter by custom fields, product category, price, status and much more.

1. To get started, in the **Sales Channels** section navigate to **Other **> **Shopping Feeds**.
2. On the already installed feed, click the **Edit** button to open the template. This is where we will be making suitable changes.
  ![image1](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/FilterProductsorCategoriesfromComparisonShoppingFeeds1.05T5g00000w6UweEAE.png)
3. Scroll down to the **File Builder** and click on the **Filter** tab. Here you can filter products you want to display on your feed. Set the filters according to your specific needs and scroll down the bottom and press **Save Changes**.
  ![image2](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/FilterProductsorCategoriesfromComparisonShoppingFeeds2.05T5g00000w6UwoEAE.png)
4. Scroll back to the top and on the top right hand side click the **Generate Data File** button to refresh the data feed.
  ![image3](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/FilterProductsorCategoriesfromComparisonShoppingFeeds3.05T5g00000w6UO9EAM.png)
5. Once this is complete, click the **Download Data File** button to review the filters to ensure the filters have been applied correctly.
