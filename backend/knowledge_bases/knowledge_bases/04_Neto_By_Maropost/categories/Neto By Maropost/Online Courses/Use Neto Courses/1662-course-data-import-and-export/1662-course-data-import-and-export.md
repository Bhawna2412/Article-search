# Course: Data Import and Export

You can leverage data import and export capabilities to efficiently manage bulk product updates, inventory synchronization, and data migration operations across your B2B ecommerce platform. This enables you to streamline catalog management, reduce manual data entry errors, and maintain data consistency between Neto and external systems such as accounting software, inventory management tools, and supplier databases.

Mastering data import and export functionality helps B2B merchants improve operational efficiency by automating routine data updates and enabling integration with third-party business systems. When you understand both simple and complex import/export processes, you can typically reduce time spent on manual data management, maintain accurate product information across channels, and support business scalability by efficiently handling large catalog operations.
