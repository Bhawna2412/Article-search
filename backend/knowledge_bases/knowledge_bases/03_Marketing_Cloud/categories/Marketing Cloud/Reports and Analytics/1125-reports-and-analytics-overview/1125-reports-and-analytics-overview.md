# Reports and Analytics: Overview

You can leverage Marketing Cloud's comprehensive reporting features to gain actionable insights from your email campaigns and make data-driven decisions that improve marketing effectiveness. These analytics tools help identify which campaigns drive the highest engagement, track customer behavior patterns, and measure the ROI of your email marketing efforts across different audience segments.

When properly utilized, these reporting capabilities enable you to optimize campaign performance by understanding what resonates with your subscribers, leading to better engagement rates and more successful email marketing outcomes.

Maropost Cloud's advanced reporting features can be used to generate a variety of reports to track performance and get analytical data.

These reports can give you valuable insight into how your campaigns are performing, the results of your A|B split tests, how contacts are engaging with your sent campaigns, as well as other important information.

In the Navigation, go to ANALYTICS, and select the desired report.

### Campaign Reports

The campaign reports help you track the performance of your email campaigns. Each report generated for an email campaign contains the following tabs:

- **Dashboard**: Provides insights on how the email campaign is performing. You can see the delivery statistics (such as delivery rate, bounce rate), user engagement ( such as opens, clicks), revenue, and other important metrics.
- **Overlay**: Provides a click-map view of the email content, displaying the links along with total clicks and percentage of the total clicks for each link.
- **ISP**: Provides insight on the performance of the email campaign based on Internet Service Providers (ISPs) of the recipients' email addresses.
- **Details**: Provides details of the email campaign.

For more information, see [**Email Campaign Reports**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/846-email-campaign-reports?utm_source=community-search&utm_medium=organic-search&utm_term=Email+Campaign+Reports).

### Recurring Campaign Reports

The recurring campaign reports help you track the performance of your recurring campaigns. You can click the eye icon against each recurring campaign report and see the individual occurrence when the recurring campaign has been sent.

### A/B Campaign Reports

The A|B campaign reports show the delivery and response data for each group of the A|B test, as well as those of the final campaign sent based on the 'Winning' criteria.

For more information, see [**A/B Campaign Reports**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/632-a-b-campaign-reports?utm_source=community-search&utm_medium=organic-search&utm_term=A%2FB+Campaign+Reports).

### Custom Reports

The Custom Reports are summary reports that you can generate based on your choices. You have the option to filter the data to be included in the custom report if you want to focus on a narrower audience than those included in the original target audience. You can also set the option to have the report sent to any number of email addresses and also to have the report generated on a recurring basis.

For more information, see [**Custom Reports: Overview**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/994-custom-reports-overview?utm_source=community-search&utm_medium=organic-search&utm_term=Custom+Reports%3A+Overview).

Currently, the following custom reports are supported:

- **Custom Campaign Reports** - To learn how to create a custom Campaign report, see [**Custom Email Campaign Report**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/835-custom-email-campaign-report?utm_source=community-search&utm_medium=organic-search&utm_term=Custom+Email+Campaign+Report).
- **Custom SMS Campaign Reports** - To learn how to create an SMS Campaign report, see [**Custom SMS Campaign Report**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/841-custom-sms-campaign-report?utm_source=community-search&utm_medium=organic-search&utm_term=Custom+SMS+Campaign+Report).
- **Deliverability Reports **- To learn how to create a Deliverability report, see [**Deliverability Report**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/839-deliverability-report?utm_source=community-search&utm_medium=organic-search&utm_term=Deliverability+Report).
- **Growth and Attrition Reports** - To learn how to create a Growth and Attrition report, see [**Growth & Attrition Report: Overview**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/838-growth-attrition-report-overview?utm_source=community-search&utm_medium=organic-search&utm_term=Growth+%26+Attrition+Report%3A+Overview) and [**Creating Growth & Attrition Reports**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/837-creating-growth-attrition-reports?utm_source=community-search&utm_medium=organic-search&utm_term=Creating+Growth+%26+Attrition+Reports).

### Test Campaign Reports

The Test Campaign Reports show the same information as the Campaign Reports. The main **difference** between them is that the test campaign cannot be sent to more than** 20 contacts.**

For more information, see [**Test Campaign Reports**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/631-test-campaign-reports?utm_source=community-search&utm_medium=organic-search&utm_term=Test+Campaign+Reports).

### Website Reports

The Website Reports are displayed only if you are using Marketing Cloud's [**Web Tracking**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Introduction-to-Web-Tracking) feature. These reports show you details on web pages that have been viewed and how many times they have been viewed. The report also includes drill-down reports showing which contacts have viewed which pages and how many times they have viewed them.

For more information, see [**Website Reports**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/630-website-reports?utm_source=community-search&utm_medium=organic-search&utm_term=website+reports).

### Journey Reports

The Journey Reports provide you with detailed information on how a particular journey is performing. On the index page, you can see all the journey reports along with their important statistics (such as sent, delivered, opens, clicks, bounces, total revenue, and last updated).

By clicking the eye icon against a journey report, you can see the email campaigns within the journey. You can further click the campaign names to see the corresponding campaign reports.

For more information, see [**Journey Reports**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/629-journey-reports?utm_source=community-search&utm_medium=organic-search&utm_term=Journey+Reports).

### Transactional Campaign Reports

The Transactional Campaign Reports help you track the performance of your transactional campaigns. Each report generated for a transactional campaign contains the following three tabs:

- **Dashboard**: Provides insights on how the transactional campaign is performing. You can see the delivery statistics (such as delivery rate, bounce rate), user engagement ( such as opens, clicks), revenue, and other important metrics.
- **ISP**: Provides insight on the performance of the email campaign based on Internet Service Providers (ISPs) of the recipients' email addresses.
- **Details**: Provides details of the transactional campaign.

For more information, see [**Transactional Campaign Reports**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/628-transactional-campaign-reports?utm_source=community-search&utm_medium=organic-search&utm_term=Transactional+Campaign+Reports).

### Log Inspector

The Log Inspector report shows the failed API calls.
