# Email Campaign Reports

You can access comprehensive email campaign performance data through Campaign Reports to make informed decisions about your email marketing strategy. These reports help optimize campaign effectiveness by providing detailed insights into delivery, engagement, and subscriber behavior patterns, enabling you to identify successful tactics and areas for improvement across your email marketing programs.

Campaign Reports serve as your central hub for performance optimization, helping you understand which campaigns drive the best results and why, so you can replicate successful strategies and refine underperforming elements to maximize email marketing ROI.

The **Email Campaign Reports** help you track the performance of your email campaigns.

### Accessing the Report

In the Navigation, go to ANALYTICS, and select Campaign Reports. The Campaign Reports index page displays all campaign reports along with a quick glance at the campaign's performance metrics. Click the name of a campaign to see the detailed report.

> **Note:** You can also see the same campaign report on the Email Campaigns index page (Navigation > CAMPAIGNS > Email Campaigns) by clicking the campaign name.

### Report Structure

The Campaign's Report includes four tabs, each displaying specific information about the campaign and its performance:

- **Dashboard** - An overview of the delivery and response data for the campaign. See [**Campaign Report - Dashboard**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/845-campaign-report-dashboard?utm_source=community-search&utm_medium=organic-search&utm_term=+Campaign+Report+-+Dashboard).
- **Overlay** - A click-map view of the email content displaying which links were clicked and the total clicks on each link. See [**Campaign Report - Overlay**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/844-campaign-report-overlay?utm_source=community-search&utm_medium=organic-search&utm_term=Campaign+Report+-+Overlay.).
- **ISP** - A delivery and response breakdown of the Top 25 ISPs based on the campaign recipient's email addresses. See [**Campaign Report - ISP**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/843-campaign-report-isp?utm_source=community-search&utm_medium=organic-search&utm_term=+Campaign+Report+-+ISP).
- **Details** - A detailed summary of the campaign. See [**Campaign Report - Details**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/842-campaign-report-details?utm_source=community-search&utm_medium=organic-search&utm_term=+Campaign+Report+-+Details).
