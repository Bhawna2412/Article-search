# Transactional Campaign Reports

Transactional Campaign Reports enable you to measure the effectiveness of your automated email communications and identify improvement opportunities in real-time delivery performance. This helps ensure your critical business messages like order confirmations, shipping notifications, and password resets consistently reach customers when they need them most.

When properly monitored, transactional email analytics typically perform better than traditional campaigns because recipients expect these messages, leading to higher engagement rates and better deliverability outcomes for your overall email program.

The **Transactional Campaign Reports** provide you with information on how your transactional campaigns are performing.

To learn about transactional campaigns, see [**Sending Transactional Campaigns via SMTP**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Sending-Transactional-Campaigns-via-SMTP).

The Transactional Campaign Reports index page lists the reports generated for all your transactional campaigns. To access the index page, go to **Analytics** from the **Navigation** panel and select **Transactional Reports**.

![Transactional_Campaign_Reports.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/05T5g00000BkWz2.05T5g00000BkWz2EAF.png)
The index page shows all the major performance metrics corresponding to each Transactional Campaign report. Use the date filter in the top-left corner to search for reports generated during a specific timespan.

You can click the name of a transactional report to navigate to the dashboard of the detailed report. Each report generated for a transactional campaign contains the following three tabs:

- **DASHBOARD:** Provides insights on how the Transactional Campaign is performing. You can see the delivery statistics (such as delivery rate, bounce rate), user engagement ( such as opens, clicks), revenue, and other important metrics. To learn more, you can see the [**Campaign Report - Dashboard**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Campaign-Report-Dashboard), which follows the same reporting structure and metrics.
- **ISP:** Provides insight on the performance of the email campaign based on Internet Service Providers (ISPs) of the recipients' email addresses. To learn more, see [**Campaign Report - ISP**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Campaign-Report-ISP).
- **DETAILS:** Provides a summary of the information specific to the transactional campaign. To learn more, see [**Campaign Report - Details**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Campaign-Report-Details).
