# A/B Campaign Reports

A/B Campaign Reports enable you to measure which version of your email campaigns drives better engagement and conversions, helping optimize future campaigns through data-driven testing insights. This helps maximize email marketing ROI by showing definitively which subject lines, content variations, or send times perform best with your specific audience.

When properly analyzed, A/B test results can substantially improve engagement by revealing which campaign elements resonate most with subscribers, enabling you to apply winning strategies to future campaigns for consistently better performance.

The **A/B Campaign Reports** show the delivery and response data for each group of the A/B test, along with the final campaign sent based on the winning criteria.

### Accessing the Report

From the **Navigation** panel, go to **Analytics,** and select **A/B Campaign Reports**. The A/B Campaign Reports index page displays all A/B campaign reports along with a quick performance summary. Click the name of a campaign to see the detailed report.

> **Note:** You can also see the same campaign report on the Email Campaigns index page (Navigation > Campaigns > Email Campaigns) by clicking the campaign name.

### Report Structure

The report shows the results from all the test groups in the A/B campaign side-by-side. If a winner has been chosen, then the split campaign is highlighted as the winner.

![User-added image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/ABCampaignReport.05T5g00000e4CXyEAM.png)
For each test group, the following engagement metrics are highlighted on top, followed by more specific details:

- **Total Sent**: Total emails sent to the test group.
- **Total Opens**: Total open events registered. You can click the displayed count to see a detailed open report. You'll find more information about the open reports in the [**Campaign Report - Dashboard**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/845-campaign-report-dashboard?utm_source=community-search&utm_medium=organic-search&utm_term=Campaign+Report+-+Dashboard).
- **Total Clicks**: Total links clicked. You can click the displayed count to see a more detailed report.
- **Click-to-Open**: Calculated as the number of unique clicks divided by the number of unique opens.

Scroll down to see more analytical data and other details related to the A/B campaign. You can also click the metrics to see their respective drill-down report.

> **Note:** When you have many split groups, you can scroll horizontally to access data from all the split groups. The data labels remain static when you scroll, so that you can see and compare the data from all your split groups.

The campaign information is available under the Overview section.

![User-added image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/ABReportOverview.05T5g00000bCTKTEA4.png)
The order and revenue information specific to the campaign is available under the Conversions section.

![User-added image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/ABReportConversions.05T5g00000bCTP7EAO.png)
More details of the campaign (such as lists, segments, and campaign tags used in the campaign) can be found under the Details section.

![User-added image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/ABReportDetails.05T5g00000bCTPCEA4.png)
