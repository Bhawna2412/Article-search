# SMS Webinar

SMS marketing can substantially improve engagement rates compared to traditional email campaigns, with messages typically opened within minutes of delivery. This webinar helps optimize your mobile messaging strategy by covering compliance requirements, list-building techniques, and proven best practices that can enhance subscriber interaction while maintaining regulatory compliance.

You can leverage the strategies and techniques covered in this comprehensive SMS marketing training to streamlines your workflow and improves operational efficiency across your mobile marketing campaigns.

**Webinar Recorded On:** February 4th, 2021

Listen to the host. Madeleine Kanoza (Product Marketing Manager at Maropost) runs you through how to make the most from your SMS marketing campaigns.
