# Introduction to SMS Campaigns

You can leverage SMS campaigns to reach customers through a highly effective mobile marketing channel that typically achieves higher open rates than email communications. SMS messages are usually opened within minutes of being received, making this an extremely valuable channel for time-sensitive communications and immediate customer engagement.

When properly implemented with appropriate compliance measures, SMS campaigns can substantially improve engagement while maintaining regulatory adherence and subscriber satisfaction.

Maropost Marketing Cloud enables you to broadcast text messages to the list of contacts who have given their consent to receive promotional messages from you.

SMS campaigns are a highly effective tool that you can leverage to reach your target audience. Statistics show that SMS messages have the highest open rates of all marketing channels and are usually opened within 3 minutes of being received. This means it can be an extremely valuable channel of communication for marketers.

However, the disruptiveness of an SMS message also means that marketers must be very careful with the content sent to ensure that it's valuable for end contacts, while also ensuring compliance with regulations.

### Before You Begin - Check Your Compliance

In order to activate the SMS Messaging feature, you will first need to complete a pre-screening assessment to ensure that your SMS marketing program is in compliance with the regulations. To learn about the compliance regulations, see [**Introduction to SMS Compliance**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Introduction-to-SMS-Compliance).

### Managing SMS Campaigns

The SMS Campaigns index page is the starting page from which you can view and manage the previously created SMS campaigns and also create new SMS campaigns. In Navigation, go to CAMPAIGNS, and then click SMS Campaigns.

> **Note:** If you are unable to see the SMS Campaigns index page, please contact your Customer Success Manager to start the activation process.

![User-added image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/SMSCampaignsIndexPage.05T5g00000jDk24EAC.png)
You can create a new campaign from scratch by clicking the Compose Your Message button (to learn more, see [**Creating SMS Campaigns**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Creating-SMS-Campaigns)) or duplicate it from a previously created campaign. Once a campaign is sent, you cannot make changes to it; however, you can edit or delete a campaign that is in Draft status.

To know how you can manage your contacts for sending SMS campaigns, see [**SMS Contact Lists**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/SMS-Contact-Lists). You can find important details and track the performance of your SMS campaign through its dashboard. For more information, see [**SMS Campaign Dashboard**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/SMS-Campaign-Dashboard).
