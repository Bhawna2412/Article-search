# Contact Lists Overview Page

You can access comprehensive contact list analytics to make data-driven decisions about your email marketing campaigns, leveraging detailed engagement metrics, revenue tracking, and insights into subscriber behavior. This significantly improves campaign effectiveness by helping you understand which lists generate the most engagement and revenue, allowing you to optimize your targeting strategies and improve subscriber interaction.

When properly utilized, the contact list overview provides essential intelligence for audience organization and targeting, helping you identify high-performing segments and automate routine tasks while maintaining organized subscription management across your email marketing ecosystem.

This page provides a comprehensive 360° view of a contact list, which primarily includes customer engagement and response metrics from contacts present in the list.

This page allows you to manage contacts in the list, refresh the count, and view key statistics about the contacts' responses to your campaigns.

![Image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000Y4WuUMAV.jpg)

## Accessing the Contact List Overview Page

To access this page, you need to first visit the **Contact Lists** section under **Audience** from the Navigation panel. From the contact list index page, click on a contact name whose overview page you would like to see.

![Image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000Y4Wv8MAF.jpg)

## Breakdown of Contact List Overview Page

### Contact List Card

The contact list card displays all the details (such as name, modification dates, list type, brand affiliations, etc.) associated with a contact list. You can edit and delete the list by clicking on the vertical ellipsis (⋮).

![Image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000Y4WvIMAV.jpg)

While editing the list, you can select or clear the contact list fields and rearrange their order. Any changes made to the list arrangement are applied to all the contact lists.

![Image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000Y4WvJMAV.jpg)

By default, all contact lists are selected as **Normal** type, i.e., contacts in a list receive campaigns that are scheduled for them. However, if a group of contacts is found to be dormant or inactive with your brands, you can add them to a suppression list by selecting the **Suppressed List** type option. This ensures that no contact in that list receives future communications from Marketing Cloud.

![Image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000Y4WwBMAV.jpg)

> **Note:** **Note:** Contacts marked in a suppressed list will still receive those emails if their campaigns were scheduled prior to being added to the list. To learn more about such lists, read our [**Suppression (Do Not Mail) Lists**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/1370-suppression-do-not-mail-lists) article.

![Image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000Y4WwaMAF.jpg)
If you wish to give contacts an option to subscribe/unsubscribe from a list that receives promotional messages, you can enable the **Add to Unsubscribe Page** toggle.

![Image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000Y4WwpMAF.jpg)

### Customer Interaction Cards

The interaction cards provide an overall tally of contacts currently present in the list and display the overall earnings generated by them.

- **Total Contacts:** It indicates the total number of contacts in a list.
- 
- **Total Unsubscribers:** It displays the total number of contacts who have opted out of your email and SMS campaign subscription.
- **Total Revenue:** It reveals the total sales conversion generated by the list towards your business.

![Image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000Y4WwuMAF.jpg)

Additionally, we offer seamless bulk operations through the **Export** and **Import** tabs. Such functionalities enhance automation and reduce mapping errors to a great extent. If you find any discrepancies in the count, you can refresh the list by clicking on the **Refresh** tab.

![Image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000Y4WybMAF.jpg)

### Timeline

The timeline provides a detailed account of each contact’s interaction with your email and SMS campaigns, listed in chronological order from newest to oldest.

Before examining engagement metrics at the email and SMS levels, you can use the **Filters** menu to view the overall activity based on your selection. Click on **Apply** to save the changes made.

![Image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000Y4WygMAF.jpg)

The timeline is subdivided into two sections:

**Email:** Important email metrics such as **Sent, Scheduled, Soft,** and **Hard Bounce rates** are shown here. In addition, details such as **Total Orders** and **Items Purchased** are also displayed.

![Image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000Y4WyvMAF.jpg)

**SMS:** This section highlights key SMS stats, such as the number of campaigns sent and upcoming campaigns scheduled for contacts in the list. The campaign volume timeline ranges from **7 days to 90 days,** and even a **Year To Date** range is available at your disposal.

![Image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000Y4Wz0MAF.jpg)

Apart from general timelines, you can check the **Historic Timeline** section to view current email and SMS campaigns sent to contacts in the list. You can further analyze the sent campaign by clicking on the **Details** tab.

![Image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000Y4WYUMA3.jpg)

### Automated FTP Imports

This option enables you to upload contacts to a list using our FTP server. To use this option, you must first set up the FTP imports on the Connections page. There is no file size limit for FTP upload. To learn more, see [**Using FTP Import**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/7-using-ftp-import).

![Image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000Y4X0cMAF.jpg)

### Cart Integration

This section allows you to link contacts in the list with a product or any other entity of a third-party e-commerce platform that is integrated into your Marketing Cloud account. If you haven't linked before, click on the edit icon or the add button. Under the integration slider, select the platform and save the changes made to it.

![Image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000Y4X0mMAF.jpg)
