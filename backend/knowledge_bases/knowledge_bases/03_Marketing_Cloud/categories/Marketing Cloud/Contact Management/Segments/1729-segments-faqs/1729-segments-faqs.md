# Segments FAQs

You can utilize advanced segmentation features to create more targeted email campaigns, which significantly improve engagement rates and campaign effectiveness. Understanding segment functionality helps ensure your marketing messages reach the most relevant audience segments while optimizing your Marketing Cloud platform's powerful contact organization capabilities.

Effective audience segmentation enables more relevant messaging by allowing you to group contacts based on behaviors, preferences, and characteristics. When properly implemented, strategic segmentation can substantially improve your email marketing performance by delivering personalized content to targeted subscriber groups.

**Q1)** **What does the "Include All Active Contacts" checkbox do?**

If you click the information icon (i) next to the **Include All Active Contacts** checkbox, you will see that it will pull in all contacts who are not on any list but are active in the account. This means it can pull in transactional contacts or contacts who are unsubscribed.

**Q2)** **Why are my products not showing up in the segment?**

Products only appear in the Segments drop-down after the information has been pushed to Maropost.

**Q3)** **When do "Segment Counts" refresh?**

Segments refresh when they are in use. The number in the Maropost UI updates in real-time with the following details:

- Being Sent To
- Segment Event in a Journey
- Being referenced in a Journey/Campaign/Segment/API Call
