# ReCAPTCHA in Signup Forms

You can enable reCAPTCHA on your signup forms to protect your contact lists from spam and fake subscriptions. This helps ensure higher list quality by filtering out bot submissions, which typically improves your email engagement rates and protects your sender reputation with email providers.

Clean contact lists with genuine subscribers typically achieve better deliverability and more meaningful engagement metrics, making your email campaigns more effective and cost-efficient.

You can enable reCAPTCHA for your Signup Forms as an additional layer of security from spam and abuse. Currently, we offer Google reCAPTCHA services that help distinguish between human and automated access to websites.

When creating a Signup Form, upon selecting "**Enable reCAPTCHA,**" you are required to provide the **Site key** and **Secret key** values. These keys are generated for each reCAPTCHA created at the Google reCAPTCHA website and are unique to one or more registered domains. You are required to create a Google reCAPTCHA and copy-paste the keys from there.

> **Note:** The Site key is used to render the reCAPTCHA on a page, and the Secret key is used for server-side validation.

Perform the following steps to render a reCAPTCHA in Signup Forms (includes steps to create Google reCAPTCHA):

> **Note:** You must have a Google account.

1. In step 1 of the Signup Form creation flow, select **Enable reCAPTCHA**.
2. 
  In the expanded panel below, click the "**Get Google reCAPTCHA Site Key and Secret Key for your account**" link.

  ![Enable_reCAPTCHA.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/05T5g00000BkWyi.05T5g00000BkWyiEAF.png)
3. On the "**Register a new site**" page, enter a label name for the reCAPTCHA**.**
4. In reCAPTCHA type, select **reCAPTCHA v2** and then we recommend selecting the "**I'm not a robot**"** **checkbox.
5. Add the domains that you want to register for the reCAPTCHA. You can use the reCAPTCHA for the domains and sub-domains added here.
6. Optionally, you can add more owners for the reCAPTCHA. Owners have access to the reports and receive alerts.
7. Select the "**Accept the reCAPTCHA Terms of Service**" checkbox. Click **Submit**.
8. Copy the Site key and Secret key to the fields in step 1 of the Signup Form.
