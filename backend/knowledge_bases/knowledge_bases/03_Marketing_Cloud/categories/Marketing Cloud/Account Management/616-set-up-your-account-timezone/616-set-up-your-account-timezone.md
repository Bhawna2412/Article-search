# Set Up Your Account Timezone

You can configure your account's timezone to ensure your email campaigns are scheduled and delivered at optimal times for your target audience. This helps optimize campaign effectiveness by sending messages when your subscribers are most likely to engage, while also improving your reporting accuracy by aligning campaign data with your business hours and local market expectations.

When properly configured, timezone settings streamline your workflow by automatically handling campaign scheduling across different regions and ensuring your analytics reflect accurate timing for performance analysis. This approach helps ensure your marketing messages reach recipients at the most effective times for your business objectives.

Maropost Marketing Cloud users can select the timezone for their account. This enables you to set your account timezone to your local or preferred timezone and thereby manage campaign scheduling and reporting more efficiently.

> **Note:** **Note:** If you are a new Marketing Cloud user or have not previously updated your account timezone, the default timezone is **EST (UTC-04:00)**.

## Selecting a Timezone for Your Account

Your account timezone is displayed on the top panel of the application UI. You can click it to navigate to the Preferences page of the Maropost Identity app, where you can select a different timezone.

You can also access the Identity app from the User Profile drop-down menu. See [**Using Maropost Identity App**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Using-Maropost-Identity-App) to learn about the app and its use.

![TimezoneDisplay.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000HmMJJMA3.jpg)

On the Preferences page, select your desired timezone from the **Timezone** drop-down menu and save the changes.

![PreferencesPage - Timezone.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000HmMJYMA3.jpg)

## Exceptions to Account Timezone

Following is the list of features/functionalities that don’t support local timezone settings yet and still function based on Eastern Standard Time (EST):

- 
- 
  **Custom Reports**
The custom reports are created based on EST.

  ![CustomReports.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000HmMYYMA3.jpg)
- 
  **Date Filters**
The date selection filters across the UI are shown as per EST.

  ![DateFilters.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000HmMjMMAV.jpg)
- 
  **Export Functionality**
The export functionality across the application works as per EST.

  ![Export.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000HmMjCMAV.jpg)
- **Send Time in Recurring Campaigns**
When scheduling a recurring campaign, your day and time selection for sending the campaign is considered in EST.
  ![RecurringCampaign.jpg](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000HmMixMAF.jpg)
- 
  **Contact Count in Segments**
The contacts in each segment are calculated as per EST.

  ![ContactCountSegments.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000HmN4AMAV.jpg)
- 
  **Datetime Custom Field Type**
The values in the “datetime” type of custom fields are considered in EST.

  ![DatetimeCustomField.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000HmN4oMAF.jpg)
- **Datetime Field in Relational Tables**
The “Datetime” data type fields in relational tables store data as per EST.
  ![acctimezone0.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/2Q202DP7YD46/acctimezone0.png)
- **Social Campaign Calendar**
The Social Campaign Calendar follows EST.
  ![SocialCampaigns.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000HmN5XMAV.jpg)
- [**eRFM Analysis Report**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Unlock-Customer-Insights-with-eRFM-Analysis)
The eRFM report's customer count is updated daily at 1 AM EST.
  ![erfm12.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000LVWkMMAX.jpg)
- [**Cleansing Rules**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Cleansing-Rules)
A cleansing rule automatically removes internet bots and dormant/inactive contacts at 3 AM EST every day.
  ![cleanse5.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000LVXVXMA5.jpg)
