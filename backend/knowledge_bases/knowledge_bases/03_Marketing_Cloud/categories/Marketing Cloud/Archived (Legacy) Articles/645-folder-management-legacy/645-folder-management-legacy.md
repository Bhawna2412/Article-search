# Folder Management - Legacy

Folder Management enables you to organize your email marketing campaigns, content, and segments efficiently, helping streamline your workflow and improve team collaboration. This helps optimize marketing effectiveness by allowing you to categorize assets by brand, campaign type, or user, making it easier to locate and manage your email marketing resources.

When properly organized through folders, marketing teams can substantially improve operational efficiency by reducing time spent searching for assets and ensuring consistent organization across different types of marketing content.

Folders are an extremely helpful option to help a user organize their data in Maropost. The folder option is available on many different pages, and gives the user a unique way to organize their data for many different purposes; by the user, by campaign type, by brand, and so on. You can create multiple folders, and then drag and drop the data into them.

> **Note:** Currently, we support both legacy and new folder panels. The new folder panel is currently available in the index pages of the following: Email Campaigns, Segments, and Content. For information on the legacy folder panel, see Folder Management.

### Accessing Folders

Along the right side of the Navigation, you'll notice a small grey bar with an arrow. Click the bar, and the folder panel for that page expands.

![Legacy_Folder_Closed_2.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/05T5g00000BkWy6.05T5g00000BkWy6EAF.png)

### Managing Folders

You can use the action icons in the folder panel to create, rename, and delete folders. Use the Privacy icon to assign privileges to other users. When you leave the page, the folder panel retracts back to the left side. If you want to keep the Folder panel open when loading the Index page, click the padlock to lock the folder panel. Click the padlock again to unlock the folder panel.

![Legacy_Folder_Open.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/05T5g00000BkWy4.05T5g00000BkWy4EAF.png)

### Setting Folder Privacy or Assigning Privileges

To edit the privacy settings of a folder, that is assigning privileges to other users, select the folder and click the Privacy icon. To assign a user as one of the owners of the folder, select the user in the OWNERS tab. To assign a user as one of the editors of the folder, select the user in the EDITORS tab. You can remove the privileges by deselecting the user in the relevant tab. After you are done making the changes, remember to click Save.
