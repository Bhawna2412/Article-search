# Integration with Digioh

You can integrate Digioh with your Marketing Cloud account to automate lead capture from website forms, pop-ups, and landing pages directly into your email lists. This significantly improves subscriber acquisition by capturing visitor information in real-time and helps optimize marketing effectiveness through seamless data synchronization between your website engagement tools and email campaigns.

Digioh integration enables you to expand your contact collection beyond manual processes while maintaining organized list management across multiple forms and landing pages, ensuring every captured lead immediately becomes part of your email marketing ecosystem.

The [**Digioh**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/home/leaving?allowTrusted=1&target=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.digioh.com%2F%3Futm_medium%3Dreferral%26utm_source%3Dmaropost) platform enables you to easily build email capture forms, pop-ups, surveys, preference centers, and landing pages that can be integrated with Maropost. Digioh offers hundreds of form themes and supports multi-step forms, capturing phone numbers, and forms with custom or hidden fields.

In the Digioh platform, you can create an integration with Maropost that sends captured emails and other user data to Maropost in real-time. You can set up multiple Maropost integrations that sync different forms and landing pages to different Maropost lists.

### Setting Up Integration between Maropost and Digioh

If you want help with your first integration with Digioh, please reach out to the Digioh team.

Follow these steps in the Digioh platform to create an integration with Maropost:

1. 
  First, create your box in Digioh. You can choose from one of our hundreds of built-in themes, or select a blank box to start from scratch.

  ![Choose_a_Theme.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/05T5g00000BkXdq.05T5g00000BkXdqEAF.png)
2. 
  Go to the Integrations page, click **Create New Integrations**, and then select Maropost from the drop-down menu.
3.   - 
    Account ID - Enter your Maropost account ID.
  - 
    List ID - The Maropost list into which the data from Digioh will be synched.
  - 
    API Key - Enter your Maropost API Key.
  - 
    Box - Optionally, select a box that you have created in Digioh to be linked with the integration.

  Enter the integration details and then click **Create Integration**.

  ![Digioh-Maropost_Integration.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/05T5g00000BkXdr.05T5g00000BkXdrEAF.png)
4. 
  Add this Maropost integration to your box or landing page, and that box or page will send emails to the specified list in real-time.
