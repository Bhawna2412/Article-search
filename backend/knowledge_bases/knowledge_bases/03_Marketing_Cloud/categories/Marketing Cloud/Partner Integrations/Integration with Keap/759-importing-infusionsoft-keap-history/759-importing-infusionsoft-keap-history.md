# Importing InfusionSoft/Keap History

You can preserve years of valuable customer relationship data when migrating from InfusionSoft by importing your complete historical database, including contacts, tags, products, and purchase records. This comprehensive data migration enables you to maintain sophisticated audience segmentation and personalized messaging strategies from day one in Marketing Cloud, protecting your marketing investment and ensuring campaign continuity.

When properly configured, historical data import helps optimize your email marketing effectiveness by preserving customer lifecycle insights, purchase behavior patterns, and engagement history that inform better targeting decisions and campaign performance.

Using the **Import History** option, you can import the historical data from InfusionSoft to Maropost. The historical data includes contacts, contact tags, product details, and orders.

### Setting Up the Import History Integration

Perform the following steps to set up the integration for importing historical data from Infusionsoft into Maropost's list:

1. On the Connections page, in the Infusionsoft tab, click Add Settings.
2. 
  In the Integration Types dialog box, click Import History.

  ![Import_History_Option.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/05T5g00000BkXFj.05T5g00000BkXFjEAN.png)
3. 
  On the Import InfusionSoft History Settings dialog box, select the Maropost list into which the contacts and related details will be imported.

  ![Import_InfusionSoft_History.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/05T5g00000BkXFk.05T5g00000BkXFkEAN.png)
4. Click Connect.
5. You'll be redirected to the InfusionSoft login page where you must enter your username and password, and log in to your Infusionsoft account.
6. 
  Upon logging in, you'll be prompted to authenticate the connection. Click Allow.

  ![Connection_Authentication.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/05T5g00000BkXFl.05T5g00000BkXFlEAN.png)

  This creates the integration connection and you'll be redirected back to the Maropost application. As soon as the connection is created, the contact history will start syncing to your chosen Maropost list.
