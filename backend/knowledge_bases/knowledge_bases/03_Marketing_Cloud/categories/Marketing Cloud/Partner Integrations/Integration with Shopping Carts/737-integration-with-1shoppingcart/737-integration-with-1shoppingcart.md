# Integration with 1ShoppingCart

You can connect your 1ShoppingCart store with Marketing Cloud to automatically import customer data and streamline your email marketing operations. This integration enables you to create more targeted campaigns based on actual purchase behavior and helps optimize your marketing effectiveness by ensuring you're reaching customers with relevant messaging based on their buying history.

When properly configured, this integration eliminates manual data entry and enables you to leverage purchase data for better segmentation, which can substantially improve engagement rates and campaign performance.

An integration between Maropost Marketing Cloud and 1ShoppingCart is easy to create and enables you to import customer data from 1ShoppingCart to Marketing Cloud.

> **Note:** All customers, including those with or without purchases, are imported. However, customers without any email addresses are not imported.

### Managing the Integrations

From the Navigation panel, go to **Integrations**. On the Integrations page, select **1ShoppingCart**. On the 1ShoppingCart Integration page, you can add new integrations and manage the existing integrations.

![User-added image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/1ShoppingCartIndexPage.05T5g00000VK43aEAD.png)

### Acquiring Merchant ID and Merchant Key

To add a new 1ShoppingCart integration, you first need to acquire your Merchant ID and Merchant Key. Follow the steps below before you proceed to add a new integration.

1. 
  To get your Merchant ID, either visit the **Account** section or you can find the Merchant ID at the top right corner of the **Admin Panel**.

  ![User-added image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/1shop0.05T5g000014XR2TEAW.png)
2. 
  To acquire your Merchant Key, visit the **Account** section and click on **API Settings**.

  ![User-added image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/1shop1.05T5g000014XR4oEAG.png)
3. 
  Copy the key from the given field and paste it into your 1ShoppingCart integration in Maropost Marketing Cloud. If you generate a new key in the future, you'll have to paste and again configure the 1ShoppingCart integration on your Marketing Cloud account.

  ![User-added image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/1shop2.05T5g000014XPyNEAW.png)

### Adding a New Integration

Perform the following steps:

1. On the 1ShoppingCart Integration page, click **ADD**.
2.   - **Name**: Name for the integration.
  - **Merchant ID**: Your merchant ID on your 1ShoppingCart account.
  - **Merchant Key**: Your merchant Key on your 1ShoppingCart account.

  In the Add Integration dialog box, enter the following information:

  ![User-added image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/1ShoppingCartAddIntegration.05T5g00000VK4WEEA1.png)
3. Click **CONNECT**.

Now, you will see this cart when creating a new list or editing any existing list in Marketing Cloud. To associate the cart to the list, select the cart (you can also enter specific Product IDs/Items you want to be integrated into this list) and then click Save.

### Verifying the 1ShoppingCart Integration

Configuring and Verifying 1ShoppingCart's Instant Notification URL:

1. 
  To configure your Notification URL, visit the 1ShoppingCart Integration page on your Maropost Marketing account. Copy the Notification URL of an unverified integration.

  ![User-added image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/1shop4.05T5g000014XSckEAG.png)
2. 
  Log in to your 1ShoppingCart account, go to **Account,** and select **API Settings**.

  ![User-added image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/1shop6.05T5g00001DibOXEAZ.png)
3. 
  Paste the URL in the **Notification** field and click on **Save**.

  ![User-added image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/1shop3.05T5g000014XT0tEAG.png)

If it's working, the Notification URL gets verified. After the verification, you will be notified to confirm the changes you have made. Once the checks have been carried out, the integration verification process is completed.
