# Automation: Overview

Email marketing automation through Journeys and Data Journeys enables you to nurture leads automatically and deliver timely, relevant content based on specific triggers and customer behaviors. This automated workflow capability significantly improves campaign effectiveness by ensuring personalized communication reaches customers at optimal moments, while scaling personalized marketing without manual intervention.

When properly configured, automated journeys can substantially improve engagement rates and helps optimize email marketing ROI by delivering the right message to the right person at the right time through systematic, data-driven workflows.

Maropost empowers you with the ability to automate your email marketing in the form of **Journeys** and **Data Journeys**.

The Journeys and Data Journeys present a flowchart view of your automated process, complete with drag-and-drop controls for triggers, actions, filters, and delays. You can leverage the Journeys and Data Journeys to perform various actions, including sending emails based on specific triggers.

To learn about Journeys, see [**Introduction to Journeys**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Introduction-to-Journeys).

To learn about Data Journeys, see [**Introduction to Data Journeys**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Introduction-to-Data-Journeys).
