# Dashboard Walkthrough

The homepage of Maropost Marketing Cloud features an easy-to-read dashboard with an extensive amount of information at your disposal. You can customize the dashboard to suit your specific needs with a variety of widgets. The dashboard can also help you track and tweak your campaigns and present reports to your team by providing clear, simple, and visually appealing data metrics.

You can add or remove the widgets to customize the dashboard as per your business needs. You can further personalize the dashboard by rearranging the widgets with a simple drag-and-drop.

![dashboard0(1).gif](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/CS2UYHC6AMGX/dashboard0-281-29.gif)

> **Note:** **Note:** A personalized dashboard is tied to a user account and is available to the user across multiple accounts.

Listed below is a detailed summary of all the widgets available to you:

## Calendar

The **Calendar** widget makes it easier to view all your scheduled campaigns for the month. You can click on a date to see the details of the campaigns scheduled for that day. Click the link icon against the campaign detail to go to its performance page.

![image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000UH7JyMAL.jpg)

## Performance Summary

The **Performance Summary** widget shows you how your email campaigns are performing over time through a visual summary. It provides essential details related to campaign delivery and performance, such as sends, deliveries, opens and clicks, bounces, and complaints. You can use the filter drop-down menu to select the period for which you want to see the summary.

![Performance_Summary.jpg](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/05T5g00000BkWrO.05T5g00000BkWrOEAV.png)

## Contact Search

The Contact Search widget lets you quickly find contacts with just a few clicks. You can use it to search for both email and SMS contacts.

**Contact Search by Email or Phone:** To find an email contact, enter their full email address. To find an SMS contact, enter their mobile number in international format: a plus sign (+) followed by the country code, area code, and phone number. If the contact exists, you'll be taken to their [**contact 360 overview**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/10-contact-360-overview) page, where you can view and update their information.

**SMS Search Requirements:** You can only search for SMS contacts if all of the following conditions are met:

- SMS functionality is enabled for your account.
- SMS functionality is enabled for your user profile.
- A "**From Number**" has been configured for the account in the Admin panel.

![dashboard5.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/L0TT9SBU8C6W/dashboard5.png)

## Recent Sent Campaigns

The **Recent Sent Campaigns** widget is a great way to see the status and track the performance of your most recent campaigns. You can use the action menu to quickly duplicate a campaign and view the campaign report metrics. If you have enabled "**Revenue Tracking,**" this widget displays the related revenue as a column.

![Recent_Sent_Campaigns.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/05T5g00000BkWrQ.05T5g00000BkWrQEAV.png)

## ISP Report

The **ISP Report** shows you all the deliverability data for your campaigns over a period that is categorized based on various domains, such as Gmail, Yahoo, Outlook, and so on. The ISP reports are generated for all the ISPs to which you send campaigns and can help you identify and troubleshoot deliverability issues faster.

![image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000UH75JMAT.jpg)

## Recent Updated Lists

The **Recent Updated Lists** widget shows you the lists that have been updated within a specific timeframe. If you have multiple lists and campaigns running at the same time, then this widget can help you keep track of updates to the lists.

![Recent_Updated_Lists.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/05T5g00000BkWrK.05T5g00000BkWrKEAV.png)

## Email Address by Domain

The **Email Address by Domain** widget helps you understand your customer base by viewing the breakdown per domain. Our Deliverability team continuously monitors all usage updates made by these ISPs to ensure that your emails adhere to new rules and your deliverability is always top-notch.

![Email_Address_by_Domain.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/05T5g00000BkWrL.05T5g00000BkWrLEAV.png)

## Total Campaign Revenue

The **Total Campaign Revenue** widget shows you the total revenue generated from all your campaigns.

![image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000UH72vMAD.jpg)

## Email Usage

The **Email Usage** widget enables you to see your email utilization. You can use the radio button to toggle between the monthly and annual usage data. The "**Contract Annual Usage**" option shows the annual email usage and is calculated starting from your contract start date up until the current date.

![Email_Usage_Widget.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/05T5g00000BkWrI.05T5g00000BkWrIEAV.png)

## Email Volume

The **Email Volume** widget shows you the email volume in a waveform. You can see the campaigns that have been sent and delivered over a period of time.

![image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000UH5VyMAL.jpg)

## Average Contact Lifecycle

The **Average Contact Lifecycle** widget shows the average lifecycle (in the number of days) of all your subscribed contacts. The information can help you monitor your contacts' behavior towards your marketing campaigns or branding promotions.

![Average_Contact_Life.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/05T5g00000BkWrD.05T5g00000BkWrDEAV.png)

## Subscriber Summary

The **Subscriber Summary** widget shows the overall growth summary of your account, including the count of new subscribers and unsubscribers, along with the lifetime total data.

![image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05TJ100000UH7ZbMAL.jpg)

## Engagement

The **Engagement** metric shows an insight into user interaction with your application. The users’ app engagement is a critical data point for business owners to judge the performance of the app. This widget shows you the time spent by users of iOS and Android on the app and their engagement. If you notice that people are in fact spending a lot of time on the app but are not contributing to sales, it might be time for a UI/UX update for the app.

![Engagement.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/05T5g00000BkWrF.05T5g00000BkWrFEAV.png)

## Push Session Tracking Summary

The mobile's **Push Session Tracking Summary** widget provides you with a summary of mobile device sessions based on applications and operating systems.

![Push_Session_Tracking_Summary.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/05T5g00000BkWrG.05T5g00000BkWrGEAV.png)

## Mobile App Analytics

The **Mobile App Analytics** widget shows you the session-by-session breakdown of your application users. You can choose to view data from a certain time frame and compare the app performance across iOS and Android. The widget also allows you to see both unique and new users, which can help you sharpen your in-app promotion campaigns and notifications.

![Mobile_App_Analytics.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/05T5g00000BkWg7.05T5g00000BkWg7EAF.png)

## Opens and Clicks Source

The **Opens and Clicks Source** widget enables you to view the percentage of opens and clicks based on whether they originate from data centers (denoted as "**Data Center**") or non-data centers (denoted as "**Other**"). You can use the drop-down menu to select the period for which you want the data.

![User-added image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/OpensandClicksSourceWidget.05T5g00000ELYiUEAX.png)
