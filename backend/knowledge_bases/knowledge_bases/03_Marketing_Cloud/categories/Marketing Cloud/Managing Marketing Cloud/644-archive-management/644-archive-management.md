# Archive Management

**Archive Management** enables you to organize and maintain your email marketing assets efficiently while keeping your workspace focused on active campaigns and content. This helps optimize your marketing workflow by allowing you to preserve important historical data while reducing clutter in your primary working areas.

When properly managed, archived items can substantially improve operational efficiency by maintaining clean, organized interfaces that help marketing teams locate active resources quickly while preserving valuable campaign history for reference and compliance purposes.

Marketing Cloud supports archiving functionality for many of its items and also enables you to view and manage all your archived items in one place.

The archiving functionality is available for the following items: [**Campaign Tag**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Campaign-Tags), [**Contact List**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/5-contact-lists-overview-new?utm_source=community-search&utm_medium=organic-search&utm_term=contact+list), [**Segment**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Introduction-to-Segments), [**Content**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Managing-Email-Content), and [**Dynamic Content**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Dynamic-Content). 

On the index page, you can see and manage your archived items on the Archives page. The Archives page is accessible from the Navigation panel.

You can also access the Archives page from the index pages of the supported items by clicking the **View Archives** tab next to the page heading.

> **Note:** **Note:** To archive an item, use the **Archive** option available on the **Actions** drop-down menu of the respective item. For example, if you want to archive email content, go to the **Email Content** index page. Select the content you want to delete. Then, from the Actions drop-down menu, select **Archive Content**.
