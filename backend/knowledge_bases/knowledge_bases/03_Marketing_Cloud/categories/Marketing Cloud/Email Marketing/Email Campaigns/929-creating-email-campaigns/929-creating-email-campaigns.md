# Creating Email Campaigns

The process of creating an email campaign is divided into 4 steps. While you are required to fill out the mandatory fields to move to the next step, you can use the breadcrumb trail to move back and forth between the steps.

**Note**: Before creating an email campaign, you must create the contacts (in the form of a list or table, or segment) and content.

Perform the following steps to create an email campaign:

4. In step 2, you select the contacts for sending the campaign. Optionally, you can also select the contacts from whom to suppress the campaign. Refer to Step 2 - Contacts section of this article.
5. In step 3, you select the content for your campaign and check your spam score. Refer to Step 3 - Content section of this article.
6. In step 4, you select a delivery method and schedule for your campaign. Refer to Step 4 - Schedule section of this article.

After a campaign is successfully scheduled or sent, you will see a success message.
