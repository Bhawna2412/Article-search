# Release Notes v3.15.1 - POS and Back Office

In this release we've made a small number of improvements and resolutions to Retail Express.

This update will be made available to all customers between **7th February** - **15th February**.

  
  

---

### Help & Support Menu

We've updated the Back Office "Help & Support" menu to make it more useful!

![mceclip0 - 2023-07-09T185948.800.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppfO6EAI.jpg)

With the new menu update, you can now access important information faster than ever, including:

* Product Portal (to view our development roadmap, and provide feedback on ideas)
* Announcements (stay up-to-date with the latest news and Release Notes)
* Training Academy
* Contact Support (although you can also reach support directly from the ["Help" button](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Announcing-the-Knowledge-Base-in-Back-Office) now available in the Back Office)

Please note we've also renamed several options:

* "Implementation" has been renamed to "Onboarding Console"
* "My Retail Express Invoices and Payments" has been renamed to "My Account"

Access the new menu by clicking the Help & Support menu in Back Office.
