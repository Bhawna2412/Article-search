# Release Notes v3.16.01 - POS and Back Office

This release will be available to all customers by 7th February 2023.

## New Features

### New Payment Type - Woolworths and TAGR

![everyday-market.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppfjXEAQ.jpg)

![tagr.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppfjcEAA.jpg)

We've added two new non-integrated Payment Types for you to allocate your payments against, making it easier to reconcile your transactions at the end of the day and allowing for more detailed reporting.

You'll find both the Woolworths and TAGR Payment Types available to enable within the Payment Type settings. Both are disabled by default. Click here for instructions on [Configuring Payment Types](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/article/Configuring-Payment-Types).
