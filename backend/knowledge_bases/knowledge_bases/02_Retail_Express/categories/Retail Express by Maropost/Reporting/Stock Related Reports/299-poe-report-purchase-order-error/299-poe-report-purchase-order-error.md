# POE Report (Purchase Order Error)

You can identify and resolve purchase order receiving variances by accessing the POE (Purchase Order Error) Report, which highlights discrepancies between ordered and received quantities. This report helps retail operators investigate supplier shipping errors, identify damaged goods, and manage variance resolution through appropriate corrective actions like supplier returns or invoice adjustments.

Using the POE Report helps retail operators maintain accurate inventory records by promptly identifying and resolving receiving variances, ensuring supplier accountability, and preventing inventory discrepancies from affecting stock accuracy.

The Purchase Order Error (POE) Report is designed to show all products sent to the POE Manager at the time of stock receipt. View the PO and Invoice number, product details and action applied to deal with POE managed products (i.e. received, credited, back-ordered, re-ordered, written-off).

To use the POE Report:

1. Navigate to **Reports > Stock Reports > POE Report**
  ![poe_report_filters.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppW3mEAE.jpg)
2. Enter the **PO **number from the original Purchase Order to view results for that order only
3. Enter the **Generated PO Number **(the new Purchase Order number if you chose to Re-order stock from the POE Manager) to filter by the new order
4. Select a **Sort** option to sort the results by your preference (Date is the Date Received and will sort in descending order i.e. most recent date at the top)
5. Select from the remaining **Search Fields **as required
6. Click **Search**
7. The **Results **will be displayed accordingly
8. Where 2 x **PO#** are displayed, this indicates that the product has been re-ordered. The top listed PO# is the original PO raised, the bottom PO# is generated upon re-order.
  ![poe_report_results_twoorders.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppW3rEAE.jpg)
9. Click **Print **to print a copy of the report

![poe_report_results.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppW3wEAE.jpg)

> **Note:** Click the PO number or ProductID hyperlink to open direct access to the Purchase Order or Product for editing or review.
