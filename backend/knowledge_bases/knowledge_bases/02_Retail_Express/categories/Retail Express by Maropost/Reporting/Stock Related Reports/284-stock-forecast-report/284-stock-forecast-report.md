# Stock Forecast Report

You can predict future inventory needs based on historical sales patterns by accessing the Stock Forecast Report, which projects product demand and suggests optimal reorder quantities. This report helps retail operators make data-driven purchasing decisions, reduce stockouts of popular items, and maintain appropriate stock levels that balance customer demand against inventory investment constraints.

Using the Stock Forecast Report helps retail operators optimize inventory purchasing based on actual sales trends, reduce emergency reorders, and maintain better in-stock positions on high-demand products while avoiding overstock situations.

Find **related articles **at the bottom of this page

The Stock Forecast Report provides detailed information on retail pricing, sales, inventory quantities and ordering information for each Outlet.

**Considerations when using this report:**

- this report will be exported to Excel, *not* displayed on-screen.
- a maximum of 2,500 products will be displayed per export

To access the Stock Forecast Report navigate to** Reports > Stock Reports > Stock Forecast Report**

## Special Filters

Most of the filtering fields are self explanatory, however see the detailed information below for the more advanced fields.

**Include Regions -** un-tick to exclude completely from the results

**Include Inventory Outlets? - **un-tick to exclude completely from the results

**Pricing Outlet - **Pricing for a maximum of 5 individual outlets will be shown for outlets selected in the Pricing Outlet filter

**Master Pricing Outlet **- Pricing for the selected Outlet will be used in the product pricing fields, such as Supplier Buy and COGS.

**Group Inventory Outlet** - Aggregated inventory, purchase order, and sales information will be shown for all Outlets selected. Also used to calculate inventory figures in the Required To Order section results

**Inventory Outlet** - Inventory for a maximum of 5 individual outlets will be shown for selected Outlets

**Region** - A regional breakdown of inventory and sales for a maximum of 10 regions will be shown for selected Regions

Apply filtering as required > click **Search** > Open or Save the report.

> **Note:** This is a Legacy Report, commissioned in the early days of Retail Express and which may or may not be suitable for your business. We welcome you to explore the Legacy Reports, however please note that support for this report is no longer available.
