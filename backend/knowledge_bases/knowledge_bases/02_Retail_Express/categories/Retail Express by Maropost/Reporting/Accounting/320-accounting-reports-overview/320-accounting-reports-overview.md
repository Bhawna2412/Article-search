# Accounting Reports Overview

You can access comprehensive financial and accounting reports within **Retail Express** to support bookkeeping, financial statement preparation, and tax compliance requirements. Understanding available accounting reports helps retail operators provide accountants with necessary data, maintain accurate financial records, and ensure compliance with Australian accounting and tax requirements.

Using accounting reports effectively helps retail operators streamline month-end processes, support year-end financial reporting, and maintain accurate financial records that facilitate efficient collaboration with accounting professionals and tax advisors.

The Accounting suite of reports is designed to assist in verifying Retail Express data that has been uploaded to your accounting package.

Click here to view details on our Accounting Integration Options.

The Accounting Reports can be accessed via **Reports > Accounting Reports**

## Report Recommendations

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| Summarised Accounting Data | The Financial Summary Report is the go-to report for a high-level overview. |
| Detailed Accounting Data | Use the remaining Accounting Reports to drill down into individual transactions and investigate discrepancies. |
