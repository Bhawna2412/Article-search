# Financial Summary Report

You can access comprehensive financial performance data through Retail Express financial summary reporting, helping your retail management understand revenue, costs, and profitability across all operations. This capability provides executive-level financial views that consolidate sales, expenses, and financial metrics, which helps support strategic decision-making and business performance monitoring.

Setting up regular financial summary reviews enables your retail operations to track business health systematically, helping optimize financial performance and strategic planning. This helps retail businesses maintain clear visibility into financial outcomes while identifying trends and opportunities for improving profitability across all store locations.

The Financial Summary Report shows a detailed view of an account-level verification of data imported from Retail Express to your accounting package. In this report, transactions are broken down by Income, Payment Method, Sales (type of sales, both taxable and tax-free), Freight, Surcharges, Gift Vouchers Sold, Layby Movement, Accounts Receivable Movement, Inventory Movement, Direct Costs and Purchases.

## Using the Financial Summary Report

To access the Financial Summary Report:

1. Open Retail Express
2. Navigate to **Reports > Accounting Reports > Financial Summary Report**
3. Select from the **Filters **as required (see below for a detailed explanation)
  ![Screenshot 2023-01-25 at 2.02.28 PM.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppNcJEAU.jpg)
4. Click the **Search **button
5. The **Results** will be displayed matching the filters
6. Click the **Export** button to export the report in an Excel spreadsheet
  ![Screenshot 2023-01-25 at 2.08.37 PM.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppNcOEAU.jpg)

## Filters

![Screenshot 2023-01-25 at 2.02.28 PM (1).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppLLZEA2.jpg)

| **Filter** | **Details** |
| --- | --- |
| Date From/ Date To | The date the transaction applies to your accounts. These are inclusive dates, meaning if you select from 24/12/2022 to 24/01/2023 it will include transactions on those dates  > **Note:** If you entered a payment on the 1st of Feb, but backdated the payment to the 30th of Jan, the payment will appear on this report on the 30th of Jan. |
| Accounting Method | Only the Accrual Method is supported; you must confirm this method with your bookkeeper or accountant. |
| Country | Used to limit the report to locations within that Country only. Countries are configured via **Settings > Locations/Outlets > Countries** |
| Store Type | A categorisation method used to separate your individual outlets. Refer to the Outlets - Store Types article for more information |
| Outlet | The individual Outlets in the database |

## Results

The Results tab displays detailed information on products included in the Financial Summary Report
