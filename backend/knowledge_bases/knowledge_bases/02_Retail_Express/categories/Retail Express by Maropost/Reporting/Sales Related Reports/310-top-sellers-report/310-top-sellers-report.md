# Top Sellers Report

You can identify your best-performing products by accessing the Top Sellers Report, which ranks products by sales volume, revenue, or profitability. This performance ranking helps retail operators focus inventory investment on proven winners, plan promotional strategies around popular items, and optimize shelf space allocation to maximize sales from high-demand products.

Using the Top Sellers Report helps retail operators ensure adequate stock levels of bestselling products, leverage popular items in marketing campaigns, and make strategic decisions about product assortment based on actual customer demand.

The Top Sellers Report offers insights into your most popular products based on sales performance. This report This report will show you the top selling products matching the filters that you selected.

To access the Report, Go to **Reports** >** Product Performance**

The results will display the quantities of the products, the total sales, total GP values, average sales and average GP values.

You can also print this report or export it to excel, as found in the bottom left corner.
