# Sales & GP Report (Payments)

You can analyze sales profitability and payment method performance by accessing the Sales GP Report Payments, which shows gross profit margins alongside payment type breakdowns. This combined financial analysis helps retail operators understand profitability trends while monitoring payment method usage patterns, supporting both margin management and payment processing optimization.

Using the Sales GP Report Payments helps retail operators evaluate profitability performance, identify margin trends, and understand how different payment methods contribute to overall revenue across retail operations.

The Sales & GP Report (Payments) allows you to review payments accepted within a date range (not related to the date the invoice was created), as well as review invoices with outstanding balances.

Only sales that have a payment processed against them in Retail Express will be displayed

To use the Sales & GP Report (Payments) Report:

1. Navigate to **Reports > Sales Reports > Sales & GP Report (Payments)**
