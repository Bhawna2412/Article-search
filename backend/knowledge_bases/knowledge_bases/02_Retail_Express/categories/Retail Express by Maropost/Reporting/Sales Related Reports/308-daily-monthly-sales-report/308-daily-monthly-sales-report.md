# Daily / Monthly Sales Report

You can track sales trends over time by accessing the Daily Monthly Sales Report, which compares sales performance across days and months to identify patterns and trends. This time-based analysis helps retail operators understand seasonal fluctuations, identify growth trends, and plan inventory and staffing levels based on historical sales patterns throughout the year.

Using the Daily Monthly Sales Report helps retail operators forecast future sales, plan for seasonal peaks, and make informed decisions about inventory investment timing based on proven sales seasonality patterns.

The Daily / Monthly Sales Report is designed to give a snapshot of the number of transactions, sales value and GP%, across the days of the week or months of the year to aid you in reflective trend analysis of sales.

To access the Daily / Monthly Sales Report:

1. Navigate to **Reports > Sales Reports > Daily / Monthly Sales**
2. Select your **Date Range**
3. Select the **Report Type **of Daily or Monthly (to review the results as totals for each day or the week or for each month)
4. Tick **Inc. GST **to include tax in the totals
5. Select your **Outlet/s**
6. Click **Search**
