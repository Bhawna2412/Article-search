# POS - Product Sales Dashboard

You can use the Product Sales Dashboard to track your top-performing products and identify sales trends in real-time, helping optimize inventory planning and merchandising decisions. This improves your retail operations efficiency by providing immediate visibility into your best-selling items, with properly configured product sales tracking typically enabling better stock replenishment decisions and merchandising strategies that drive increased revenue.

Understanding product sales dashboards involves recognizing style-level versus SKU-level reporting, revenue versus volume analysis, and multi-outlet comparison capabilities to help retail operators focus on the right inventory and merchandising opportunities, ensuring your retail strategy consistently drives better product performance outcomes.

The POS Product Sales Dashboard offers a comprehensive view of product sales performance directly from your POS system. Designed to provide real-time insights, it helps users track sales trends, monitor top-performing products, and identify areas for improvement. 

This report shows you the 25 top selling products based on sales revenue for the specified date range (by default this report will show today's date range)

Keep in mind that by default that this report will by default also only show you the sales for the outlet that your currently logged into the POS with, however you can select more outlets to view by clicking on the drop down menu in the top right hand corner here:

You also have the ability to choose whether to show the Sales by Sales $ Amount or by Sales Qty Sold amount by selecting the "Show by Volume" check box.

You can then choose to display the results by Style for example you have T-Shirt that comes in Red , Blue , Black and White in Sizes 6, 8 , 10 , 12 , 14 and those are part of the 1 product style, now you don't want to see which size or colour sells best but rather see how that style is performing against other styles then you would check this box.
