# EOM Inventory Valuation Summary

You can quickly assess month-end inventory financial position by accessing the EOM Inventory Valuation Summary, which provides high-level inventory value totals without detailed line-item breakdowns. This summary report helps retail operators perform rapid financial assessments and month-end checks, providing accountants with essential inventory asset figures for financial statement preparation without overwhelming detail.

Using the EOM Inventory Valuation Summary helps retail operators efficiently communicate inventory asset values to accounting teams and support streamlined month-end financial closing processes.

The Inventory Valuation Summary Report allows you to view and export your product stock quantity information, including stock quantities for the available, on-order, and transfer in and out stock buckets.

This report uses the same filters as the Inventory Valuation, however, COGS is also available across each stock status for review.

To access the Inventory Valuation Summary:

1. Navigate to**Reports > EOM Reports > Inventory Valuation Summary**
2. The **Qty On Hand **filter allows you to filter by a minimum quantity
  ![mceclip1 (37).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000powgLEAQ.jpg)
3. Update the remaining**Filters **as required
4. Click the **Search **button located at the bottom left below the filters
5. The **Results** will be displayed showing all individual items matching the filters
6. To **sort **the report in ascending or descending order, click the arrows in the column headings
7. Click the icons in the top-right-hand corner to export the report in an Excel spreadsheet or CSV![mceclip1 (38).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000pox0OEAQ.jpg)
8. Click **Refresh **to view the latest details
