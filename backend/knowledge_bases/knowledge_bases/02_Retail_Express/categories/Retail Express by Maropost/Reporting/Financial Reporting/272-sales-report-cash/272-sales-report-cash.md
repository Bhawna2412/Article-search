# Sales Report - Cash

The Sales Report - Cash report is used to view fulfilled sales information for each Outlet, separated by Brand.

- The value of the sale is determined by what has been fulfilled, regardless of the Payment Type selected at the time of processing the sale
- The calculation used for results is: Qty Fulfilled x Unit Price for Processed/Awaiting Payment Orders, with the Fulfilment Date within the selected date range, separated by Brand
- Products without an associated Brand will display in the results as 'Undefined'

To use the Sales Report - Cash:

1. Navigate to: **Reports > Finance Report > Sales Report - Cash**
2. Update the **Filters **as required
  ![blobid0 (34).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000pphnREAQ.jpg)
3. Click **Search**
4. The results will be displayed
  ![blobid1 (22).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000pphQOEAY.jpg)
5. Scroll to the right to view full details
6. Click **Export to Excel **if required
