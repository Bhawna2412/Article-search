# Product Report

You can analyze comprehensive product performance metrics by accessing the Product Report, which shows sales data, profitability, and inventory information for individual products. This detailed reporting helps retail operators evaluate product success, identify top performers and underperformers, and make data-driven decisions about product assortment, pricing strategies, and promotional focus.

Using the Product Report helps retail operators optimize product mix, allocate inventory investment effectively, and identify opportunities to improve profitability through better product selection and pricing optimization.

The Product Report provides indicators on how individual products are selling for each Outlet. Using the report you can compare:

- The quantity available and on-order
- The number sold in month-to-date and year-to-date
- Cost vs value (for both Available and On-Order)

> **Note:** This report is limited to 10,000 lines in the results. Each product is displayed once per Outlet i.e. 1 product for 10 outlets will be 10 lines. If you have more than 10,000 lines you will need to use the filters to narrow your results.

To use the Product Report:

1. Navigate to **Reports > Product Performance > Product Report**
2. Use the **PLU **field to search for specific products

> **Note:** To search for multiple products separate the product codes with a comma e.g. 124001, 124002

3. Use the Filters in **Refine Search **to narrow your results
4. Click **Search**
5. The results will be displayed in a table
6. To **sort the results **click the arrows in the column headings to sort the results
  ![mceclip0 - 2023-07-09T002447.112.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppWpvEAE.jpg)
7. To **reset **the sort order click the Refresh icon at the top
  ![mceclip2 (52).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppWg1EAE.jpg)
8. To **export,** click the purple disk icon at the top and select the preferred format of the output file
