# Reports - Freight Report

The Freight Report displays a list of sales with Freight charges in POS, including a Delivery Due Date (if specified).

A series of Filters are available to help narrow the sales down, including delivery mode (Fulfilment Method - e.g. home delivery, store pickup etc), Order Status, and Outlet.

![39097fde9a568b22dc9d282ebeaf01fc9332352eae5c39de0006f6c623917c63.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000pphQSEAY.jpg)
To use the Freight Report:

1. Navigate to **Reports > Sales Reports > Freight Report**
2. Select the **Filters** as required
3. Click **Search**
4. The results will be displayed

> **Note:** amounts displayed inside brackets () are negative amounts.

5. To **export **report click the **Save **icon and select the preferred Output Method
