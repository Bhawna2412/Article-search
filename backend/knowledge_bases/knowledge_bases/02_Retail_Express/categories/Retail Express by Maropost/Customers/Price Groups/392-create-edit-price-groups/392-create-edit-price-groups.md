# Create & Edit Price Groups

You can create price groups to offer different pricing tiers to customer segments like wholesale, VIP, or trade accounts, helping maximize revenue through targeted pricing strategies. This improves your retail operations by enabling flexible pricing structures, with properly configured price groups typically supporting better customer segmentation and increased margins through differentiated pricing.

Understanding price group configuration involves recognizing percentage-based discounts, customer assignment rules, and pricing hierarchy to help retail operators focus on the right pricing strategy workflows, ensuring your retail strategy consistently drives better revenue optimization and customer segmentation outcomes.

Price Groups allow you to create different groups for your customers to automatically apply a discount for all sales for the customers in the group e.g. a VIP discount group that receives 25% off all purchases. Using Price Groups will allow you to easily manage the discount value for all customers.

To create a new Price Group:

1. Navigate to **Settings > Price Groups > Price Groups**
2. Enter a name for the group in the **Group Name **field e.g. VIP
3. Enter the **value** for the discount e.g. a 25% discount would be entered as "25"
4. Select the **Group Rule **to determine how the discount is calculated (see below for further details)
5. Click **Create**

Price Groups can be assigned to customers by editing the Customer directly in Retail Express or using the Customer Mass Download/Upload process

> **Note:** You can edit or delete Price Groups by clicking the Pencil or Trash Can icon in the table of displayed Price Groups (click the Tick icon after editing to confirm the changes).

**Group Rules:**

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| Cost Price Plus % Value | The value entered as a percentage (%) added to the *Cost* Price of the product. e.g. If the cost Price for a product was $50, entering a Price Group rule of 25% Cost Price Plus % Value would make the product available for $62.50 |
| Cost Price Plus $ Value | The value entered as a dollar ($) added to the *Cost* Price of the Product. e.g. If the Cost Price for a product was $50, entering a Price Group rule of $25 would make the product available for $75. |
| Sell Price Minus % Value | The value entered as a percentage (%) value subtracted from the *Sell *Price of the Product. e.g. If the Sell Price for a product was $50, entering a Price Group rule of 25% would make the product available for $37.50 |
| Sell Price Minus $ Value | The value entered as a dollar ($) value subtracted from the *Sell* Price of the product. e.g. If the Sell Price for a product was $50, entering a Price Group rule of $25 would make the product available for $25. |
