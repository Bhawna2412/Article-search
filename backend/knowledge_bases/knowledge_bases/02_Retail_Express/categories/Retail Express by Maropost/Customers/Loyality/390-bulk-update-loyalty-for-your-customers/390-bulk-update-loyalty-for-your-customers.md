# Bulk Update Loyalty for your Customers

You can perform bulk updates to customer loyalty balances for promotional campaigns or program adjustments, helping manage large-scale loyalty initiatives efficiently. This improves your retail operations by enabling mass point adjustments without individual customer processing, with properly configured bulk loyalty updates typically supporting promotional campaigns and program corrections at scale.

Understanding bulk loyalty updates involves recognizing import file requirements, validation processes, and audit trail considerations to help retail operators focus on the right mass customer management activities, ensuring your retail strategy consistently drives better loyalty program flexibility and operational efficiency outcomes.

Bulk editing of your customers can be managed using the Customer Mass Download/Upload functionality, making it highly efficient when you need to make changes quickly. This includes the ability to bulk enable or disable loyalty, for example when launching a new incentive program for your customers.

To perform a Mass Download of all your Customers:

1. Navigate to: **Customers > Customer Management > Customer Mass Download**
2. Click **Start New Download **if required
3. Use the filters to select your customers as required
4. Click **Download**
5. A file will be generated based on the filters

> **Note:** You can navigate away from this page and come back.

6. Once the file has been generated click **Download CSV**
7. Open the downloaded file
8. Locate the column **LoyaltyEnabled**
9. To **enable **loyalty, enter **TRUE **into the cell for each of your customers

> **Note:** If you're enabling Loyalty for all customers, you can copy/paste this value into all cells.

10. To **disable **loyalty enter **FALSE **into the cell
11. **Save **the file to your computer
12. Within Retail Express navigate to **Customers > Customer Management > Customer Mass Upload**
13. Follow the instructions in the Customer Mass Upload article to process the changes to your customer records, and enable loyalty
