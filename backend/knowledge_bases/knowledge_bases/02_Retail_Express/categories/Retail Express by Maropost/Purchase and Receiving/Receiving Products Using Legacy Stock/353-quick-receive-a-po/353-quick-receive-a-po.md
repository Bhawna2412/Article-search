# Quick Receive a PO

You can use Quick Receive functionality to expedite purchase order receiving when all ordered quantities arrive as expected, helping reduce administrative time for straightforward deliveries. This improves your retail operations efficiency by streamlining the receiving process for complete deliveries, with properly configured quick receive workflows typically enabling faster stock availability and reduced time spent on purchase order administration.

Understanding quick receive functionality involves recognizing when to use standard versus quick receive methods, quantity verification requirements, and stock availability timing to help retail operators focus on the right receiving workflows, ensuring your retail strategy consistently drives better operational efficiency and inventory management outcomes.

- The legacy "Quick Receipt" stock receipt method is no longer supported and will soon be retired.  Please switch to using the updated stock receipt and invoicing process *(Launched in 2023) *for improved functionality and experience.

- Simply select the **"Receive & Invoice"** option from the popup when starting a new receipt to follow the new flow.
