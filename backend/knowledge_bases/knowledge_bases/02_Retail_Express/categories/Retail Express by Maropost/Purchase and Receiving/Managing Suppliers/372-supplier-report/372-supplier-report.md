# Supplier Report

You can use supplier reports to analyze purchasing patterns, spending levels, and supplier performance across your retail operations, helping make informed supplier selection and negotiation decisions. This improves your retail operations by providing data-driven insights into supplier relationships, with properly configured supplier reporting typically enabling better supplier consolidation decisions and improved purchasing leverage.

Understanding supplier reporting involves recognizing spend analysis metrics, purchase order frequency patterns, and supplier performance indicators to help retail operators focus on the right procurement analytics workflows, ensuring your retail strategy consistently drives better supplier management and cost optimization outcomes.

The Supplier Report is one of the top recommended reports, designed to assist in review of Supplier performance. Using the Supplier Report you can identify your top performing (and under performing) suppliers, and review sales $ and Stock Value. You may wish to use the Supplier Report as a supplement to the Supplier Statement when summary information is required.

Suppliers that are not listed as the default Supplier for products will be excluded from results.

## Using the Supplier Report

To use the Supplier Report:

1. Navigate to: **Reports > Supplier Reports > Supplier Report**
2. Select your **Report Type **(refer to the Report Type section below for a detailed explanation on each option)
3. Update the **Filters** as required
  ![blobid2 (12).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppiGYEAY.jpg)
4. Click **Search**
5. The Report will be displayed as per the selected **Report Type **e.g. Supplier Summary below
  ![blobid3 (8).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppiGdEAI.jpg)
6. Use the icons on the right of the header to export to Excel if required

## Report Type

Most of the filters are self explanatory, however see the detailed information below for the more advanced filtering explanations.

**Report Type**

- **Supplier Summary** - summary for each supplier of: Qty sold, sales $, Margin, GP % and Stock Value.
- **Supplier by Product Type** - summary for each Supplier as above, separated by Product Type
