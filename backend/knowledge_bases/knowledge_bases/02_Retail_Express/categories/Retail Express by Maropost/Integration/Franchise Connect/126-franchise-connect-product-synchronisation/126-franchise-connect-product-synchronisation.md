# Franchise Connect - Product Synchronisation

Find [**FAQs and related articles**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/126-franchise-connect-product-synchronisation#Faqs) at the bottom of this page

You can synchronise your products between Franchise Connect and the Franchisee. Depending on your settings, a variety of fields may be overwritten.

In this article:

- [Fields Always Overwritten](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/126-franchise-connect-product-synchronisation#always)
- [Fields Overwritten depending on settings](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/126-franchise-connect-product-synchronisation#settings)

## []()Always Overwritten

The following fields will be always overwritten during the product synchronisation:

- Manufacturer SKU
- Short Description
- Custom 1
- Custom 2
- Custom 3
- Long Description
- Warranty Details (legacy field)
- Lead Time
- Carton Qty
- Core Product
- Manufacturer
- Length
- Depth
- Breadth
- Shipping Cubic
- Weight
- Freight
- Requires Assembly
- Disabled
- RRP
