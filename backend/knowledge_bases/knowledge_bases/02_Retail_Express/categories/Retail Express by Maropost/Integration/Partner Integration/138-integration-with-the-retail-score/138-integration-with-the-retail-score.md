# Integration with The Retail Score

You can gain competitive advantage through advanced retail analytics by integrating **Retail Express** with The Retail Score, a business intelligence solution that brings your retail data to life with instant access to critical insights including point-in-time stock history and live performance dashboards. This helps optimize your retail operations by enabling data-driven decision making across inventory planning, pricing strategies, customer analytics, and store performance management.

Implementing The Retail Score integration provides hundreds of pre-configured retail metrics and powerful analysis capabilities through web-based dashboards and Excel integration. When you configure this solution, you can expect better visibility into key retail KPIs, automated reporting distributed to stakeholders, multi-calendar support that reflects your business cycles, seamless integration of data from multiple sources including budgets and payroll, and powerful visualization options that turn insights into actions driving bottom-line performance improvements.

- Publish performance insights at Head Office and Store level via a web-based Portal
- Enable power users to run deep analysis in Excel - slice and dice the data any way you choose
- Visualise data using Multiple calendars that reflect the way you run the business
- Automated reporting and distribution to key business users
- Pre-configured calculations across 1000's of retail measures including Stock position back through time
- Seamlessly pull data in from other sources including Sales & Product Budgets, Payroll, digital marketing e-com channels and Time & Attendance
- Output critical data to existing business intelligence platforms such as Power BI and Tableau

Turn insights into actions that drive KPI's and dramatically improve your bottom-line performance.
