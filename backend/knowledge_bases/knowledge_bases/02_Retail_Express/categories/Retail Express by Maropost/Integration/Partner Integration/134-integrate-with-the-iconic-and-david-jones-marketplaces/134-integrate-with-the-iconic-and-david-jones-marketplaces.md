# Integrate with The Iconic and David Jones Marketplaces

You can expand your Australian retail presence by integrating with The Iconic and David Jones marketplaces, two of Australia's leading premium retail channels, significantly improving your multi-channel sales potential. This helps optimize your retail operations by automating product synchronization, inventory management, and order fulfillment across these major ANZ marketplace platforms.

Connecting to premium Australian marketplaces through the Crossfire middleware solution enables automated data flow between **Retail Express** and these high-traffic sales channels. When you implement marketplace integrations, you can expect improved seller ratings through automated order processing, reduced operational costs from eliminating double-handling, and enhanced revenue opportunities from accessing premium customer segments in the Australian and New Zealand markets.

## Who is Crossfire?

Crossfire is a technology company that provides and manages API & EDI integration solutions.

Having in-depth experience with the Retail Express API solutions, Crossfire acts as a middleware platform to connect and enable data communication and processes between Retail Express and various other platforms.

Crossfire provides a fully managed service, taking care of the technical setup and configuration of EDI, ongoing monitoring and issue resolution, hosting, backups, and security.

## The Iconic Integration

The Iconic Seller Centre allows you to:

- List your products for sale on The Iconic
- Review product status and submit products for approval
- View any customers orders that are pending fulfilment
- View your Metrics & Seller Ratings

You can learn more about The Iconic Seller Centre [here](https://sellercenter.theiconic.com.au/academy_home).

## David Jones Online Integration

The David Jones Web Portal allows you to:

- List your products for sale on David Jones Online
- Review the status of your products
- View any customers orders that are pending fulfilment
- View your statistics & reports

You can learn more about David Jones Web Portal [here](http://davidjones.webportal.leadtec.com.au/Documentation/David Jones - Quick Reference Guide v1.4.pdf).
