## About RIOT RFID

By using the RIOT RFID & Retail Express integration you can complete your stocktaking digitally within minutes using RFID readers and RIOTs ground-breaking software app.

Automate traditionally manual data entry processes and ensure accurate inventory levels are captured and centralised.

The RIOT solution allows retailers to achieve 98%+ inventory accuracy and to complete stocktakes more regularly.

By having an accurate picture of what stock is in each location, you can ensure your omnichannel practices like Click & Collect and Ship From Store fulfilment for web orders run smoothly. Customers won't be upset when they place an order using these options and your store doesn't actually have stock available.

Then leverage the powerful inventory optimisation and automated replenishment features of Retail Express to minimise understocks and overstocks and save hours of purchasing admin.

You can also ensure that your store teams replenish your floor stock with inventory that is sitting in the back room. The RIOT app knows where all your stock is located and can highlight how you can quickly replenish items you've sold the day before with stock located elsewhere in your store.

## What is RFID?

Radio-frequency identification, or RFID, is a form of wireless technology that is encoded in smart tags and captured by a reader through radio waves.

An RFID system consists of:

1. Scanning antenna
2. Transceiver
3. Transponder

RFID is the most advanced technology for retail stores to use.

Inventory check-in, inventory counts, salesfloor replenishment, and product location can all be done in minutes by using RFID technology.
