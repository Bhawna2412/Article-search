# Integration - Retail Express & Oracle Netsuite

We are pleased to announce the direct integration between Retail Express and Oracle Netsuite ERP.

The Retail Express Oracle integration bi-directionally synchronises key data between two platforms in near real-time, enabling you to:

- Unify your B2B & B2C operations - wholesale, in-store and eCommerce
- Leverage all your stock across all channels to maximise sales
- Access powerful financial management and reporting capabilities

![blobid0 (14).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppPtIEAU.jpg)

## **Fully integrated solution**

The integration is a fully integrated solution supporting all standard Retail Express workflows, including:

- Orders synchronise between Retail Express and Netsuite to allow them to be fulfilled directly from your warehouse or dispatched to stores for Click & Collect
- Maintain optimal inventory levels within Netsuite with near real-time stock levels across stores and warehouses.
- Netsuite becomes the single source of truth for inventory management and pricing, synchronised to all locations
- Centralised discounting, refunds, exchanges, store credits, in-store laybys and payment methods

## **Bi-directional data synchronisation**

The integration offers a near real-time, bi-directional data synchronisation, with both on-demand and scheduled options available. The data synchronised includes:

- Vendors
- Sales
- Returns & refunds
- Fulfilment
- Transfers
