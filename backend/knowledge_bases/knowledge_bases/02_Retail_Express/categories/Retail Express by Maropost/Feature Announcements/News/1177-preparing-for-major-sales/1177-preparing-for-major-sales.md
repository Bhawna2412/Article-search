# Preparing for major sales

You can prepare your **Retail Express** operations for major sales events to ensure smooth order fulfillment and maintain customer satisfaction during high-volume periods. This preparation helps maximize sales opportunities while minimizing operational stress by systematically addressing inventory, staffing, and fulfillment requirements before promotional campaigns begin.

Preparing for major sales involves reviewing inventory levels, assessing fulfillment capacity, planning staff schedules, and establishing expedited workflows. When properly prepared, your operations typically lead to successful promotional execution, maintained customer service levels, and better ability to handle increased order volumes across retail channels.

# Preparing for major sales

Make sure you're fully prepared for Black Friday with these tips from the Retail Express Support Team.

## Plan ahead

Using Scheduled Pricing you can prepare in advance, giving you more time to focus on making sales. Select your time and date, which price to change and by how much (either by dollar or percentage) and more.

## Make noise

Grab your customer's attention with some promotional campaigns to advertise your sale. Retail Express offers integrations with a range of [third-party marketing platforms](https://www.retailexpress.com.au/pos-integrations#Marketing) so you can get results from your campaigns.

## Keep it simple

Make it easy for your customers! Automatically apply promotions at checkout, or give them a simple, easy-to-remember redemption code. Make sure there are no roadblocks on your web store preventing customers from checking out.

## Beat the shipping rush

Retail Express offers Click and Collect and Shippit integrations. Give your customers the confidence to purchase online by giving them control of their shipping options.

![mceclip6 (5).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppWSXEA2.jpg)

## Become a fulfilment ninja

Dispatch your sales faster and more efficiently than ever using the Retail Express Fulfilment Report. Get immediately notified of sales to action using the POS Alerts so you can be processing sales without delay.
