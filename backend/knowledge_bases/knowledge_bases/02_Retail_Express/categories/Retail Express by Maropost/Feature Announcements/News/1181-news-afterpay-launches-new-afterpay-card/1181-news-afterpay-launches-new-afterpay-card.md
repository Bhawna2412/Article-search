# News: Afterpay launches new "Afterpay Card"

Afterpay has launched a new method for customers to pay for sales using the new "AfterPay Card", replacing the previous barcode system.

![afterpay_logo.jpg](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppVgJEAU.jpg)
You can use either method with Retail Express. Once you have submitted your details to Afterpay for the new Afterpay Card you will be able to use both the card/pin-pad method and the former barcode method until you disable the Barcode method.

**Update:** Afterpay has announced Barcode payments will no longer be accepted after 30 September 2021.

## []()Using the new Afterpay Card

If you have been migrated to the new system by Afterpay already, you will just need to use your standard EFTPOS payment methods to process a payment (not the traditional Afterpay button within POS).

See the Afterpay Knowledge Base article for more information:[How does In-Store Afterpay work?](https://www.afterpay.com/en-AU/afterpay-card#:~:text=It’s really easy to shop in-store with Afterpay.)

## []()Reporting

You will need to use the [Afterpay Reconciliation Report](https://help.afterpay.com/hc/en-au/articles/217427506) to obtain a list of transactions paid via Afterpay.
