# Announcing the new Back Office Dashboard

You can access the new back office dashboard in **Retail Express** to gain improved visibility into your retail operations through enhanced reporting and analytics. This dashboard helps improve business decision making by providing real-time insights into sales performance, inventory levels, and operational metrics in a centralized, easy-to-use interface.

Using the new dashboard involves understanding available widgets, configuring display preferences, and leveraging real-time data for operational decisions. When you effectively utilize the dashboard, you can expect faster access to business insights, improved ability to identify trends, and better informed decision making across your retail operations.

# Announcing the new Back Office Dashboard

We're excited to announce the release of our new Back Office Dashboard.

The Dashboard is available right from Back Office, making it easier than ever to get the information you need quickly to make informed business decisions.

This feature will be available to all Retail Express users by **20th April 2022**.

## Highlights

### Trend Indicators

The new Trend Indicators highlight whether there has been a positive change (green) or negative change (red) for data comparison.

![DBIndicators2.gif](https://ress.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/4634393344783/DBIndicators2.gif)

### Comparative reporting

Filter by Instore vs Online sales, or use the Date Range options to compare all sales by previous periods or previous years.

![DBComparative.gif](https://ress.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/4634339605519/DBComparative.gif)

### Comprehensive range of reports

We've included the most vital and frequently- requested reports, so you can view your data easily with only a few clicks of the mouse.

![DBtabs.gif](https://ress.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/4634529123343/DBtabs.gif)

### Fulfilment Preferences

We've added a new tab to the dashboards - Fulfilments. On this tab, you'll be able to see at a glance whether your customers prefer Pickup or Delivery, and how that changes over time (great for anticipating peak sale periods).

![mceclip1.png](https://ress.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/4634419507983/mceclip1.png)

### Customer Reporting

The Customers tab provides a handy overview of your customers, including whether you're capturing customer data for sales ("Names" sales), the rate of repeat customers, and your top 20 customers by revenue.

![DBCustomers.gif](https://ress.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/4634509037583/DBCustomers.gif)

## Accessing the new Back Office Dashboard

To access the Dashboard click the "Dashboard" button from the menu at the top of Retail Express Back Office.
