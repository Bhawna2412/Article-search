# Retail Express joins Maropost

We're very excited to announce a major milestone for Retail Express, that promises to offer our valued customers even greater technology solutions, opportunities, and support into the future.

**Retail Express has agreed to be acquired by **[**Maropost**](https://www.maropost.com/)**,** the market leader in Marketing Automation, Ecommerce, Retail, Wholesale, and Service Desk software.

## About Maropost

Founded in 2011, and a Deloitte Technology Fast 500 company for multiple years running, Maropost offers a best-in-class cloud platform that helps retailers boost engagement, customer acquisition and revenue growth.

Along with Maropost's acquisition of Neto in 2020, and now Retail Express, Maropost is well on the way to delivering a retail value unlike any other vendor in the industry.

We know what it takes to run a successful retail business. We started our journey as retailers ourselves and set a vision to create the world's first cloud-based retail operating solution that truly solved the unique challenges of retail.

By joining Maropost, we're proud to now serve over 10,000 retail merchants with improved capacity to accelerate new innovative products and services.

**I, along with the CEO of Maropost, Ross Andrew Paquette, give you our assurance that your user experience will continue to align with Maropost's "Customer First" values and will continue to be our top priority.**

- Aaron Blackman, Founder and Head of Retail Express

## What happens now

We want to assure you that everything remains the same as it is today.

You and your team will still have:

- the same access to our **Australian-based expert retail support team**
- the same level of **service without disruption**
- continue to access** the same Retail Express software and integration capabilities** with your other software systems
- access to **innovative retail solutions** as they're released into the future

Please [contact our Support Team](https://ress.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/requests/new) at any time if you have any questions.

Of course, there will be a range of exciting new and additional products and services for you to consider as part of the extensive Maropost platform.

We thank you for your ongoing support in helping us to build an industry-leading omnichannel POS solution for Australian and New Zealand retailers. We're laser-focused on taking the value we deliver to your business to the next level as we join Maropost.
