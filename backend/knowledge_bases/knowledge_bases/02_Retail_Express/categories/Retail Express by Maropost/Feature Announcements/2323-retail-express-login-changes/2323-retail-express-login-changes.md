# Retail Express - Login Changes

As part of our ongoing Maropost Unified Login initiative, we're rolling out a refreshed login experience for Back Office. This marks the first step toward a more streamlined and secure login process across Maropost platforms.

**What's Changing:**

- You'll notice a **new look and feel** during login.
- The **password field is no longer visible initially**.
- After entering your **User ID** and clicking **Login**, you'll be redirected to a **Maropost-branded interim page** where you'll enter your full credentials before being taken to Back Office.
- This redirect is **expected and part of the new login flow**.

**Why It Matters:**

- This update lays the foundation for future enhancements, including **multi-factor authentication** and unified access across Maropost products.

**What You Need to Do:**

- Continue logging in as normal-no changes to your credentials or process.
- If you see the Maropost-branded page during login, rest assured it's part of the new experience. There is a screenshot below of the pages, so you'll know what to expect.

Stay tuned for further updates as we continue improving your login experience.
