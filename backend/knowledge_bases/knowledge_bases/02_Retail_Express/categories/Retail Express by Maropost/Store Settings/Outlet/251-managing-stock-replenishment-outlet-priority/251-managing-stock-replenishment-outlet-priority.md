# Managing Stock Replenishment Outlet Priority

You can optimize inventory distribution across your retail locations by configuring outlet priority settings that control how stock replenishment suggestions allocate inventory between stores. This helps ensure high-performing or strategic locations receive appropriate stock allocation, improving overall retail network inventory efficiency and reducing stockouts at priority locations while maintaining appropriate stock levels across your entire retail operations.

Setting up outlet replenishment priority based on sales velocity, strategic importance, or customer traffic patterns helps retail operators make better inventory allocation decisions across multi-store operations.

The Stock Replenishment Priority feature is used in conjunction with the Stock Replenishment via Transfer feature. The order of the Outlets in this list determines which Outlets will have their transfer requests fulfilled when insufficient stock is available for the entire replenishment run.

![stockreplenishment_outletpriority.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppTxrEAE.jpg)
For example, there are 100 units of a product available in the Warehouse.

- Bondi and Queen St Mall both request 70
- Bondi is higher in the list, so will receive the full 70
- Queen St Mall will receive the remaining 30

If the Outlet has a Minimum Replenishment Quantity configured the Transfer will only be created if the minimum quantity has been exceeded.

To rearrange the priority order:

1. **Click and hold the icon** in the first column
2. **Drag & drop** the Outlet to the new location
3. **Release** the mouse button
