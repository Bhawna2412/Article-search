# Duplicating Products

You can accelerate product catalog expansion through product duplication in **Retail Express**, which enables creating similar products by copying existing product configurations and modifying specific attributes. This efficient product creation method helps retail operators reduce data entry time for similar products, with strategic use of product duplication typically achieving faster catalog expansion and consistent product configuration across Australian retail operations.

Product duplication functionality in **Retail Express** enables retail operators to leverage existing product setup for new variants or similar items, helping maintain configuration consistency while reducing administrative effort. This duplication capability helps ensure your retail operations can efficiently expand product offerings while maintaining standardized product attributes and supporting accurate catalog management.

Find [**FAQs and related articles**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/455-duplicating-products#Faqs)** **at the bottom of this page

The ability to Duplicate a Product allows you to quickly and easily create new products based on existing product information within your database.

> **Note:** If you have a lot of products to duplicate we recommend using the Mass Download/Upload feature to easily copy information in an Excel spreadsheet and upload into Retail Express.

The duplicated Product will have the same Master information as the product it was copied from, excluding:

- Product ID
- Supplier SKU
- Product Type
- Inventory Levels
- Additional Barcodes

You will be prompted to enter the new Supplier SKU and select a Product Type prior to completing creation of the duplicate product.

## Duplicate a Product

1. Navigate to **Inventory > Duplicate a Product**
2. Enter the** Product ID** or **Supplier SKU **of the product you wish to duplicate
3. Click Search
  ![blobid0 (9).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000pownjEAA.jpg)
4. The **Results** tab will be updated with the information of the product that has been copied
  ![mceclip1 (34).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000pownoEAA.jpg)
5. Enter a unique **Supplier SKU**

> **Important:** After the product has been created the SupplierSKU2 field will match the original product - you will need to open the product and edit the Supplier SKU2 manually.

6. Update any other fields if required
7. Click **Duplicate Product**
8. A **link will be displayed **along with the new Product ID
  ![mceclip2 (22).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000powntEAA.jpg)
9. **Click the link **to open the new product
10. Change the **Supplier SKU2 **field
11. Click **Save Changes**

### [Can I delete duplicated products?](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/455-duplicating-products#delete-duplicate)

Products cannot be deleted once they have been created, however they can be disabled. Products can be disabled individually, using Product Search and Management or through the Mass Upload feature. Refer to the Inventory - Disable Products article for more information.

> **Note:** To exclude disabled products from reports you can rename the products to "Disabled" then assign them to a unique Product Type and Supplier e.g. Disabled. You may need to create the Product Type and Supplier first.
