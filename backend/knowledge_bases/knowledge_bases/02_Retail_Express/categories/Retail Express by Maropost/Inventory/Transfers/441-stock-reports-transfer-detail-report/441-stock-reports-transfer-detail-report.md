# Stock Reports - Transfer Detail Report

Find [**related articles**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/441-stock-reports-transfer-detail-report#Faqs)** **at the bottom of this page

The Transfer Detail Report is used to review all Products associated with internal transfers. View the Transfer status, stock quantities at each stage of transfer, any associated customer order number and more.

![reports_transferdetailreportresults.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppiAuEAI.jpg)

> **Note:** The Transfer Detail Report contains a lot of information - scroll right to view all data.

To Access the Transfer Detail Report:

1. Navigate to:** Reports **>** Stock Reports **>** Transfer Detail Report**
2. Make selections from the **Filters **as required
  ![reports_transferdetailreport.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppiAzEAI.jpg)
3. Click **Search**
4. **Scroll right **to view all columns of the report
5. 
  ![User-added image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/reportstransferdetailreportresultsscroll21.05T5g00000qWtUDEA0.gif)
6. Click the **Column heading arrows **to sort the column
7. To **save/export **the report click the **Save **icon and select the desired output e.g. Excel
  ![reports_transferdetailreport_export.gif](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000pphQiEAI.jpg)
