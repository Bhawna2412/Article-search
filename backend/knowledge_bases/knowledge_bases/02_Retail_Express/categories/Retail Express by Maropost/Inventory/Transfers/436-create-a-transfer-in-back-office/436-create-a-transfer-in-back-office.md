# Create a Transfer in Back Office

You can create inter-store transfers efficiently through the Product Search method in **Retail Express** Back Office, enabling systematic stock movement between locations with full control over transfer quantities and dispatch status. This transfer creation approach helps retail operators coordinate inventory distribution across their store network, with the ability to create multiple transfers simultaneously supporting efficient multi-location stock replenishment.

The Product Search transfer method in **Retail Express** provides Australian retail operators with flexible filtering options and immediate transfer processing capabilities, helping streamline inventory distribution and maintain optimal stock levels across outlets and warehouses. This systematic transfer workflow helps ensure your retail operations can quickly respond to stock imbalances while maintaining accurate tracking through printed transfer slips and comprehensive transfer reporting.

A Transfer is used to move stock from one location to another location, tracking the status of stock through each phase. Retail Express offers both Simple and Advanced options (depending on the level of detail required), and the ability to transfer to multiple different locations at the same time.

To create a Transfer:

1. Navigate to **Inventory > ****Transfer Management > Create**
  ![createitoman.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000povvNEAQ.jpg)
2. Choose the **Transferred By** name (name of the staff member creating the Transfer)
3. Select the Transfer Method "Product Search"
  **Related:** Inventory - Create a Transfer using an Excel Upload
4. Choose the **Outlet From** location  (the location where the stock is being taken from)
5. Select the **Outlet To **location (the store/warehouse where the stock is to be transferred to)

> **Note:** Hold the Ctrl button on the keyboard to select multiple locations - this will create multiple transfers

6. Use the filters in the **Product Search Fields**
7. Click **Search**
8. Enter the amount to be transferred in the **Tfr** column
  ![mceclip0 (22).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000povvSEAQ.jpg)
9. Click **Add to Transfer** at the bottom of the screen
  ![mceclip1 (8).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000povvXEAQ.jpg)
10. The product will be displayed on the **Finalise **tab
11. Click the **Search **tab and repeat the steps to add additional products
12. To complete the transfer click the **Finalise **tab
13. To instantly process the Transfer select the **Dispatch **status of either Dispatch or Make Available

> **Note:** Click here For more information on the Advanced method and Transfer Stages

14. Click **Create Transfers**
15. The Transfers Report will be displayed showing the newly created Transfers
  ![mceclip2 (11).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000povvcEAA.jpg)
16. Click the **Print** button next to the Transfer to print the **Transfer Slip**
17. Click the **Pencil **icon to edit the Transfer
