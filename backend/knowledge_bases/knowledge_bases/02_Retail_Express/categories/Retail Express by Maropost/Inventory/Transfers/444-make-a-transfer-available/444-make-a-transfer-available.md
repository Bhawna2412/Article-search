# Make a Transfer Available

You can complete the transfer workflow by making received stock available for sale in **Retail Express**, which finalizes the transfer process and updates inventory to saleable status at the destination outlet. This final transfer stage helps retail operators ensure transferred stock is properly accounted for and ready for customer transactions, with timely stock availability processing supporting improved sales opportunities and inventory accuracy across Australian retail operations.

The Make Available transfer stage in **Retail Express** enables retail operators to systematically convert received transfer stock into available inventory, helping ensure accurate stock levels and supporting seamless sales operations at destination outlets. This transfer completion workflow helps ensure your retail operations maintain efficient inventory turnover while providing full transfer lifecycle tracking and supporting accurate stock management across your store network.

The **"Make Available" stage** represents the final step in the **Advanced Transfer process**, marking the completion of the stock movement from one location to another within the inventory system. At this stage, the stock that was transferred is fully processed and updated in the system as ready for sale.

This ensures that the inventory is no longer classified as "in transit" or "pending" and is now considered part of the active stock at the destination outlet.

The "Make Available" stage is the last phase of the Advanced Transfer process, where the stock has been received and is available to sell.

## Make a Transfer Available

To make a Transfer Available:

1. Navigate to **Inventory > Transfer Management > Make Available**
  ![blobid2 (3).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppRYCEA2.jpg)
2. Update the **Filters** as required
3. Click **Search**
4. The results will be displayed
5. Use the Column headings to sort the results before entering quantities
  Sorting of the columns after you have begun to enter quantities will erase the entered quantities
6. Update the quantity for the Transfer by either: 
	- Scan product barcodes directly into the PLU Scan field (each scan will increase the "Made Available" quantity by 1)
	- Enter quantities directly into the Made Available field
	- Click **Copy Across** to process in bulk (the Received Qty is copied into the Made Available field)
7. Click **Save Changes**

## Filters

Most of the filtering fields are self explanatory, however see the detailed information below for the more advanced fields.

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| Transfer Number | Entering a transfer number in this field will ignore all other search options. A transfer number that is not available to be moved to the Allocated status will return no results. |
| Transfer Type | Limit the results to only the ticked Transfer Type. Multiple options can be ticked |
