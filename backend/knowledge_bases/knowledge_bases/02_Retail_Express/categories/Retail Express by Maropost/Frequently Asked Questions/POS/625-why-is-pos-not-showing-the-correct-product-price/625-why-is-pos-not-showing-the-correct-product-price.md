# Why is POS not showing the correct product price?

You can troubleshoot pricing display issues in Retail Express to ensure customers see accurate prices during checkout, helping your retail operations maintain pricing integrity and customer trust. This capability helps identify why prices may display incorrectly in POS, which helps ensure transactions process at the intended prices and prevents customer confusion or disputes.

Setting up proper pricing configuration and validation enables your retail operations to maintain price accuracy, helping optimize customer confidence and reduce pricing errors. This helps retail businesses ensure consistent pricing across all sales channels while resolving issues that could impact customer satisfaction or revenue.
