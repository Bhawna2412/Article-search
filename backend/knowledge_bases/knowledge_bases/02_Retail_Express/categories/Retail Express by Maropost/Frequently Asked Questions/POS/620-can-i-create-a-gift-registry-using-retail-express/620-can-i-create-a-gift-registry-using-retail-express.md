# Can I create a Gift Registry using Retail Express?

You can implement gift registry functionality in Retail Express to provide customers with convenient wish list services for special occasions, helping your retail operations capture additional sales opportunities and improve customer service. This capability enables customers to create registries for weddings, baby showers, or other events, which helps drive sales while providing a valuable customer service that builds loyalty.

Setting up gift registry features enables your retail operations to offer services that compete with larger retailers, helping optimize customer engagement and event-based sales. This helps retail businesses expand their value proposition by providing customers with convenient registry management while creating opportunities for gift purchasers to shop at your stores.

Using Retail Express you can offer gift registries to your customer's for baby showers, weddings, parties and other events.

The registry will be created in POS as a sale, allowing you to view the Registry in both POS and the Fulfilment Report for advanced monitoring. As products are purchased you'll be able to remove them from the registry and sell them using a regular sale in POS.

## **Setting up the Registry**

1. Setup a new Delivery Zone called "Gift Registry"
2. **Create a sale** in POS
3. **Add the Registry products** to the sale
4. Update the **Fulfilment Method** to **Layby/Pickup**
  ![User-added image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/Screenshot20241106at72805PM.05TJ100000LV2DmMAL.png)
5. Click the **Customer** tab
6. Add the Customer Details (the recipient of the registry)
7. Click the **Fulfilment **tab
8. Click **Bulk Edit All**
9. Set the **Delivery Zone **to "Gift Registry"
  ![User-added image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/Screenshot20241106at73643PM.05TJ100000LV2INMA1.png)
10. Enter the **Due Date **as the date of the party/event the Registry is for
11. Click the **Invoice **tab
12. Tick the **Quote **button in the top right-hand corner to save the sale as a quote (optional)
  Marking the sale as a Quote will prevent the stock from being Allocated (reducing the Available quantity to sell)
13. Provide the Customer with the **Invoice **number to use as a Registry Number for the details to be provided to their friends and relatives

## Selling an Item from the Registry

To sell a product from the Registry you'll need to open the Registry sale in POS (to remove the products), before creating a new sale for the person purchasing the items.

1. **Open the Registry sale** in POS using the POS Lookup feature
  You can use the Invoice Number or Customer Details
2. Click the **Qty **column and change the **quantity to 0 **for the products to be purchased
3. Click **Update**
4. Create a **New Sale **for the customer purchasing the products
5. Click the **Invoice **tab
6. Enter a **Comment **noting the Gift Registry number for refunds/exchange purposes
7. **Finalise** the sale as usual

## View an open Registry

You can review the original registry list by:

- Opening the sale in POS
- Using the Fulfilment Report

### POS

To open the Registry sale in POS:

1. Open POS
2. Click **Lookup**
3. Using the Filters to locate the sale
4. Click **Open**

![User-added image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/Screenshot20241106at74118PM.05TJ100000LV2JBMA1.png)

### Fulfilment Report

The Fulfilment Report has an extensive number of Filters. This can come in handy, especially when using the Due Date to track the date of the event. You will be able to filter by any historical dates to see if the Registry sale should be closed.

1. Navigate to **Inventory > Fulfilment Report**
2. Select the **Delivery Zone **of "Gift Registry"
3. Change the **Order Status **filter to be Quote (if the sale was not ticked as a Quote this step can be skipped)
  ![User-added image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/Screenshot20241106at74340PM.05TJ100000LV2JQMA1.png)
4. Update the **Ordered From/To **date range if required
5. Click **Search**
6. Any products that remain on an outstanding Gift Registry will be displayed
	Any products that were purchased had their quantity reduced to zero and are no longer outstanding
7. The **Del Due Date **column displays the Due Date from the sale (i.e. the date of the event if entered)
8. To cancel the Registry sale click the **E **button
  Click here for detailed information on voiding sales
