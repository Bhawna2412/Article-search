# Why are there buttons missing in POS?

You can troubleshoot POS interface issues in Retail Express to restore full functionality quickly, helping your retail operators maintain access to all necessary transaction features. This capability helps identify why interface elements may be hidden or unavailable, which helps ensure your team can complete all transaction types without unnecessary delays or workarounds.

Setting up proper POS configuration and troubleshooting knowledge enables your retail operations to maintain consistent system functionality, helping optimize operational efficiency and user experience. This helps retail businesses ensure all POS features remain accessible to authorized users while resolving interface issues that could impact transaction processing.

If you're experiencing issues with buttons missing from POS, the issues are typically caused by the screen needing a refresh or the display settings on the monitor are incorrect. For example:

- Menu or header missing from Back Office
- Buttons missing from the bottom of Cash Up or when creating a sale

To resolve the issue please try the following steps:

1. **Refresh **the screen by pressing **Ctrl** + **F5** on the keyboard
2. **Resize the screen **by pressing **Ctrl** + the **+** or **-** buttons
3. Check the **Resolution size**
