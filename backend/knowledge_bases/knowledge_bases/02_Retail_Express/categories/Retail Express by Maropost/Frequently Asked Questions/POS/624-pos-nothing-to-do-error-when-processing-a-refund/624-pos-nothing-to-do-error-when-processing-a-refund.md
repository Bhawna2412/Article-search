# POS - "Nothing to Do" error when processing a Refund

You can resolve refund processing errors in Retail Express by understanding the specific requirements for return transactions, helping your retail operators complete customer refunds efficiently. This capability explains how to address error messages that occur during refund processing, which helps ensure your team can handle returns smoothly and maintain positive customer experiences.

Setting up proper return processing procedures enables your retail operations to handle refunds systematically, helping optimize customer service and financial accuracy. This helps retail businesses maintain professional return handling by ensuring staff understand how to process refunds correctly and resolve common error conditions.

If you are trying to perform a refund and encounter an error "Nothing to Do" it's likely there is already another Refund in progress for the same sale. The original refund will be marked as "Incomplete", most likely because the sale is On Hold, or the browser was closed before the sale was finalised.

![Refund.JPG](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppW41EAE.jpg)
To resolve the issue:

1. Click the **Look up** button from the main window of POS
  ![mceclip0 - 2023-07-08T232007.995.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppW46EAE.jpg)
2. Enter your **User ID**
3. Click **Search **in the "Search Sales Orders, Invoices & Quotes" section
  ![mceclip1 (78).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppW4BEAU.jpg)
4. Click the **Status **filter
5. Tick **Incomplete**
  ![mceclip2 (48).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppW21EAE.jpg)
6. Use any other **Filters** if required
7. Click **OK**
8. Refunds will be indicated by the little R in the circle
  ![mceclip3 (32).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppW4GEAU.jpg)
9. Click **Open **to open the refund and finalise the sale
