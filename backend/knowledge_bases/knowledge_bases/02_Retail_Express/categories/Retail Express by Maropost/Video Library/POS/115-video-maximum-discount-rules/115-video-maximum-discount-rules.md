# Video - Maximum Discount Rules

In this video we show you how the Maximum Discount Rules apply within POS. For example, if a staff member tries to discount a product beyond the maximum discount rule an alert will be displayed. If required, staff members can be configured with permissions to override the maximum discount rule e.g. Store Managers.

> **Note:** For more information on configuring Max Discount Rules refer to the Maximum Discount Rules article.
