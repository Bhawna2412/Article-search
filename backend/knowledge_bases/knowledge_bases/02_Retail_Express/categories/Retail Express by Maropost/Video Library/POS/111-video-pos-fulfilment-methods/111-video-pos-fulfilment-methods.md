# Video - POS Fulfilment Methods

In this video we explain the different Fulfilment Methods within POS. These Fulfilment Methods give you greater inventory control with regards to the fulfilment of your sales. The available methods are:

- Cash & Carry (default)
- Layby & Pickup
- Delivery
- Warehouse Pickup
