# Money In / Out

You can maintain accurate cash drawer records through Money In/Out functionality in **Retail Express** POS, which enables tracking non-sales cash movements like expenses, petty cash, or bank deposits. This cash management capability helps retail operators maintain accurate till balances and support financial accountability, with effective money in/out tracking typically improving cash reconciliation accuracy and reducing discrepancies across Australian retail operations.

Money In/Out functionality in **Retail Express** enables retail operators to record cash drawer adjustments with proper documentation and tracking, helping ensure accurate end-of-day reconciliation and supporting audit trails. This cash tracking helps ensure your retail operations maintain accurate cash records while supporting financial accountability and simplifying daily cash-up processes.

The Money In / Out feature allows you to track banking and petty cash withdrawals. Any amount you withdraw from the till using the Money In / Out feature will automatically reduce the expected amount in the till when you perform a Cash Up.

## []()Before you begin

Before you can use the Money In/Out feature you will need to ensure you have configured the appropriate Security Permissions

1. Open Back Office
2. Navigate to **Staff > Users > POS Security Features**
3. Choose the **Security Profile** you want to allow access to Money Out

> **Note:** All staff users assigned to this profile will have the same access

4. Look for the option titled ?Function ? Allow Money Out? and tick the check box to the right
  ![mceclip0 - 2023-07-08T215234.421.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppVJtEAM.jpg)
5. Click the ?Update? button at the bottom of the page
6. Login to POS again and any user assigned to the profile you edited in step 2 will be able to use their login when they click on the Money Out feature tile on the main sale login page

## []()Withdraw Money

1. Log into POS
2. Click **Money In/Out**
3. Enter your** User ID**
4. The Money Out feature is split into 2 tabs; by default the **Take Money** tab is selected
  ![mceclip2 (43).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppUdKEAU.jpg)
5. Select the **Transaction Type **of "Take Money Out"
6. Select a **Reason**

> **Note:** You can enter custom Money Out reasons in Back Office by navigating to **Settings > POS Settings > Money In/Out Reasons**

7. Choose the **Cash****Tender Type**
8. Enter some **comments** to describe the reason for the withdrawal (optional)
9. Enter the **amount** you are taking out of the till
10. Tick **Taxable **if required
11. Click **Save**
12. Click **OK **to the warning
13. The withdrawal will be processed and display a **Confirmation message **next to the **Save **button:
  ![mceclip3 (29).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppVK8EAM.jpg)
14. Click **Open Register **to kick the cash drawer
15. Click **Done**

## []()View Previous Withdrawals

1. Log into POS
2. Click **Money In/Out**
3. Enter your** User ID**
4. Click the **Report **tab
  ![mceclip4 (17).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppUzGEAU.jpg)
5. By default the **Date** is set from the date you last completed a cash up on that register until the current date
6. Use the **Filters **to refine your search
7. A list of the transactions will be displayed
  ![mceclip5 (10).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppVKDEA2.jpg)
8. Click **Done **to remove to the main window of POS

## []()View Withdrawals During Cash Up

1. Follow the standard Cash Up process
2. In **Step 2 ? Cash Summary** the?Cash Removed from Register? is deducted from the ?Cash Payments Received? to give you an expected cash amount in ?Cash Expected?
  ![mceclip6 (4).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppVLpEAM.jpg)
3. Click the ?pen Money Out link to open the Money Out report in a window

> **Note:** Your Cash Up will not be put On Hold or Void.

  ![mceclip7 (5).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppVMYEA2.jpg)
4. Click **Done** to return to your Cash Up
5. In **Step 3 ? Reconcile your Other Payment Methods** if you have withdrawn any Cheque amounts from your till using Money Out, the Amount Expected will automatically deduct that amount.
  E.g. I have taken $1000 in cheque payments today but banked $500 of that using Money Out, the Amount Expected will be $500.
6. In  **Step 4 ? Summary** you will find the same ?Open Money Out? link at the bottom of the Cash Counted and Other Payment Method columns:
  ![rtaImage (55).jpeg](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppVMdEAM.jpg)
