# POS Themes

You can customize the visual appearance of your Retail Express POS interface with themes, helping create a comfortable working environment for your retail staff and reinforcing brand identity. This capability allows you to adjust colors and visual elements to match your brand or improve screen readability, which helps reduce eye strain during long shifts and creates a more cohesive brand experience.

Setting up appropriate POS themes enables your retail operations to create an interface that supports staff productivity while reflecting your business identity. This helps retail businesses maintain visual consistency across all customer-facing and operational systems while ensuring the POS remains comfortable for extended daily use.

POS Themes offer a flexible way to personalize the look and feel of your point-of-sale system, ensuring it aligns seamlessly with your store's branding and aesthetics. With options to adjust colors, fonts, and layouts, you can create a cohesive brand experience that reflects your identity.

This article provides a step-by-step guide to configuring POS Themes and tailoring them to enhance your store's visual appeal.

> **Important:** Users will need to have the security permission "Allow Change Theme".

To change the POS Theme:

1. Click **POS **to open POS
2. Enter valid **User Account **details
3. Select the **Register** you would like to change the Theme for
4. Click **Login**
5. Click **Change Theme** (displayed in the top right-hand corner)
  ![pos_changetheme.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppW8UEAU.jpg)
6. Enter a valid **User ID**
7. Select a Theme
  ![mceclip0 - 2023-07-08T234914.581.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppW5aEAE.jpg)
8. Click **Done**
9. Log out of POS to refresh the settings; the new theme will be displayed when you log back in

> **Note:** Closing the browser tab will not log you out of POS, and the changes will not be applied.

You will be returned to the main POS screen. Log out and in again to begin using the new theme.
