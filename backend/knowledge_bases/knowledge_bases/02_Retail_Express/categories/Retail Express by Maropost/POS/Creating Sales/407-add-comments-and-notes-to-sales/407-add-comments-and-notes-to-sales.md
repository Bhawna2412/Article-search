# Add Comments and Notes to Sales

You can add comments and notes to sales transactions to document special instructions, customer requests, or order details, helping ensure accurate fulfillment and clear communication. This improves your retail operations by providing transaction-level documentation, with properly configured sales notes typically reducing fulfillment errors and improving customer satisfaction through accurate order execution.

Understanding sales comment functionality involves recognizing internal versus customer-facing notes, visibility settings, and fulfillment communication to help retail operators focus on the right order documentation workflows, ensuring your retail strategy consistently drives better order accuracy and customer satisfaction outcomes.

Comments can be entered against a sale for reference later down the track, for example, if the customer was advised of certain policies, or notes for the customer to refer to regarding the product they purchased.

There are two types of comments that can be entered via POS:

- Public Comments (displayed on invoices for the customer)
- Private Comments (internal only)

You can also add comments directly to the Customer Account via Back Office.

Add Default Comments (Refund Policy etc) via **Settings > POS Settings > Invoice & Receipt Settings > Default Invoice Comments.**

## **Adding a Public Comment to a sale**

To enter comments against a sale:

1. Open POS
2. Create a sale by adding products and a customer, and any other requirements of the sale
3. Click the Invoice Tab
4. Click the Comments button under the Invoice tab
5. Enter the information as required
6. Click Done

> **Note:** To view comments made in one or more sales in POS from the Back Office navigate to Inventory > Fulfilment Report. Use the filters to search sales and click Search. The comments (both Public and Private) will be displayed in the Comments column.

## **Adding a Private Comment to a sale**

Customer Comments will always be visible in every sales transaction that this customer makes.

To enter a Customer Comment:

1. Open POS
2. Create a sale by adding products and a customer, and any other requirements of the sale
3. Click the Invoice Tab
4. Click the Comments button under the Invoice tab
5. Click the Customer Comments tab
6. Enter the information as required
7. Click Add Comment
8. Click Done

> **Note:** Historical Customer Comments are displayed under the New Comment input field.
