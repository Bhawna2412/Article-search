# Create a Sale

You can streamline your retail transaction processing in Retail Express by mastering the core sale creation workflow, which helps your retail operators complete customer purchases quickly and accurately. This fundamental capability enables efficient cash-and-carry transactions with intuitive product search, flexible payment processing, and automated receipt generation, which helps improve checkout speed and customer satisfaction during peak sales periods.

Setting up proper POS sale workflows enables your retail team to handle high transaction volumes while maintaining accuracy, helping optimize point-of-sale operations and reduce customer wait times. This helps retail operations achieve better efficiency by giving operators quick access to product information, payment processing, and invoice management all within a single streamlined interface.

Using the Retail Express POS it's possible to create a very simple and fast sales transaction without using any of the advanced features within POS. This is useful for standard **cash and carry **transactions, where there are no customer details and the order has been paid in full.

**Before you Begin**: You will need to log into POS using a valid User ID and Password. The User ID can either be entered by using your keyboard or if you have your User ID printed as a barcode, swipe or scan your code.

## Create a Quick Sale

To quickly create a sale within POS:

Each tab is described in more detail below

1. In the **New Sale **field enter your **User ID**
2. A new blank Sales Transaction window will be displayed
3. [Add Products](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/560-create-a-sale#product) to the sale on the Products tab
4. [Take payment](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/560-create-a-sale#payments)** **for the sale on the Payments Tab
5. [Finalise the sale](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/560-create-a-sale#finalise)
6. [Print a receipt/invoice](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/560-create-a-sale#print)** **on the Invoice tab, or [email](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/560-create-a-sale#email) a copy of the invoice to the customer
7. **Close **the window to return to the main window of POS ready for the next sale

See below for detailed information on each step.

## Product Tab

When you first open a blank sale within the POS, the **Products **tab is the first page you will see. To add products to the sale you have the following options:

- PLU Scan field
- Product Search
- Quick Products (refer to the Quick Products article for more information on this functionality)

> **Note:** If a product added to the sale has Serial Numbers enabled, POS will prompt the operator to record serial numbers depending on the product’s configuration. 
> 
> **For more details on serial number capture, **[**click here**](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/2373-serial-number-recording-at-pos?)**.**

Typically in a standard quick sale, the first two options are used:

#### [PLU Scan](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/560-create-a-sale#products-pluscan)

To use the PLU scan field you can use any of the following options:

- Type the **Product Code **and click Enter
  ![pos_addproduct1.gif](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000povmLEAQ.jpg)
- **Scan the barcode** for the product
- **Type the partial product code** to display a drop-down of matching options (the more characters typed the more refined the results will be), **click the appropriate product** or use the **down arrow** on the keyboard and press **Enter**
- You can use** '**Core Products' Checkbox to filter only core products as search results

#### [Product Search](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/560-create-a-sale#products-search)

If you don't have a code to enter you can use the **Search **field to search for the product instead. To use the search functionality:

1. Click into the **Search** field
2. Begin typing into the field - this can be part or all of the **Product Short Description**
3. As the characters are entered the predictive search will begin to display results to select from - the more characters entered the more refined the results will be
  **Did you Know?**: You don't need to type in consecutive letters in the search, the feature will also work with partial letters from different words in the Short Description. For example, to find a product called "Business Shirt" typing the letters "Bus Shi" will still display the "Business Shirt" in the results.
4. Use the **Down Arrow **on the keyboard to select the product and press **Enter** or **click on the product** using a mouse
5. The product will be added to the sale

Using the **Product Search **feature it's possible to view additional information about the product, including:

- Product attributes - size, colour, brand
- Available Inventory (the total number of products available to be sold *not including* products committed to other sales/transfers etc)
- Total Inventory on Hand (the total number of products *including *those committed to other sales/transfers)
- Price

**Removing products:**

If you make a mistake and need to remove a product from the sale:

1. **Click on the item row** to highlight the product
2. Click the **Trash Can **icon on the far right of the column headings

> **Note:** Click the Trash Can icon without selecting any products to bulk delete all products from the sale.

## Take a Payment

To take payment/s for the sale:

1. Click on the **Payment **tab
2. The bottom right will display the **Order Total **and **Balance Due**
3. If taking **cash payment/s **click on the icons for the notes received (press repeated for multiple notes) e.g. if the customer provides a $5 note, clicking $5 four times will add $20 in cash
  ![POSPayments_CashTender.gif](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000povmaEAA.jpg)
4. For other payment types including **EFTPOS/Credit Card or Finance**, you can either
	- Click on the** arrow** under the payment type to add the value to the input box, then click it again to add the payment to the sale

	- Click in the **white input box** for the payment type > **type in a value **> click **Enter** or the **Pay **button
  ![POSPayments_tendertype_arrow (1).gif](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000povmfEAA.jpg)

  ![POSPayments_tendertype.gif](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000povMdEAI.jpg)
5. As payments are recorded they will be displayed on the right-hand side of the screen, and the Balance Due will be updated
6. Add any remaining payments until the **Balance Due **is zero
7. To **delete a payment** click on the payment line on the right-hand side and click the **Trash Can **icon

**Did you Know?**: Retail Express offers [integrated payment solutions](https://galaxy.maropost.com/s/retail-article), including **Tyro** and **PC EFTPOS/Linkly**.

## Finalise the Sale

To finalise the sale:

1. Click the **Finalise** button
2. If the **Balance Due **value is $0 the sale will be finalised (if there is a balance remaining you will be prompted to either enter a customer for a Layby or add any additional payments)
3. Press **Close **to close the window and return to the main window of POS ready for the next sale

![mceclip9 (1).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000povmkEAA.jpg)
You can select to bypass the Invoice tab when the Finalise button is pressed (which completes the sale and returns to the main window of POS ready for the next sale). This feature can save a lot of time during peak sales periods; refer to the POS Order Settings article for more information.

## Print a Receipt or Invoice

To manually print a Receipt or invoice:

**Auto-print receipts: **refer to the Invoice and Receipt Settings article

1. Click on the **Invoice **tab
2. A preview of the invoice will be displayed
3. To print an invoice click the **Print Invoice **button
4. To print a receipt click the **Print Receipt **button

If one of the options is greyed out you may not have an appropriate printer installed. Refer to the Receipt Printer article for more information.

## Email an Invoice

Using POS you can also email an invoice, rather than print a physical copy. The invoice is attached as a PDF to the email, with the body of the email customisable through the use of POS Email Templates. This includes the ability to have a personal greeting using the customer name.

**Related:**POS Settings - Email Templates

To send the Invoice as an email:

1. Click the **Invoice **tab of the order within POS
2. Select either the **Invoice, Dispatch **or **Delivery **tab depending on the type of email and Invoice to be sent
3. Navigate to the **Email **section of the order on the bottom right-hand corner:
  ![pos_orderemailpos.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000povmuEAA.jpg)
4. Select a **Template **from the drop-down list
5. The **To **email will automatically update with the customer's email address, this can be overwritten if required
6. Enter a **CC **to include any additional recipients
7. Enter any additional text in the **Email Message **field

> **Note:** The default text is configured in the Email Template

8. Click **Preview **to see a preview of the email
9. Click **Send Email**

The email will be sent to the customer with the invoice attached as a PDF.
