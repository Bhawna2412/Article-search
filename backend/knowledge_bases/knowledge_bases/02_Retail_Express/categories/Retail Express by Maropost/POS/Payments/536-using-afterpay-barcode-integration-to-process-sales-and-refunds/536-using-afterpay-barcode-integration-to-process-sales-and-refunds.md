# Using Afterpay Barcode Integration to process Sales and Refunds

You can streamline Afterpay transactions through barcode integration in **Retail Express**, which enables processing Afterpay sales and refunds using simplified barcode scanning workflows for efficient payment handling. This payment integration helps retail operators reduce transaction complexity and processing time, with effective Afterpay barcode integration typically improving checkout efficiency and supporting seamless buy-now-pay-later adoption across Australian retail operations.

Afterpay barcode integration in **Retail Express** enables retail operators to process Afterpay payments and refunds through systematic barcode workflows, helping ensure efficient transaction processing and accurate payment reconciliation. This integration capability helps ensure your retail operations can efficiently handle Afterpay transactions while maintaining simple staff workflows and reliable payment processing.

![afterpay_integration.jpg](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppiwjEAA.jpg)
Afterpay is available as a direct integration with Retail Express for in-store transactions. With Retail Express, customers can process a live payment at POS. Customers will need to sign up with Afterpay using the mobile app and have their mobile logged in ready to process a new transaction.

> **Note:** The Afterpay Barcode integration is being phased out by Afterpay. Click here for information on using the new Afterpay Card payment method.

## Perform an Afterpay Payment at POS

Afterpay can be used to process either the entire payment, or as partial payment where multiple tender types are used e.g. both Afterpay and a cash payment.

Once the integration is enabled manual payments using the Afterpay Payment Type will not be available - selecting the Afterpay payment method within POS will prompt the integrated payment process.

[Processing an Afterpay Payment](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/536-using-afterpay-barcode-integration-to-process-sales-and-refunds#afterpay-process-payments)

**Step 1:** Click the **Payment **tab at POS![pos_paymentstab.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppix8EAA.jpg)

**Step 2:** The customer logs into their Afterpay account (or creates a new account) on the mobile app![afterpay_mobileapp_login.jpg](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppiXfEAI.jpg)

**Step 3:** In POS click the **Afterpay ****arrow icon **twice (this will load the amount of the payment and submit it, prompting the integration process)![pos_afterpay_arrow.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppixSEAQ.jpg)

**Step 4:** The customer clicks the **Barcode **tab; they will see a maximum available to spend and a barcode.![afterpay_mobileapp_barcodebutton.jpg](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppixXEAQ.jpg)

**Step 5:** The customer presents the **Barcode **to the sales assistant![afterpay_mobileapp_barcode.jpg](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppitREAQ.jpg)

**Step 6:** The Sales Assistant enters/scans the barcode into POS and clicks **Confirm**![afterpay_barcoderequest.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppixwEAA.jpg)

**Step 7:** A Processing Transaction message is displayed![afterpay_processing.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppiy1EAA.jpg)

**Step 8:** Once complete click **Finish**![afterpay_transactioncomplete.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppiY9EAI.jpg)

**Step 9:** The Customer can view their purchase on the Afterpay App![afterpay_payment_dashboard.jpg](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppifFEAQ.jpg)

**Step 10:** POS will return to the Payment tab and the Afterpay payment will be displayed![pos_afterpay_paymenthistory.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppizJEAQ.jpg)

**Step 11:** The Afterpay payment and reference number is displayed in the Payment History on the invoice![pos_invoice_afterpaypaymenthistoryreference.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppieDEAQ.jpg)

**Step 12:** Click **Finalise **to finish processing the sale in POS![pos_finalisebutton.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppizTEAQ.jpg)

[Process an Afterpay payment (multiple tenders)](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/536-using-afterpay-barcode-integration-to-process-sales-and-refunds#afterpay-multiple-tender)

POS supports multiple Payment Types being used within a single transaction. Afterpay is used to make a partial payment against a transaction with the remainder paid using an alternate payment type e.g. half of the sale paid with Afterpay, half paid with cash.

> **Note:** Afterpay does not support multiple Afterpay payments in a single invoice i.e. two separate Afterpay accounts, or multiple separate payments from the same account. Any other payment type can be used in conjunction with Afterpay to process the full amount of the order.

## **To process a partial payment:**

The process is the same as above, however instead of clicking twice on the Afterpay arrow to prompt the integration:

**Step 1:** Click the **Afterpay arrow icon **to load the amount outstanding![afterpay_pos_payment_amountoutstanding.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppj0lEAA.jpg)

**Step 2:** Click into the field and change the value to the amount to be paid via Afterpay![afterpay_pos_payment_manualinput.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppiJ1EAI.jpg)

**Step 3:** Click the **Pay** button or press **Enter **to prompt the Afterpay integration![afterpay_barcoderequest (1).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppj1tEAA.jpg)

**Step 4:** Continue the steps in the previous section to process a payment leaving an outstanding balance![pos_payments_afterpayoutstandingbalance.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppiRNEAY.jpg)

**Step 5:** Once the Afterpay payment has been processed use the remaining tender types to process the outstanding balance![pos_payments_cash.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppj2DEAQ.jpg)

**Step 6:** Both payments will be displayed in the payment history![pos_afterpay_multipletender.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppj2IEAQ.jpg)

> **Note:** Afterpay payments cannot be edited within POS, only refunded. This is due to the payment already having been verified with the merchant (and is the same with all integrated payments within Retail Express). The date field displayed in the payment history above indicates the payment has been verified and committed; the cash payment can still be edited until the order has been finalised.

## Perform an Afterpay refund at POS

Refunds for Afterpay at POS can be performed in multiple ways:

**Using Shopify?**: View the detailed step-by-step guide for refunding Shopify orders paid through Afterpay here.

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | **Description** |
| Integrated refund using the original order | This is ideal when the Afterpay payment was processed directly within POS using the integrated method. The reference number has been stored against the sale (visible on the invoice). |
| Blank refund i.e. refund without the original order | If the original order wasn't processed through Retail Express POS, or the original order number is not known, a blank refund can be processed |
| Partial refund | Where a transaction was processed for a sale where only part of the payment was through Afterpay |

> **Note:** Refunds of Afterpay can only be processed through Afterpay itself i.e. the refund cannot be processed in exchange for cash.

[Process a refund (with the original Sales Order number)](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/536-using-afterpay-barcode-integration-to-process-sales-and-refunds#refund-afterpay-original-order)

An Afterpay refund processed from the original Sales Order number follows the standard return process.

**Related: **For detailed Refund instructions refer to the POS - Process a Refund/Return or Exchange article

## To Process the Refund:

**Step 1:** Follow the **Refund **process until the refund product/s and value are populated into POS. Click the **Payment **tab![afterpay_refund_paymentstab.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppiROEAY.jpg)

**Step 2:** Click the **Afterpay** Payment Type to load the amount to refund and prompt the Afterpay integration to load![afterpay_refund_paymenttender.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppj2SEAQ.jpg)

**Step 3:** POS will connect to Afterpay and process the refund automatically.![afterpay_processing (1).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppj2XEAQ.jpg)

**Step 4:** The **Transaction Complete **message will be displayed. Click **Finish**.![afterpay_transactioncomplete (1).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppj2cEAA.jpg)

**Step 5:** The sale will be updated to show a negative amount and 0 balance. Click **Finalise**![afterpay_refund_paymenthistory.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppiRzEAI.jpg)[Click to enlarge](https://ress.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/360001591715/afterpay_refund_paymenthistory.png)

**Step 6:** Customers will be able to view the refund when they log into their account online.

## [Process a Blank Refund (without original Sales Order number)](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/536-using-afterpay-barcode-integration-to-process-sales-and-refunds#afterpay-blank-refund)

A refund without an original Sales Order number is referred to as a "Blank Refund".

**Related: **For detailed Blank Refund instructions please see the POS - Blank Refunds article.

An Afterpay blank refund follows the standard Blank Return process, however it still requires the original Afterpay Reference number (this can be obtained by the customer on their account history online).

### To process the refund:

**Step 1:** Follow the **Blank Refund **process until the refund product/s and value are populated into POS. Click the **Payment **tab![afterpay_refund_paymentstab (1).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppj2hEAA.jpg)

**Step 2:** Double click the **Afterpay** Payment Type to load the amount to refund and prompt the Afterpay integration to load![afterpay_refund_paymenttender (1).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppiAIEAY.jpg)

**Step 3:** Enter the original **Afterpay Reference Number **and click **Confirm **to begin processing the refund.![afterpay_refund_originalreferencerequest.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppj3fEAA.jpg)

**Step 4:** The refund will be processed and the **Transaction Complete **message will be displayed. Click **Finish**.![afterpay_transactioncomplete (2).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppj37EAA.jpg)

**Step 5:** The sale will be updated to show a negative amount and 0 balance. Click **Finalise**![afterpay_refund_paymenthistory (1).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppj1uEAA.jpg)

**Step 6:** Customers will be able to view the refund when they log into their account online.

## [Process a Partial refund (with original Sales Order number)](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/536-using-afterpay-barcode-integration-to-process-sales-and-refunds#afterpay-partial-refund)

POS and Afterpay support multiple refund Payment Types being used within a single transaction (only one of these types can be Afterpay). Afterpay is used to make a partial refund against a transaction with the remainder refunded using an alternate payment type e.g. half of the sale refunded with Afterpay, half with cash.

> **Note:** The original order number is required for this process.

### To Process a Partial Refund:

**Step 1:** Follow the **Refund **process until the refund product/s and value are populated into POS. Click the **Payment **tab![afterpay_refund_paymentstab (2).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppj4EEAQ.jpg)

**Step 2:** Double click the **Afterpay** Payment Type; the amount paid by Afterpay will be automatically retrieved from the original sale.![afterpay_refund_partialafterpaytender.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppiO5EAI.jpg)

**Step 3:** POS will connect to Afterpay and process the refund automatically.![afterpay_processing (2).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppi9dEAA.jpg)

**Step 4:** The **Transaction Complete **message will be displayed. Click **Finish**.![afterpay_transactioncomplete (3).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppilcEAA.jpg)

**Step 5:** The sale will be updated to show a negative amount and 0 balance.![afterpay_refund_paymenthistory (2).png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppiMOEAY.jpg)

**Step 6:** Process the rest of the refund using one of the remaining available tender types e.g. Cash.![afterpay_refund_partialcashtender.png](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/image.05T5g00000ppj5HEAQ.jpg)

## Frequently Asked Questions

[Processing Exchanges with Afterpay](https://galaxy.maropost.com/kb/articles/536-using-afterpay-barcode-integration-to-process-sales-and-refunds#exchanges)

Afterpay doesn't support the modification of an order once it has been placed. You will be able to process partial or full refunds, but you cannot replace a product or add a new product to the order.

If you needed to exchange a product, it's recommended to refund the order and payment, and process a new order with a new Afterpay transaction.

Afterpay have further details available on their online [Help Centre](https://help.afterpay.com/hc/en-au).
