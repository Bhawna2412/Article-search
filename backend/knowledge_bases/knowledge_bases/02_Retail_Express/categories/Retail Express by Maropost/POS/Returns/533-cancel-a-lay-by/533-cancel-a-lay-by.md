# Cancel a Lay-by

Lay-bys can be cancelled if the customer doesn't want the product anymore, or if the Lay-by time has expired and the customer has not paid for the product.

## To refund a layby item to a customer:

1. Open **POS**
2. Click **Returns**
3. Enter a valid **User ID**
4. Use the filters to find the original sale
  If the sale isn't displayed, the user may be restricted from viewing sales for that Outlet.
5. Use the button in the **Select **column to select the sale with the product to be cancelled/refunded
6. Click **Next**
7. Enter the **Layby Qty to Cancel**
8. Press **Next**
9. Select a **Return Reason**
10. Click **Create Refund**
