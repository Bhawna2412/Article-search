# Key Features

You can leverage Retail Express core capabilities to manage all aspects of your retail operations efficiently, helping your business handle point-of-sale, inventory, multi-store management, and customer service from a unified platform. This comprehensive feature set supports end-to-end retail operations, which helps streamline business processes and maintain operational consistency across all locations.

Setting up and utilizing key Retail Express features enables your retail operations to optimize performance across all business functions, helping improve efficiency and customer service. This helps retail businesses maximize their platform investment by understanding and implementing features that drive operational improvements and support business growth.

Retail Express offers several key features to assist you in running your business more efficiently and to offer your customers more value.
