# Role-based Training Planner

To use the training planner:

1. Click [here](https://forms.zohopublic.com/virtualoffice112/form/RoleBasedTrainingPlan2022/formperma/6lSjyi9OloZnudyZ3zOPdTgYVrJt2nUCdctiBY0bRHQ) to go to the **Build your training plan** form.
  ![User-added image](https://us.v-cdn.net/6038474/uploads/attachments/migrated/Screenshot20241007at54117AM.05TJ100000HkoyQMAR.png)
2. Enter the **staff details** below
3. **Select the role/s** by ticking the associated box next to each role (a list of the content will be displayed)
4. Click **Create my training plan!**

You will be able to download the training plan as a PDF file to save and refer to later as required.
