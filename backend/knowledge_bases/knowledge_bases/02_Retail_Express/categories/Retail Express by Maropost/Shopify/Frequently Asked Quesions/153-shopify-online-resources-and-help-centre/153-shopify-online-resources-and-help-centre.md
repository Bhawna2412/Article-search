# Shopify - Online Resources and Help Centre

Once Retail Express has been connected to Shopify it's time to populate the online store.

Shopify has an online Help Centre available to guide users through the process; this same Help Centre is also directly available through the search tool from within admin.

[Click here to visit the Shopify Help Centre](https://help.shopify.com/)

Some useful links include:

- [Products](https://help.shopify.com/manual/products/)
- [Payments](https://help.shopify.com/manual/payments/)
- [Shipping](https://help.shopify.com/manual/shipping/)
- [Orders](https://help.shopify.com/manual/orders/)
- [Customers](https://help.shopify.com/manual/customers/)
- [Analytics](https://help.shopify.com/manual/reports-and-analytics/)
- [Productivity Tools](https://help.shopify.com/manual/productivity-tools/)
- [Marketing and discounts](https://help.shopify.com/manual/promoting-marketing/)
